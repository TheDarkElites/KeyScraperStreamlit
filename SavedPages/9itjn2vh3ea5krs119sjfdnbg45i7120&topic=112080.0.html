Its external material is dry oil skin. See the #materials section for more details on this fabric.
The bag has padded front and back. It offers a zipperless design - instead of a zipper it has a flap that is held in place by a strap (which is also an accent line) made out of dry oil skin.
The front is square stitched to give it a more qualitative look and feel.

The inner lining is made out of sturdy cotton canvas and is rub resistant. You have dry oil skin color options for the main body and accent line.
For the optional custom label you can choose one of the following materials: smooth, soft and nappa imitation leather.


More
If you are like me you have all your cables or tools flying around on the table or in the drawer or in the backpack - chaos.
Together with keebstuff (www.keebstuff.com) we designed a utility bag to hold all your precious cables, tools, etc. in a tidy way.
It is a rollable bag secured via cord - no zippers or flaps needed. Here you can stuff in your stuff in the different sized compartments - be creative.

The outer material is made out of dry oil skin. The inner lining is made out of sturdy cotton canvas and is rub resistant.
Check out the #materials section to get more details. As mentioned everything is held together with a cord.
Here you have the option between a colored imitation suede cord in a bunch of different colors or a real leather cord which is way sturdier.
The cord is held by a real leather patch, stitched with our Logo.
As a last customization detail there is a small leather strap at the other end of the cord where you can get your initials or whatever letters you like punched in - as long it consists of 0-2 letters.
2. M A T E R I A L S  A N D  C O L O R S

The soft imitation leather is a very soft fabric and feels especially nice in combination with the padded/cushioned parts of the keyboard bag.

The smooth imitation leather feels sturdier than the other leather types. It has a smoother surface and feels a little heftier.

The nappa imitation leather is somewhere in the middle between soft and smooth. It feels a bit sturdier than the soft but rougher than the smooth imitation leather.

For our Premium keyboard bags we use soft imitation leather. Its distinctive look and feel make it a very pleasing fabric to the eyes and skin.

The vintage imitation leather is a very smooth fabric. Its unique patterns give the keyboard bags a very unique and vintage look and feel.

For the Adventure and Split keyboard bags  and the Utility bag we use a fabric called dry oil skin. It is a high quality and slightly waxed fabric, which feels nice and is water repellent. It holds its form very well and frequent use gives it a beautiful look.

The canvas or inner lining of our bags is made out of sturdy cotton canvas, rub resistant and thus very durable. You have the options between uni colored and patterned styles.


For the cord of the Utility bag you can choose between imitation suede cords which come in various colors and real leather cords that comes in four different colors.
