Hi everybody,
I'm Bruce Dinh, a mechanical keyboard designer. Owned by ORI-CLUB, my personal studio.
I was the former designer of the Makeaboard team (MAB). Team MAB has a bad past when the team disbanded and was unable to complete the GB 60S keyboard - 2018. And yes, MAB team would have said it was scam.
Emphasize that I am only a designer and not allowed to make decisions about production and finance on team MAB.
 This is link my IC POST, which has detailed everything my story. There are also protests, some agree. Make sure if you decide to participate in GB ORI, you understand me and my story.

Money is in your hands, decide on your own. But I really want to go back to the cleanest.
 This time, ORI-CLUB is a very personal thing of my own, designed by me and I take all responsibilities. And also forever only one person belongs to ORI-CLUB, it's me.

ORI CLS-1 by ORI-CLUB

Ori X GMK Sumi

ORI CLUB's social network:

Discord sever
Instagram
Website

Design overview.
 This is a design I have wanted to make for a long time, especially the side profile combined by 2 materials // 2 different colors.
ORI CLS-1 inside isolate isolate custom gasket + leafspring plate switch, Infinity 87 PCB + daughter pcb by Mechlovin '.
The logo of the sun and the paper crane reach out to the sun, which means good luck and always want to reach for better and more perfect things.
The extra weight will have a combination of 3 materials to create a contrast and emphasize the sunrise.




ORI CLS-1 Features:
TKL Form
7° Typing angle
6xxx aluminum top and bottom, brass accent
Polished or sandblasted accent options
Isolated gasket sandwich mount
Leaf spring switchplate
PCB : ORI CLS-1 custom designed by Mechlovin'
Kit includes case, plate, PCB, custom gaskets, bumpon feet, case screws, Travel case ( same with TX bag )

Configuration:

 Case top and Bottom colors:

  • Anodized Burgundy

  • Winkeyless (with blockers)

  • Anodized Burgundy
  • Sandblasted Brass
  • Stainless Steel Polished
All options

Limited-run:
ORI CLS-1 will be limited to 25 Slot, using Raffle.
ORI CLS-1 raffle goes live at 10PM (GMT + 7) August 1st. The window to submit will only be open in 1 hour. One entry per person.

20 slots worldwide

ORI-CLUB is my own studio and is very new so if you have any questions please join the Ori-club Discord sever. Sever is the only manager, so you can text me directly.

Group Buy will be hosted on my website: www.ori-club.comMy website is still in the process of completion so please be aware of some minor errors (even the website is self-edited!)

* Limited 25 slots because my financial capacity is limited, and I want to have 05 slots opened at Local market to prove that I am clean in GB this time.
