Maxkey Berserk SA Group Buy - Now Available on Kono!

Base Kit


Novelties Kit


LINK TO GROUP BUY:
 Click Here to Join


After a long Interest Check, Berserk is finally available on Kono Store. Designed by thesiscamper and produced by Maxkey, this is a doubleshot SA profile keycap set that utilizes the new tooling developed for our previous group buy, Lime.

The base set is priced at $109.99 USD and shipping is auto-calculated based on your address. The Novelties Kit is $24.99.

This group buy began November 22nd, 2017 and will run through December 31st, 2017.

We anticipate beginning production in early January 2018 and will fulfill the keycap orders by late February, early March 2018.

The keycaps will be shipped in trays, included in the price.

To help kick off the group buy, we have also announced a giveaway that is entirely free to enter.

Giveaway Link


Participants in this giveaway must submit an email address to enter, but will not be entered onto a marketing email list. People that join this list will only receive notifications of future giveaways, as well as notifications when a winner is announced.
