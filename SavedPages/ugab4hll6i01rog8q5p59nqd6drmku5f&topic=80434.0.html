Reliving the classic series, GMK G80-9009HAU.

(NEW UPDATE)
We will be compiling the orders today, expect to receive invoices soon. Updates on the remaining orders will go up after we done compiling the orders, so everyone knows if they got it or not. Again, thank you all for your love and support in this groupbuy! Our biggest regret is not having enough to go for everyone who's interested.

Justin.

------------------------------------------------

hi everyone, we have receive a few requests of transfers.

we have to be fair to those in the waiting list, which means we will not entertain any request of transferring priority rights.
if those in the priority list orders something, we will invoice that person and ship to that person only. if we noticed the name of the person or the address is different or the paypal is different, we'll cancel your order.

we all know this is a nice set, and a lot of people wanted to get their hands on it. some asked as early as after the groupbuy ends. assuming if they are in the 6th position of the list, and one person from the first 5 didn't order the full set, it means #6 got the chance to get his/her full set. but what if someone from waaay down below in the list asked the person from the first 5 who didn't order to order for him/her, then in this sense, #6 was robbed of his/her full set. how would you feel if you are #6? we don't want that to happen, and hence we will try our best to make this a fair game to everyone.

we hope that everyone understands this and we hope that everyone will work together to keep this community a clean and faithful community of keyboard enthusiasts.

Justin.

FINALE Thu, 24 November 2016, 23:36:09

CLOSING DATE: 2 Dec 2016

We are finally coming to an end for this groupbuy despite the many hurdles and problems that we hit into along the way. A big thank you to all participants to make this a success! We appreciate your patience with us, and we are very sorry for the unwanted delays but FWIW we still manage to keep the time frame well within a year , we have tried out new methods and we have learned much from it, we'll improve ourselves and we hope to see you in our future groupbuys!

A big shout out to kekkon for taking the baton from me when my hands are full. Without him, we would probably be in a state much worse than this. He spend a lot of effort and time doing the sorting by himself when the rest of us couldn't due to various reasons. In the end, we are all very happy to see the new owners of the 9009 set proudly posting their photos!

As promised, we now will go to the leftover phase. There isn't much left, which we felt both thankful and regretful at the same time. Thankful because we manage to ship out *all* our sets, regretful because we don't have enough for everyone. Alright, the reaction I get from you all are a bit unexpected, so I think I'll explain and clear it up.

First of all, yuktsi is not buying 50 sets and clobber up all the MOQ. Geez.

As radio_killah guessed it correctly, I wanted to "finalize" whatever order we have first and then make orders with GMK.
Seeing how the orders are quite slow, I am guessing probably due to the fact that the MKB community's trust in the forum GB style is at it's lowest point, no thanks to the Miami Night's drama.
So if I could get the proof of purchase from GMK early, and post it up, and then re-opens the GB, it'll regain the momentum from the community as there is valid proof to show that this GB will not go south and that this GMK 9009 set will happen.

The spacebar kits are probably just 20-30% ordered from the MOQ, but I really like the color of the spacebar and I don't want to disappoint you guys (I'm sorry that I have already make some of you felt disappointed from my announcement of the premature order closing.) who bought the spacebar kits, I will make sure the spacebar goes into production.

Sorry again for causing the emotional turmoil

SURVEY FOR ISO USERS ONLY

Survey form

After running the new Cherry logo keycaps groupbuy, we are back this time with the GMK 9009|2016. The GMK 9009|2016 is inspired by the Reuters Battlecruiser (G80-9009HAU) – credits to sixty from DT for the awesome shots.

This groupbuy will be offered in 4 separate packs which supports ANSI/ ISO/ 1800/ HHKB/ winkey/ winkey-less layouts.

Main kit (140 keys) – $140.00
LED pack (4 keys) – $9.00
Esc pack (4 keys) – $6.00
Spacebar pack (1 pair = 7u + 6.25u) – $6.00 for ONE colorway (3B or 3C or L9 or U9)

New Renders *NEW UPDATE*
Main kit - $140.00

**NOTICE**
New updated render from GMK with correct colour for the 2 ISO key and 1 HHKB key, extra scoops F and J key and nips numpad 5.
**NOTICE FOR ISO USERS**
The 3 1u keys for ISO that is in modifier colour in the render will be change to follow the alpha colour in the end product.

LED pack (4 keys) – $9.00


Esc pack (4 keys) – $6.00


Spacebar pack (1 set = 7u + 6.25u) – $6.00 for ONE colorway (3B or 3C or L9 or U9)


About this group buy
Unlike most of the other groupbuy, this gb will proceed regardless whether the MOQ is achieved or not, simply because we love the vintage look of the Classic Beige (CB) colorway with a little hint of light colours.

The order will be placed with GMK by end of March. We hope to achieve a groupbuy with a short lead time, just like the Cherry logo pack.


Shipping
Flat rate $15 with tracking per main kit or full set (main kit + LED pack + Esc pack + Spacebar pack).
$10 if you order other kit(s) without the main kit.
If you order more than one main kit or full set, or a large quantity of the child kits, we will calculate the shipping cost upon checkout.


How to order
Order close!
*UPDATE* Realizing it may hold back people from joining this GB as ordering means paying the full amount at the point of checking out.
We will provide an alternative for all of you who have not join the GB yet.
You can opt to pay 20%, 30% or 50% of your total order first, and pay the remaining when we are at the sorting/packing stage.
Shipping will be held until the outstanding amount has been cleared off.
Why we do this is because we want to achieve a fast turnaround time for this GB and we require co-operation from everyone to make this possible.
Use one of these coupon codes if you can't/don't want to pay the full amount yet.

Deposit Refund Policy *NEW* (Tue, 15 March 2016, 03:13:05)
While we don't enforce a non-refundable policy (people still can trigger a dispute anyhow). We strongly encourage participants to not request for a refund as we use the deposits for the initial payment to GMK, while we pay the remaining, just so we could get the production to start as soon as possible.

NOTICE:
To those wanting to pay in RMB/CNY, can order from taobao. Yongjk is helping me to handle the orders in taobao.

MOQ
There will be 150 MOQ for each of the add-on packs but no MOQ for the Main set. Depending on the final order count of the Main set, the MOQ of the add-on packs may be renegotiated with GMK.
