SERIES 00 CNY HU (虎)

A bronzeware scripture inspired Series 00 to welcome the Year of the Tiger.



我们的团队祝大家新年快乐，恭喜发财，虎年吉祥！

The team from esc lab wishes everyone a happy Chinese New Year, fortune & wealth, and a prosperous Year of the Tiger!

esc lab is welcoming and celebrating the firey and feisty Year of the Tiger by releasing a special edition Series 00 CNY Hu (虎）made of Sandblast Bronze-tinted PVD Brass.

Inspired by traditional Old Chinese bronzeware seal script (小篆), bespoke calligraphy of the word 虎 (Hu, or Tiger) was designed and laser-engraved onto the familiar Series 00 sculpt.

Series 00 CNY Hu will only be available from the first day of Chinese New Year (春节) Feb 1st 2022 to Feb 15th 2022.



DESIGN









A BIT OF HISTORY

To Chinese people, 春节 and the fifteen days of festivities that come after are of family, gathering, tradition, renewal, feasts, and of course, red pockets.

As 春节 is a tradition shared by all three founders of esc lab, we wanted to share a piece of work that would allow us to pay respect to our heritage.

Chinese culture follows the Chinese zodiac, which assigns an animal to each year in a repeating cycle. Tradition also claims you share the characteristics of said animal.

2022 is Year of the Tiger, 虎. Those born in the Year of the Tiger are born leaders, courageous, love a challenge or competition, and have short tempers.

As someone with both parents being Tigers - I tend to agree. A lot.

Do you share your zodiac’s characteristics?
