« on: Wed, 20 January 2021, 10:53:33 »
Hey friends!
The time is here! Today we begin the Group-Buy for the Campsite!

"Park your Van in a Campsite!"


Campsite Raffle is CLOSED
Campsite FCFS Sale is CLOSED
Raffle was open until the 27th for 100 units. Winners will receive an email tonight with a code to buy the board at GB price. On February 2nd, all unclaimed boards and another 100 units were for sale FCFS on Walletburner.co.








Features:
  • Compatibility with all previously offered Minivan, Kumo, and Jetvan layouts.
  • Sandwich-style gasketed case with a sandblasted brass weight and 50A nitrile gaskets.
  • 4 degree typing angle.
  • Case alone should is 2.8lbs, or 3 built OG Minivans.
  • Choice of a polycarbonate half plate for extreme flex, a PC full plate for medium-high flex, or a sandblasted brass full plate for firmness. Each plate material is available in either a split spacebar or a full spacebar layout.
  • Currently available colors: E-White, Blue Steel, Bushido Red, Drank, Space Grey and Jet Black.
  • Price: $299 + tax and shipping.
  • Ketch PCB available at sale for +$40.
  • Expected shipping in September/October 2021.
  • Launching on January 20th, 2021.

Check out the Campsite listing on Walletburner.co!

Group-Buy Rules:
  • There will be two stages: (1) a Raffle Stage beginning today, January 20th, with the form open until January 27th for 100 units, and (2) a FCFS sale opening at noon on February 2nd on Walletburner.co for another 100 units.
  • Raffle winners will be posted here and emailed a randomly generated coupon code to make the product available for purchase.
  • 1 raffle entry per customer.
  • Limit 1 board per customer, with additional plates and PCBs available at purchase.
  • Botting orders will get rekt.
  • Raffle form CLOSED.

alright a quick update: as some of you may know from updates in the Discord, i decided to shift factories after some B-stocking issues from Cajal. the plan was to use the same factory but after discussing the issues with them, i think trying a different factory that has a great track record so far is the move. About a month ago, I green-lit 3 prototypes and anodizing sample from every color and finish in the buy. They are hoping to finish that prototyping run within a month then we will move on to the full GB, with a quoted 90 day turnaround time. as those who were with me through Cajal (or any GB, really) may know, these are just estimates, but if the prototypes are of sufficient quality then I think we can jump right into production. thanks to everyone for supporting the GB and let's crush this  Will send updates when any major movements occur.

Update: We received v0.2 prototypes last week, and everything, in general, looks great. We need to go lighter on the blue in this picture, but all other colors will be approved as-is. I will green-light CNC production this weekend. All PCB orders were placed last week (300 Ketch PCBs!), and will be here in the next month or two. Some small changes such as adding 1.5mm underneath the PCB to accommodate some of the bulkier aftermarket Minivan PCBs (and to allow higher level of flex on the half-plate models) have been integrated. Overall we are in good shape, even with the delay starting production.



I was nervous when I changed factories after seeing huge issues with Cajal production, but this is a step up in quality. In addition to the offered GB items, I am investigating adding PU and EVA foam travel cases, which if everything goes to plan will be provided to GB participants completely free of charge. Thank you for your trust, your support, and your enthusiasm with this project! Hyyyype
