by Captus Keycaps






Hello there!
The time has finally come, our very first Group Buy will go live on 29 May, we hope you like the final designs of these deskmats! Although titled as such, these are not exactly "minimalistic", however,  they do share a sleek and austere aesthetic and monocolored or essential palette. We hope you guys will find these mats interesting and worthwhile! All of these are priced at US$23, similar at each vendor in their corresponding currencies. MOQ is 100 for each color.
