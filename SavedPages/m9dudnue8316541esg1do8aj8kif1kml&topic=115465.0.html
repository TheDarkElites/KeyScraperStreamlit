DSS HAIL SATAN

Announcing DSS Hail Satan, a 40% keyset that supports most staggered 40% keyboard layouts, as well as Planck/Ortho. The set uses stock ABS colors from Signature Plastics, in the most evil profile available...DSS. Succumb to the fires of hell and join us in worshipping our Lord and Master. Adorn your keyboards with these devilishly crafted keycaps. All Hail Satan!!

Gallery: https://imgur.com/a/0lSJEo7

Here is the Group Buy Pricing:

The Group Buy runs November 28th - December 26th





* Base kit: $95 (MoQ 75)
* Normie kit: $45 (MoQ 50)
* Satan's Sack: $20 (No MoQ)
* Color codes: Black (NN), Red (RA)
* Doubleshot ABS in DSS profile (1-2-3-4-3 sculpt)
* Manufactured by Signature Plastics
* Trashcon Icons by Trashman
* Vendor: https://keyboardarcade.xyz/
* Estimated manufacturing completion date is April 2022
