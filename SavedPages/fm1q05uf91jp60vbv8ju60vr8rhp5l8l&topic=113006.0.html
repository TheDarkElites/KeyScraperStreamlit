Color matching:

Unfortunately Pantone doesn't sell plastic CMY chips so we are having to compare with paper. The magenta is off by quite a bit so we are looking to have that remade. The others aren't identical matches, but we believe they are close enough tonally that they can be made as-is, and this also has the benefit of not further delaying the whole set.

Pantone doesn't sell CMY Plastic chips, but I've asked them to sell me a magenta one, or at least one very similar to magenta. This is because in order to have magenta remade, SP needs us to send them another Pantone color code. If any of you have suggestions for a Pantone color in magenta, or very close, and has a matching plastic color chip, please do let me know.


-Nasp
