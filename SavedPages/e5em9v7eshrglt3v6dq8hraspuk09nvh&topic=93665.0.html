Current Status: Completed.

Prototype:



Album: https://imgur.com/a/hhljr
More Pics: https://imgur.com/gallery/5NiVe



Anodizing Colors (Silver will also be available):



Color Options:
  • Rose Gold
  • Gray
  • Black
  • Silver

Plate:
  • Brass or Aluminum
  • Sandwiched
  • Supported Layouts
  • I'm open to accommodating more layouts. Please contact me after raffle phase

PCB:
  • QMK
  • USB Type-C port for all connections
    • Left Half plugs into Computer
    • Left and Right Half plug into each other
  • In Switch LED Backlighting
  • Only switch soldering required

Price (fees + shipping not included):
  • Case - $350
    • 1x Top Left (Aluminum 6063)
    • 1x Top Right (Aluminum 6063)
    • 1x Bottom Left (Aluminum 6063)
    • 1x Bottom Right (Aluminum 6063)
    • 1x Weight Left (Brass)
    • 1x Weight Right (Brass)
    • 1x Screws for Assembly (M3 Hex Countersunk)
    • 8x 3m Bumpons (8mm round)
  • Plate - $40
  • PCB - $45


Please feel free to email me at: sx60keyboard@gmail.com with any personal questions

If anyone wants more photos of anything feel free to ask me as well. I'll happily take more photos!
