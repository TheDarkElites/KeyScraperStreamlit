Dodici




Product Page: https://merisi.works/collections/merisi-dodici

Group Buy Information:

Group Buy Date: October 1st
Group Buy Type: Raffle via Google Form
Entry Window: 24 hours
MOQ (min and max): 60 units

Available Extras:
- Carbon Fiber Plate
- Extra Adelais PCB
- Polycarbonate Wrist Rest

Price (USD):
- Dodici Kit: $565
- Extra Aluminum Plate: $50
- Extra Carbon Fiber Plate: $58
- Extra PCB: $50 (Max 1 additional PCB per GB order)
- OPTIONAL Polycarbonate Wrist Rest: $95



Supported Plate Layouts::
- Standard Alice Layouts
- Stepped or Normal Caps Lock
- Split Right Shift
- Split Backspace





Specifications:
- Top mount
- Aluminum 6063 case
- Aluminum 5052 plate
- 7.8 degree typing angle
- Stainless Steel external weight (hairline brushed top, diamond cut edges, and sandblasted logo)
- QMK / VIA compatible matte black Adelais PCB from Mechlovin
- Weight: ~2580g (assembled)
- Medium TX style bag (Merisi.Works branded zipper pullers and microfiber cloth)




Color Options:
- Case: Black or Silver
- Plate: Black, Silver, or Red.
--Case renders

A separate shipping invoice will be sent out once the units are finished. The initial invoice will NOT include any shipping fees.
USPS for US addresses and DHL for internationals.
Buyers will be responsible for all shipping, tax, and import fees.

Lead Time:
Roughly 9 months with potential pandemic related delays.

A quick note on the wrist rests:
Since the material is softer than Dodici's aluminum, they are susceptible to micro scratching when placed against the keyboard.
If you have not used a polycarbonate product like this before, it is recommended that you place it gently up against the keyboard without rubbing or sliding up and down the aluminum case.
