Firmware available here until I get it into QMK https://github.com/worldspawn00/Firmwares

This GB is for the QMK compatible Pain27 v2

Don't let your memes be dreams!

Current status: All orders are shipped, EU orders are all in the hands of the proxy and should all be shipped shortly.  Extras are available in my shop.

Running the Group Buy through an Etsy shop for simplification.

US Store
Basic Kit: https://www.etsy.com/listing/684512424/preorder-pain27-kit-caps-and-switches
Frameable Kit: https://www.etsy.com/listing/684521462/preorder-pain27-frameable-caps-switches
Alpha(28) Addon: https://www.etsy.com/listing/684510800/preorder-alpha28-kit-does-not-include




Some of you may have seen the frameable version of the board at the Austin meetup a few weeks ago, I've now updated the design with FTIR LEDs under the spacebar so the engraving can be lit!

Main differnces between V2 and the original design by jonathan(uuupah) dropped the backlighting, added RGB LED connections under the spacebar for front and rear lighting, and moved the pro micro connection under the keys!













 The Group Buy will be for a PCB, a board kit, or a frameable kit.

PCB is $9



Kits include a case (with any necessary hardware), PCB and all the electronics (diodes, pro micro, RGB LEDs) and 6.25u stabilizer


Board kit is $35 for the basic case and $55 with the premium case, seperate cases are also available
https://www.etsy.com/listing/684512424/preorder-pain27-kit-caps-and-switches

Basic case is plate and bottom



premium case is a fully enclosed 5 layer acrylic sandwich case



Frameable kit is $45 for the basic and $55, add $15 for engraving with either kit, seperate cases are also available
https://www.etsy.com/listing/684521462/preorder-pain27-frameable-caps-switches

Basic is just the 8x10 (or A4 for metric peeps) plate and spacer layers


Premium includes a full rear enclosure



Pro Micros will come preloaded with the following config (no programming necessary to get started!)


Alpha28 kits, PCBs and/or cases will also be available as an addon at Group Buy pricing (details regarding the Alpha kits here: https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=99040)
https://www.etsy.com/listing/684510800/preorder-alpha28-kit-does-not-include

Group buy is open from April 16 to May 5th

Orders will be placed upon end date, Shipping targeted for the week of June 2nd
