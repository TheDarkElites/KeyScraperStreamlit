Sound Test:
POM Plate, Alpaca Switches, MKUltra Foam, GMK Metropolis



Group Buy Entry


More
One per person, no exceptions.


Raffle Google Form Link - 25 Slots

There will be two, 3-minute windows to enter the raffle at the above link. Both of these windows are to enter the same raffle, if you entered through one window there is no need to enter through the other as it uses the same form.

To lessen the chances of people double dipping, I have requested that people be logged into their google account to fill out this form.
please ensure that you are logged into your google account before attempting this form.

Window 1: Saturday, December 21 at 11a.m. EDT

Window 2: Saturday, December 21 at 6p.m. EDT

I will be using the time of New York found on this clock.

Raffle will be drawn after first come, first serve slots have been filled. Should you acquire a slot via FCFS and be drawn in the raffle, you will only be considered for one slot.


First Come, First Serve Google Form Link - 20 Slots

For first come, first serve entries there will be 20 slots.

Opens: Saturday, December 21 at 7p.m. EDT

For this one as well, I will be using the time of New York found on this clock.

Note: For the sake of fairness to all buyers, expect to have to fill out every field. This way, those who do and don't want extra components are required to do just as much reading and clicking. POM Plate Color selection order will be the same as that in the picture.

Auction - 5 slots

This will be conducted after all other slots have been delivered.


How it's gonna all go down...
steps will be crossed off of and added to this list.

Raffle slots will be drawn after FCFS slots are filled, repeated winners will be skipped and the next drawn name will acquire that slot.
Invoices will be sent out throughout Sunday, Dec 22.
All invoices are to be paid by 11:59pm EDT Monday, Dec 23. Unpaid orders will move on down the list.
Unpaid FCFS orders will be taken from the waitlist of FCFS, Unpaid Raffle orders will be re-raffled.
Order will be placed with factory.
Material order will be placed but production will begin after Chinese New Year.
shipment received, QC'd, Packaged
