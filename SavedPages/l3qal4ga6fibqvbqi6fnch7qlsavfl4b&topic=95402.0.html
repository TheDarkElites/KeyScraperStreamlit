Welcome to the group buy for JTK Red Riot and JTK Yolch

These sets are running in tandem, and will be produced at the same time, but they have separate MOQs (100 each).



JTK Red Riot is the first set of its kind. Red ABS blanks have NEVER been done before, and have long been a grail set of mine. I decided it was finally time to make it a reality.



JTK Yolch is a classic colorway that is finally coming to fruition. I've been assured by JTK that their latest revision to the legends will be an improvement over the current iterations. I have seen pictures, and I can assure that this is the case.

Set specifics:
Red Riot is 131 keys, Yolch is 148 keys.

Compatible layouts include:
  • TKL
  • Fullsize
  • Leopold 660M/C
  • 1800 (7u only)
  • HHKB (7u only)
  • 65%
  • 75%
  • Split space layouts
  • Partial 40% compatability
  • Tsangan (Winkeyless)

Prices vary slightly by proxy, but they are around $50USD for Red Riot and $95 USD for Yolch.

MOQ 100 for all regions, with price breaks at 200 and 300, as well as 400 for Yolch only. This may not be reflected in all proxies. I will keep this post updated with numbers.

All sets will include a JTK tray unless you select the trayless option on Switchmod (saves $5). Switchmod will have limited stock of JTK trays after the group buy is completed.


Timeline:
GB opens: May 1
GB closes: May 28 June 1
Production/sorting: 12 weeks 20 weeks (plus some more for RR)
Shipping to vendors: 2-4 weeks
Vendors ship to you! <-- We are here

Expected ship date: August 15th

Actual ship date: Yolch December 1

Red Riot March 8
