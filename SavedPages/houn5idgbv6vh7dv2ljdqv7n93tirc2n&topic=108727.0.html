[GB] TGR 910 Replacement PCBs and Plates
« on: Wed, 23 September 2020, 01:35:54 »
Replacement QMK PCBs and plates for TGR 910 series.



Initial prototypes. Final product will be identical to those in renders.


Replacement PCBs and plates for the TGR 910 are now available to order.  Adjustments to the plate and PCB have been made according to input from the IC.

PCB designed by kacklappen23. The PCB retains the same stab orientations so compatibility is maintained stock 910 plates. Flex cuts are added in this PCB for added compliance for softer plates and have been modeled after those on the OTD 356 mini. 1.6mm FR4 construction, Hirose Electric Mini USB for maximum longevity.

Plates designed by me. Plate border taken from the original 910 plate files provided by yuktsi. ISO and split space layouts omitted for better fitment. Stabilizer orientation has been retained so that functional PCBs can be rebuilt using this plate. Available in black anodized aluminum, polycarbonate, and sealed brass.

Available Here. GB ends Friday, October 2nd
