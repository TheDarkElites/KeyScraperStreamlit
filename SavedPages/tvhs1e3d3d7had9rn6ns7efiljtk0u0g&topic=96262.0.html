SA KOBAYASHI
Groupbuy


USA :    https://novelkeys.xyz/collections/frontpage/products/sa-kobayashi-gb
EU :    https://www.candykeys.com/product/gb-sa-kobayashi/
Asia :    Coming Soon


KITS




Alphas - Includes Novelties



Modifiers - FOR 100%, TKL, 65-60%, Whitefox/Tsangan



40% Modifiers for Planck, JD45 and other 40-45% Boards



INFORMATION


From the creator of SA Grand Budapest, Madēo is back with his latest design inspired by Wes Anderson's Isle of Dogs.
This is a fully sculpted SA set with the rows being 1-1-2-3-4-4.
The set is split into 3 kits - Alphas/Novelties, Modifiers, and 40%'s.


Off-white: WCK
Red: OAV
Yellow-Orange: YBP
Brown: TGG
White: WA

 

MOQ is 100 for the Alphas and Modifiers. 50 for the 40%'s.

GB will run until July 20, 2018.
