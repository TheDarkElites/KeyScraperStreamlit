RAMA WORKS U80A + SA Jadeite Alphas and Modifers

What is Jadeite?
More
Jadeite is one of the two mineral species that fall under the classification of jade (the other one is called nephrite).

___________________________________________________

And the theme?
More
This past winter, I had the opportunity to travel to Taiwan with my S/O as well as my Taiwanese friend and his S/O. During that time, I got to experience all there was to do in Taiwan. One of the most memorable places we got to go to was the National Palace Museum. I was fortunate enough to see the Jade Cabbage, which this set is based off of. Yes I did IC KAM Jade but this set will probably replace that.

The image I took of the Jade Cabbage is below.
