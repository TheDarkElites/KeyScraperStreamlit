The group buy will go live on October 19th, 2019 at 9am EDT worldwide! We expect shipping to conclude in March, 2020.

Vendors and Quantities

We're very proud to announce we've partnered up with CannonKeys in the US, MyKeyboard in the EU, and ILUMKB in the AP/China market to handle all sales of the Iron165 SE.

Founder's Edition

The Founder's Edition presale is already over. Grats to the winners!

Just like the SE, updates will posted here throughout the group buy. Thanks again for the support!


Iron165 Features
  • 7 Degree Typing Angle
  • Full Gasket Isolation Mount
  • Low-profile Front Height (less than 17mm)
  • Seamless Design
  • USB-C daughterboard, centered (designed by ai03 and friends)
  • Plate Optimized for consistency on every key press (see analysis below)
  • PCB compatible with QMK, and hopefully VIA support (designed by Upas)

Founder’s Edition Features



  • Full copper base, satin finish, clear-coated
  • Copper plate, satin finish, clear-coated
  • Copper blocker badge, satin finish, clear-coated
  • Stainless steel top shell, available in E-white or E-black
  • Precision-cut foam between PCB and plate by MKUltra Corp
  • Limited to 26 serialized boards
  • Custom hard carrying case
  • $885 USD

Standard Edition Features



  • Aluminum base, anodized
  • Large brass weight, satin finish, clear-coated
  • Aluminum top shell, anodized
  • Aluminum blocker badge, anodized
  • Brass plate, satin finish, clear-coated
  • Accented colors (top shell will be one color, badge and base will be another)
  • Custom hard carrying case
  • $475 USD / 480€ inc/VAT

Standard Edition Colorways
  • Plum with Black base and badge
  • Navy with Silver base and badge
  • Graphite with Silver base and badge
