Click here to join this GB if you have an interest.

FEATURES:
  • 2-in-1 design, both MX and Kailh box switch compatible
  • Made of Aluminum, CNC processed and anodized

Color options: Black, E-White, and Grey.
Price: $30 for one popper.
Quantity for Round2: Total 70 Go-poppers. 40 in Black, 20 in E-White, 10 in Grey.
Shipping: All orders are shipped 3 days after the end of GB.

TIMELINE
Starts on Aug 26, 2021 EST
Closes on Sep 7, 2021 EST

PRICING
The estimated price per switch opener should be US$30, including the global shipping fee.
Getting two Go-poppers would be US$60 with no extra costs, getting 3 Go-poppers would be US$90 with no extra costs, and so on.
*Limited 2 purchases on each color per order.

PAYMENT
Paypal transfer is the primary payment that is available at the moment. A Paypal digital invoice will be sent to you via email after we collect all the information required by the logistics for the shipment. Once the payment is confirmed, we will ship out the switch opener when it is all set. A notification email will be sent out to you with a tracking number after the package is shipped out.

Cancellations are accepted up to 48 hours after the payment is received. The request will be gathered and submitted to deliver the best delivery time to members, making all sales final. Be sure to check the discussion page for updates : )

PACKAGING
Custom EVA foam with cardboard shipping box. We will make sure that the opener will be fully protected and stay fixed in the box.

SHIPPING
All orders will be conducted and shipped by entergogo directly from the factory.

IMPORTANT
Defective items need to be reported to us via email (proof included) within 10 days of receiving the item for free replacements.
