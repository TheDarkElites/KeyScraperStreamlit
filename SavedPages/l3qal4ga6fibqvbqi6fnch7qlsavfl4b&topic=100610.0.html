[GB] Kepler (GB slots full)
« on: Thu, 09 May 2019, 22:21:26 »



Specifications:
  • Top: Aluminum 6063 (Anodized RAL 9006 or E-Black)
  • Bottom: Brass (PVD Black for Black tops / PVD Silver for Silver tops)
  • Plate: Brass (PVD Finish)
  • Layout: WK and WKL
  • Mounting Style: Isolation Mount, 3-piece gasket (included)
  • Typing Angle: 7°
  • Front Height: 19mm
  • Connector: Integrated B-Series LEMO Socket (EGG.1B.305)
  • Cable: B-Series LEMO Plug (FGG.1B.305)
  • PCB: ARM Powered PCB
  • Packaging: Pelican V300 Vault Case
  • Price: $850
  • Units: 50 (max) + 20% extra for contingency

Add-ons:
  • Plates: Brass: $50, Carbon Fiber: $30, FR4: $20, POM: $40, and Polycarb: $40
  • PCB: Signature87: $45, H87a: $45, or h87alps: $48
  • Gaskets: 3-Piece Gasket: $25

LINK TO STORE

Preorder Time
  • Start: May 10
  • End: May 31
  • Spots: 50/50[

Lead Time: End of October

LINK TO STORE

To help reduce the pressure of choosing options and changes to orders, I have split the group for Kepler into two parts.
  • "Kepler" secures your preorder spot.
  • "Kepler - Options" defines your kit options.
If you would like to reserve your spot but choose your options later, checkout with "[PREORDER] KEPLER" in your cart.
You will be allowed to change your "KEPLER - OPTIONS" and any additional extras until the deadline.
