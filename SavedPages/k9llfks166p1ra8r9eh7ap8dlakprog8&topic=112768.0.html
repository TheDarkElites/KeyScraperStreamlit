Hello everyone, we are finally starting the pre order for Serratus on May 15th at 11AM Eastern Time.

There will be 2 different forms, 1 for Chrome Edition and 1 for Anodization and Powder Coating Editions. (E-coating White has been replaced with Powder Coating White)

Form for the Chrome Edition will open for 1 hour and form for Anodization/Powder Coating Editions will stay open for 24 hours, and the winners will be raffled, 10 spots for Chrome Edition and 50 spots for Anodization and Powder Coating Editions.

***You can enter for both Chrome form and Anodization/Powder Coating form. Chrome participants will be raffled first and non winners will then be raffled for Anodization/Powder Coating editions.***

More information about Serratus: https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=111866.0

What comes in each kit
-Top and Bottom Cases(same color)
-PCB(C type)
-Aluminum plate OR PVD Brass plate(additional cost)
-Polycarbonate plate
-PVD Brass Weight
-Bumpons, screws, keyboard bag with foam

Specifications
-6061 Aluminum
-10 Degree Typing Angle
-Sandwich Mount with 2mm Plate
-Top Case: Winkey or Winkeyless
-Front Height: 20.5mm
-Total Weight: About 6lb and 3oz/ 2.8kg Unbuilt(Case, Brass plate, Brass weight)
-Plate Materials: 1) PVD Brass 2) Aluminum 3) Polycarbonate
-Plate Layout: Ansi with Universal Bottom Row
-PCB: QMK/ VIA compatible PCB(USB-C type) made by Mechlovin'. Also compatible with USB mini-b a.87-layout PCBs and USB-C type PCB with the connector on the upper side
-Brass Weight Bar and Brass Plate: PVD Coating to a Mirror Finish

Anodization Edition
-Top & Bottom Case: Burgundy, Blue Gray, Silver, Black
-Aluminum plate(Black, Silver, Red), +$20 for PVD Brass plate(Gold, Silver, Black, Rose Gold)
-Polycarbonate plate
-PVD Brass Weight: Gold, Silver, Black, Rose Gold
-PCB: Mechlovin' PCB(USB-C type)
-Custom Carrying Case, Case Foam
-569 USD

Powder Coating Edition
-Top & Bottom Case: White
-Aluminum plate(Black, Silver, Red), +$20 for PVD Brass plate(Gold, Silver, Black, Rose Gold)
-Polycarbonate plate
-PVD Brass Weight: Gold, Silver, Black, Rose Gold
-PCB: Mechlovin' PCB(USB-C type)
-Custom Carrying Case, Case Foam
-619 USD

Chrome Edition
-Top & Bottom Case: Chrome Plating Finish
-Aluminum plate(Black, Silver, Red), +$20 for PVD Brass plate(Gold, Silver, Black, Rose Gold)
-Polycarbonate plate
-PVD Brass Weight: Gold, Silver, Black, Rose Gold, Rainbow(multicolor PVD finish, only for chrome edition)
-PCB: Mechlovin' PCB(USB-C type)
-Custom Carrying Case, Case Foam
-Limited to 5 10 units
-769 USD

Extras
-PCB: Mechlovin'(USB-C type): 55 USD
-Plate: Aluminum: 45 USD, Brass: 65 USD

Payment method and invoice deadline
Raffle winners will be invoiced with Paypal and prices are not inclusive of Paypal fees. Invoices will be due within 24 hours of receiving.

Shipping fee and time
We assume to start shipping on late August and shipping cost will be calculated based on your location. Shipping invoice will be sent after production is finished.

Join us in the Discord for more pictures and updates!
IG: http://instagram.com/keynergy.co
