SHIPPING COMPLETE

Ice Box Round 2 is here!

When: Until January 18, 2020

Where: https://robotcandy.company/products/ice-box-artisan-case

Complete Kits are $99.99 plus shipping



Ice Box Topre and MX caps


Ice Box is a 70 slot (10x7) Artisan keycap display, storage and transportation box

Features:
  • 70 cells for MX and Topre keycaps 
  • Machined from frosted polycarbonate 
  • Friction fit acrylic lid 
  • Rubber 3M Bumpon feet for traction & stability 
  • Foam insert to prevent cap movement during travel 
  • Two elastic straps for extra security and comfort when traveling (black or blue options) 
  • Designed to securely store Topre caps by accounting for their longer stems 
  • Optional aluminum legs to angle the case for display (20° or 15°) 


Ice Box with bands and tilting legs

Specs
  • Cell size: 0.78" x 0.78" x 0.91"
  • Cell spacing: 0.079"
  • Outer dimensions: 1.23" x 9.29" x 6.71"

Quote from: ranonranonarat on Mon, 06 January 2020, 18:06:04
Interested on this but I've seen a few comments on lid tolerance issues. Will this be fixed down the line?

The lids were designed to fit tightly. A small number of people in the round 1 group buy expressed concern (no one at any meetup going back to May of 2019 who got to handle the case gave this feedback), so I've been tinkering with the lid cuts since depending on which side is down, the lid can be slightly tighter.

In round 1, I had all the lids before the cases arrived. Moving forward, I will be carefully test-fitting lids in smaller batches during QC so that no one feels like they're too tight.
