GROUP BUY: September 3rd – October 1st 2021
Discord Instagram

GMK Panels aims to incorporate color-blocked art on keycaps.

The set primary draws influence from the VW Harlequin but also from Piet Mondrian, LEGO BRICKS, and 90s aesthetic.

Trailer
Trailer by Fresh. Live on Sept 3rd! 2pm EST.


Vendors

NA: NovelKeys_
EU: Oblotzky Industries
UK: Proto[Typist]
AUS: Daily Clack
ASIA: zFrontier
SEA: iLumKB
CA: DeskHero

Prices

Estimated shipping in October 2022.

Base Kit
Base kit features support for 60, 65, 75, TKL, HHKB, Alice (with spacebars), 1800, Full size, and Minivan 40% keyboards.

Additionally, the extra bars and Yo keys can be used as an accent kit with a WoB, BoW, or other keycap set to your preference.

Purchase anytime during sale window with confidence. No sales data will be released for this project.
