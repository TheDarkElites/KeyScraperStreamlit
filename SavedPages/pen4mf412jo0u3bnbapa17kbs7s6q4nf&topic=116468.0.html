﻿﻿ePBT Rin
♫And it's a long way forward, so trust in me~♫

Group Buy: March 11 - April 11 2022
Estimated Delivery: Q1 2023 (exact dates to be announced after GB ends)

﻿﻿The Inspiration
This set was inspired by the colours of the main character of an anime-style music video, for one of my favourite songs (Shelter), by two of my favourite artists (Porter Robinson & Madeon)!

Hey everyone! Been a bit quiet around here, but just wanted to give a small update for May:
- Our pantone chips (that were sent to KBDFans at the beginning of April) got returned this month due to the lockdowns and them not being able to deliver in that area We are checking if they are accepting packages again and will try again in the coming weeks.
- The design file is completed and was sent in to KBDFans!
- The good news is, for the NorDe kit, we were able to add in the 2 extra keys (the 4 and 6)! And the "E" key was also corrected in the final design file
- Will be following up with the last couple of artisan collabs to confirm if they have a sale date/time frame (sorry for the delays!) MelonKeys has given an estimate of mid-late June for their Rin collab so I will update once the dates are confirmed!
- Still waiting for updates on the custom aviator colour for the cable collab
