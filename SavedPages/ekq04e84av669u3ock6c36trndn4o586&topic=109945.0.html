  • 80 Units:
  • 40 Space Gray w/ red plate: $420 + Shipping

Primary Engraving Option DISCOUNT CODE: DEFAULT ENGRAVING

Secondary Engraving Option DISCOUNT CODE: IGRIS ENGRAVING

No Engraving Optional DISCOUNT CODE: NO ENGRAVING

Prototype Pictures in Space Gray and Red





Technical Specs & Information
  • WKL TKL
  • 0.25mm engraving
  • 20mm tall flat keyboard with screw in cone feet to adjust the angle. 
  • Top Mount only (not compatible with gummy-worm style gasket)
  • 2mm polycarbonate plate and 1.5mm aluminum plate Koala/Whale/Jane V2 compatible plate
  • 15 mm feet = 8°
  • H87A compatible
  • 6063 aluminum
  • Available in Space Gray, Red, and Black

Buy here: https://invis-board.myshopify.com/

Special Thanks to Biso, Dale, Ip, Velvet, Shaolin, Alessio for encouraging me to pursue cone feet, and special thanks to Hiney, Pwner, and Zekkin for advice in CAD.  Shoutout to #kb-kustoms. 

More Prototype Pictures in Space Gray with NCR dyesubs courtesy of Maxf






Changes from Prototype to production model:
  • Internal rails to try to prevent warping
  • Moved bumpon locations
  • Alignment rails changed to alignment pins
  • I like black colored screws, you guys like black colored screws? I got some regular steel screws if you want that instead, pm me.
  • Fixing gap that makes installing plate harder, plate should be aligned properly now
