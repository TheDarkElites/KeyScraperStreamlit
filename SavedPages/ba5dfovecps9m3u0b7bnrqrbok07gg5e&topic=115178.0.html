Hi all,

When i received color samples of this set and looked at a single color sample cap,  the alpha color (GR1) is fine to me so i approved.
After i received the full base kit samples and i realized that the products in reality is a little bit different from the renders in different lightning environment.
Renders are always made in a perfect lightning environment.So the alpha color (GMK GR1)is a little lighter than the real products.
I tried to take some photos in different lightning environment. The alpha color of this set is influenced by different light.
Colorway of alpha is GR1 and the accent is WS1.

These photos are taken in lightning environment. Shotted by my photographer friend.
https://imgur.com/a/QP5LT2b




These photos are taken in nature light environment. Shotted by kbdfans
https://imgur.com/a/54PKSbv



These photos and the real products still have color difference, but I hope it can give you a reference.
If you are still excited about this set, please be patient for a while, the production is already 70% finished
If you're not satisfied with the color, contact your vendor and KBdfans for a refund.
