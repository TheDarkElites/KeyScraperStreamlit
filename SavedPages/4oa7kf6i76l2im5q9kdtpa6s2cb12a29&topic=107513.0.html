This. I would imagine OP could run buys for these mats indefinitely as a recurring buy like Dixie Mech has for his designs.

That may defeat the purpose of the GB style and make them not as sought-after or unique, but the mats are so well-designed they could be appreciated by people who just like the aesthetic.

Note that when it comes to the “purpose” of a GB, it’s usually less about creating a rare item and more about ensuring that a niche item has enough demand for a production run.

Those goals aren’t mutually exclusive, necessarily, as the belief something will be “limited” can drive demand. However, a designer shouldn’t make design decisions benefiting the secondary market at the expense of the primary market. If they do, they’re leaving money on the table.
