FAQ
Q: Why the Price is really expensive?
A: It's hard to get cheap price when you are going in small number. Especially for the PVD SS.

Q: Is there any other plate material for extra?
A: I there would be alu and brass for additional. BUT we will share the plate files at the End.

Q: Can i take more than 1 Board?
A: Please be consider if you are submitting every color and win 2 of them you can't cancel the other 1.

Q: ISO Support?
A: Yes for sure!

Q: What PCB?
A: We are developing some TKL PCB that has FLEX cutout on the alpha.

Q: Extra Plate and PCB ?
A: There will be additional Plate and PCB

Q: Extras?
A: We are going to order more than requested number to keep this product in A Stock Condition. So there would be an Extra! [Extra will be raffle too]

Timeline: Estimated for 3 months to shipping day.
Shipping: Shipping from Indonesia through DHL or EMS
