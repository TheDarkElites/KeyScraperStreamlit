Hello GH, we're happy to work with Okonomiyaki to bring some really nice pink switches. Details are below:

Link: https://monokei.co/collections/monokei-x-okonomiyaki
Design: Okonomiyaki
Manufacturer: JWK
Vendors: monokei , DailyClack
Renders: motohiro
Sale Format: GB (no limits) , 100 pcs / pack
GB date: 30 July 2020 - 15th August 2020
Timeline: Delivery in Q4 2020
Price: See below



Pinoko Linears:

  • Linears (65g springs)
  • POM stem
  • PCB mount (5 pins)
  • LED compatible
  • Polycarbonate housing
  • Each pack comes with 100pcs
  • Price: SGD85 / 100pcs




Okomochi Tactiles:

  • Tactile (67g springs)
  • POM stem
  • PCB mount (5 pins)
  • Nylon housing
  • Each pack comes with 100pcs
  • Price: SGD85 / 100pcs





Okomochi Deskmats:

  • Design: Shirogane
  • Dimensions: 900mm x 400mm x 4mm
  • Materials: high quality fabric, rubber
  • Edges: stitched
  • Price: SGD30
