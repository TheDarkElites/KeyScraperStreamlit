Hello GH,   

As you might know, a GB for the JTK PoW started and it will be handled by the known zFrontier. The PoW was runned already in the past with the JTK debut and due the high request and the succesful IC, zF decided to bring it back!

To join the GB:  
  • INTERNATIONAL LINK
  • CHINA LINK
   
Price: $63

Extras: 2 JTK polycarbonate trays + JTK costar inserts

Group buy end: September 1, 2017

Estimated ship date: October 2017 

Other: Free shipping worldwide on orders over $100 (you can mix GBs with non-GBs products) 

Base set: (141 keycaps, Cherry profile 1.5mm ABS doubleshot) 


Also polycarbonate trays (x2) 
 

... and JTK costar inserts   
 



Notes: 
Some of you might know this, initially we wanted to add extra-modifiers to the GB, aka PoW modifiers to also make the whole set White with Purple legends. 
However for now this is not going to happen, at least with this GB because zF is concerned that the MOQ will not be hitted for that kit. 
However if there is enough interest and if this GB will be successful, we might consider to run a GB later for only extra-modifiers for this set (nothing planned yet)

More pics and renders:







Here are the IC forms:

Reddit IC: https://www.reddit.com/r/mechmarket/comments/6qy4n9/ic_jtk_purple_on_white_the_return/
GH IC: https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=90843.0

Note: I did the IC on reddit while lucidreams did the IC here on geekhack, we are doing this togheter.

If you have any question, please ask below, me and lucidreams will try our best to answer everyone. 
Thanks!       


UPDATE 11/08 We are currently sitting at 75 purchases, so we are more or less around 1/2 of the MOQ
UPDATE 16/08 We are at 78 units still      
UPDATE 24/08 8 days left. We are at 80-81 orders, stuck for a while now, We needed at least 130-140 orders initially but at this point i don't think we can hit 130+ orders. However zf wanted to cover the remaining part to hit the moq to not let this GB fail but it could be good if we could reach at least 100 orders.
