INTRO


  Chroma is the new 60% keyboard around. With tops of room for customization, and some premium features, Chroma hopes to be a keyboard that is perfect for beginners and veterans alike.

Aside from producing an awesome keyboard, our goal is to release a product platform that we can stick to for restocks, replacements, and new iterations. Often, new members of the hobby have issues with the cyclical nature of items, lack of post sale support, and complicated distribution makes it hard to newbies to decide to begin their keyboard journey.         

HIGHLIGHTS

PREORDER DATES:   Now - June 14th
Aluminum Body
Brass, Copper, and Aluminum Backweight Options
Wide Color selection, including Cerakote options
Wired (Wireless version planned)
ANSI and ISO Layouts
O-ring mounted
Typing Angel: 7

PRICING

Chroma complete includes TX stabs, Vertex switches, and its very own GMK Chromatic keycap set
