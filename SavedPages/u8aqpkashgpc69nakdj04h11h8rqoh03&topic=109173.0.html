[GB] Karina - 37 keys, 2 encoders, and an LED bar! - Now moving into production
« on: Tue, 20 October 2020, 16:32:08 »

(formerly the MisonoPad)

GB is now closed, but you can preorder extras at https://p3dstore.com/products/karina-group-buy!
Week 1 Post-GB update: PCBs have been ordered, cases are almost done. PCBs are anticipated to be a month out. Knobs arriving soon...

Week 1 GB Update: 37 kits sold!
Week 2 GB update: 53 kits sold!
Week 3 GB update: 78 kits sold!

KIT INCLUDES:
P3D 3D printed case in your color of choice.
FR4 Split Space Plate
Frosted LED diffuser
Acrylic case cover
Fully assembled and flashed PCB
2 20mm Knobs
2 detented rotary encoders
Hardware for case assembly and rubber feet

The Karina is a 35% keyboard meant to use as little keys as possible while still keeping a practical amount of functionality for those who aren't used to typing on 40% boards.





Layout


(Space, backspace, and meta can be replaced with a 6.25u spacebar with the purchase of the 6.25u spacebar plate.)

Features rundown:
Full QMK support
0 degree typing angle
USB-C
Two rotary encoders, using a 20mm knob
Recessed knob wells
19-bit RGBLED light bar
Option for a fullsize 6.25u spacebar
Buttons on rotary encoders to bind keys to
FR4 plate
Case manufactured by P3D
Two dedicated layer keys
.5u uniform stagger
Optional Etch-A-Sketch mouse mode
