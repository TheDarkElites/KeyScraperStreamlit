GMK Seafarer

"Master of the seven seas"

This is the GB post for GMK Seafarer, I hope you enjoy your stay.
GMK Seafarer was originally inspired by our Navy sailors on the high seas, but has transitioned to a broader theme of sailing and nautical adventure.
Here is the IC post for more renders/details: https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=110169.0

Colors

COLOR SAMPLES



More
GMK WS1, RAL 5003


GB Period: April 16 - May 16 (Some vendors will be at earlier times than others. The US launch time will be 3pm EST)

Here is my Discord server. This is where updates and teasers are released first. This is the fastest way to contact me as well, just give me a ping if you have a question.

Renders

DISCLAIMER: Renders do NOT show the final product and how the colors will look in real life. I have attempted to match the renders as closely as possible to the color samples I have, and I will be color matching to the physical sample, and not the renders. Please understand that my monitor is not the same as yours, and differences in lighting and texture also play a role. Colors may seem different in the renders in relation to each other, but this is intentional as it is meant to mimic different lighting conditions.

Boards (left to right, top to bottom): F1-8x by Geon Works, Adelie by Abec13, Iron165 by S+R, Cypher by westfoxtrot, J-02 by Prototypist






Deskmats

Deskmats were made with the same theme and overall minimalistic look as the set. I didn't want the deskmat to be the main star of the show, just a decoration on the side that is both functional and pleasing to look at.
