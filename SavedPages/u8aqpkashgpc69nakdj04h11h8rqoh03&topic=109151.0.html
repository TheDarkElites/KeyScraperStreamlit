ISO Macro

Hello,
After the interest check, I’ve finally setup everything to start the group buy!
Group buy is ended, get updates here

The kit price is £50. The aluminium knob is £3 each.

Two configurations
Single encoder and dual encoders.



Colours

Galaxy Black


Galaxy Purple


Marble Effect


Vertigo Gray


Orange


Gentleman Gray


Specifications
  • QMK Firmware
  • VIA Supported (except encoder functionality)
  • USB-C
  • LED backlight
  • Integrated plate
  • ⌀15mm encoder knobs
  • PLA 3D printed enclosure and encoder knobs.
  • Dimension: W125xD61xH24 mm

What’s in the box
  • 1 x PCB
  • 1 x 3D printed PLA enclosure
  • 1 x 3D printed PLA rear cover
  • 2 x 3D printed PLA encoder knobs
  • 1 (single) or 2 (dual) x EC11 encoder(s)
  • 1 set of fastening
  • 1 set of rubber feet
Aluminium knob is optional and purchased separately


Things to note

Estimated shipping date is 3 months after group buy closes (Q1 2021). Please join the discord server to get the latest updates. Major updates will be posted on here and on the store page as well.

PCB is NOT hot-swappable. Therefore, it requires soldering for key switches and encoders.

Due to the nature of FDM 3D printing, layer lines and z-seams are unavoidable. Filaments contain glitters like Galaxy Black, Purple, Marble Effect and Vertigo Gray are very effective in hiding those artifacts, which makes them almost invisible. However, solid colors like Orange and Gentleman Gray, z-seams are located at the rear of the enclosure.


Finally, please join the Discord server, follow my Instagram and sign up the newsletter to receive more future update!
