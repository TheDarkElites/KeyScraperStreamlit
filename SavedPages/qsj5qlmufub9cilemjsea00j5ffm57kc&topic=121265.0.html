Yes, But where is the spacebar?

1 : There is an "invisible spacebar" by the use of the QMK Combo feature.
You hit "space" with your thumb by hitting both 'B' and 'V' keys at the same time ( So the location of where the space bar would be )
One of the advantage of combos is that it allows you to keep the same alpha layout as a standard keyboard and does not change your muscle memory.
You can also have a clicky key-switch on either 'B' or 'V' to have a sound and/or sensory feedback each time you hit the "invisible spacebar"

2 : 2U space bar on the bottom row is also possible with the solderable layout.
