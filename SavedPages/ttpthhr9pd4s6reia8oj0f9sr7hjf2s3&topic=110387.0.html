O V E R V I E W
We proudly welcome you to our 2nd Group Buy!
We ran a Group Buy for the first time in Spring 2020 and it was so much fun that we decided to run another Group Buy.
We hope you enjoy this process as much as we do!

First GB: https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=105047.0

Please read everything carefully in the following lines!

S T Y L E  O P T I O N S
You can choose between 3 styles: Classic, Classic Deluxe and Premium.

You can choose the colors and materials for: the main body, the outer pocket,
the inner material, the label and the accent line:


The inner material is made out of printed cotton canvas which is both durable and
nice to work with.

For the accent line you can choose between soft imitation leather and cork:


Custom Label
You can personalize your bag with an optional custom label. Here you can have stitched
up to 3 characters or numbers. The dots Between the characters are optional. You can also
choose the color of the label.


For the zipper we use YKK zippers, since from experience they offer the best quality.
Do not worry about your precious keyboard, the safety lip keeps it safe and sound:


The Classic
The Classic bag is made out of soft imitation leather (main body + outer pocket + accent line).


The Classic Deluxe N E W
The Classic Deluxe is our whole new creation. It is made of soft imitation leather, but offers additionally
a square stitching and padding for the outer pocket which gives it a more premium feel
and safety for your keyboard.


The Premium
The Premium bag is made out of soft imitation suede leather. It has a square stitching
and padding of the whole side under the outer pocket to give even more safety to your keyboard.

S I Z E S
You can order your bag in any size you like. Prices are accordingly.
For standard sizes we use the following keyboard dimensions:
Numpad: 9.5 x 15.5 cm
40%: 25 x 8.5 cm
60%: 30 x 10.5 cm
65%: 34 x 10.5 cm
75%: 32 x 13 cm
TKL: 36 x 14 cm

Example:


Our assumed height is always 5 cm:


Important
You should measure your keyboard/send us the exact measures of your keyboard if they exceed our standard sizes so that we can adjust your keyboard bag to it. So if for example you have a 65% keyboard that is 35 cm and thus 1 cm longer than our standard size (34 cm), please tell us so.
Price will stay the same (65% price).

P A Y M E N T
Via: Paypal
I will send you a Paypal-Invoice. Because of the Paypal fees for transactions and currency conversion ($ to €) please add 5% of the buying price (only for non EU buyers).
EU buyers please pay in € and add 2,49% paypal fee.

End price: Bag + (Label) + Shipping + PP fee

But shipping prices have normalized in the mean time.
Please be aware that there could be delays concerning shipping time due to the pandemic.

We will ship within 2-3 weeks of receiving your order depending on number of orders.
We make the bags in the order we receive the orders and ship them accordingly.
