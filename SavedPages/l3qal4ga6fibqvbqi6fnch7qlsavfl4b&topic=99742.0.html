Calm Depths is an incredibly beautiful set, orginally designed by CalmB4tehPwn - and one of my personal favorites. It has excellent contrast and a perfect pairing of blue and grey.

Kono Store Group Buy - https://kono.store/products/sa-calm-depths

 I actually began taking photos of mechanical keyboards because of this photo -



At Kono - we have the ability to make certain sets much more readily available, sometimes on a permanent basis. My goal is to accomplish this with Calm Depths in SA Profile - by putting together a group buy to be produced by Maxkey. This group buy will launch on March 7th, 2019, close on April 12th, 2019 and production time will take 10 weeks. Expected shipping is July 2019.

We plan to work with mykeyboard.eu as a European proxy and may include additional proxies later.

The group buy price will be $109.99 for a complete set.

Orders will ship from Kono's Utah based warehouse to the rest of the world - shipping is a approximately $10 in the US and $20 anywhere else in the world.

Honestly I think a lot of that is just the photography myself. If you go over to the SA Calm Depths groupbuy post from the previous run you'll find a decent amount of pictures of sets in various lighting conditions, including a similar slightly more washed out one from me.
