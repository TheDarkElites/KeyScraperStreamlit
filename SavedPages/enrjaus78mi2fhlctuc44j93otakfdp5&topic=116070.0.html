That's really a shame. This set is really nice, and personally I joined in for Base, Novelties, and 40s, but I don't think I'd want to get the set without all the kits I've ordered making it. With the numbers as-is I think it might be best to move this back to IC and figure out why people weren't interested. GMK lead times for sure play a part, but I think there's more going on here than just that. I do recall there being some feedback in the IC that went ignored. A few things to consider off the top of my head:

  • A good number of people are apprehensive about Kono as a vendor. Their reputation is not the greatest (I'm not saying whether or not it's warranted, but just saying how people feel)
  • There were some concerns about price. For example, GMK DMG R3's base kit had nearly the exact same coverage as Base + Numpad, however it was $125 rather than the $167 it is here
  • I don't think a full 40s kit was a good match for this set. Many sets are going with the 3 key physical support in base route since it's much cheaper and much more likely to run since 40s child kits don't really sell these days. I even brought up this concern during the IC (https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=112243.msg3102551#msg3102551), but it went ignored

I think the design you have here is great. The colors are very nice, and the novelties and deskmats are beautiful. However, I think there were some unaddressed issues regarding kitting and pricing that should have been address prior to moving to GB and are likely a leading factor in the poor performance.
