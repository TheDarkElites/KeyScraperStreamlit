KAT Classic Retro



Hello there, I'm incredibly excited to introduce you to KAT Classic Retro!
It's KAT, it's beige and it's got accents, what's not to like?


GB DATE
September 5th, 2023 to October 5th, 2023


ETA
Q1-Q2 2024

You may be asking, when can I expect to see this set in my mailbox? Great news though, is that all preperations for the set has been finalised, which means molds, colors and packaging has been approved and we are ready to start manufacturing as soon the the order has been place, and currently, there is practically no queue for the double-shot molds. This also means that unless we somehow see sales exceeding that of KAT Mizu (4000+ kits), we're looking at a manufacturing and packaging timeline of 3 months, with an additional 1-2 months of shipping with sea freight directly from the factory in China to your local vendor.


PRICING:



SPECIFICATIONS
Double-shot PBT
Alphas similar to GMK L9
Mods similar to GMK U9
Legends similar to GMK CR
Red accent similar to GMK 3C
Green accent similar to GMK 3B
KAT Profiles (5-4-3-2-1-1)
Compatible with MX style switches
1.6mm thick double-shot PBT Plastic


KITS








VENDORS
EU: KEYGEM
OCE: ALLCAPS
NA: DIVINIKEY
UK: PROTOTYPIST
SEA: MECHA MY
SCANDINAVIA: MAXGAMING


RENDERS










Samples in various Colortemperatures
