First thanks to everyone who supported us in our WongBox GB! It is now complete. Extras are available on our site.

Introducing the MAK WongBox Mini!




Building off the huge success of the MAK WongBox, the Mini is a 3x3 premium 3D printed artisan case with an acrylic lid, great for holding small collections, sets and grail caps. Pricing starts at $20.99 for the case and $5.99 for the stand. Orders of three or more cases will receive a $5 discount!

Join the GB here!

The group buy will run from 8:00AM PST July 24th- 12:00PM August 14th and run on makeyboard.com. Production will begin at the end of August. Length of production will depend on the number of orders, we will update with an expected date at the end of the group buy. We expect 2-4 months depending on volume.

You can check out our work and customer pics (tagged posts) on our Instagram.

Product Specs:
  • 3x3 artisan case
  • 37° display stand
  • 7.6cm x 7.6cm x 2.5cm
  • Standard colours: Black
  • Premium colours: Carbon Dust, Stone White, Titanium Dust
  • Acrylic lid colours: clear, smoke

Pricing

Standard colours
  • $20.99 per case
  • $5.99 per stand

Premium colours
  • $24.99 per case
  • $6.99 per stand

Extra lid
  • $6
