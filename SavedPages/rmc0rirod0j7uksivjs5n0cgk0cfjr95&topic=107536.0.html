[Timeline]

The Think6.5V2 GB will have an LE (limited edition) version, and an SE (Standard Edition) version, as detailed below.

[Limited Edition/LE FCFS High-Gloss Chamfer Version]



GB starts: Sunday, July 19, 20:30 EDT (Sold out 20:31)
Price: $449
GB Format: FCFS, limited to 88 sets for the 1U version and 88 sets for the 2U version. Limit to 1 per person.
GB Link: https://graystudio.club/products/limited-edition-think65v2
Notes: The shipping for LE has not been charged yet. Shipping invoices will be sent for the LE versions upon GB ends ($70). If you have purchased another SE board, the orders will be combined automatically and shipping for LE will be waived. Ships worldwide via DHL Express International.

[Standard Edition/SE Group Buy]
Standard Edition GB starts: July 20, 10:30 AM EDT
GB Format: GB will be open for 24 hours; GB ends on July 21, 10:30 AM EDT
    For those who missed the GB, we will make a capped number (TBD) of SE boards (with the option to purchase accessories) available for purchase when Alexotos live streams the e-white Think6.5V2 prototype giveaway build on his Twitch channel this Friday (July 24).
GB Link: https://graystudio.club/products/gb-think6-5-v2

[SE Price (PayPal fees included)]

Anodized Aluminum:  $349
E-white/e-White Dual/Schrodinger: $369
Clear Frosted Polycarbonate (PC): $379
The shipping fee for the first board is $70, each additional board is $20, ships worldwide via DHL Express International.

[1x Think6.5 v2 Package Includes all the follows]

1x case, top & bottom
1x badge (aluminum, lightbulb), with magnets installed.
1x Dual-color FR4 plate
1x 16 gaskets
1x PCB (Type-C, out of box VIA support, QMK compatible, ESD protection, 18 underglow RGB LEDs + 2 or 4 Badge LEDs, production version will be a physical switch to switch off the underglow LEDs)
1x weight
1x vibration reduction pad
1x noise reduction pad
1x LED diffuser(s)
1x screwdriver (Graystudio brand)
1x tweezer (always test the PCB before soldering!)
Screws, rubber feet, some backup SMD LEDs
Designer packaging
