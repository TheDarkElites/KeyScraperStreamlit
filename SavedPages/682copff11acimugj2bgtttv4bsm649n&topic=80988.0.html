Orders Closed
Hello and welcome to TheVan 44 Group Buy!



PCBs, cnc milled aluminum cases and plates, stabilizers, hardware and keycaps!

$164.00 for the kit (pcb, case, plate, stabilizers, and hardware)
$56.00 per set of keycaps

TheVan 44 is unique in that it was designed around the use of two function layers accessible from either side of the keyboard to maximize layer usability.

I am working with local manufactures to bring you a mostly made in USA keyboard.  The PCBs are printed in China and assembled in the US.  The case and plate are manufactured and anodized in Arizona.

This Group Buy is limited to 100 Kits.  We need to reach 50 kits for this to happen.

1. This buy will run from April 1st until April 30th, unless all 100 kits are claimed sooner.
2. From the end of the buy I will give everyone 1 week to pay their invoices.
3. Orders will then be submitted to manufacturers.  I have been quoted with 4-6 week lead time.  Of course this could go over by a week or two. 
4. Once I receive the goods I will be mailing out all the orders.  I'm expecting to have all orders sorted and shipped within a week.

Expected Ship Date of June 1 to June 22.

TheVan 44 PCB
TheVan 44 PCB designed by evangs supports a simple staggered layout with modifier keys surrounding the alphas.  The PCB works with TMK firmware and is fully programmable.  LED Backlighting is supported.  The PCB contains smd components and comes fully assembled.


TheVan 44 Case
TheVan 44 is housed in a custom cnc milled aluminum case and waterjet cut plate designed by evangs.  The plate and case can be anodized independently in the following colors: Black, Silver, Teal, Purple, Red and Orange.



Midnight Drive Keycaps
The Midnight Drive Keycap set is produced by Signature Plastics in the DCS profile with the rows 1 2 3 3.  The keycaps are optional but highly recommended as they will give you 100% key support for TheVan 44 layout.


Stabilizers + Hardware
All screws required for case assembly are included.  Costar stabilizers are included.

What Else Do I Need?
The only other things you will need are:
Switches - any mx style switch should work
Keycaps - Why not grab a set of Midnight Drive?
LEDs - only if you want to backlight your board
Mini USB Cable

Shipping
US Shipping is $13.45
Canada is $15.50
International Shipping is $22.50

Contact Info and Support
For any questions or concerns about this group buy and throughout the duration of this group buy please email
thevankeyboards@gmail.com
