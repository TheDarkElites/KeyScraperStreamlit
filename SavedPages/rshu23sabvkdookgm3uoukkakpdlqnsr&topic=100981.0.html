The Commando Project started in 2013, and the Group Buy for 23 Commando keyset was a success. Some people still ask me for extras till recently. That's something I do appreciate (and no, I have no extras of 23 Commando, sorry!!).
The Project continued with 88 Commando and countless specialized legends were designed. My dream military-themed keyset was one more step closer to completion.
2020 Commando should be the first keyset design of mine having custom colors. That's something I would like to do since long ago, but only now I have the chance.

Thanks to eveyone who provided feedbacks in the IC, and special thanks to people who joined my previous GBs and supported the Project all this time. That's the reason I can go on with this project. THANK YOU!
My gratitude also goes to the ladies and gentlemen at zFrontier, Mykeyboard and Kono for their wonderful support.
