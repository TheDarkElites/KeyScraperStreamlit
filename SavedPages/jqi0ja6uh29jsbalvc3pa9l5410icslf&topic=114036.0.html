(1)   Introduction
About Zero-G Studio
Zero-G, G as in Gravity, is a China-based designer studio. They are dedicated in bringing more fantasies to your desk that will take your gravity away and float you to a dream world in the sky. This keycap design is a greeting "gift" from Zero-G, which is inspired by the famous movie Castle in the Sky. The story tells the adventure of Sheeta and Pazu to discover the mystical floating city.

Domikey Triple Shot Cherry profile
DMK is launching their brand new triple shot manufacturing technique. See some sample pictures to illustrate the "Triple shot" technique and the typing test comparison here: https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=113405.0. The sample keycaps are not related to "Gift".
