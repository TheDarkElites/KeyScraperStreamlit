Block-67 R2 - Budget CNC Gummy O-ring 65%

The Block-67 is based on the open-source CNC Bakeneko65 but with improvements in every major area. The multi-layout hot swap PCB provides the same compatibility as a solder PCB. The sharp, blocky external design language is one of a kind. As for the internals, it's been completely reworked to optimize sound and resulted in a front height 1.5 mm lower than the Bakeneko65 (17mm vs. 18.5mm). Round one GB participants have praised the Block-67's slightly higher 7-degree typing angle. We are working with reputable international vendors to make this a compelling offering for everyone, not just Americans - which is a first for budget O-ring mount 65% boards.



Features and Specifications:
  • Anodized CNC 6061 Aluminum case
  • 17 mm front height
  • 7-degree typing angle
  • Multi-layout hot-swap PCB
  • O-ring gasket mount
  • QMK & VIA support
  • Aluminum plate
  • Daughterboard and JST cable
  • Universal 8mm rubber feet
  • 55A O-ring

More
