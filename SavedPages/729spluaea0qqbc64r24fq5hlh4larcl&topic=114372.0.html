Intro
Hello everyone, we are finally starting group buy for Duck board weights.
GB in Korea has already been completed. There is also demand overseas, so we proceed with sales.

Photos
Inner Weight (Orion V2.5, Orion V3)

Stainless Steel / Weight: ~570g / $40

External Weight (Orion V2.5, Unicorn X Legend)

Stainless Steel / PVD Coated / Mirror Polished / Weight: ~300g / $75

Side Weights (Orion V2.5)

Stainless Steel / PVD Coated / Mirror Polished / Weight: ~131g / $90


Available PVD colors

Black, Gold, Silver, Rainbow, Rose Gold


Information
There are three types of weights.

- Inner Weight : Staneless Steel, sandblast, about 573g
Compatible : Orion v2, Orion v3, Orion v2.5

- Outer Weight : Staneless Steel, about 300g, polished PVD coated(gold, rose gold, sliver, rainbow, black)
Compatible : Orion v2.5, Unikorn Lengend

- Side Weight : Staneless Steel, about : 131g, polished PVD coated(gold, rose gold, sliver, rainbow, black)
Compatible : Orion v2.5


Prices
Each price is as follows.
- Inner Weight : $40 per 1
- Outer Weight : $75 per 1
- Side Weight : $90 per set (Two are a set.)
- Shipping fee: issue individual invoice before delivery.

precautions
To prevent discoloration, coat outside and sides with pvd except inside with pvd. The outer weights are also coated only on visible parts. Similarly, the side weight scale screw tightening area is not subject to QC. Screws will also be coated!

Schedule
1. Google Form fill out and Payment period
: ~ September 4 12AM Eastern Time
2. Production period
: ~ End of October
3. Delivery and delivery invoice delivery period
: November 1st to November 7th (using DHL), Shipping will be from South Korea

Questions
Please contact the discord channel https://discord.com/invite/89Ptrrrwm2 or rookybomb#4430.

MOQ
There is no moq restriction on internal weights. in case Outer and side, There are at least 10 weights for each color.

I will do my best to produce within the period. Thank you.
