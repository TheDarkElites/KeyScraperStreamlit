This set draws inspiration from a pre-pandemic excursion to Taiwan where we were enraptured by arguably its
most widely recognised dessert, the Taiwanese Pineapple Cake (鳳梨酥 fèng lí sū; 王梨酥 ông lâi so).
Fenglisu are delicate little treats with a crumbly pastry exterior that are filled with a sweet pineapple jam.

In the Hokkien dialect, ‘pineapple’ (鳳梨 ông-lâi) is a homophone for the phrase ‘prosperity come’ (旺來 ōng-lâi).
As a symbol of good fortune and prosperity, we wanted to pay homage to this culinary icon in the form of a GMK keycap set.


Consider following our socials (IG @mksensa) and joining our Discord server for all project updates.


The GB for GMK fenglisu will run on all participating vendors from April 8th to May 9th.

Expected delivery will be Q4 2023.

Cangjie Sublegend Alphas Kit - 'Back to roots'


Note: we have updated our Alt Alphas Kit, please check our updates section below for more details.

A complete alphanumeric alphas kit with Cangjie Chinese sublegends with the inclusion of an additional tab key.

COLOURS


The colour palette we have chosen for GMK fenglisu uses two stock GMK colours (GMK CP and BR1).
The browning of fenglisu is represented by GMK BR1 and provides legibility and contrast when paired with GMK CP.


An update on our colours can be found here: https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=116844.msg3148175#msg3148175

It’s been a while since there was an update here…

A month ago, it was brought to my attention that although our first batch of samples were a fantastic match to the physical RAL colours, they do not accurately represent our kitting and board renders. Our orange and yellow samples are much warmer than what is shown online – which I fully agree with. First and foremost, this is an error from my end that should not have occurred and for that, I’d like to apologise.

To overcome this hurdle, together with our lead vendor, we made an adjustment from RAL to Pantone codes (1215U and 123U) but were unaware that GMK only accepted coated Pantone codes. In light of that, we (again) made an adjustment to (Pantone 1215C and 137C) to more appropriately portray what we have shown in the renders and what everyone has purchased.


Shown here are the newly selected Pantone colours (1215C and 137C) with our first round of GMK RAL1017 and RAL 2000 samples.

Plastic Pantone chips were then ordered and have now been shipped off to GMK for another round of colour matching.

Again, I’d like to apologise for this and thank everyone for their patience. I will provide another update when we receive our new samples.
