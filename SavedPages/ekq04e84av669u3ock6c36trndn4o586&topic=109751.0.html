Hello and welcome to the Interest Check form for Acrylic Keyboard Stands by Keebit Studio.
We are happy to announce that the Keebstand Solo and Duo are out of the Interest Check phase and are ready for Group Buy!
This group buy will run from 21.11.2020 to 05.12.2020 in the first two weeks it's going to be unlimited GB.
If the MOQ isn't reached in this time period the GB will keep running until MOQ of 30 each is reached.

Estimated production time: 1-4 month after group buy ends.
Shipping time 2 to 7 days depending on where you live.

Group Buy now Live. If you have any questions or comments or want to keep up with the progress, you can follow my socials Discord or on my socials! Thanks

Specifications:
- 5 mm Acrylic
- Aluminum Spacers
-Rubber Feet
-Rubber Protectors
-MOQ 30 Pieces

There is an option for Silver or Black Aluminum Spacers.
Clear will come with Transparent Rubbers.
Smoky will come with Black Rubbers.

Price: $25 for the Solo and $35 for the Duo

Shipping
I'm working with FedEx to provide you with the best price and fast shipping times during the Pandemic. The shipping with FedEx will be more expensive compared with shipping with the Swiss Post but there is the added benefit of tracking and guaranteed in time delivery even during the pandemic. Shipping price is calculated at Checkout.


I'm working on another project as well. If all goes to plan it may be ready for interest check in the following weeks and group buy soon after.
