Hi everyone!

We are very excited to announce that Zoom65 Essential Edition pre-order is live on Meletrix and most other vendors!






Essential Edition at a glance
For those who don't know, the Essential Edition includes everything you need to build your keyboard except for switches and keycaps.
Essential Edition starts at just $159. Just the case itself is available for $99
You can find more information at Zoom65.com

80 possible case + weight & knob color combinations
Choose from 10 different case colors and 8 different weight & knob finishes. 80 possible unique combinations!
Additional weights and knobs are sold separately. A barebones edition with case + case foam + gaskets is available for $99 only!
View all the combinations in a configurator at Zoom65.com

Bluetooth 5.0 PCB
The default PCB in each Essential Edition kit that
Is hot-swap and EST protected.
Supports Bluetooth 5.0 connectivity. Can also be used in wired mode.
Includes a 2300 mAh battery.
Supports split left shift, split spacebar, split backspace, ISO enter and stepped or normal caps lock. All hot-swap.

Pre-order bonus
Every Zoom65 Essential Kit in this pre-order batch, from Meletrix and vendors, will come with a free Wuque Titanium Switch Puller in a random color (not Prism).

Internal brass weight
We are offering an internal brass weight (weight 400g) as an add-on. It should add even more heft to your keyboard and offer a little something more in the sound department. Like all the other add-ons and accessories, the internal brass weight is also compatible with Zoom65 Olivia Edition
Zoom65 internal brass weight sound test

Kit Contents
Aluminum top & bottom case of buyer's choice

Aluminum / brass rotary knob matching the external weight.
Hot-swap, multi-layout Bluetooth / VIA PCB
Polycarbonate plate
Full foam kit
Gaskets
Screws and other installation tools and accessories
Set of WS Stabilizers
2300 mAh Li-ion battery
Coiled USB-C cable
Faux suede storage case

Add-ons and pricing
We are providing an introductory price for the first round of these colors!



Vendor price & shipping method.
Most vendors listed below have two methods of importing Zoom65 into their country before they can start shipping them out to customers.

Ocean freight - Relatively cheap but takes anywhere from 45 to 90 days to arrive. This is how most keyboards in the industry get to the vendors.

Air express - Arrives in 5 - 10 business days but it is very expensive, several times higher than ocean freight. This shipping method to vendors is becoming more common as of late.

Each Zoom65 vendor may choose to use ocean freight, or air express or a mix of both to bring in their units. For air express shipments, please keep in mind vendors may add a small surcharge on top of the MSRP above to account for the higher shipping costs.
