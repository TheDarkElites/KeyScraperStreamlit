About Grand Tour

More
Inspired from a poster (I love it) that my best friends gave to me for my birthday a couple of years ago.


The poster itself is from NASA/JPL.
The Grand Tour is the route the Voyager 2 spacecraft took to visit all four outer planets. It's a flight plan that's possible every 175 years for the alignment of the outer planets of our solar system.

My friend Alessandro Maranta worked on the novelties design and he made a great job.

Update!

Hi everyone, we are close to the final stages of the gb, so this is the state of things right now:
  • Keycaps:
    Basically all the bases have arrived to Kbdfans warehouse, but some kits are still in production. Production is expected to finish on September 13, once they arrive they will start arranging delivery immediately after.

  • Deskmats:
    First round of samples was good about deskmat quality and details, but the colors were off. Currently waiting for secound round of samples, if they are good, production will start immediately after and should not take long. I'll keep you updated on this.

  • Thok Artisans:
    There were some problems about this, qc were not good enough and vendors shipped the caps back to Thok, who's redoing QC and fix the issues. So Saturns are almost delivered to vendors, rockets are still waiting for the fix (some keycaps still have to arrive to Thok).
    This should not take long, i'll keep you updated.
