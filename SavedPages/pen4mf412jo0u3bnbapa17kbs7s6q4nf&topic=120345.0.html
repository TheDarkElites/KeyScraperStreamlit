SPACE CRISIS KEYCAPS SET
Designed by Cat Keys Studio



Intro
Hey guys!
Velocifire is here again. Hope you all have been doing well.
We believe that most people have infinite longing and curiosity about the universe and space. If you happen to be looking for a space-themed keycaps then please give us a few minutes to introduce our newest keycaps--Space Crisis PBT Keycaps Set.



Inspiration
The design of the keycaps is inspired by the awakening of artificial intelligence which is the cause of the crisis. We use red lines to represent the sight of AI.




Design
Alpha: The content is the English and Russian versions of planet names to express the imagination of future technology.


Mod: We designed a set of arrows and incorporated the iconic red line into it to show the behavior of artificial intelligence manipulating human beings in the future. : “⇑” stands for lift, “⇓” stands for landing, “⇐” stands for delete, “⇌” stands for handover, “←” stands for acceleration.


Novelties: We made the Enter key the carrier of the walkie-talkie, and reflected the danger and suddenness of the space crisis through the content of the dialogue that there was no response after asking for support.



Specifications
Profile:Cherry Profile
Material: High content PBT semi-shading material
Process: Full five-sided heat sublimation
Quantity: 140 + keys in a full set
Thickness: 1.6mm



Support
Adaptation: Cherry MX cross switch,Gateron switch, TTC switch, KTT switch, JWK switch, KailhBox switch and other types of customized switch. However, Logitech Omron switch is not applicable.
Matching Layout: 60/61/64/65/68/75/84/87/96/98/104/108/etc. 40% and HHKB and other special layout can not be perfectly adapted.
Do not support split backspace.



GB Information
GB Time: May 19th - June 9th 2023
Price: 49USD
Estimated Delivery Time: Half a month after the end of GB
Vendor:Velocifire (Worldwide)
