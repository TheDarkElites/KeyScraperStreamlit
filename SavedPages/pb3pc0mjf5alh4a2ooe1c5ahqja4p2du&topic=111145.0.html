« on: Wed, 10 February 2021, 10:41:30 »
Hello All,

We are back for a new collaboration (open to anyone) for a new marble wrist rest.
This one will be made of Italian Carrara marble and shaped to a completely new design !

As well, there will be the option for an engraved Parthenon logo filled with a metallic paint close to RAL 1024 (as per the keycaps set).
This engraving is made by water jet with a size of 29mm*25mm // 1.14*1 inch for a stroke thickness of almost 2mm (0.08inch) and a depth of 1mm (0.04inch).

GB Details:
This GB will stay open for one month and half (until 28th March).
Estimated shipping date is July 2021

We will ship worldwide with DHL / SF Express (depending of your location)
Shipping to US/Canada/West Europe will be 25USD (from Taiwan)

3 sizes will be available and the prices will be:
- 60%: 63 USD
- 65%: 66 USD
- TKL: 68USD
The engraving will be a +5 USD option (it is not possible to customize it)

Available now on:
https://marblek.com/product/pre-order-antique-white-wrist-rest-x-gmk-civilizations/

Kindly note that each piece that will be produced will be unique and so not exactly the same as on the render (= the grey veining may vary a bit)

If you have any question, please, let us know
