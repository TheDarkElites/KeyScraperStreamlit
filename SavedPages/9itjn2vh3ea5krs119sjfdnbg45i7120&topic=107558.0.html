Infinikey Port


General Information

The theme of this set is close to my heart as an Australian. It's inspired from a family-owned business that's been a part of children's lives since 1928, and their port wine jelly has been a part of the slice for just as long. It's the perfect dessert for hot Aussie summer days, whether you're on the beach, in the backyard, or trying not to cook indoors.

KEYCAP INFORMATION
Manufacturer: Infinikey
Plastic: PBT Dye-Sub & Reverse Dye-Sub
Profile: Cherry
Sculpt: 1, 1, 2, 3, 4, 4
Colours: Pantone P 1-2 U | 222 U | 4022 U
