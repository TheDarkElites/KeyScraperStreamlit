Orders are now shipping! Production went quick. Can't wait to see VT on all your desks!



We had a big push at the end, and right now, this is how the ending numbers are looking:
Base Kit: 217
Spacebar Kit: 124
Accent Kit: 117

Now we'll get everything over to GMK, and get this rolling into production!

June 6th, 2018 Order accepted and invoice paid!
___________________________________________________________________________
Hi folks! The time has finally come, starting on Sunday, April 22nd, you can get in on the GMK Violet Tendencies Group buy!  For every 25 kits sold, we'll be giving away (RNG style) a themed artisan made by the wonderful Keybai to a lucky buyer!





This is the base kit, it will fit a wide range of boards, including most 40% and ortho;

$160 at MOQ ($150 at 250 kits)


This is the spacebar kit, pretty self explainatory;

$35 at MOQ (Then lower as we go)


Here's a little accent kit;

$14 at 100 MOQ - If you've already ordered the other kits, there's a text field to enter your order number, you'll be refunded the double shipping!


Here's the pretty package it'll come in!


Group buy will run from April 22nd to May 20th

NA folks can order it here https://dixiemech.com/shop/gmkviolettendencies

EU folks will be able to order it from https://mykeyboard.eu/catalogue/category/group-buys/gmk-violet-tendencies_46

Asia proxy https://www.zfrontier.com/m/3609

Link to the interest check page https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=90994.0

I greatly appreciate everyone's interest, and feedback/help throughout this process. I apologize if some of your desired changes didn't make it into the kit, I had to balance a lot of factors.


Timeline -
GB opens April 22nd
GB closes May 20th
Within a week of close order will be placed with GMK
3-4 months after payment until order is shipped to dixiemech
Dixiemech will then ship it out to NA orders inhumanly quickly
