In the underground city of Ember, the population has flourished for years under the glimmering light of the all-powerful generator. However, once the generator starts to fail, things become chaotic fast.

Inspired by the 2008 film City of Ember,  the MG Ember keycap set recreates the glory and heartbreak of the shadowed metropolis.

 
Kit information
MDA profile is essentially a reduced-height SA variant, though it has a few unique tweaks for usability.
Be prepared for wonderful sculpting — not flat keys like DSA.
PBT plastic and dye-sublimated legends.

Made from durable and long lasting PBT with legends that will not rub off easily over time .
