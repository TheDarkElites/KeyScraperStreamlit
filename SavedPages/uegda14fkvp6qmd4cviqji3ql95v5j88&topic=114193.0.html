Kit Details:

Materials: Anodized Aluminum Case, 1.6mm FR4 Plate, Metal Knobs
Case Color Options: Black, E-White (comes with black infilled logo)
Plate Color Options: Black, White
Knob Color Options: Black, Black Nickel, Chrome, Gold
More

Case Angle: 5°
Mounting Method: Top Mounted PCB (PCB is screwed to the case)
PCB Details: Solder, No RGB Underglow, Compatible with VIA/QMK. Easily change keymapping (including the encoders) using VIA
Rotary Encoder Details: Genuine ALPS encoder with push button, No Detents, D-Shaft

Kit Inclusions:

 - 1 Top and Bottom Aluminum Case
 - 1 FR4 Plate
 - 1 Solder PCB
 - 2 Metal Knobs and 2 ALPS Encoders
 - Rubber Feet and Screws
