Moonphase 60
60% Keyboard Case by Rationalkeys
An aftermarket case for popular O-ring mount keyboards

1. Introduction

Moonphase is an aftermarket case for popular O-ring mount keyboards. Moonphase will be compatible with PCBs supporting symmetrical 8 pole cases such as TGR-Unikorn PCB v1.3 and CK Bakeneko60 PCB.




2. At a glance

Here are the basic specs:

60% o-ring gasket mounted keyboard case replacement

 • CNC machined 6063 aluminum
• Case only
• 8 pole o ring mount 
• 6 degree typing angle
 • 1.2 kg weight unbuilt
• Professional 2K Acrylic Paint (Car Paint), cured under 110°C (This unique and luxury coating participates in the relatively higher price point)
• Dark (Gray) texture topcoat 7190 Color
• Harvest (Yellow) RAL 2007 Color
• Ships with custom thick rubber feet.
• Manufactured in Turkey.
• Price $330 USD plus shipping (Shipping from USA)






















3. Group Buy Details/Price

Group-buy is starting on 5th of December 2022 and will end on 6PM ET 31st of December. We will start production right after the GB stage is complete. We are planning to begin shipment in late Q2 or early Q3 of 2023.

Fulfillment estimates can fluctuate due to the nature of manufacturing and is subject to change. Current estimate is late Q2 or early Q3 of 2023, if fully sold out.

The colors shown in renderings are a close representation of what the final product is expected to look like. There are many factors ranging from the paint chemistry to the device or monitor you are viewing from that may result in slight variation from the renderings to the final product.

MOQ: 50 Units. Products will be manufactured only if we reach the MOQ. If not the pre-order purchase price will be refunded.

Price: $330 USD +shipping from USA and applicable taxes and duties, if any.
Sale method: Only sold on Rationalkeys Website.
