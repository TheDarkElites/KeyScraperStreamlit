What's changed since IC

▼ The weight is surface treated with PVD coat then mirror polished. Two different weight designs will be offered in the GB. (Due to the low weight production yield for dual process weight, the entire weight piece will be PVD Mirror.)



▼ According to the suggestions from creators, Helix Lab has re-designed the scarab and added a POM insert to provide better stability and prevent it from falling off or rattling.


Please see the IC post for more details: https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=118707.0

PCB and Layouts

▼ PCB has two option, both use QMK firmware and are VIA compatible:

└ Hotswap: ANSI layout, 6.25u spacebar, per-key RGB, supports SignalRGB (https://signalrgb.com/)
└ Soldered: ANSI and ISO layouts, split backspace and left shift support, stepped caps support, 7u spacebar support.
