"I want you to make a contract with me and become a Magical Girl!"

SA Magica is a sculpted SA set based on the popular Magical Girl series, Madoka Magica. The colorway is inspired by the series protagonist, Akemi Homura, as well as Walpurgisnacht's barrier.
Join the MisonoWorks Discord for updates: http://discord.gg/Tk6Jw3Z

Final Week Sales Figures

Update 12/15

Production is finished. Asia orders are now fulfilling and global orders are on the boat.

Update 4/28

After further deliberation with Kono and Maxkey, we have come to the consensus that all of the kits will be produced! Yes, that means 40s, ISO, etc. will go through Maxkey and will end up with you if you ordered them. Awesome!
Invoice is to be sent in soon.

As well as this, the cut Akemi mat is being run independently in my server. Join up if you want one!
