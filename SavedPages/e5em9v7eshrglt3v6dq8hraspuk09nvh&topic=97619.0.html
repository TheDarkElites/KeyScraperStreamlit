[GB] [I]NETT- CROSS 70% FRL keyboard (GB close)
« on: Fri, 05 October 2018, 12:27:15 »






Link to Interest Check.

  • FRL: function row less
  • 3 degrees
  • PCB: QMK, Type C
  • Fox-Lab factory
  • Shipping from China. Estimated shipping fee is $45.
  • Pricing:
    • Aluminum top and bottom cases + brass weight + aluminum plate + 4 nameplates in 4 colors + PCB + PayPal fee = $327
    • All aluminum kit (replacing the brass weight with aluminum inserts) = $290
    • Additional brass plate: $30
    • Additional aluminum plate: $25
    • Additional PCB: $38
  • Layout:










Options

1. Top case

  • Three layouts: HHKB, Regular, WKL
  • Colors: Black, Red, Army Green, MAO White



2. Bottom case

  • Colors: Black, Red, Army Green, MAO White

3. Switch Plate

  • Aluminum Colors: Black, Red, Army Green, MAO White
  • polished brass (+$25)


This photo only shows how the polished finish looks.

4. Bottom weight

  • Two piece aluminum: Black, Red, Army Green, MAO White
  • One piece polished brass



The aluminum version has convex engravings. The engravings cannot be removed.

The brass version has laser engravings. You can choose to remove the engravings if leave a message in the form.


Timeline:
  • GB opens for 15 days or when MOQ 30 reached
  • 90 days for production and QC
  • Shipping around mid Jan 2019 or earlier
