Group Buy for the NFR-70

Extras available at EuroKB


Hello there, it's me again, Xonuss, this time to announce that the group buy for the NFR-70 is finally live.
The NFR-70 is a ProMicro/Elite-C powered 70% DIY kit, essentially a 60% with F-row.


Details

  • Available over at EuroKB.com
  • The group buy runs from January 29th and ends on Tuesday March 1st at 8PM CET
  • Estimated shipping: End of July, hopefully before.
  • QMK and VIA compatible
  • Typing angle: ~5.8 degrees
  • Available in Black and White

Stay in touch over on Discord





Price:
DKK: 549.99 kr. incl. Danish VAT (25%)
EUR: ~€59 excl. VAT
USD: ~$66 excl. VAT

Layout Support
Apart from standard ANSI, the NFR-70 also supports the following:
  • Tsangan bottom row
  • ISO enter
  • Split left shift
  • Split right shift
  • Split backspace



Due to the limited amount of I/O pins, regular ANSI bottom row and split right shift are unusable at the same time, as the right Alt on ANSI bottom row and the fn key on split right shift are using the same signal trace.

The Kit Includes
  • Switch plate and bottom plate, in selected color of your choice
  • PCB, colored black
  • Elite-C v4 microcontroller
  • Diodes and a reset switch
  • Standoffs and screws, colored black, and rubber bumpons.
