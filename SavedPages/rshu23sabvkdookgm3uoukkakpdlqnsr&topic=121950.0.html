Greetings Everyone! We are MM Studio and we are glad to present the MM-HHKB today: Happy Hacking all time!

Changes from IC:
1.Prices re-edited.
2.Add PC case option according to IC form.
3.MX PCB and MM-EC PCB (with free stabs) options added for those who don't have original HHKB PCB.
4.Extra topre supported housing is available.

1.Color Options: Ano Black, Ano Purple, PC are provided. And E-white is confirmed based on vote.( Thanks for you all effort)


2.Weight options: Brass and PC are available , and standard badge is the same color as the case.

3.Plate options: We provided Black Sandblasted PVD Stainless steel and Black Ano-Black Alu due to
 the original brushed stainless steel seams be more protruding after installing the keycaps.

4.PCB layout: (6u, Split BS & RS)

5.Compatibility: Replace two different weight for HHKB Pro2 and Pro3 PCB (Other HHKB PCB please check the size pictures we provide).
Meanwhile we provide PC material for third party BLE PCB such as Pro2 YANG Bluetooth control board.
P.S: MX PCB or MM-EC PCB that we provided only supports Pro3 Single-port option.
