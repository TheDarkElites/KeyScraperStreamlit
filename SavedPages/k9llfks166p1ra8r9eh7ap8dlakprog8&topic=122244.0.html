MKC65 information

Features

○ Compact 65% layout.
○ 6.5° typing angle for a more comfortable typing experience.
○ 100% CNC-machined with 6063 aluminum case.
○ Alu Internal Weight, adding to the overall weight and stability.
○ Simultaneously support two functional modules (Rotary Knob & Light Sign), also compatible with ○ JRIS80 Rotary Knob/LED Light Sign.
○ Three-in-one design with Top Mount, Plate Gasket Mount, and PCB Gasket Mount for an enhanced keyboard experience.
○ Supports tri-mode PCB (Wired, Bluetooth and 2.4ghz).
○ Supports Plate and Plateless Build.
○ Supports Caps Lock Indicator Light.
○ 2 x 2200mha large battery capacity.

                                                             

MKC65 Specifications

○ Typing angle: 6.5°
○ Front height: 9 mm
○ Material: Full CNC 6063 aluminum
○ Dimension: 319.85*110.3*29.95mm
○ Three-in-one Structure: TOP Mount / Plate Gasket Mount / PCB Gasket Mount
○ QMK/VIA Supported（wired）
○ Support PCB stabilizers
○ Unbuilt Weight
  With PVD WEIGHT: 1.23kg
  With Alu WEIGHT: 1kg
○ Built Weight
  With PVD WEIGHT: 1.45kg
  With Alu WEIGHT: 1.22kg



                                                             

Three-in-one Structure

Three-in-one structure (Top Mount, Plate Gasket Mount, and PCB Gasket Mount)
is a versatile keyboard mounting system that combines the distinctive features of these three structures for comprehensive performance.
Users can customize based on personal preferences and usage scenarios.

○ TOP Mount



○ Plate Gasket Mount



○ PCB Gasket Mount



Support Rotary Knob & LED Light Sign at the same time

The four keys located on the rightmost row of the MKC65 keyboard (Insert, Delete, PgUp, and PgDn keys) can function as a customizable module,
providing the flexibility to arrange and combine a Rotary Knob and Light Sign.



It supports a maximum of two functional modules simultaneously.
For example, 2 * Rotary Knob, 2 * Light Sign, or 1 * Rotary Knob + 1 * Light Sign.



                                                             

What can you customize for MKC65?

8 Case Options:

○ 4 E-coat case：E-white, Milky White, Pink, Mint Green
○ 4 Anodized Case: Wine Red, Black, Grey, Silver




8 Weight Options:

○ 4 Alu Weight: Gold/ Silver/ Rose Gold/ Black
○ 4 PVD Mirror SS Weight: Gold/ Silver/ Black/ Chroma




5 PCB Options:

○ Wired Flex Cut Hotswap PCB
( 1.2mm, Multi Layout , 7u split spacebar, No Per-key RGB, support QMK and VIA )

○ Wired Non-Flex Cut Hotswap PCB
(1.6mm, Multi Layout, 7u split spacebar, No Per-key RGB, support QMK and VIA )

○ Wired Solder PCB
(1.6mm, Non-Flex Cut, Multi Layout, 7u split spacebar, No Per-key RGB, support QMK and VIA )



○ Wired Per-Key RGB Hotswap PCB
(Add-on only, 1.6mm, Non-Flex Cut, ANSI Layout, support QMK and VIA )

○ Tri-mode Hotswap PCB
(1.2mm, Non-Flex Cut, ANSI Layout, No Per-key RGB, support Mykeyclub Software）

Noted: The Tri-mode technical support is provided by IRISLab.



5 Plate Options:

○ Plateless

○ Flex Cut Plates: FR4, ALU, Brass (Brass Plate Add-on only)

○ Non-flex Cut Plates: PC, POM

Noted: We highly recommend using a PC/ POM/FR4 plate for Tri-mode PCB
because the Brass and ALU plate will have a little affection for connectivity.
