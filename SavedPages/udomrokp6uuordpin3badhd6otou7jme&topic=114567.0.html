Block-67: The budget 65% Gasket Mount Full CNC Aluminum Keyboard

Hi everyone! We have just launched our second group buy - Block-67. This board aims to offer a package with all the features the community loves at an accessible price, and without the hollow sound that most budget gasket mount boards have. Sound Test Here



Features and Specifications:
- 65% Layout with arrow blocker
- Gummy worm O-ring gasket mount
- Screw-in stabilizer support (inc. Durock V2)
- VIA Compatible PCB
- USB-C daughterboard
- Seamless case design
- 7 Degree typing angle
- <17mm low front height
- Universal 8mm diameter rubber feet
- Plate cutouts for easy disassembly with your standard wired keycap puller




Group Buy Details:
- 200 A-stock units
- Small number of B-stock units available
- Group buy runs from 12/9 to 25/9
- Lead time of 3 months
- Group buy website: ENGstudio.co

Kit Includes:
- E-white or Anodized Black CNC aluminum keyboard case
- Solder PCB
- FR4 Plate
- Daughterboard, with accompanying screws and cable
- 4x Universal 8mm rubber feet

Pricing:
Block-67 A-stock kit (E-White or Black): 144
B-stock kit: 139

Extras:
Foam kit: 18
Daughterboard: 7.5
FR4 Plate: 10
PCB: 35
Durock V2 Stabilizers: 17

Layouts:
Standard or Split Backspace
Standard or Stepped Caps Lock
ANSI support only
Standard 6.25u bottom row only
