GMKBETA.COM


Welcome to the GMK βeta group buy thread.


In collaboration with Krelbit of Switchmod.net and Oblotzky, we have been able to translate one of my favorite Betamax cassette tape sleeves into a keyset, using stock GMK colors.


Colors


The colors used will be L9, 2B, RO2, and V2. The legends will be CR, which is black.




Kits


We are offering TWO base kits. One is the βeta Base, including Latin alphas and orange accent keys. The second base kit is the JS Base, featuring Hiragana sublegends and red accent.
Additional accent keys are included to emulate the JustSystems HiPro keycaps.





Regarding split/extra spacebars, OG Spacekeys is currently running on my site until September 4th, 2020; so you can get L9 spacebars from that.


RAMA CAP


We are proud to announce a collaboration with RAMA WORKS, an aluminum CNC-machined keycap in red with a black β. The anodization will be matched as closely as possible to RO2.
