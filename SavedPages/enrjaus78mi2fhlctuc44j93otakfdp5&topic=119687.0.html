Kitsune is a mythical creature from Japanese folklore that is often portrayed as a fox with the ability to shapeshift into a human form. Kitsune are considered to be mischievous tricksters, but can also be seen as wise and powerful beings, with some stories depicting them as protectors and messengers of the gods. This set has been inspired by the legends of the mythical nine tailed fox.

We are proud to present our collab with the guys over at Wuque and Meletrix to bring a very special GMK Kitsune themed Zoom75 keyboard.
The goal of the design was to keep it as minimal or clean as possible while still having that oomph factor!

It features engravings with infills on both sides of the board and on the top portion of the USB port. The board will also come with silver polished inner chamfers.
A fully custom engraved and infilled, navy anodised **2u** badge will also be provided in case you wanna use it!

The back is where the real spice is

A fully custom engraved and infilled backplate and weight featuring our rendition of a Kitsune in human form.
The backplate will be rose gold anodized to mimic the accents of GMK Kitsune and the weight will be navy anodized. All the engravings are infilled with white/red/black to really make it pop!

More details and pricing will be added in this post later once I get them!
