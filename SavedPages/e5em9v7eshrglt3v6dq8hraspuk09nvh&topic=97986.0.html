Update 2:
The GB is now full and production is progressing for the newer test parts.

Production is also booked for the full run. I will update here when there is more news

Update 1:

Album of all the renders made so far


Update with answered questions:

As of 21st November there are 2 PC units and 3 Aluminium units left available.

Custom QMK PCB (click to see the KLE)

Split backspace is supported on both the PCB and the ANSI plate.
PCB is a mini-USB connector

ANSI plate is being made in aluminium, polycarbonate and brass. ISO only in brass due to the lower order qty.

I've decided to limit the time period left so that the process can be kicked off well in advance of Christmas shutdowns. That being said I'm imposing a time limit of two weeks from today for orders to complete. There's a mere 5 units left so don't miss out if you're interested. The buy will be going ahead regardless at this point and I'm looking forward to the final results.

At the moment there is a trial running with the factory to ensure the standards are excellent. I will be working closely with them to make sure the product is the best it can be!

Original post:



Hello GeekHack and welcome to the Aanzee GB by Westfoxtrot!

After the positive feedback recieved I'm delighted to share the start of the GB for the aanzee designed by westfoxtrot.

Here's the link to the IC

Here's a link to an album of renders

The buy is structured as follows:

25 units in Aluminium
10 units in Polycarbonate

Features of the board:
  • 5° typing angle
  • 7-9mm sloped high profile
  • sandwich mount
  • sandblasted brass weight (numbered)
  • Choice of 7 custom colours for all aluminium parts
  • CNC Machined Plate
  • 19mm height at front of board
  • Custom QMK PCB (click to see the KLE)

List of Colours(click here for images):
  • Charcoal
  • Aubergine
  • Olivia
  • Ocean
  • Royal Green
  • Anthracite
  • Tuscany Yellow

Pricing is as follows:
  • €385 (~$440) for the kit, this includes everything you need except for switches and keycaps
  • €65 (~$75) for an additional plate
  • €40 (~$45) for an additional PCB
  • €22 (~$25) for an additional nameplate

Finish on all parts is all follows and is included in the price:
  • Aluminium parts: beadblasted and anodised in your choice of colour
  • Polycarbonate parts: Sandblasted
  • Brass parts: sandblasted
  • Brass plates: optionally black plated

Click here to visit the store page to see the listings and buy your keyboard

Final link for the spreadsheet where Max will keep details about the orders so far

The buy will finish once all the spots are taken.
Once the orders are all confirmed I will place the order with the manufacturer and we expect to have all units ship out from them by the beginning of March 2019, shipping will then be handled by myself and @westfoxtrot.

Please feel free to PM westfoxtrot or comment here with any questions

Thanks for the support!

Jae & Max

Quote from: Ashardalon on Thu, 08 November 2018, 13:05:07
Is there any chance of hotswap? And also what is the cumulative weight of the plates?

No to hotswap.

Plate: ~267g in brass
Top: ~137.5g in alu, ~62g in PC
Base: ~610g in alu, ~280g in PC
Weight: ~450g

Alu unbuilt: ~1.5kg
PC unbuilt: ~1.05kg

For reference my prototype (PC) weighs: 1.45 kg built
