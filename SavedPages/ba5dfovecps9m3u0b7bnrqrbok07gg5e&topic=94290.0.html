Hi all,





This is the GB for the KIRA EXS by thesiscamper, group buy will be done via monokei.us

Included in the set:


  • Top piece
  • Insert (acrylic)
  • ANSI plate (no ISO option)
  • PCB (mini USB)
  • Bottom piece
  • Brass weight (MATTE finish, Sun and Stars design)
  • typing angle is 8*
  • PCB will have resistors and SMDs soldered

Info:

  • GB limited to 25 sets
  • Only top left engraving with KIRA logo, (ignore the KingSurfer one you see, that's just the prototype
  • Plate supports varied bottom row (6.25u, 7u spacebars)
  • Additional polycarbonate, aluminium and brass plates available. Additional aluminium inserts and PCB available as well.


Additional pictures
https://imgur.com/a/4CH6k



Prices (USD):
  • $390 for one set (case, insert, PCB, brass weight, aluminium plate), does not include shipping.
  • $50 for additional brass switch plate
  • $38 for additional polycarbonate switch plate
  • $33 for additional aluminium switch plate
  • $48 for additional PCB
  • $23 for additional acylic insert
  • $38 for additional aluminium insert

Shipping:

  • Shipping from Singapore.
  • Shipping cost will depend on location.
  • Base set weighs about 3.3kg.
Estimated shipping cost (USD):
  • Singapore: $4
  • Malaysia: $17
  • Australia: $44
  • Korea: $27
  • USA: $62
  • Germany: $51

Payment:
  • Prices listed are in USD. Payment in SGD as the shop can only process in SGD, sorry
  • No PayPal invoice. All payment via Shopify payments so buyer protection still available
  • Shipping will be calculated once the orders are ready to go out, so you'll receive additional instructions then

Misc:

  • Deadline for raffle entry is 12am on 02 March 2018 (SGT, GMT+8).
  • Deadline for payment is 48 hours.
  • Countdown timer.
  • Raffle entries will be RNG-ed, and then you will receive an email regarding payment and other things.
  • For any changes to your order, please send a PM when necessary. Please note that this is a small buy and we will proceed with manufacturing very soon, so changes might disrupt the process.
  • Please include your order number when sending a PM for changes.
  • No refunds or transfer of spots once payment has gone through to the factory. These will be possible once we receive the items to minimize too many refunds/transfers/sales of spots that tend to happen

Quote from: Bastimret on Wed, 28 February 2018, 13:04:14
First Kustom raffle for me, explain why I would want extra diffusers or switchplates or PCBs?  Poly vs Alu vs Brass?  Much confuse on all the addons.

In high end customs it's pretty common to get an extra PCB and plate so you can have two completely different configurations that are easily changeable when the board uses a top mount (plate screwed to the top part of the case rather than to the bottom) design.

In my opinion, plate material variation is meant to provide a choice of feeling beyond what you choose for switches.

  • Polycarbonate is a durable, flexible plastic, feels sorta soft but similar to aluminum.
  • Aluminum is a flexible, durable metal. Most common of plate materials.
  • Brass is strong, rigid, and in my opinion provides the best sound. It retains some of the thock of aluminum albeit with a slightly more muted sound. When I say muted, I mean in the sense that a musical instrument can be muted; you still hear the instrument but with a slightly different sound profile. Brass is by far my favorite plate material.
