________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

Hi-Profile Mahjong Info
For the first group buy there will be 50 of these cases made.
I will not be shipping outside the US for the first group buy.
Kit includes: Top, Bottom, emblem, Switch Plate, PCB, Mounting Hardware (screws, VHB Tape), and Hotswap Sockets.
You will have to Purchase on your own: 2 Aluminum feet, 6 2u Plate Mount Stabilizers, 1 6.25u Plate Mount Stabilizer, Key caps, Key switches,and a USB-C power cable.
The only component you will have to solder are the hotswap sockets.
There will be 5 Powder coat options available and can be different between each bottom and top (or you can leave the case smooth/bead blasted): https://imgur.com/R2IOqIs
Only layout will be 65% + LH Numpad with 2 1.25u right Ctrl/Alt:  https://imgur.com/bVz2kv2

Tech Specs
Material: Top & Bottom (Case) are 6061-T6 Aluminum, Switch Plate & Inlay Design are C360 Brass.
Dimensions (LxWxH): 401mm x 111mm x 21mm
Weight: ~700g (case, plate, inlay)
Angle: None, or 7 deg (with feet)
PCB: Hot-swap switch attachment, Plate mount stabs

Price
- Bottom, Top, Switch Plate, Emblem, PCB, mounting hardware, Hotswap Sockets --- $200
- Buffer for 5% Paypal invoice charge & shipping materials --- $20
- US Shipping --- $30

Additional Parts:
- Bottom (powder coated aluminum) $65
- Top (powder coated aluminum) $55
- Emblem (smooth brass) $25
- Switch Plate (smooth brass) $25
- PCB $30 (If available)

Pictures: https://imgur.com/gallery/0sVRzqA
Color Examples: https://imgur.com/zklCIZ7

Low Profile Canvas
For the first group buy there will be 25 of these cases made.
I will not be shipping outside the US for the first group buy.
Kit includes: Bottom, Switch Plate, PCB, Mounting Hardware (screws), and Hotswap Sockets.
You will have to Purchase on your own: 2 Aluminum feet, 6 2u Plate Mount Stabilizers, 1 6.25u Plate Mount Stabilizer, Key caps, Key switches,and a USB-C power cable.
The only component you will have to solder are the hotswap sockets.
There will be 5 Anodize options available and can be different between each bottom and switchplate (or you can leave the case smooth/bead blasted) Note that anodize colors cannot be produced very accurately and may slightly different in color: https://imgur.com/kW7wvRv
Only layout will be 65% + LH Numpad with 2 1.5u right Ctrl/Alt:  https://imgur.com/d3mPGkM


Tech Specs

Dimensions (LxWxH): 396mm x 111mm x 12mm
Weight: ~500g (case, plate, inlay)

PCB: Hot-swap switch attachment, Plate mount stabs

Price

- Buffer for 5% Paypal invoice charge & shipping materials --- $20
- US Shipping --- $30

Additional Parts
- Bottom (anodized aluminum) $70
- Switch Plate (anodized aluminum) $25

Extra Info

Keyboards will be programmed using QMK from: https://kbfirmware.com/

For more background information on the project: https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=104023.0

All files can be downloaded from here: https://github.com/Neil-de-grass-eating-Bisson/Canvas-Mahjong-Keyboards

Link to aluminum Feet: https://kbdfans.com/products/anodized-cnc-aluminum-feet-1pair
You can use any feet you want; though the hole size is made for m4 screws, 100o countersink on the Canvas, and 90o countersink on the Mahjong.

Link to Stabs: https://novelkeys.xyz/products/cherry-stabilizers?_pos=1&_sid=dbac4755f&_ss=r
Make sure you get 6 2u, and 1 6.25u Plate mounted.
