Inherit The Classic

Inspired by the best selling computer - Commodore 64. Command 65 project was well supported by the keyboard community. It took us more than a year to develop and revamp the original design of Commodore 64.

The whole new Command 65 has the retro functions such as toggle switches and buzzer; along with the modern keyboard layout and wireless connections.
Groupbuy Page: https://www.play-keyboard.store/products/command-65

GB Starting Date: 2022.07.20 09:00pm Pacific Time
GB Period: a month
Shipping Date: 3 month after GB ends
Price: 219USD
Units: 500 for each color, 2000 total
Materials & Finish:

High quality injected ABS cases with spray painting
Black POM Plate
Black Anodized Aluminum Plate

Connections:

USB C/Bluetooth

Supported Layout:


Features:

Made In Taiwan
Special Designed Top Mounting
High Quality NKK Toggle Switches From Japan
Buzzer
Flex Cut PCB
Hotswappable
Programmable
Silicone Plate Dampener
Silicone Bottom Dampener
QMK Firmware, supports VIA & LINK
