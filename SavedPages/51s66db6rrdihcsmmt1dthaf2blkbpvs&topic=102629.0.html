« on: Thu, 26 September 2019, 08:09:28 »


Date: September 28th, 5PM EST
Interest Check Thread


Pricing & Availability:
  • Cannonkeys: 100 units - $270 + shipping
  • Mykeyboard.eu: 30 units - €297 (vat included) + shipping
  • Yushakobo - Unlimited - ¥29500 (including shipping, excluding tax of 10%)
  • Extra PCB's will be $30 (or equivalent based on chosen vendor)
Group Buy Close: October 12th, 2019
ETA: Late January 2020


Specs & Features:
  • 6° typing angle
  • 6063 aluminium
  • 17.25mm front height
  • QMK PCB designed by ai03 with VIA support
  • Gasket PCB mount
  • Backlight LED support
  • Brass internal weight (Sandblasted & Coated)
  • Weight: 740g fully assembled
  • Case colors: Black, Blue Grey (Pantone 8181c), Rose Gold (Pantone 7520c), Dark Grey (RAL 7016), Japan Blue (Pantone 7700c) [Japan Exclusive]
  • Render Gallery courtesy of ai03
  • Case options: With or without wkl blocker options available
  • Compatible with most standard keyset base kits!


Layout support:



FAQ:
  • "What happened to the option for a plate?" - There wasn't much demand during the Interest Check phase for a plate although a file will be made available after the group buy.
  • "I don't live in Japan, can I get the blue case?" - The Japan blue is an exclusive to Japan.
  • "Can I use a proxy to buy from another vendor?" - Do so at your own risk.
