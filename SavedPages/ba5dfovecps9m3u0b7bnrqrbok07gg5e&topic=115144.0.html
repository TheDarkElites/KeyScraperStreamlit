Delta
GROUPBUY NOVEMBER 12

A modern, industrial 65% keyboard.


DISCORD             CONTACT


Specs
Typing Angle: 7°
Weight: 2.4kg built (3.9kg w/ Brass)
Mounting Style: Gasket Mount
Plate Materials: FR4, Aluminum, Brass
Custom No-Screws Dial & EC11 Encoder
Dial Finishes: Silver Anodization, Brass, Mirrored Stainless Steel
QMK & VIA Compatible PCB designed by lfgberg
MX & Alps Compatible


Layout
More



Pricing & Availability

Starting at $479


   iLumkb               Vala Supply           Proto[typist]


Delta will be sold FCFS beginning on November 12
Taxes and shipping may differ based on location or vendor
Base model ($479) is a full kit with aluminum weight, aluminum dial, and FR4 plate
Upgrading to a brass weight will be +$120
Estimated ship date is Early Q3, 2022
