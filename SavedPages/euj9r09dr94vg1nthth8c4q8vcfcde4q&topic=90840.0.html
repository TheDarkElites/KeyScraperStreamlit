Extra Mechmini 2's in stock here! - https://mechkeys.ca/products/mechmini-2-0


Hey everyone! Dave from mechkeys.ca here. I want to introduce you to the Mechmini 2!





When my passion for building, buying and eventually selling mechanical keyboards began, i started looking into different form factors and layouts. After successfully creating an iconic 40% keyboard, the mechmini, i decided it was time to create a more premium version! Mechmini 2! Thank you for joining this group buy, hope you enjoy this one of a kind keyboard, and wishing you all well.

Price
$235 US Dollars - $299.95 Candadian dollars

Features
  • High profile anodized aluminum case
  • Top mounted plate
  • RGB Diffusing Polycarbonate floating style bottom case part
  • QMK Ortho/Staggered PCB Pre soldered
  • USB-Mini
  • Ortho / Staggered layouts all in one

Kit Includes
  • Aluminum CNC Milled Anodized Case
  • Poly Carbonate base to diffuse the RGB Led Light
  • Ortholinear Plate
  • Staggered Plate
  • QMK Compatible 40% PCB that has both ortho/staggered holes drilled, with RGB Underglow
  • Hardware for Assembly
  • Your choice of Gateron switch
  • Stabilizers

Terms & Conditions
This is a completely 100% custom designed keyboard. We hired community member /u/hineybush (Josh) to model this case and he did a fantastic job. Community member, Pearl GB runner and personal friend u/Koobaczcech (Koobae) also helped dial in some last minute changes such as the QMK reset hole, some plate changes and the Maple leaf engraving. Thanks to them and everyone else this board came out very nice!

Timeline
  • Group Buy is open Monday July 31st 2017
  • Orders remain open until August 20th
  • Order placed with factory on August 21st
  • If you require a refund - the refund window is closed August 15th - after August 15th, you will not be able to obtain a refund on your purchase.
  • The images on this page are of the prototype. This is the quality of product you will receive. Every keyboard will undergo an inspection for visual and mechanical defects before shipping to you.
  • Once keyboards arrive at mechkeysca - please allow 7 to 10 business days for shipping and handling. Orders placed with assembly will be processed after orders without.
  • Estimated production completion November 30th. This is an estimate.
  • Each keyboard PCB is pre-programmed with our own custom MM2 layout(s). Programming support is available on our discord server https://discord.gg/JxkC94q . Mechkeysca does not offer exchange/return/refund(s) on PCB's that have been programmed incorrectly. Do so at your own risk.
