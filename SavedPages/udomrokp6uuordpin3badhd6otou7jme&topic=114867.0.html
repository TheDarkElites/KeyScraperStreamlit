Welcome to the GB for Utopia, I always want to experience different styles in my design as well as in my products.
This time, Isometric style was my choice and therefore, "Utopia" was born.
Apart from the smallest details added to bring life to Utopia, we also included quite a few of easter eggs about our previous products into this product (hopefully someone will notice 🙂)
In conclusion, this is a project that I personally enjoyed creating and I hope you feel the same way!
