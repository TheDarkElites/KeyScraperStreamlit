Hello keyboard peeps, we've concluded the IC's for the 6060 and we're now launching the GB!!!

Just for those who havent seen the IC posts the 6060 is a WKL layout gasket hybrid that can use universal 60% plates and left sided PCBs!

Our philosophy behind this keyboard design was to provide a reasonably priced gasket mount WKL. We also realised that one issue with gasket mounts is that it's not always the easiest to experiment with different plate & switch options, as the plate has to be specifically designed to fit the keyboard. Hence, we added removable standoffs to our design, so you can swap in your trusty traymount PCB-plate-switch assemblies easily.

GB LINK: https://groupbuys.mechboards.co.uk/shop/lx-keebs-6060/
Discord: https://discord.gg/JJAxDJ5bZJ
SMOKEY plates: https://smokeyplates.eu/

GB Starts on the 3rd of November @ 17:00 BST and end on the 14th of November @ 17:00 BST. We're expecting to fulfil all orders by early Q2 of 2022.

Exciting news - we've managed to pare down the pricing further. We've teamed up with LukePanda to bundle his LH60 hotswap PCB which supports ANSI/ISO layouts, split or full backspace for a total kit price of only £260 incl. UK VAT*!
