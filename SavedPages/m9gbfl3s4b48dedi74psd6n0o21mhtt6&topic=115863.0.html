Uhm hmmmm

I really like the approach to using numrow for numpad, but it doesn't really work like this. Adding the 2u 0 key is the right step, but there isn't a 2u enter key to make a full numpad, and people are forced to split the 2u + key. I think this works best for 3 row numpads, or even 4x4, but at that point you're including too many unnecessary keys.
1.5u spacebars is widely uncommon, especially not enough reason to nix 1.75u for them.
I really find no reason to have both a 1.25u fn key and a 1.25u shift key with DSA trashcons since they're essentially the same icon flipped vertically. This can go for a few of the 1u keys as well.
The menu key being on caps lock is really awkward. There are three different lock icons you could have picked from.
Be weary about using mono icons for backtick and quote, I just got back sets for Historic and they were aligned like this not being centered.

I wish this was fleshed out and took feedback before going to group buy. The colors are really cute and my lilac p3d corne needed something more permanent on it, so I might pick it up, but what I see is kitting that is extremely bloated and the price absolutely suffers from it being that really high moq.
