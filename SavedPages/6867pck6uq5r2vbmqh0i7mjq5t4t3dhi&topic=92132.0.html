Hi guys, i am going to help  XTgg run this group buy.
Inspird by the Eye of Horus，the acient egyt mystry, Ace Team came up  with their own design.
PCB board is the eyeball and it  is counterweight as well.In order to present authentic eye shape look CNC technique was used to make a concave cut.

Furthermore, to provide case and plate with better quality, Ace team cooperates with the factory that manufactures Revo custom mechanical board.

Final Design for the back


Sample Pictures on
More

Layout option:



Color option:
Black, Gray, Red, Blue, Silver, Purple, Pink, Green


Tech Specification:
  • MX aluminum Reinforcement Plate (Aluminum 6063)
  • Case has an angle of 5 degrees for the Aluminum 6063
  • PCB presoldered based on Bface(Source: http://blog.winkeyless.kr/)
  • Fixing bolts and rubber pads
Group buy Information:
  • Price for one kit: $260 excluding the PP fee and shipping fee
  • One kit consists of 1 case, 1 PCB,  1 plate, 1 polished brass weight,fixing bolts and rubber pads
  • Shipping from China. The shipping price depends on your location
  • Estimated shipping time: January 1st,2018
  • Deadline for order: Oct 31st,2017
  • Weight: Post later
  • Limit of 100 sets with MOQ 30, no R2
  • Switches and stabilizers are not included
  • If you want to change the order, please PM me before the deadline of payment.
  • Color options:
