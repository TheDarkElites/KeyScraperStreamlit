[Aster Ergo TKL GB]

Hi! Let me introduce our fifth GB, Aster Ergo TKL.

Space inspired ergo tkl layout keyboard.

Below are some pics of protos.


























[About the GB]

- GB Date
   till : 30th April 2022

- There will be three versions
   Premium : with brass weight
   Standard : with aluminium weight
   Economic : without weight(only engraving)

- MOQ
   Premium + Standard version : 50 sets
   Economic version : 50 sets

   If one of both versions does not match the MOQ, will give chance to opt for another version.
   If both versions do not match the MOQ, will make a vote and will leave just one version and the other version will be cancelled.

- Max Quantity
   Premium version : 150 sets
   Standard version : 100 sets
   Economic version : 150 sets

- Estimated delivery time : Dec. 2022 or Jan. 2023


[Specification]

- Typing Angle : 7 degrees

- WKL only

- Supports ANSI/ISO

- Mounting Style : Top mounted

- With or Without Patterned Top

- Weight : Premium 3.48 Kg / Standard and Economic 2.0 Kg (unbuilt and w/ plate and w/o PCB)

- Effective Keyboard Height : 23mm(from bottom of the bottom case to the top of the space bar swtich)

- Color :
   Top - Navy, Gray, Silver, Rose Gold
   Bottom - Navy, Gray, Silver, Gold
   Brass Weight(for Premium version) - Sandblasted and UV Silkscreen printed on the visible side(Gold or Silver)
   Alu Weight(for Standard version) - Same color as the top case
   Plate -  the plate colour will be Silver for Navy, Gray, Silver Bottom and Gold for Gold Bottom

- Badge : Aluminium / colour will be same as the bottom case

- Cat logo and indicators : there are two options
   With cat logo and two indicators
   Without cat logo and no indicators

- Includes
   Keyboard Top/Bottom (and Weight for Premium and Standard version)
   Aluminium Plate
   PCB (Solder) - QMK/VIA compatible
   Bumpons/Screws/Microfiber pouch/Package etc.

- Extras : PCB(solder) / Plate(top mount or o-ring gasket)

- Final Price :
   Economic version - 590 USD
   Standard version - 680 USD
   Premium version - 780 USD

More pics and details can be found on our discord server : https://discord.gg/bRxfrav25t

You can participate in this GB from this site : https://bahmstudio.com/collections/keyboard-set


Thanks for watching!
