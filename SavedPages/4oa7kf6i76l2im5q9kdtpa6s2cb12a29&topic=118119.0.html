« on: Wed, 17 August 2022, 10:20:19 »


PWDS presents, RGBYK 2022!

It's no secret, this colorway isn't new to keycaps. It's simply, classic and timeless. RGB accents are a must-have for your 50 beige/9009 colorways, or whatever you see fit! To return RGBYK modifiers with a bang, we're launching in two distinct keycap profiles, GMK and DCS! To be clear, the two profiles are sold in GB as two separate product listings in the same timeframe, and produced/shipped separately in conjunction with GMK and Signature Plastics estimates. If you want to purchase both GMK and DCS RGBY, you will essentially be joining two different group buys that will ship independently. I'm just keeping everything on one Geekhack GB Thread to keep things simple and organized.

TL;DR: You need RGBYK modifiers for your 50 beige keysets... or for whatever you see fit

GMK and DCS RGBYK group buys are live, and are available to purchase right now! Please refer to below for all the information:


GMK RGBYK Add-on kit Group Buy Information:

  • GMK - Cherry profile
  • Materials - ABS plastic
  • Legends - Double-shot Cherry legends
  • Row height - R4 -R4 - R4 - R5 - R4 - R5 - R4 - R5
  • Colors - P3, N7, V4, CV, CR
  • Includes native R4+R5 support, icon only, and icon+text modifier support



Retail price (US): $60, please view other individual vendor listings for other regional pricing.

Minimum order quantity: 250

Timeline:
Group Buy: From August 16, 2022 to September 16, 2022
Delivery: Roughly 15 months after the group buy has ended. According to GMK, lead times have peaked and should be going down from here.

Vendors:
United States - https://thockeys.com/group-buys/live-group-buys/gmk-rgbyk-group-buy/ (Live)
Canada - https://www.deskhero.ca/collections/open-group-buys/products/gmk-rgbyk (Live)
Europe - https://mykeyboard.eu/catalogue/category/group-buys/keycaps/gmk-rgbyk_776/ (Live)
United Kingdom - https://prototypist.net/collections/live-group-buys/products/group-buy-gmk-rgbyk (Live)
Korea - https://swagkeys.com/collections/group-buys-1/products/gb-gmk-rgby (Live)
Oceania - https://dailyclack.com/products/gmk-rgbyk-addon-kit-2022 (Live)

DCS RGBYK Add-on kit Group Buy Information: (CANCELLED)

  • Signature Plastics - DCS profile
  • Materials - ABS plastic
  • Double-shot Cherry legends. Alternative Gorton Mods are included as well.
  • R4 -R4 - R4 - R4 - R4 - R4 - R4 - R4
  • Colors - RPA, VDX, BHC, YY, NN
  • Includes native R4 support, icon only, icon+text modifier support, and cherry+gorton legend support



Retail price (US): $60, please view other individual vendor listings for other regional pricing.

Minimum order quantity: 150

Timeline:
Group Buy: From August 16, 2022 to September 16, 2022
Delivery: Roughly 12 months after the group buy has ended
