[GB] Mason60 engineered stone 60% keyboard case
« on: Fri, 13 August 2021, 09:58:26 »
Hey guys, I posed an IC a while back about the Mason60 my engineered stone 60% keyboard case.  I'm happy to say after refining the design and taking aboard suggestions from the community that we are now a GO for Group Buy!  I've included some info below but for more details you can revisit my IC page.

The Mason60 is cast from a gypsum resin composite.  Once the case is demolded and then sanded, the result feels like an engineered stone like Corian.  I thought this material would make an awesome keyboard case because (1) its heavy (2) you can make almost any color (3) I have lots of flexibility with regards to shape and design.

In terms of design philosophy- I designed the shape of of the case to highlight the uniqueness of the material.  I wanted a fat top bezel to show off the surface pattern  and a chunky profile with very round edges which fit the "heavy" and "smooth" feel of stone or marble.

MATERIAL PROPERTIES:
 After casting, the case needs to be sanded down to reveal the chips inside.  This is quite a laborious process, from 80 grit to 2000 grit.  The case is then sealed with a special wax, to provide water and stain resistance.  The resulting finish is smooth enough to see reflections, like polished marble.  Due to the casting process there may be some small air bubbles on the surface, the largest of which will be filled in, but small ones may remain.  In my opinion the small bubbles add some character to the piece and makes it feel more stone like.
Bear in mind the hand-made nature of this case means the external dimensions will not be perfect like CNC aluminum, but this is part of the charm!

CARE AND MAINTENANCE

The Mason60 is sealed with beeswax and is water resistant, but please wipe off any moisture straight away to avoid leaving a mark on the wax coating.  Please also keep your neon orange Doritos grease away!  Clean with a damp cloth and if needed you can reapply some wax, though this shouldn't be needed for a long while (each case will come with a small amount of extra wax).
While strong and impact resistant, it will shatter (contrary to what I said in the IC) if you drop it from a height onto a hard surface.

QUALITY GUIDE

There will be some very small air bubbles on the surface due to the casting process.  Due to the hand made nature of this product, no two pieces will be identical in terms of pattern.  This product is sanded by hand and so the external dimensions and contours may vary slightly and some minor sanding marks may remain.   Colors will be very close to what you see in the photos (as far as one can tell from a phone or monitor).

As we make everything inhouse, we have more direct control over quality and timeliness vs outsourcing the manufacturing.
