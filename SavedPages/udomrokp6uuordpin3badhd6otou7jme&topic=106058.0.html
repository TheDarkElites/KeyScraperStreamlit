GB May 1st - June 1st

Discord for updates: https://discord.gg/eGnjxGx



Hi all,

Welcome to the GB for GMK Deep Navy! There won’t be a lengthy explanation as to why I chose this theme - I just like it; having been surrounded by nordic design since I was young I’ve grown quite fond of this dark navy colour. As a fan of minimalism I've kept it as an uncontroversial colour scheme that I very much enjoy; something that is stylish in home use and not too outlandish to bring to the office. This is the first full keyset I’ve designed so I’m keeping it nice and simple with just 2 kits.

GMK Deep Navy is a sleek and professional keyset featuring custom dark navy keys with WS1 legends.

Estimated shipping Q1 2021

Base:

The base kit provides compatibility for 60%, 65%, 75%, TKL, 1800 and full-size boards, with support for ISO. Accent keys are included for those who want to bring a bit of flare to their board.



Addition:

A spacebars kit with 40% and Alice-layout compatibility.



RAMA Cap:

Anodised aluminium with white enamel infill.





Deskmat:

Nordic night - designed by Sour.
