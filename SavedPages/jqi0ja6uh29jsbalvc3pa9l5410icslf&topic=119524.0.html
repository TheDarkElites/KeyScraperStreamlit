Hello,Glad to see you again!Createkeebs bring Venice Rialto GB to you today.

The proto 1.0 Interest check for it: https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=118759.0
The proto 2.0 finished now.Let’s straight to the point and talk about what updates we did the for proto 2.0 of Venice Rialto.
1.Add the words "Rialto" carved by CNC in the position of type-c outlet, and the appearance of the top arch is no longer monotonous.

2. The word "Venice" on the weight changes the previous handwritten Venice into a neat font, which looks more comfortable and more in line with the overall literary style of the keyboard.

3. Badge design changes. three colors respectively correspond to three different badge, diversified styles, the overall tone is more harmonious.


Let’s see more renders about the proto:



About the layout
Tented ergo.Simply speaking, the word “Tented” refers to the left and right sides of the keyboard like a tent, which is tilt down from the middle to the two sides, so as to achieve the purpose of more in line with the natural and comfortable placement of hands when typing.

1.6mm black core PCB, which is to ensure the fullness of the sound and strength of gasket mount pcb, and it is also more friendly to plateless options, so as not to cause the inner excessive soft to collapse. Separate flexcuts between rows can provide a certain deformation though.
Besides, we give up the Leaf-spring design, instead we decide to choose the traditional gasket way, to achieve the effect of soft and steadiness.

Pc and Alu material plate will be optional.
