SA Taboo


Group Buy is Complete

Combining striking red legends on a jet black base surrounding the warm grey alpha keys, SA Taboo tempts you with its bold design.
Will it peak your curiosity, appeal to your inner desires, or inspire more intriguing emotions? You are invited to this secret rendezvous as your senses of sight and touch feast on the flavors of SA Taboo!
This is a Maxkey set done in SA profile 1-1-2-3-4-3.

Price is $85.
Group buy: April 15 - May 14, 2017
Estimated ship date:  mid-July 2017

Order Qty Update - 100 Orders as of 5/14/17
Thanks everyone! I'll update when I get more info on manufacturing/shipping dates.
06/28/2017 - Shipping still on track for mid-July
07/15/2017 - SA Taboo is being sorted and will begin shipping soon
08/07/2017 - Should be getting shipping notifications today/this week!








Thanks Oblotzky for the Renders!

Base set (105 keys)
   ANSI 104 layout
   Alpha base color: GFW, Alpha legend color: NN
   Modifier base color: NN, Modifier legend color: RA
   Includes an extra R1 1u “ESCAPE” - Base color: RA, Legend color: NN
Additional keys (22 keys)
   R1: 1u “DELETE”, 1u “END”, 1u “Page Down”
   R2: 1u “-”
   R3: 1u ”0”, 1u “+”, 1u “PAGE UP”, 1.5u “ALT” ×2, 1.5u “CTRL” ×2, 1.75u Stepped “CAPS LOCK”, 7u Spacebar, 1u Blanks ×3
   R4:1u “PAGE DOWN”, 1.75u “SHIFT”, 1u Blank, 2.25u "SHIFT"
      Base color: NN, Legend color: RA
   R3: 6.25 Spacebar, 7u Spacebar
      Base color: GFW

Pantone references:
Warm Grey alphas - Pantone 402C
Black modifiers/text - Pantone 419C
Red modifier/text - Pantone 485C

One footnote, the R3 1u blanks will be NN/NN doubleshot of the R3 Numpad period, per Maxkey there is a possibility of seeing small outline where the character would be, so I picked the smallest surface area character, the period, to minimize it.
The R4 1u Blank will be of the R4 Page Down, as it's the only 1u R4 mold to use.
