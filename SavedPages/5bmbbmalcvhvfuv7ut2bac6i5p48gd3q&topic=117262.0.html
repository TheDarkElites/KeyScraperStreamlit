DCS Windbreaker
GB Live May 20th to June 30th!


The time has come, DCS Windbreaker is live. It's inspired by the most heinous, beautiful, beautifully heinous, heinously beautiful jackets to ever be a cultural phenomenon. While your windbreakers from back in the day may not fit anymore or got thrown out, this keyset will send you back in time and keep your keyboard looking fly for years to come. If you're looking for something subtle, you can talk to the hand.

Kits


A phat base kit for almost everything you could need to cover: TKL, 65%, 60%, Alice, and 4-key 40% support!


Some choice novelties referencing some of your fave goodies of the past, also features orange gamer WASD.


Got a lot of numbers to enter? Chill out man, we got you covered. Includes R1 = and 00 keys for you true numpad enthusiasts.


Wanna make your keyboard extra funky fresh? This kit has some extra colored spacebar options just for you. Also has some fly 3u bars for you folks on the cutting edge and a 10u bar that is just da bomb.

Hey everyone, sorry for a bit of delay on relaying news on the samples.

Just took this pic a little bit ago, it's super overcast where I'm at today.



This update is a bit of good news/bad news.

Bad news first.

The 2 pink colors are less vibrant than the selected Pantones. I knew going into this set that the paper versions of the Pantone neons would be impossible to hit, which is why I purchased the plastic chips and rendered the set based on those.

I requested a re-sample on those 2 colors but unfortunately, according to SP's supplier, Pantone uses PP as the plastic to make their chips, while obviously SP uses ABS. PP starts off clear, while ABS starts off fairly white and PP has less limited pigment options compared to ABS. Due to these differences, there's simply nothing more that can be done to make these colors more accurate.

Now for the good news.

The rest of the colors are incredibly accurate to their Pantone chips. While the pinks may not be 1:1 with the Pantones I chose, I am very pleased with how they all work together. The pink colors still play off of each other as I'd envisioned and they absolutely still fit the aesthetic of the set. Which is to say, they're all obnoxious as hell in the best way.

Thankfully, it's not so far off from what I'd envisioned so as to require having them attempt a different color reference or anything like that. And honestly, any other choice of reference would pale in comparison to what was rendered for you fine folks anyway.

With that in mind, I'll be confirming that these samples are acceptable and we'll be getting this show on the road!

I'll keep ya updated as things progress.

PWade3
