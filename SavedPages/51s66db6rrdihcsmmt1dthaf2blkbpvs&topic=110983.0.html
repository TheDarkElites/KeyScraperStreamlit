[GB] Copper, Bronze and Aluminum Space Bars By MTB KEYS - CLOSED - Update
« on: Mon, 01 February 2021, 16:27:41 »
Thanks to everyone who supported this Group Buy for the Copper, Bronze and Aluminum Spacebars! It was a success!
The group buy is now closed, but we will leave the store front open a few days for those assured stragglers.


UPDATE March 26 2021: Manufacturing Complete - Now polishing and then off to Cerakote and anodizing.





Welcome to Group Buy #2 – In this GB we have added COPPER to the list.

Timeline – The GB will run from: February 1, 2021 to March 1, 2021
Expect delivery 6 weeks after the end of the group buy.

You can join the Group Buy on our website: mtbkeys.com

Choose from:

Aluminum: Album: https://imgur.com/a/IzSH6Eu Color Choices
     Copper: Album: https://imgur.com/a/8CR4BPT
     Bronze: Album: https://imgur.com/a/pUSwR39

Prices:
Aluminum - $60.00 to $65.00 usd
Bronze - $120.00 to $130.00 usd
Copper- $130.00 to $140.00 usd
 

Here is what is available to order:

-Two profiles: Cherry & OEM
-6.25u & 7u
-Polished or Brushed
-Centered stems only
-Cerakote Clear on the Copper, Bronze & raw Aluminum

Springs: We will be including 3 different weight springs depending on aluminum or bronze (74g, 89g, 98g & 150g) so you can test what you like.

Sound Test Courtesy of Discord member “Tim Raines”
Aluminum Sound-test : https://imgur.com/ptN780f
     Copper Sound-test: https://imgur.com/XfsuCqt




Picture shows the two profiles, Cherry & OEM
 


Front and back picture for product reference



Packaging:
The spacebars will come in a sealed plastic sleeve in a black Cigar style box.



Join the Group Buy on our website: mtbkeys.com

And you can follow us on our discord channel for updates!
https://discord.com/invite/fUyWvek

Thanks!
