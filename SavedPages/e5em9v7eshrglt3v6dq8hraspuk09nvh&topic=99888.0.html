What happened with Kira?

I am in for a few Kono GBs and have not had an issue. Main issue for me was the additional sales tax, but that's just something we cannot escape.

So there was a lot on Reddit about it. Basically it comes down like this.

They missed the shipping deadlines several times, misleading people. Once it did ship people who backed the kick starter were not given priority. Instead they seem to ship to just who ever. There were people posting board's on reddit who ordered months and months after the Kickstarter to fund the project had happened. There was one person that ordered in Oct. and had gotten there board before people that ordered almost a year before. There was also least one person who admitted having ordered the board 2 weeks prior and getting it.

QC on the board was horrible. The majority of boards I have seen have the H and J key's misaligned. The reason is there a screw between them thats raised enough that it causes the switches to pivot at a slight angle. IC solution to this is to file the screw flat. They won't do this for you under warranty. There also issues with the way the PCB was designed. They seem to have a issue with interference from other devices. People have reported the board blinking and what not when using radios and I would assume cell phones. Inputs answer to that is to take it apart and install a ferrite choker. That doesn't even include the firmware that seems to be buggy.

Keep in mind all those issues happened with a board that was delayed by 6-7 months. There was also the fact the original controller they planned to use had to be switched at the last moment during production.

There were other issues but there minor compared to those.
