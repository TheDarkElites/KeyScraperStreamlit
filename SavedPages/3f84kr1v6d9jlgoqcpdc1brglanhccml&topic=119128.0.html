Design inspiration
The theme of "Game Master" is designed around the D&D theme. It is a game I have played for a long time, a very hardcore tabletop role-playing game.

Its full name is Dungeons & Dragons , and its first edition was released by TSR in 1974.

It has a complete world view, rules, order, system, etc. It can be said to be a very influential RPG 3A masterpiece. It can be called the ancestor of role-playing games. The various systems it created are still standard in many games.

At present, there are still a lot of D&D game fans all over the world, but they are more pursuing quality, immersion, and ritual in the current games. So they will make a real scene sandbox to play the game, so that everyone seems to be immersed in the virtual world.

Next, let’s talk about the theme design of “Game Master”.

Color matching: The source is the color matching of the sand table. The dense forest and the earth color similar to the parchment map complete the main color blocks of the theme. The graphite gray represents the city and the mountain.

The font: This time I used serif font as the font of Latin characters, and matched it with dragon characters. I used dark gold to show the ancient and mysterious characters of dragon characters. I believe this is also the first set of keycap design with complete dragon language characters you own, you can try to learn a few words of dragon language magic! Ha ha ha ha!

Personalized design: We adopted the most classic dragon and knight elements in the game, the iconic dice and "roll" slogan in the Dungeons and Dragons system, and the most classic three-treasure book of the whole game, 《the Player's Manual》, 《the Dungeon Master's Guide》, and 《the Monster Map》.

It is worth mentioning that in order to protect rare animals, I also designed an ICON that prohibits dragon slaying, I hope you like it.
