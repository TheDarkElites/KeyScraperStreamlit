[GB] Sisi R2 Artisan Box
« on: Wed, 19 August 2020, 06:46:57 »
Hello, everyone! Hope you guys stay healthy and safe in this 'special' year. But there is something really special for you, Sisi R2 is right here. It's called gourd, but why? This is one of my favorite Chinese cartoon when I was a kid and I used one of the roles as my ICON in GH. They are all with special skills, like my Artisans cases design, they have magical functions --- Absorbing all your Artisans caps, lol.

As the time passed by, I believe that your collection is increasing  and enriching day by day. Please let me tell you guys one of my weird hobbies, I prefer putting some grail Artisans in a box, preparing a box for each different Artisan authors or putting the same artistic styles from different authours in the same box. That's one of the reasons that I designed R2 which will be a little bit larger than Sisi R1.

And here are some more differences from the R1 version.
1. Added laser Logo on one of the corners of the case.
2. Put magnets in stead of screws on the top plastic cover.
3. Addded Brass logo weight in the bottom.
4. Removed aluminum feet.

And then, R2 is not only the Aluminum version, but also came with Polycarbonate version. You can purchase Extra top transparent plastic covers when you join the GB. And there are two types of top plastic covers, one is common transprant PC and the other one is anti-ultraviolet PC.  (Sorry, the UV top cover which I wanna put it in the option at the beginning is too expensive and I don't wanna increase the cost any more, so I cancelled the Box stand and THE UV cover. Please forgive me for this decision. I think you guys can find a better display stand by yourselves.




Specifications:
-  There are 2 different kinds of materials at this time, PolyCarbonate and Aluminum
-  The size of the box is 195*195*26MM.
-  Magnet adsorption.
-  The total weight of PolyCarbonate is 0.73kg
-  The total weight Aluminum is 1.27kg
-  Can accommodate 60 caps in total.
-  Price: ($130 )(not inclusive shipping fees)
-  Production time: 3 months days

Expected shipping：4th December

The form will be open for 15 days from August 20, which maybe increased or shortened time limit. Depending on the amount of responses and my work.


Delivery costs are undetermined and unstable at this moment. The cost of delivery to each of you will be determined when most of the GB products are finished and will be calculated it with the shipping outbox.
Expected: $25-$50 shipping all over the world.


GB form ：https://forms.gle/q8J7qu9eS4ZeoqDL6

Color:
Anodized Colorway: Black, Silver, Dark gray, Purple, Pink and Tiffany(Teal)
I personally recommend black. I have tried many color boxes before and the black one will work much better.
