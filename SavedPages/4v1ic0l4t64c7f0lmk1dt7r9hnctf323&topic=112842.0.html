About Master98 and 1800mini:
    1800mini is an extension of 40% keyboard inspired by classical CHERRY G80-1800, and Master98 is based on the 1800 layout and has a complete alphabet area and numpad area.
   Both of them can be arbitrary programmed up to 8 layers no matter wired or wireless, has featuring customizable RGB backlighting and underlighting.
   The sunken design of plate makes the hotswap switches more stable, and these two kinds of keyboards also have different structure.
   You needn’t solder any switches and just pop them in and ready to enjoy it.

Specifications of Master98:
*Layout: Compact-1800 layout
*Material: Aluminum 6063 and polycarbonate
*Mount: Top mount with O-ring, 4.5mm plate
*Stabilizer: Pre-assembled with Everglide Panda Gold-Plate Mounted Stabilizers
*Price: $600 + shipping
*PCB:
  Type-c
  Fully programmable via ydkb.io(based on TMK)
  Hotswappable(pre-assembled with Kailh hotswap sockets)
  Full RGB LEDs
  Dual mode(usb cable/bluetooth4.0)
*Battery: 3800mah
*Color offerings: Preview through above link

Specifications of 1800mini:
*Layout: 1800mini
*Material: Aluminum 6063 and polycarbonate
*Mount: Integrated
*Stabilizer: Pre-assembled with Everglide Panda Gold-Plate Mounted Stabilizers
*Price: $380 + shipping
*PCB:
  Type-c
  Fully programmable via ydkb.io(based on TMK)
  Hotswappable(pre-assembled with Kailh hotswap sockets)
  Full RGB LEDs
  Dual mode(usb cable/bluetooth4.0)
*Battery: 3800mah
*Color offerings: Preview through above link
