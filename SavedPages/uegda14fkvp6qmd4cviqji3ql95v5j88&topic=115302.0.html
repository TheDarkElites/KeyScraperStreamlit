Hey all!
After multiple prototypes and almost a year in the making, we are excited to announce the sale of the

BEAK 6.5


More photos on our instagram: https://www.instagram.com/stiloustudio/

The BEAK 6.5 will be available for purchase on the
19TH of November @ 4PM CEST
Until the 19TH of December @ 4PM CEST
The BEAK 6.5 will be sold exclusively through our website:
https://www.stiloustudio.com

Pricing:
The BEAK 6.5 will cost:
Ä399 ($460)
Excl. Tax

Availability:
There will be a total of 400 units available.
All of which will be sold through an FCFS format

Configurations:
There will be 2 available configurations:
E-WHITE and E-BLACK

Features:
Typing angle: 6.5∞
Weight: 1.9 KG (4.2 LBS) Fully built
Layout: Unique 65% with an EC11 rotary encoder (ANSI and ISO)
Mounting: Top mount
PCB: Custom designed Solder PCB by trip-trap, Unified C3 daughterboard
Plate: Sandblasted polycarbonate
Case: E-coated 6063 Aluminium
Weight: Sandblasted and clear coated brass

Shipping:
The BEAK 6.5 is estimated to ship from us around
6 months after the GB has ended
