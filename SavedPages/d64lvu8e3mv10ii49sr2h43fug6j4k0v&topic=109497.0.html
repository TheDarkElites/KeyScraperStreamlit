Product Page: https://merisi.works/collections/merisi-sessantacinque
IC Thread: https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=108371.0
Pronunciation: https://streamable.com/3njl6a (Thanks, Lightning )



Sessantacinque












LightningXI Typing Test and Build Stream:




Group Buy Information:

Group Buy Date: November 15
Entry Window: 24 hours
MOQ (min and max): 60 units

Supported Layouts::
- WK or WKL
- ANSI only (NO ISO)
- Stepped or Normal Caps Lock
- Normal or Split Backspace


Specifications:
- Top mount 65%
- Aluminum 6063 case
- Aluminum plate
- 7.8 degree typing angle
- Stainless Steel weight (brushed with sandblasted logo)
- QMK / VIA compatible matte black PCB from Mechlovin (Daughterboard and JST cable included)
- Weight: ~1600g
- Small TX style bag (Merisi.Works branded zipper pullers)

Color Options:
- Case: Black or Silver
- Plate: Black, Silver, or Red.

Available Extras:
- Carbon Fiber Plate
- Brass Plate
- Extra PCB (daughterboard and JST cable included)

Price (USD):
- Sessantacinque kit: $465
- Extra Aluminum Plate: $38
- Extra Carbon Fiber Plate: $45
- Extra Brass Plate: $45
- Extra PCB: $45

Shipping
A separate shipping invoice will be sent out once the units are finished. The initial invoice will NOT include any shipping fees.
USPS for US addresses and DHL for internationals.
Buyers will be responsible for all shipping, tax, and import fees.

Lead Time:
Roughly 6-8 months with potential COVID delays.
Updates will be posted regularly on Geekhack, Instagram, and Discord.

Links:
- Merisi.Works
- Instagram
- Discord

11/16/2020
Raffle closed. Invoicing.

11/18/2020

Invoicing done.

Thank you everyone for joining the raffle! I wish I could have invoiced everyone

Some raffle winners are reporting that their invoices ended up in "Spam" or "Promotions" folders. Be mindful of this when you check your emails.

11/22/2020
Order places with vendors.

12/01/2020
Weight screws will be hex instead of Phillips that was used for the prototype.

02/16/2021
Case and plate manufacturing started.
2nd proto version of the PCB and daughterboard cable received.
 - Reviewing the new components later this week.

--
04/12/2021

PCB:
Flying has been working on finalizing the daughterboard cable and is finally happy with the latest version. It will now sit more flush in the bottom case cable routing. It will also allow for easier bending around the corners.
PCBs are on scheduled to ship to me in May.

--
04/18/2021

Case:
Machining is done and we are now working on the surface treatments. The stainless steel weights have to be brushed, the logo sandblasted, and the cases anodized.

PCB:
Estimated to ship to me sometime in May.





Things are moving! 

--
05/04/2021:

PCB:
Mechlovin' received the initial batch of PCBs and they didn't pass QC. The black surface is splotchy so we requested a new batch to be made.
We do not anticipate this new PCB order to affect our shipping timeline.

Case:
Cases are being anodized.

Bags:
The bags are being manufactured this month.

--
05/14/2021

PCB:
New batch of PCBs have passed QC by Mechlovin and will be shipped to me within the next few weeks.



--
05/18/2021:

Case:
Cases and aluminium plates are done anodizing.
Carbon fiber plates ready.
Brass plates being sandblasted.

Bags:
Bags will be done manufacturing at end of the month and shipped to me shortly after.

Other:
No other updates to report.

--
06/13/2021:

Case:
Boards are finished and being assembled now.

Bags:
Bags are done and being shipped to me.



PCB:
Support page for the PCB (Technical Specifications, Firmware Flashing Guide, etc) being done my Mechlovin and should go live once the boards are being shipped to you. Link to be shared once the page is live.

06/20/2021 Update:

PCB:
Sessantacinque PCB layout added to the VIA configurator.
PCBs currently in transit to me.

Other:
No other updates to report.

06/27/2021 Update:

Cases:
The cases are currently shipping to me via sea freight. Its going to be a few weeks before they arrive here in Houston, TX.
Once the units are here they have to pass through customs and I don't know how long that is going to take.
Please know that I might be completely in the dark when it comes to timelines from now on. I promise to do my absolute best to update you on anything that happens.

PCB:
Created a build with the production unit PCB on Friday night and it worked perfectly.
The new JST cable fits really well in the bottom case.
The matte black/gold colors look great! Thanks, Mechlovin',  for your hard work on these!
Seeing the PCB automatically show up on VIA when plugging in the USB-C cable was a special feeling

No other updates to report.
