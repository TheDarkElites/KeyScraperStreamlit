zen aesthetic with vaporwave flavor

Story
Calligraphy, which was brought to Japan from China along with Kanji characters, is not only a method of recording and weaving eternal history and literary works, but also an art form in itself and an embodiment of Zen. The world painted with a pitch-black SUZURI inkstone and ink is a minimalistic yet meaningful representation of the spirit of Zen.

With deep black keycaps and jet black mono legends, MDA Future Suzuri is sure to become the inkstone of our time, and of the future, by bringing meaningful ideas into this world with one keypress at a time.

Get MDA Future Suzuri to type the future.


Legend Kit
 
- 191 keys, including Japanese calligraphy vaporwave novelties.
- The all-in-one kit is compatible with 40%, 60%, 65%, TKL, Ortholinear and most of split layouts like Corne.
- 1u, 1.25u, 1.5u, 2u, 2.25u, 2.75u and 3u convex keys available for split spacebar layouts.
- All MONO legends.
- ISO Enter and some ISO/JIS symbol keys.
- 'F' key and 'J' key with scoops and bars.
