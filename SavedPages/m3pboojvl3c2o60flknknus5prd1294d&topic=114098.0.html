Hi all,

First and foremost, I would like to apologize that I failed to let y'all know that there was a change. I thought I had let y'all know or posted somewhere but I clearly didn't do so.

In mid-July 2022, Novelkeys reached out to me and notified me that the novelty design (Cherry Enter and Cherry) fell below GMK's required specs. I was presented with 2 options.

Option 1: Increase the spacing around the cherries to 0.35mm
Option 2: Change to padprint to maintain the tightness around the cherry as well as some details




I made the decision to change to padprint strictly because they would not be able to do the leaf section correctly and you would lose a lot of details with going doubleshot. It wasn't a simple decision to make, since I have made the promise that these would be doubleshot. It was either go doubleshot and you get something that wasn't reflected in the renders OR go padprint and offer you something visually equal to the renders but not 100% as promised.

I understand not everyone is happy with the decision to move to padprint and I apologize for not informing you, but I hope with this, you can understand why the change was made.
