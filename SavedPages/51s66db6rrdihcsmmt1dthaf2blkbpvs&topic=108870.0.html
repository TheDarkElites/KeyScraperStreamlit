Hello Geekhack!


The LastPadawan team received a large amount of feedback on the designs featured during the interest check.

With that in mind, we are going to offer ALL of the colorways previously seen in the interest check, as well as some additional colorways!



The 'Mono' Collection, now available for Pre-Orders | Designs by Misobee, video courtesy of Nicholai.Design | Brought to your desk by the LastPadawan Team





Let's recap:

The 'Mono' Collection consists of a Yin and Yang design featuring Kuro/Shiro inspired logos.

We begin with our two featured designs:



GROUPBUY WAVE 1:




Presenting "Yin"

Featuring a Kuro/Shiro inspired logo by Misobee.

Offered in 700mm x 400mm x 4mm

OR

900mm x 400mm x 4mm for 24 hours only, with unlimited purchases during this window.









Presenting "Yang"

Featuring a Kuro/Shiro inspired logo by Misobee.

Offered in 700mm x 400mm x 4mm

OR

900mm x 400mm x 4mm for 24 hours only, with unlimited purchases during this window.










GROUPBUY WAVE 2 - Launching Thursday, October 8 at 5 PM EST via the LastPadawan Store:


 
The 'Mono' Collection also includes Yin and Yang options without the Kuro/Shiro inspired logos and 9 additional complementary color offerings based on previously released GMK keycap color palettes.


All of WAVE 2 will only be offered in 900mm x 400mm x 4mm sizing.


Yin, without Kuro/Shiro inspired logo.




Yang, without Kuro/Shiro inspired logo.





Presenting 9 other color offerings in the 'Mono' Collection.


Dawn - Featuring the GMK Carbon Colorway.





Dusk - Featuring the GMK Mizu Colorway.





Sunrise - Featuring the GMK Red Samurai Colorway.





Eclipse - Featuring the GMK Dark Colorway.





Midnight - Featuring the GMK N9 Gray Colorway.





Vapor - Featuring the GMK Laser Colorway.




Gorudo - Featuring the GMK Lux Colorway.




Blood Moon - Featuring the BRED PBT Colorway.




Midori - Featuring the GMK Wavez Colorway.




PRICING:

For the LastPadawan Store (US) - the price will be $16.99

Proxy pricing is TBA but will likely be the same as our Duckmats which are currently in GB with the same proxies for the 'Mono Collection.'




Schedule:

The WAVE 1 Groupbuy start date is October 3rd at 11 AM EST (for the LastPadawan Store), with a staggered launch for our other proxies.

Updates to be posted on our discord in real-time. Our website, store proxy links, Reddit, and Geekhack posts will all be incrementally updated as time allows as proxy links go live.

The WAVE 1 Groupbuy end date is October 4th at 11 AM EST (for the LastPadawan Store), with a staggered end date for our other proxies.



Wave 2 launching Thursday, October 8th at 5 PM EST via the LastPadawan Store!.




Confirmed Vendors/Proxies:


Wave 1 and Wave 2:

It will be available with the following vendors, links to be updated as they go live:


Asia: iLUMKB (Proxy)

CA: DeskHero (Proxy)

EU: CandyKeys (Proxy)
Yin
Yang:

OCE: WAVE 1 is being handled by the LastPadawan Store for your region (send us an email via fitz@lastpadawan.com or contact us over Discord from the link below for further details)

US: LastPadawan Store

WAVE 2 ONLY:
OCE: Daily Clack (Proxy)
