GMK Alpine
Welcome to Switzerland!


GB December 1st - January 12th


Have you ever been in the Alps and wanted to take a piece of it home with you? GMK Alpine is a Keycap set revolving around the beautiful Swiss Alps.

Discord Server

Price:


MOQ (Updated 25/01, Extras included):


Base: 750 / 250
Ascents: 367 / 100
Spacebars: 205 / 100
NorDeUk: 100/100
40%: 101 / 100
Deskmat Edelweiss: 50
Deskmat Matterhorn: 63
Deskmat Gray Alpen: 154
Deskmat Purple Alpen: 72


Vendors:
Lead Vendor EU: Candykeys
US: Kono.store
Asia: KBDFans
Australia: Daily Clack
UK: ProtoTypist
Canada: Ashkeebs


Kits:










Colors:
For the colors I wanted a simple look that kind of resembles a mountain.
The 4012 is a gray with a hint of purple as to not make this a boring gray set.
The WS2 resembles the Snow and the beautiful Edelweiss, the national Swiss flower.






Renders (Done by me):


Nemui by Bachoo


Whiskey by Me


Program Yoink by Steve Laroo

Renders do not reflect final product.


Deskmats:





Thank you so much to Virtual Designworks for the amazing art. It's been an absolute pleasure to work with you and I love what you did!





Collaborations:

Keebstuff Kabelmanufaktur

I'm very happy to announce that I will be working with Keebstuff on an unlimited number of high quality custom cables!
He puts amazing and careful work into each cable and they end up looking fantastic!

The cables will be coiled with the coiled side being Purple and the non coiled side will be white. They will come with LEMO Connectors (Made in Switzerland )!
You can see more pictures of the cable and his other awesome work on his Instagram

GMK Alpine x Keebstuff Cables will also be available for preorder at the same time as the GB Dec 1st - Jan 12th






Color matching complete! I approved both colors, they're spot on! Really like how the purple turned out!
