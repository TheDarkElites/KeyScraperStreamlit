[GB] Roadkit Mini Numpad by TheVan Keyboards
« on: Wed, 05 October 2016, 22:55:17 »
The Roadkit is a 100% programmable macropad.  The case is designed to fit perfectly along side a MiniVan or ride solo.  The PCB and Plates support both MX and Alps/Matias switches. 

Order Here https://thevankeyboards.com/products/roadkit-pre-order

Pre-order closes on October 17th 2016

Each kit contains the following:

- CNC machined aluminum case
- 2 plates offering both the traditional numpad layout and all single unit keys layout
- PCB
- Screws and Allen wrench

To complete your macropad you will need to source the following

- Switches 13 or 16 depending on layout (http://www.switchtop.com/ or https://mechanicalkeyboards.com/)
- USB Mini B cable
- Keycaps (https://thevankeyboards.com/products/lightcycle-keycap-set)
- Stabilizers (cherry or costar)
- LEDs (optional)

The Roadkit is available in black, silver, and teal anodize as well as two premium finishes - Nickel and Jet Black.
