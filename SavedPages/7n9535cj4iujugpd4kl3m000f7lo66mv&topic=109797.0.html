TSUKUYOMI - A 65% With Left Macro Column



Black & E-White Tsukuyomi


IC Thread
CannonKeys discord
persocom discord



Group Buy Information

The group buy will begin on:
CannonKeys - November 28th at 11AM EST
Desk Hero- November 28th at 11AM CST
protoTypist - November 28th at 11AM EST
ILUMKB - November 29th, 11 AM SGT
Daily Clack - Please check the Daily Clack server for updates on GB time
and will continue until December 4th

Extra plates and PCBs will be available for the whole duration even if the base kits sell out sooner.
There will be roughly 350 units TOTAL.

PRICING
Base kit - $440 + shipping

The base kit includes:
-Case top (Choice of WKL or WK) and bottom
-Weight with internal engraving and badge insert (Sandblasted brass with protective coat)
-Plate (Sandblasted aluminum - Black)
-PCB (Designed by Upas, QMK/VIA compatible, SOLDER ONLY)
-All screws (hex) needed to assemble the keyboard
-O-rings for optional burger mounting style
-Custom case feet (these will not be the bumpons shown in the prototypes)
-CannonKeys carrying case with custom foam to fit Tsukuyomi
(for reference, see the small variant of these)

EXTRA PARTS
PCB - $45
Aluminum Plate (Black) - $45
Polycarbonate (Sandblasted) - $45
Carbon Fiber Plate - $40
FR4 Plate - $25
Brass Plate (Sandblasted) - $50

VENDORS
CannonKeys - US, Central & South America
Desk Hero - Canada
protoTypist - UK/Europe
ILUMKB - AP/China
DailyClack - Australia

REGION LOCKS ARE PLANNED

TIMELINE
The current ETA of fulfillment is Q3 2021
This is subject to change as the unexpected can always happen.
I will keep everyone up to date on the status of things as we go.
That said, if you have questions please consider joining my discord server, as I tend to post new information there before I can get to GH because it's a bit easier.

Typing test with various keycap profiles by Minterly

Everyone in the Tsukuyomi private GB server for being into this enough to convince me to run it.

  • 12/03/2020 - All vendors are sold out of units.  Extra parts are still available until Dec 4th.  Once the GB closes, CannonKeys will begin collecting all of the international order info, and we will prepare to submit the order to the factory.  I will update again once the order has been submitted.

  • 12/22/2020 - End of year check-in. Nothing major to report. Last I've heard, we are in the production queue.  I'll be making an effort to provide "Update - No updates" posts periodically during long stretches of silence in the production period.  If something major happens, I'll update the post title and leave a new comment in the thread.  Thank you for your patience.
  • 01/12/2021 - Happy new year!  We received the sample PCB and it has been approved for production
  • 01/28/2021 - Machining prep will begin before CNY!
  • 03/04/2021 - Just checking in.  The factory seems to be back from the holiday so machining preparation will continue soon, if it hasn't already.
  • 05/26/2021 - Production PCBs and carbon fiber plates have arrived at the factory. Cases are in the machining stage. Sorry for the large gap in updates, there hasn't been much to report so most of the monthly check-ins have been on discord.
  • 06/22/2021 - It looks like a bunch of units are ready to be sent for blasting and ano/e-coat. The photos I received were all WK units so I haven't yet confirmed that all configurations are done machining, but this means we're still progressing. Thank you all for your continued patience! I will be sure to add more info as I receive it.
  • 08/02/2021 - According to the CannonKeys news letter, it looks like the updated ETA is looking more like October/November 2021. Thank you for your patience!
  • 09/23/2021 - Nothing substantial to report. According to the most recent communication we've had, we are anodizing and still on track for the ETA in the previous update.
  • 11/22/2021 - Slow moving for the last few months, but we made it into anodizing. It looks like the updated ETA is more likely to be Q1 2022. Thank you all for your patience!
  • 01/31/2022 - Sorry for the gap between updates, it's been a lot of "in production" non-updates. We are currently waiting for the units to ship to the vendors. Please continue to follow updates from the vendor you placed your order with as they will likely be updating faster than I can.
  • 03/24/2022 - Units have started shipping from Cannon Keys. If you have not received tracking yet, please check your vendor's updates for any information on shipments in your region.  Different vendors have different project backlogs, so shipping schedules will vary.
