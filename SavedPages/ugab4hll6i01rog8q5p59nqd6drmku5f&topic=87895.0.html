Hi,

Back in 2013, Originative made a set called Olivetti. They used U9 and CP for the alpha base and V4 for the legends. I want to bring this set back, but in colors I like. The colors we will be using are U9 for Modifiers and L9 for Alphas. The legend will be V4. I also decided to add support for a Row 5 and 1800 compatible layout.

A similar set was produced by Olivetti, an Italian company who produces typewriters and mechanical keyboards. They used Cherry Molds to produce the keycaps for their Cherry board, but they are now some of the most sought after keycaps.
