Invoices will go out periodically when I hit enough orders to fill a batch.  Experience is about 20 orders per set of blocks.
If you already submitted your order, make a new order and add that to the comments and I will take care of it, or just PM me.  I will add it myself.  This is a standing offer that I give any GB I conduct.
