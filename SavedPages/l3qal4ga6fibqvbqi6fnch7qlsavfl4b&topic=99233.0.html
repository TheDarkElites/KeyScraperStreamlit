Hey everyone! Honch0 here. You guys spend alot of time on mechmarket, and you know as well as I do that there has been a MASSIVE uptick in group buys lately. Seems at any given time there are 3 or more GMK buys going concurrently. GMK sets are great. They are, in my humble opinion, the finest keycaps money can buy. But compatibility drives the cost up, limited font options, and they just dont fit everyone's budget.   
 
Last year I started working with a new factory in China (new to me at least, these guys have been making kits awhile now). I wanted to bring something to market that was different, affordable, fun, and high quality. After months of screwing around on Illustrator and getting samples back and forth allow me to present: 
 
TKC Katakana Cherry PBT
 
- PBT dye sublimation 
- Target price $75 +/- depending on color way
- Mac keys included in every kit 
- UK ISO keys in every kit 
- Ortho Keys Included
- Waiting on factory to see if we can add a 6u centered stem spacebar and R3 Esc
- 4 color ways available (for now) 
- Estimated delivery April 2019
- Kits packed in trays
 
Proxies:
- Americas - The Key Dot Company

- Asia / Oceana - Dailyclack
