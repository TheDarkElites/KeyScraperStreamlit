Intro

Hi there~ This is a product from Stilou Studio, attracted by the beautiful curves and minimalist design, the team (KEZEWA) have reached a cooperation intention with the designer to jointly launch this graceful and gentle Keyboard--Spectacle60.

Spectacle design is unique, with soft curves on the side profile, which is visually appealing. We hope to create a striking product while maintaining the delicate and gentle design. With the help of existing technology, Spectacle uses 5-axis CNC machining technology to make our imagination a reality.

Design

▼ The gentle curves of the side profile are the main selling point of Spectacle. This appearance is achieved through the use of 5-axis CNC machining technology.



Another highlight of this kit is the extensive color selection and matching options. Players can freely create their own unique combinations from the parts below:


└ Top case color options:
Grey; Purple
Silver; Wine Red
Blue; Chinese Red
Black; Pink


└ Bottom case color options:
Purple; Gray
Wine Red; Silver

Update 1: According to the suggestions from creators and players, we add another color option -- Dark Green.



Update 2: Add one more option for the bottom case, players can choose brass bottom for GB. The price will be higher than Aluminum version.



Looking forward to seeing sophisticated gamers create stunning exclusive keyboards through color combinations.

Structure

▼ Given the extremely minimalist design of the keyboard, we do not want any exposed screws on the exterior to break this ultimate simplicity. Therefore, we have designed a screw-less structure that secures the bottom cover from the inside of the keyboard.
The mount chosen for this keyboard is a standard gasket mount, however this uses gasket socks, for ease and precision of placement of the gaskets onto the plate tabs. These gasket socks are thinner on the top and thicker on the bottom to try to optimise sound and feel at the same time~




Update 3:The structure will be changed to support both Top and Gaskets.

PCB and Layouts

▼ PCB has two options, both use QMK firmware and are VIA compatible:

The PCB options on offer will be 1.6mm, non-flex cut or 1.2mm flex cut, hotswap or soldered, all of these four choices will support the following layouts.



Specification
 
► Materials: 6063 aluminium top, 6063 aluminum or brass bottom case
► Layout: 60%
► Mount: Top + Gasket mount (thinner on top, thicker on bottom)
► Plate material: PC, optional Alu, POM and FR4
► Typing angle: 6°
► Front height: 18.98mm
► Foam: optional foam offered: 3.5mm poron foam, 0.5mm switch pad, 2mm case foam, 2mm pcb foam
