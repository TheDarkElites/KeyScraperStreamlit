[GB] Custom 60% keyboard kit (Campine x Gazzew)
« on: Sat, 01 April 2017, 16:58:02 »
Tl;dr:
Group Buy for a stainless steel 60% plate, bent steel case and PCB supporting arrow cluster on the primary layer. Order form below:

https://goo.gl/NgqV2t

WHAT:
I'm doing a group buy for custom keyboard kit. The parts are compatible with all popular 60% PCBs and plates, so you could potentially part out the full kit if you chose.
- The cases are bent 304 18 ga steel with several mounting points for compatibility.
- The switchplates are 16 ga stainless steel supporting all PCB layouts and both MX and Alps switches. Both steel parts take to aesthetic modifications very well.
- The PCB supports several different bottom row layouts, including an arrow cluster on the top layer. It has oversized LED holes for proper SIP socket insertion. NOTE: This version of the PCB does NOT support alps switches. There is also monochrome underglow. PCBs are flashed with ps2avrGB and fully support bootmapperclient.

If the information is relevant, the response will be cross-posted to GH and r/mk.

I should have the parts and will prepare them for shipping in the 3 days following the order fulfilment, with everyone receiving tracking info to their Paypal email.
