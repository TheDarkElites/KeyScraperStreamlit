GMK Moonlight
---------------------------------------------------------------

The inspiration for this set came from my love for the great outdoors, specifically the ambiance from the moon. This set uses a dark blue-grey to represent the cool color of the night, and a beige-gold to signify the yellow hue the moon gives off on a night in the wilderness.

The novelties have been catered to create a visual representation of the outdoors, with rocky mountains, toasty campfires, as well as camping!

Groupbuy Live May 21st through June 26th!
Begins at 3:00 pm EST

Hey all!

Sorry for the lack of communication here. I posted pretty fairly regular updates on /mechmarket and /mechanicalkeyboards on Reddit, and answered questions on the vendor discords as frequently as I could. I apologize for not giving geekhack the same attention that I gave the other venues. I genuinely do not check GeekHack as often as I should compared to Reddit and discord. I know that some of you chose not to join the group buy as I missed some of the comments and posts on this thread, so I am sorry for that. If you have any questions or concerns regarding the set that you want to reach out to me about, please feel free to message me on discord (Fox404#1741) or on Reddit (u/mkljfox). That said, I know that limited extras will be available down the road for every kit. I'll try to address some of the comments (albeit late) below. Thanks so much to everyone who supported the set and joined the group buy.

As far as the "unofficial renders" that were posted: the individual reached out to me privately, so we spoke there and the renders were removed by that user so I did not comment on them publically. They were not an accurate representation of the colors used in this set. I tried to get additional renders done but the individual who did the original renders was no longer taking commissions due to time constraints. I reached out to a few others, and those that replied had no time to get them done due to other obligations. I (poorly) attempted to do some myself, but they didn't turn out well at all so I chose not to post them (Rendering well is difficult; I find it pretty dang tough to do). The colors used in this set have both been used by GMK in the past, so matching should not be a problem.

Numbers: They were great. I mentioned in an earlier post that all kits were being made, but below are the final numbers.



Thanks again for the support, and be sure to look for updates from your local vendors on the production timeline. Joining their discords is a great way to get the most up-to-date information.
