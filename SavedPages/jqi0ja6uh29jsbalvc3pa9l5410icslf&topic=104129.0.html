Hey there!
I'm eskimojo, and have been in the community for a year or so. This is my first set, and I'm super excited to share this with you.
This set is inspired by the character Wiz from the anime Konosuba, who is a kindly undead shopkeeper.


IC link

Vendors

EU - MyKeyboard
Asia - zFrontier (EN) zFrontier (CN)
Canada - DeskHero

Prices

Please note that the MOQs noted above are purely suggested, and Keyreative's MOQs work in a very different manner to any other manufacturer.
MOQ has now been hit, and all kits will be made.

KAT Lich launches on the 7th of January 2020, and run until the 31st!

The current estimated shipping date of this keyset is October 2020.


Kits

Base alphas for ANSI, ISO US and UK.


Modifiers for boards sized 60% to TKL. Includes ISO, tsangan, HHKB, and most others.
This also includes an R3 up arrow, as this was a feature from MT3 I particularly enjoyed and wanted for KAT.


Coverage for those inclined for smaller boards.


For those who like their board a little more gridlike.


Covers ergo boards and Boardwalk.


Covers ergo boards and Boardwalk.


Support for those who like their boards big and beautiful.


Add some magic to your board with these icons. Accent arrows also included.


For those who like their spacebars accented (or smaller). Includes extra B, for Alice users.


Adds coverage for northern international languages. Essentially a NorDe+ kit.


Adds coverage for those further south, covering both Spanish and Portuguese layouts along with Italian.


Coverage for AZERTY users (including France, Belgium and Northwest Africa).


Coverage for Dvorak and Colemak derived layouts.

Profile
I have chosen to design this set for KAT, as its silky smooth PBT surface combined with a comfortable and subtle sculpt makes it my favourite profile by far.
Additionally, they have the capability to do reverse dye sub, as seen in samples for KAT Eternal and KAT Oasis.
The MOQ system for KAT is also unique, and is done in such a way that allows for child kits that may not usually get enough support to be made. I've taken advantage of this by offering multiple international kits and colevrak support.

Colours
This set uses four Pantones as these are preferred for dye sublimation.
I have samples of these pantones in-hand to match to, and am happy with them.




I have a channel for updates and discussion in my Discord server, and will of course keep this thread updated.

Update: R1/R2 Colour Samples

Full album (warning, some potato pictures)

The first colour samples are in! Overall I think they look great, and are very promising. I've fed back to Keyreative with some suggested tweaks, which I'll list below.

  • Alphas (2085C) - Current samples appear to be more saturated than intended. I've requested a lighter version.
  • Accent 1 (2008C) - Current samples appear to be a little darker than the pantone I have on hand. That said, I do like the current colour a lot. I've requested a lighter version, and will see which I prefer.
  • Accent 2 (4119C) - Current samples are a little more blue than I'd like. I've requested for them to be made a little more purple/red.

I am satisfied with the modifier colour (4126C) - we were originally worried it would be too saturated, but after having some alternate pantone samples made up, I believe that the original colour is the best choice.

Minor update: Ergo
After discussion with a community member, it came to my attention that KAT R1 may not be the best choice of sculpt for the middle and thumb cluster keys in the ergo kits, as its faces would be pointing away from the thumb. As such, I have updated the kits to use R2, as shown below. This change has been confirmed with Keyreative.
