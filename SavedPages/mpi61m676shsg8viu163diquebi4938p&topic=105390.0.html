Yea no problem, I'll upload a few pics.

About the acrylic. It's a big problem because it's being used for PPE. Japan is shutdown, which means a lot of companies are not going to work. Acrylic companies in Japan are currently not supplying normal cut sheets of acrylic. So the market for acrylic has dried up and there is currently no way to purchase 3mm clear acrylic in Japan that I know of at a reasonable price . The quotes that I did get from Japanese companies were double the price of what their own website says. I did get in contact with a China supplier and got pictures of acrylic that would be suitable just in case this shutdown continues and acrylic is still unavailable. I figured a "wait and see approach" would be better for the time being. The timeline for acrylic has been like this:

  • Group buy ends
  • Websites in Japan shut down orders of acrylic
  • Buy from alternative place to test acrylic
  • Alternative place to test acrylic dries up
  • Found a Chinese supplier just in case with DHL shipping

If things don't ease up soon, I'm going to pull the trigger and have some sent from China or have China do the cutting and ship. Hopefully, Japan Acrylic manufacturers will open up soon.

Slice orders are being kitted and they will be shipped out starting next week!   

SHIPPING: Shipping to many countries is now more expensive than initially thought, but it's taken care of. All US orders will be shipped UPS. If there is any problem shipping to your country, you'll be contacted individually. The way some orders are being shipped you may incur a customs fee, there is nothing I can do about it, and you'll need to pay it. Thanks for your understanding regarding customs fees.

Slice Hi-Pro has also finished and can be cut right away. Here are some rough pictures of the case, more to come soon. If you are interested in one, please PM me.

This has 2x 3mm acrylic pieces to tilt the case towards the user. Followed by a 3mm backplate, 3x 3mm surrounding acrylic pieces, 3mm switch plate, 2x 3mm surrounding top plates.

The highest top plate encloses the pro micro and oled to protect it from dust.

SLICE RGB USERS: Two noteworthy items. The OLED pins will need to be removed and replaced. I will have a ez mode guide on how to accomplish this with just a cutters and a soldering iron.  Also, If you decide that you do not want to use the OLED Displays, you will need to use the QMK command line to disable OLED. If you don't do this the polling rate will be incorrect causing a delayed like response. OLED is turned on by default in QMK. If you would like your Pro Micros setup before shipping, please PM me directly.

Are you going to release the file for the case? Im reluctant to start building my kit without the case. Thanks.

Default for Slice and Slice RGB is MASTER_LEFT. You can change this by using the command line interface and changing to MASTER_RIGHT in the Config.

Additionally you can use EE_HANDS and flash each side individually with their respective hand. For example: default:avrdude-split-left for left and split-right for right. Then you can choose either hand you want to plug in at anytime. It will know which is which.
