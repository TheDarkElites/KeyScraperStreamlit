Meet my latest project, Artisans Box and I call it "Historia". I am sure some of you who collect Artisan Keycaps have a history behind it. This box has a badge that is attached using a magnet and can serve as a leg so you can display it flat or tilted, and will be equipped with a sleeve to protect it during travel.





Specifications
  • 25 Slots (5x5) & 49 Slots (7x7)
  • 6063 Aluminium with Sand Blasted Finish
  • Acrylic Display Cover
  • Stainless Steel Badge
  • Cell Dimensions (LxWxH):
    - 5x5 = 132 x 132 x 23mm
    - 7x7 = 175 x 175 x 23mm
  • Cell Wall Height: 7mm

I have received my Historia boxes!
They look great. Especially the 'patina' one.
Look at that subtle off-blue coloring. The tasteful thickness of it. Oh, my God. It even has a watermark.
The weight of the boxes alone gives them a premium feel. Matched colored sleeves are a nice touch.

But the bad thing is the magnetic leg stand. The magnets are way too weak to hold even empty boxes in standing positions and the boxes collapse. My boxes are in a standing position because I  have put other stuff behind them.
I know it's supposed to be a sleek design, but it simply doesn't work. A leg with screw mounting would be much better.
