Hi all,







This is the GB for the KIRA80 & KIRA S by thesiscamper, group buy will be done via monokei.us

1. KIRA80 (TKL):


  • Top piece
  • ANSI plate (no ISO option)
  • WKL/WK available (once winners have been announced)
  • PCB (mini USB)
  • Bottom piece
  • Brass weight (MATTE finish, K I R A)
  • typing angle is 8*
  • PCB by TGR Keyboards, bootmapper

Prices (USD):
  • $400 for one set (case, PCB, brass weight, aluminium plate) does not include shipping/fees.
  • $35 for additional brass switch plate
  • $25 for additional polycarbonate switch plate
  • $30 for additional aluminium switch plate
  • $60 for additional PCB

2. KIRA S (numpad):


  • Top piece
  • PCB (mini USB)
  • Bottom piece
  • Brass weight
  • typing angle is 8*
  • PCB by TGR Keyboards, bootmapper

Prices (USD):
  • $200 for one set (case, insert, PCB, brass weight, aluminium plate, Shopify fees), does not include shipping.
  • $70 for additional aluminium switch plate + PCB

Info:

  • GB limited to 50 sets each
  • TKL Plate supports varied bottom row (6.25u, 7u spacebars)


Additional pictures
https://imgur.com/a/52W8eL7



Shipping:

  • Shipping from Singapore.
  • Shipping cost will depend on location.
Estimated shipping cost (USD):
  • Singapore: $5
  • Malaysia: $20
  • Australia: $45
  • Korea: $27
  • USA: $62
  • Germany: $51

Payment:
  • Prices listed are in USD. Payment in SGD as the shop can only process in SGD, sorry
  • No PayPal invoice. All payment via Shopify payments so buyer protection still available
  • Shipping will be calculated once the orders are ready to go out, so you'll receive additional instructions then

Misc:

  • Deadline for raffle entry is 12am on 03 July 2018 (SGT, GMT+8).
  • Deadline for payment is 48 hours.
  • Countdown timer.
  • Raffle entries will be RNG-ed, and then you will receive an email regarding payment and other things.
  • For any changes to your order, please send a PM when necessary. Please note that this is a small buy and we will proceed with manufacturing very soon, so changes might disrupt the process.
  • Please include your order number when sending a PM for changes.
  • No refunds or transfer of spots once payment has gone through to the factory. These will be possible once we receive the items to minimize too many refunds/transfers/sales of spots that tend to happen

Production lead time:

This time around it should take about 4-5 months given that we have more units compared to previous GBs. Please be aware there will likely be long periods of time without any substantial updates from the factory other than "it's in production".
