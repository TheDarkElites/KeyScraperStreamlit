January - A 60% O-Ring Mount

by r00dy, jr2417, and kitkat. Join our discord here. You can find the latest updates here on our documentation site. All of this information can be found in this geekhack post.





Design

The January is a 60% o-ring mounted keyboard featuring accentuated curves and a copper weight. We originally designed the board to test some aesthetic choices, most notably the gentle side sweep (inspired by the Dalco 959 Mini) with a rounded front and back.






Case Specifications
  • Anodized Aluminum Options: Crimson, Steel Blue, Silver
  • Polycarbonate Options: Frosted, Smoked
  • Plates: 1.5 mm Aluminum, Polycarbonate, FR4, Carbon Fiber
  • Typing Angle: 6 degrees
  • Mounting Style: Gummy O-Ring





GroupBuy Information
  • Vendors and Opening Times
    • US: KeebsForAll - 10 AM PST, November 7th (waitlisted buyers are eligible to purchase now)
    • Canada: Deskhero - Open Now!
    • EU: Candykeys - 7 PM GMT +2:00, November 7th
    • Asia: ZionStudios: 8:00 PM PHT
    • Oceania: SwitchKeys: 11 AM AEDT, November 7th
  • Format: First Come First Serve with a generous soft cap (think of it as our human limit)
  • End Date: Dec 20th
  • MOQ: 250 units
  • ETA: conservatively Q4 2023 (may be less with lower sold qty)





PCB Options
  • The Hotswap PCB supports the following:
    • Normal and Stepped Caps Lock
    • Split Backspace
    • Split Right Shift (updated to reflect layout images on vendor sites)
    • Bottom Row: 1.5u 1u 1.5u 7u 1.5u 1u 1.5u
  • The Soldering PCB supports the following:
    • Normal and Stepped Caps Lock
    • Normal and Split Backspace
    • Normal and Split Right Shift
    • Bottom Row: 1.25u 1.25u 1.25u 6.25u 1.25u 1.25u 1.25u 1.25u
    • Bottom Row: 1.5u 1u 1.5u 7u 1.5u 1u 1.5u
    • ISO: Solder PCBs support ISO Layout, but GB plate does not. ﻿Plate files will be released to the public closer to delivery date for anyone who would want them.
  • Both PCBs have RGB Underglow
  • An Ai03 Universal Daughterboard and JST are included with each PCB





Configurations and Pricing
  • Base Kit will include the following:
    • Material and Color Options (pricing in USD, regional pricing will vary slightly):
      • Crimson Anodized Aluminum - $270
      • Steel Blue Anodized Aluminum - $270
      • Silver Anodized Aluminum - $270
      • Frosted Polycarbonate - $270
      • Smoked Polycarbonate - $295
    • Copper Weight
    • Aluminum Plate
    • O-Ring (50A)
    • Feet
  • Note: The base kit does not include a PCB. The January is compatible with both the H60 and the custom January PCB being sold as an extra. This allows buyers to swap in existing o-ring builds (with an H60) into the January.
  • Extras Pricing in USD (regional pricing will vary slightly):
    • Plates:
      • Aluminum Plate - $28.00
      • Polycarbonate Plate - $28.00
      • FR4 Plate - $28.00
      • Carbon Fiber Plate - $32.00
    • PCBs:
      • Solder PCB - $40.00
      • Hotswap PCB - $45.00
    • Wrist Rests:
      • Frosted Polycarbonate - $160
      • Smoked Polycarbonate - $175
