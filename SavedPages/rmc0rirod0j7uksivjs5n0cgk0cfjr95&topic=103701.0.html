KAM Little Pilot

This keyset is inspired by visual identity elements of Wes Anderson's Isle of Dogs, and named after the nickname of the film's main character.
Little Pilot is the second ever KAM profile keyset, and after multiple rounds of samples, it's time for the group buy!


About KAM

KAM is a uniform keycap profile from Keyreative, the makers of KAT.
It features thick PBT plastic with a glossy finish and dye-sublimated legends.

Re: [GB] KAM Little Pilot (GB Closed)
« Reply #37 on: Fri, 10 January 2020, 05:38:49 »
Quote from: i luv chuletas on Thu, 09 January 2020, 23:14:41
Hey, loved the design so much I had to make this my first GB 

I kind of realized after it closed that the alpha kit does not include homing row keys. Is there any way at all for me to get a homing kit after the GB closed??

I know it's a stretch, but figured I'd ask!
Hey, the base alphas include homing and non-homing keys for F and J (look in the lower right corner of the render). If you would like homing keys for the classic alternative homing position for D and K, I can send you those at shipping cost (I'm in the EU). You're probably not using some layout that's not QWERTY, are you? In that case you would need to pay attention to homing kits :/

/edit: To clarify and to make future buying decisions easier, the homing kits are generally just for people who use more eccentric non-standard layouts or in this case also retro middle finger homing keys. If you're using a standard layout you don't need to worry about homing kits generally. If you want to learn more about non-standard layouts, I'd suggest googling Colemak layout. (That's the one the homing T and N in the homing kit are for)
