VØID
65% Top Mount + O-Ring Keyboard



Group Buy starts on 9th - 30th Sep
Shipping in Q1 2025

Based on the feedback we've received from the community, we know how important the use of metal inserts is. After giving it some thought, we’ve decided to include metal inserts for the polycarbonate version to improve the durability and lifespan of the screw threads—especially since custom keyboards tend to be built and rebuilt often.


Interest check thread






1. Intro

After 1 year and 8 months of development and extensive prototype testing, we are proud to introduce Vøid—a 65% layout mechanical keyboard kit featuring a dual mounting system. Vøid allows users to choose between a top mount for a firmer typing experience with a pronounced sound, or an o-ring mount for a softer, more isolated typing experience. This flexibility ensures a highly customizable and satisfying typing experience tailored to individual preferences.

5. Specifications
- Layout: 65%
- Mounting system: Top mount and gummy o-ring mount
- Typing Angle: 7.5°
- Effective keyboard height: 23.35mm
- Plate: 1.5mm Aluminium, Polycarbonate, POM, Carbon Fiber, FR4 (1.6mm)
- PCB: In-house design PCB void65 1.6mm thickness solder or hot-swap - configurable with QMK/VIA
- Weight before assembly: ± 1019g aluminum kit, 1639g copper kit
- Weight after assembly: ± 1355g aluminum kit, 1962g copper kit


Parts included with the kit:
- Keyboard case: (Top case, Bottom case, Rear bumper, Bottom plate)
- Plate (Aluminium, Polycarbonate, POM, Carbon Fiber, FR4)
- 1.6mm Hotswap/Solder PCB; layout information will be shared later
- Daughterboard JST Cable
- 1x O-ring (50a) 1x O-ring (30a)
- 4x Custom-designed rubber feet
- Hand-carry keyboard bag
- Other accessories

8. Layout




Solder layout provides more flexibility, including stepped Caps Lock, ISO, split Backspace, 7u + 6.25u spacebar, and the "cursed" layout. The cursed layout allows users who don't like arrow keys to experience the Void.




While hotswap will offer less flexibility compared to the solder version, it still supports stepped caps lock and split backspace compatibility.
