Why?
After growing up using keyboards that had the BAE key, it is sad that it became redundant. I've been in the hobby for years now and there have been countless GB's but only a few had BAE.
So I decided to do this


Why Icon Mod BAE?
More

Because it's cursed beautiful, Just look at it!Also why not?

Which stablizers will be used?
BAE will work with ANSI stablizers due to a new mould developed for GMK Beige Addon Kits.
