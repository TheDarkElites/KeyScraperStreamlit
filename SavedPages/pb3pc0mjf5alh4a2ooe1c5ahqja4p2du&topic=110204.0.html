Hey everyone!

Welcome to the group buy for Vertigo, our latest project!



Vertigo is a seamless, screwless, gasket-mounted 65% with symmetrical bottom row blockers.



There will be two editions offered for this board, the standard edition and a special edition.



The layouts offered are as shown (please note that only ANSI plates will be offered at GB, with ISO plate files being available on our website).



Standard Edition Features:
   -Byzantine Purple or Charcoal Grey aluminum shell piece (see renders for more info on colors)
   -E-white aluminum base piece
   -E-white aluminum full plate
   -Custom ek65 PCB (QMK/VIA compatible) with daughterboard
   -Custom hard carrying case
   -Custom injection-molded rubber bumpons
   -Price: $540

Special Edition Features
   -E-white aluminum shell piece
   -Brass PVD Rose Gold Base
   -Aluminum full plate
   -Custom ek65 PCB (QMK/VIA compatible) with daughterboard
   -Custom hard carrying case
   -Custom injection-molded rubber bumpons
   -Price: $845

Group Buy Information:
Group buy will happen through our custom developed raffle system!
Please note you need to be a member of our Discord server to participate in this raffle.
There are 120 units of standard edition, and 15 units of special edition available to the public.
The raffle for special edition is open Wednesday, 12/16 from 9AM to 9PM EST.
The raffle for standard edition is open Saturday, 12/19 from 9AM to 9PM EST.
Invoices will be sent within 24 hours of the raffle closing.
Orders will be shipped late summer 2021.

Smooth Curves



Vertigo features smooth curves all around. All four of its sides are sculpted upwards, giving it a thin upper bezel, but also leaving enough room on the inside for its complex internals.
The back piece of Vertigo is an elegant curve that loops around the sides of the board, in a singular flush motion. 



Screwless Bottom



From a designers’ perspective, the screwless bottom on Vertigo is arguably the most exciting part of this board. The PCB and plate are designed to allow for the screws to slot in through the plate.
The screw itself is split resting between the two different halves of the cases, which not only allows an even weight distribution, but makes this whole system possible by not interfering with the switches.



Please join our Discord server for the quickest updates, and to further discuss this board. Note that you will need to join the Discord server to be eligible for the sale!

Update 6/14


It’s that time! Apologies for the delayed update this week, but it’s a good one!

Boards have shipped from our manufacturer to our shipping partners, and they have informed us that they will receive it today or tomorrow.

The Yianten port in Shenzhen is currently facing delays due to another outbreak of the virus there, which has a ripple effect in air freight pricing and timelines. Air freight usually takes around 10-14 working days, but with these delays, we expect boards to arrive in around a months time.

Please start emailing us your address change requests, by replying to your order confirmation! Let us know if you have any more questions, and enjoy the rest of your week. Thanks!
