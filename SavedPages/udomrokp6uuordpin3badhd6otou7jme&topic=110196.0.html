« on: Mon, 14 December 2020, 17:18:50 »
Previous IC thread: https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=109798.0

Price USD 380, excludes shipping and PayPal fees.
1. Case will be top mounted, 6063 aluminum - t5 (top and bottom casing)
2. Silver aluminum universal switch plate - Anodized
3. QMK PCB - USB C (Via ready)
4. Typing angle at 11 degrees
5. 8mm 3M Bump-ons.







Accent piece and inserts
1. Brass weight - Sandblasted
2. Brass accent piece - Sandblasted (interchangeable with Acrylic insert for RGB)
3. Acrylic insert for RGB - Sandblasted (interchangeable with Brass accent piece if you hate RGB)
4. Brass logo piece (bottom) - Brushed
5. Brass top piece (top) - Brushed

I have decided to add in a brass accent piece for those that disliked RGB (cos why not? I believe in options.)
Please refer to the diagram below for parts.





Logo will be brushed, same as top piece, to accentuate it. Went to sandblast my proto, was a bad mistake, final production will be fixed.
Apologies for the finger print patina, the micro scratches are due to my bad handling and self fix lol.


Brass accent piece image:


Layout: Winkeyless, Winkey and HHKB available.



Additional parts：
1. PCB - USD 40
2. Brass switch plate - Sandblasted USD 30
3. FR4 plate - USD 25
4. Additional Acrylic insert - USD 10 (if you tend to break things and build keyboards with herculean strength, I would suggest you to get it lol)

Colour options:
1. Silver - Top + Bottom enclosure
2. Black - Top + Bottom enclosure
3. Ecoat White - Top + Bottom enclosure + USD 35

Disclaimer:
The Acrylic insert for RGB is not polycarbonate, do not flex it, it will break.

Also, Discord link: https://discord.gg/SjYB7am

GB Form: https://forms.gle/ZLrnB7EMGduFg78X6 will close before 22 Dec 2020.
