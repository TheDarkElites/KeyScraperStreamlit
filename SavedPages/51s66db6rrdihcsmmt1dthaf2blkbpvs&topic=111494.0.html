1. Keyboard Details

     PCB information (Collaboration)
          - Arctangent(KB) X Mechlovin' Adelais rev 4, blue solder-mask

     Case information
          - Mounting Style: Sandwich
          - Plate Thickness: 1.5mm
          - Typing Angle: ~6.5 degrees
          - Mounting Hardware: 1/4" diameter thick brass standoffs, low-profile top screws, standard-profile bottom screws
          - Built Weight: ~3.5 lbs / 1.6 kg (metal plate), ~2.5 lbs / 1.1 kg (FR4 plate)

     Material information
          - Plate Materials: Stainless Steel, Brass, Copper, FR4
          - Plate Finishing: Vibration, Sandblasted, Brushed (FR4 single standard finish)
          - Acrylic Colors:
               - Clear: Frosted (matte), Clear (non-glare)
               - Standard: Black, White (all matte)
               - Colored: Caribbean, Cherry Blossom, Citrus, Daffodil, Fuchsia, Glacier, Grey, Lime Green, Passion Fruit, Poppy, Soft Blue, Soft Green, Soft Red, Soft Teal, Soft Yellow, Wedgewood, Wisteria (all matte)

     Foam Information (Collaboration)
          - StupidFish.Design, 2mm thick

     Keyboard Case Comes Fully Assembled
          - Similar to Section 6.

     Timeline: Estimated ship date would be 6 months (October 2021)

          - Reason why this is 6 months is due to the fact that every case will have the protective layers removed from the acrylic and every case comes assembled.

          - Constant updates will be given either through my discord server or through Instagram (both linked above).
          - However, if there is an exponential demand for this case further than I currently imagine, I kindly ask to reserve the right to either limit the number of orders,
             adjust the estimated timeline, or adjust the fulfillment schedule for order in waves
