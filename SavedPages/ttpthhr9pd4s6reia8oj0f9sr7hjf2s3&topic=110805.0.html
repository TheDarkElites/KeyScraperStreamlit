    BOX 75
    "A box that provokes."


    dks | Lin Design Studio





    SPECS
    • Type Angle: 7°
    • Construction: Top Mount/O-ring Burger Mount. Plate and Case Foam.
    • Case Materials: Anodized or E-Coated Aluminum Body. Mirror-Polished or Black PVD Stainless Steel Accents.
    • Colors: Black, Ｗhite
    • No External Screws
    • Plate Materials: 1.5mm Aluminum, Black Smoked PC.
    • PCB: Designed by Gondolindrim with QMK/VIA support, ESD protection. Solder only.
    • Layout Supports:
      • 1.25u - 1.25u - 1.25u - 6.25u - 1.5u - 1.5u
      • 1.25u - 1u - 1.25u - 7u - 1.25u - 1.25u
      • 1.5u - 1u - 1.5u - 6u - 1.5u - 1.5u
      • Split Backspace
      • Stepped Caps Lock
      • ISO Support (PCB)



    VENDORS + PRICING + DATES
    • GB Date: From January 22nd to February 19th
    • Numbers: Unlimited
    • Vendors:
      • Novelkeys_: North America
      • Typeplus: Asia
      • Monokei: Southeast Asia
      • Mykeyboard: Europe
      • Daily Clack: Australia
    • Base Kit Prices (US):
      • $550 for the polished SS variants matched with black anodize or e-white.
      • $650 for the PVD black SS variants matched with black anodize or e-white.
    • Extras:
      • $40 Extra Aluminum Plate
      • $40 Extra Smoked Black Polycarbonate Plate
      • $40 Extra PCB + Custom Washers
      • Mykeyboard.eu offers PC ISO plate during the GB
