Group Buy Period:
  • Opens - June 21st
  • Closes - July 19th
Information:
  • Compatible with beige keysets
  • GMK Doubleshot
  • L9 base, CR legends
Vendors:
  • North America: NovelKeys - 45 USD
  • Europe: Mykeyboard - 39 EUR
  • Asia: ilumkb.com - 65 SGD
  • Oceania: Daily Clack - 70 AUD

Questions & Answers:

Q - What is the lead time?
A - GMK lead time is 16 weeks once numbers are submitted.

Q - What is the MOQ?
A - 150.

Q - What is your/Noxary's involvement with the buy?
A - Just the idea and communication with GMK - everything else is handled by the vendors.
