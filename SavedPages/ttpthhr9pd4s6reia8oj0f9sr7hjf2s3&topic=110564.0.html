UPDATE

Hello, I just want to let y’all know that I am aware that the mods of Marshmallow is a bit orange compared to the OG renders of Marshmallow. We are taking proactive steps to make sure that the set matches my expectation and as well as the customer’s expectations therefore TK.C and I will closely work together to have the colors of the mods closer to the pale color that we have fallen for. Also, please do know that this isn’t TK.C fault, they perfectly color-matched the Pantone I chose.  I will try to get the colors matched to the target modifier color can be seen here

1/12/21

An update for marshmallow, I just received the prototype today and ill tell you, everything looks good, of course, other than the modifier being a bit orange the Pantone will change from 474u to 475u to get rid of the orange hue and to look paler/tan. Also for those people who are worried about the alpha color looking orange, I can tell you that it looks more light brown IRL. I will take photos of the prototype but currently, it's cloudy where I am so ill try to take a picture with neutral lighting best as I can.
