VOD of Alexotos building his personalized Flygone60 prototype and the wonderful things he has said about the kit!

Alexotos Build Stream

The Flygone60 is a stacked acrylic, 60% layout keyboard kit that is perfect for any budget

Flygone60 Feature List:

* GH60 Inspired Layout with Multiple Bottom Row Options
*Typing Angle: ~5°
*Mounting Style: Sandwich Mount
*Front Height: 23.5 mm
*POM Plate

Layout Options

COLORS OPTIONS- White, Clear, Frosted, and Radiant

Here is the album of prototypes and renders:

https://imgur.com/gallery/iYRbHsv

FAQ

  • How much will this board cost? - A kit will be $110 USD. Radiant kit will be $160 USD 
  • What is the expected lead time? - Expected Ship Date is Q4 2021
  • Is a PCB included? - Yes.  The Flygone60 PCB will be included with each kit.
  • Does the PCB have RGB? - No.  However, the case is compatible with other 60% PCBs, so you could use a PCB that supports underglow.
  • What colors will be available? - White, Clear, Frosted, and Radiant
  • What Plate Options are available? - Only POM. If there is an R2 other options will be available.
  • Will there be any limited editions? - Sort of.  The radiant edition does cost more because I do not plan to produce many of them, some boards will have custom engravings but these are for friends/partners as a thank you for helping me refine the design.
  • What Vendors are you using? - Mechs&Co
  • Who will produce the kits? - The cases for each kit will be 100% manufactured by myself!


Thank you for taking the time to look at this post. I am excited to finally bring this board to reality with all of you!
