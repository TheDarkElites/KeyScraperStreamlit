Final update

Dec2020 Shipping update
It's been a hell of a ride, as of the writing of this message, everything that can be shipped out, has been shipped out.
We are waiting for Taiwan shipping restrictions to lift for the following areas before proceeding:
Some EU countries
Australia

Oct 2020 Shipping update
All USA packages are shipped

Asia is next!

Keep eyes on the tracking spreadsheet lads https://illuminati.works/tracking


Aug 2020 Shipping update (finally)
We have had success with our test packages to the USA, which means all 32 unshipped USA units will be sent out in the next 10 days.
I'm covering all excess shipping costs out of pocket (rates are WAY higher than originally quoted in 2019)

If you are in Asia, your batch will likely be sent next, followed by Oceania and finally EU (you guys will have to wait a while sadly)

Keep eyes on the tracking spreadsheet lads https://illuminati.works/tracking



Nov 27 2019 -
All PCBs flashed and tested
All units qced and ano matched - we have enough units to ship all of GB units, extras will be around after everyone has received their iS0s
As for extras, colors are not yet confirmed - this will depend on which units can be reworked, and which will have to be scrapped
The hard cases shipped to us were amazing, with a snug fitting foam to keep your iS0 safe, but they forgot to print my damn logo on them
As a result, we have ordered another batch of cases, with printing this time in order to save time shipping back n forth
Bumpons are also still in transit, as the bumpons delivered had a rounded bottom instead of a flat bottom, and that won't work for iS0

tldr; everything checked, good to go, waiting for bumpons and hard cases - units are ready to ship pending those and will go out in December. Once all GB units are received, information about extras will be available

Pics to follow in next post (if you are reading this on first post, check most recent posts)

Sept 25 2019 -
PCB manu has finished the round of prototype PCBs with the new top-side reset switch..(pics in latest post)

These should be en-route to sennin soon to verify the new layout works, then the rest of PCB production will start after that


Sept 11 2019 -

Main manu and pcb fab have finally been paid - We had to do a fair bit of juggling in order to avoid using HK banks due to the current stuff going on there.

As we have changed the pcb design to offer a top-side reset switch, we will be doing a prototype round of pcbs to test this before doing the production run for the safety of everyone (doing this was not cheap)

Manu and PCB will deliver mid to late Oct - Will update when I have an update on the hard case situation


Sept 1 2019 -

Aug 23 2019 -

Aug 15 2019 -

GROUP BUY INFO - UPDATES ARE IN FIRST POST NOW

iS0 (avail) - illuminati keyboards
 




SPECS:
  • 18mm front height
  • 8 degree typing angle
  • 69g weight (assembled)
  • 6063 series alu
  • 6 color options
  • Top mounted polycarbonate plate
  • USB C QMK PCB with in-switch LED and dedicated reset switch
  • Custom case for iS0
  • $69.99 preorder price



Pre-order / Group buy details:
 
-Group buy to start on Aug 1st
-Preorder will be done on Doyustudio website
-150+ units will be available, FCFS
-The buy will remain open for 31 days
-Shipping will be 4-8 months after group buy close
-All units will ship from Taiwan
-There is no limit on units per person
-Orders will be shipped in batches, in the same order they are received


Typing Test











Pcb by Ai03
-Using fullsize atmega controller (32u2)
-Dedicated reset switch on bottom
-In-switch LED support
-Will be available on config.qmk website for plebs like me





Additional notes / Q&A
 
The 8 month estimate is to prevent the usual “4 month” estimated timeline that ends up being 8 months anyways. We want to take our time and deliver the most beautiful looking keyboard we can, its likely we end up shipping before then, who knows

The $69.99 price may seem high, but in reality, we will more than likely lose money on this group buy. The chosen manufacturer is very high end (I’ll refrain from name dropping, but they produce some very high end keyboards you’ve seen) And the quality control and packaging doesn’t help the fact either. In any case, the goal is to make everyone happy

Yes, we know this project is silly/dumb. It’s not for everyone, but we hope it’s for you!
