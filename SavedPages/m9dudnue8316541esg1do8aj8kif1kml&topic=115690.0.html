Project Affogato Wrist Rest
IN STOCK SALE ON DEC 22
It has been a long time for us to go through manufacturing, international shipping, QC and packaging, but finally our in-stock sale is here!
Some pictures:











IC Links: https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=113982.0
More pictures in IC Link.

1. Features:
8-degree angle, but fits most keyboards
Aluminum base & wood top panel, forged carbon fiber panel also available as addon
Magnetic connection with silicone cushioning layer
Silicone protection layer between wrist rest and keyboard
Front height 4.8mm, rear height 16.8mm including foot pads
Length 315.5mm, which matches 65% keyboards, e.g., Keycult No.2/65, Aurora Input Series 65, could also fit some 75% keyboards depends on the length of keyboards
Width 85mm

2. Sale Details
Limited In-stock at iLumkb: https://ilumkb.com/
Open time: 2021 Dec 22, 23:00 SGT (Dec 22, 10:00 EST)
Links will be announced in iLumkb discord
https://discord.gg/WPCqFrw8
Color: black anodized base with walnut wood panel, forged carbon fiber panel will be also provided as addon
Pricing: 179 SGD for standard kit A Stock (including starter kit for wood oil treatment),
            169 SGD for standard kit A- Stock (including starter kit for wood oil treatment)，
            149 SGD for standard kit B Stock (including starter kit for wood oil treatment),
            79 SGD for forged carbon fiber panel,
            bundle price -10 SGD
Shipping: All units is packed and ready to ship, but still need a few workdays to process orders.
Streaming: TBD

Thank you all for your attention! Thanks to everyone supporting us!
Hope you can enjoy this wrist rest.
