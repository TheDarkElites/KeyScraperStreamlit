Hey friend, welcome to the addictive world of mechanical keyboards!  Just an FYI, since I'm not sure it's clear for beginners, but this is a group buy just for the keycaps, not a complete keyboard.  You'll want to select the appropriate kits out of what's listed to fit a particular layout.  To have a complete build, you'll also need the keyboard itself (consisting of a case & PCB), switches, and stabilizers.  This is for sure an end-game cap set, but you'll want to make sure you choose the correct combination to cover whatever keyboard you have (or is coming in the mail).  If you already knew this then please disregard.
