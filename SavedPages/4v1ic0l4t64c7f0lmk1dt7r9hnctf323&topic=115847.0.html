What are 'Convex' shortbars?
'Convex' refers to the shape you see here. This is the shape you would expect on any 6.25u spacebar - a rounded top that is comfortable for your thumb - except this is for shorter keycaps which have typically not been convex.

You can buy one bar if that's all you need, or you can buy a black, white, or black/white kit for a discount.

There are several colors available and several sizes. The sizes available are 2u, 2.25u, 2.75u, and 3u.

* Available in all sizes - Black and white (NN & WFK).
* Available in 2u/2.25u/2.75u - Black, White, Red (RA), Cream (WBO), Off-white (WCK), Gray (GD), Blue (BBQ)
* Available in 2u only - Yellow (YBP), Dark Navy (BFR), Green (VAT), Dark Gray (GQM)
