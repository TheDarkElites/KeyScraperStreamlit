Hi all, I am finally ready to begin the group buy for the second revision of the PLA75.



All the technical info can be found in the IC https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=105722.0. The TL;DR is that I made some changes to the back of the case to make it look cooler, and did lots of internal tweaking to make assembly a breeze. A lot of people (rightfully) complained about it after the first round, but now that I'm doing the manufacturing, I can make sure stuff is up to my standards.

Timeline and Spots:

I will be doing 15 spots at a time, and aiming to have those 15 shipped within a 4-5 week window. I will repeat this for the foreseeable future, and I don't believe in artificially limiting the supply - if you want a PLA75, you will eventually get one

Pricing and Invoicing:

Price for a kit is $185 USD. Prices on my site are in CAD rounded to the nearest $5. Raffle winners will be invoiced based on their selections from a Google form. https://rndkbd.com is live! I will also be doing custom/one off colors for a little bit extra.

Time:


The next draw is TBD Early February 2021, so please enter the form if you'd like a chance to buy!


Link for the raffle https://docs.google.com/forms/d/18Yf-tF7xdMXtl4SyrgAuTUo1nCPQm9AVbvy10gjjYUQ/
