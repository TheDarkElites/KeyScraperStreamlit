Specifications:
1. Made with 6063 aluminum for case body, brass for plate and weight.

2. Original Gasket Mounting Design. (To read more about the gasket mount: https://goo.gl/LAHjJ6)

3. Typing angle is 5 degrees.

4. Nett weight of each keyboard case is approximately 2.1KG.

5. Premium materials will be used for the packaging of each keyboard. Boxes will include all case accessories inside eg: 16 pcs of rubber dampers, screws in M3 sizing and rubber feet.


Colors:
Volcano grey
Quantum blue

Carbon black
Rose gold
Wine red
Forest green

Price:
375 USD for default kit and PC kit, final price depends on the different crafts, price listed in GB order form. (exclusive PP fee and shipping)
625 USD for PVD full brass kit.

Default Alum kit: Alum top and bottom case, brass plate and weight (default will be sandblasted matte finish+ UV anti-oxidation coating)
PC kit: PC top and bottom case, brass plate, and brass weight.
Full Brass kit: Brass top&bottom case, plate, and weight.
Upgrade crafts:
Chroming for plate and weight (Silver Color, sample:https://imgur.com/a/TqBSJRS)
PVD for plate and weight (Brass Color)

Q&A
Where is the LED indicators?
A: I have changed it to invisible micro holes, details: https://imgur.com/a/J7XYOFg

What Gasket we can use?
A: We will provide a set of gaskets for you, but you also can buy whatever gasket, silicon, rubber stripes with the same size we provided. It is not hard to find from the market. (Considered the gasket aging problem in, we have chosen the popular types of gasket instead of customized gasket)

How can I get the PC or brass during the google form GB?
Updates:edit on 7th March
A:many ppl are confusing about the previous solution. I have discussed with many users in my discord server, and here is the new solution: GB will be 2 different timing, same day(9th March)but 1 hour difference. First one will be PC/brass version, second one will be alum version). I will announce the PC/Brass winner few minutes after PC/Brass GB has finished, so all the participants will know whether you get in or out. Good Luck for everyone!

More
(Here is my solution: FSFC is the baseline, 75+10+5 is 90 pieces, if you are in first 90 orders, you can get the ticket(at least a set of alum kit, because I have alum MOQ and I can't give all of your PC or Brass, if I don't hit Alum MOQ, I am in trouble and I can't answer to factory, hope you can understand). So after choosing your first alum set, you can move to section 2 which is  PC or Brass or None.
At here, at least, you get an alum set.
Second, you can pick PC/Brass/None. It will be a multiple choice.
if you are fast enough(at first 10 PC picker or 5 Brass picker), You can buy (alum + PC, 2 sets) or (alum + brass,  2 sets) or give up your alum kit(Kit will give to No.91 buyer) and just take PC/brass.)
