Berceste - WKL65% Keyboard

Welcome to the Berceste GB thread!

GB FROM 25.07.2023 TO 09.08.2023!




The GB is live on Oblotzky.industries!


Link to the Furki.customs Discord

Link to the Furki.customs Instagram






More


https://imgur.com/a/Li6tlzn


Details
Layout: WKL 65% (ANSI and ISO)
   
Mounting: Plateless PCB leaf spring mount (included gaskets are only to be inserted into the case top!)
   
Typing angle: 7 degrees
   
Front height: 14mm (18mm without Cherry lip)
   
PCB: 1.6mm soldered with daughter board, designed by langerhans
   
Features: low USB-C position, cherry lip, HG sides
   
Materials: 6063 aluminum, stainless steel
   
Manufacturer: Dadesin
