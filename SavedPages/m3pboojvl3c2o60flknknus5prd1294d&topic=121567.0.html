XMI Vintage Support



---

UPDATES

10-16-23 We've hit soft cap, the GB is now closed, thanks to people who have supported this project!
10-17-23 Order placed with manufacturer! Undergoing some last checks before production.
11-02-23 Keycaps finished production. QC is now underway. Shipping projected to be in the next few weeks!
11-09-23 GB is now concluded as all orders have been shipped out. Support will remain open until everyone is happy with their sets! Thank you all, see you in the next one!


---

IMPORTANT 10-09-23 UPDATE: Here are the changes that will be made to the GB kit.
 

IMPORTANT 10-13-23 UPDATE: Due to concerns with my PayPal/Shopify connection and PayPal locking funds, I will be changing the sale method into Google forms + PayPal invoices. This allows me to minimize the risk for both parties and make sure the payment is a smooth process. GB time remains unchanged. Thank you!


DISCORD  |  IC PAGE  |  INSTAGRAM


Hello! This is Masje.
Welcome to the GB post of XMI Vintage Support - the PBT dyesub beige set to fit all your vintage needs.


Including:
Programmable function rows PF1 - PF24
F1-F10 macro column
SSK-style numpad
Cross Nav support (R2 up arrow)
3X5 Nav support
1800 Numpad support
UK ISO
...and a base kit to go with it!




SALES FORMAT

- This will be a short GB (lead time <2 months)
- No MOQ, will be made no matter what
- FCFS, soft cap at around 30 sets
- GB from 10/13/23 11:00pm EST to 10/20/23 11:00pm EST, or when spots are sold out
- Will be sold through google forms + PayPal here.
- Price: 65 USD 55 USD (wth, happy thocctober:))
- Shipping: estimated $30-35 to US, $25-30 to EU&Oceania, $10-15 Asia, via EMS
 (This is very high, I will look into lowering this, by a lot, hopefully. If not, I will lower GB price to offset shipping costs.)

Updated shipping prices for multiple sets, as well as lowered prices for certain regions:



NOTE[1]: keycaps will be shipped in bags (well the bags will be put into boxes and shipped to you!) NOTE[2]: Icon mods
NOTE[3]: Soft cap at 30 due to me fulfilling myself. If there is enough demand I can keep running these or do rolling in-stock in the future. If you want one, I will get you one.

I also got mine in today, the stickers are awesome! The dyesubbing was decent: on par with other XMI kits I have got (better than most other dyesubs). There were a few that are more blurry than others, but it's better than most other dyesubs (as expected). Some of the very small / tight legends are very clear.


Here is a cherry key at 45x. The one on the enter is a bit smaller, but I didn't get as good a photo of it. This shows very clear separation between the very close together ink lines and overall good bleed too.


Here is "beige inside" at about 30x. The right hand side is out of focus, but you can see the bleed in the middle of the b, e, and g. This is very good.


Here is "esc" at 75x. The lower half of the "c" in one of the two escape keys is a bit lighter than the top. Overall this is hardly noticeable. Again, bleed is minimal.

Overall this was a fantastic GB and I'm very happy.
