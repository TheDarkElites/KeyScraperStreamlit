Oh yeah, I should post some pics. This is the Apple WOB kit.







I'd say the reverse dye-sub job is pretty great, the black holds up well even with a lamp shining directly on it and the legends seem really sharp. No obvious alignment issues to me, but that's also not something I'm very good at detecting. Good texture, but I think I prefer the texture of my EPBT Extended 2048 set, which feels silkier I guess. Overall, I find it superior to the EPBT set because it doesn't have any of the warping issues or stabilizer issues I had with that set.

My one complaint with the set is that the homing bars are not very pronounced at all. I believe that is something they are planning on adjusting, though, and they still work, so I can live with it. Overall, big thumbs up.
