GB Details
GB Open Date: June 15
GB Close Date: September 3rd - The long GB window is so people can check out all the different configurations, and it be live while content creators get boards. I want informed buyers as there are many options.
Estimated Fulfillment: MOQ is 50, but it is unlimited due to the high price already being a natural barrier. My current fulfillment estimate in Jan/Feb of 2024. I will give a more exact timeline once we know final quantities and I can get an estimate from Dadesin.
North America and South America: saevus
Rest of world: Prestige Keys
Other info: You must purchase case, PCB, and plate separately.
GB FAQ: READ HERE

Board Details
  • Mounting Styles: Tadpole (through PCB), Tadpole Plateless, PCB Top, Plate Top, O-ring *must be soft o-ring, 40a included in oring plate. 55a unsuccessful, 45a and 50a not tested. (made possible with 2 different plates)
  • Case Top Layout: WKL, WK
  • Typing Angle: 6.5 degrees
  • Front Height: 17.5mm
  • PCB: Custom PCB by Mechlovin. FR4 (1.6mm) and CEM3 (1.2mm)
  • Cable: JST. We tried the Pico EZmate and it was not very dependable unfortunately.
  • Indicators: Capslock and Scroll Lock
  • Support: Split Backspace, Split Right Shift, 7u and 10u spacebar. PCB supports ISO but the plate does not. This is because there were less than 20 ISO form fills out of almost 500.
  • Colorways: Bloodline, Astral, Pazuzu, Benevolence, Raven, Vision
  • Special Edition: SE but not limited! Will be all black Raven colorway. Black coated Brass weight instead of Copper or SS. You are able to choose your weight art between default and alternate. You cannot get the black weight outside of buying Raven.
  • Internal Accent: Reads "As above, So below, As within, So without, As the universe, So the soul"
  • Tops: You will be able to purchase extra TOPS
  • Badges: You will be able to purchase extra BADGES
  • Pricing: $600-$700. Yes this is an expensive board. Am I juicing the price? No. I have posted a manufacturing cost breakdown in my discord. Clip of Blacksimon doing a cost breakdown is also below.
  • Vendors: saevus (Americas) and Prestige Keys (rest of world)
  • Manufacturer: Dadesin
  • Buy Type: Unlimited
