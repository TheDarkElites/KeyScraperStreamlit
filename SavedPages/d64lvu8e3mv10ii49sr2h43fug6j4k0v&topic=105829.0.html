Hi all,

At this time, we are not satisfied with the number of Latin kits sold for GMK Frost Witch. At current projections, it would take far too long for the kit to reach MOQ, an effort that no one running the group buy wants to (or should) have to pay for, with either time or money. As indicated with the ratio of novelties to base, which is VERY significant, most of you are weebs in it for the Japanese sublegends either way.

As such, we have decided that we will be cutting the Latin kit from GMK Frost Witch.

All orders with GMK Frost Witch's Latin kit will be refunded. Please do not purchase a latin kit if you are buying through another regional vendor; your order will be cancelled. If you had a tokkipee artisan, and would still like the set either way, your artisans will be placed on hold. At any time, PM me on reddit, instagram, discord, or geekhack, and I will send you an invoice for the full amount of the kits you want, post-latin. The remaining unclaimed artisans will be sold after the group buy, with the extras. You have plenty of time. Yes, the latin B will also be cut from the spacebar kit.

Apologies for the inconvenience,

Bryan & Adam
