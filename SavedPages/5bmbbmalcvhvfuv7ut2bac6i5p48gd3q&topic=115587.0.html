MW Commute
Do you find yourself on the road as the sun rises, or as it sets? Inspired by the purple of the sky during the daily commute, MW Commute plays with the hues of both sides of the sun.

Designed by acitrin, Chris @ Mechs & Co, and stellaidoscope.

GB is live from from December 10 to January 14!

I apologize as I have neglected the GH thread. I'm sending out most of the updates in acitrin's Discord server (link in the main post). Please also stay tuned to the MilkyWay server as updates are also posted there.

Switches have been shipped out.
