"Hello, again" M0110 reboot
Inspired by the OG 0110, M0110 bring some modern touch to the classic design.


GB DATE: starts @ 10 PM GMT+10 on the 10 June and will run for 4 weeks (finish on 8th July @ 10 PM GMT+10 )
Softcap around 250 units
Purchase link will go live at the above time and date through website Thocc Supply
Estimated shipping date: begining of Q4 2021 (~3 month)




Board specs: Price $330 USD (Anodized & PC) or $370 USD (E-White & Beige)

Standard Kit includes:
  • 1 x Top & Bottom case (standard 60% or HHKB)
  • 1 x Polycarb 60% Universal plate [plate file will be released before shipping]
  • 1 x PVD brass weight with engraved "hello" design
  • 1 x 60% Universal Layout PCB - Solder version & 1 x Daughterboard*
  • 1 x set of Poron gasket conversion kit**
*No Hotswap PCB will be offered during this run due to large amount of layout that is available out there
*The daughterboad can be used with other 60% PCB with an JST connection point on the bottom
**Gasket conversion kit will be compatiable with previous run that was done by Drop. Shipping just kit itself will be slow or fast but expensive

Extras:
  • Extra Plate $35 USD each (Aluminium, Carbon Fibre, PC)
  • Gasket pad Kit $11 USD per set
  • PCB Kit $45 USD (main PCB solder only, USB-C daughterboard, JST cable

Soler PCB supported layout
Colour: 5 colour options

What's changed since IC begin?
1. Removed badge on the HHKB case due to popular demand
2. Substitute Black finish with Beige (all you beige gang is just too strong)
3. Altered Beige color after receiving prototype, the production unit will be a close match to PANTONE 11-0105 TCX rather than the yellow hue with a greenish tint (Comparison album link click here)
4. RETAINED all plate mounting points (Poron pads from the gasket conversion kit will need to stick onto the protruding plate points)
Without the spacebar and sides' mounting points, the plate's bottom and sides sections do not have enough support during typing which resulting in a very soft or sinking feeling near the spacebar, enter, backspace, L&R shift.
  • IF you want to build it with top mont and want a softer typing feel - skip screws near the space bar and left and right side when installing your plate
  • IF you want to use the gasket conversion kit, I strongly recommend using all 8 mounting points and install Poron pads on all of them.

In the prototype shown below, I didn't use any thin ones and can not hear any ping while typing
