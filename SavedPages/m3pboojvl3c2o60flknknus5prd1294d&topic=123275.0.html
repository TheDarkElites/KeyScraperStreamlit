Teton 78 GB
By Bennett Balogh - Mountain Mech Designs

Product Page | IC Page | Discord

GB Updates:

8/12/2024 - Group Buy met! Ordering items soon. 5 extras will be available currently on the website.
8/20/2024- Cases ordered, Carrying cases ordered, Feet in progress, working on logo for pcbs and carrying case





After a year of design and several prototypes, I am very excited to announce the group buy for the Teton 78! The goal for this board was a classic-style top mount with a unique layout. While it took a lot of trial and error, I have finally arrived at something I am comfortable sharing with you! Growing up around near Grand Teton National Park, I took inspiration from the Grand Teton mountain range - featured on the weight. Thank you all for the interest and help in the discord to bring this project to life, I am really excited to get this board into your hands. I am very happy with how it turned out, and I hope you will be too! For more info, visit: mountainmechdesigns.com

GB Details:

Sale Dates: July 7th - August 11th  July 28th - Extended for Alexotos' Stream on the 25th.
Estimated Shipping: Q4 2024
MOQ: 30 units Lowered to 15 units - MOQ Met
The sale will be sold and fulfilled myself https://www.mountainmechdesigns.com

Colors:

The final colors will be Light Blue and Brown for the top case both with a silver bottom case. Extra top cases will be available for +$175.
