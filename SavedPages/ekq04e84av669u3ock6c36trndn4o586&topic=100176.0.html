[GB] SA Arcane - Group buy CLOSED - Production finished - Shipping soon
« on: Tue, 09 April 2019, 04:26:49 »


Greetings traveler!

I'm pleased to announce that we are going to give the Arcane colorway another shot after it sadly fell through as a DSA set. This time I'll be focusing on one of the two colorways first, so SA Plague would follow after this is completed. The DSA buy was competing with itself so I want to avoid doing that this time.

Additionally I am skipping the IC phase for this one as the kits are pretty much final and vendors are set, and most of the required discussion was already had in the IC for the DSA version. Ergodox and such have been left out as they move very little units and can be quite expensive (40% and Ergodox kits can easily be 80$+ at low volumes for SA due to lots of mold changes required). So I am focusing on more standard layouts of this community. This doesn't mean you are forbidden from discussing the set until it launches of course, feel free to make conversation! I will remain making IC's for GMK sets and other bigger projects that require more input, don't worry.

Manufacturer: Signature Plastics
