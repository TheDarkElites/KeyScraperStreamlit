Artifact series – Cities of tomorrow artisan keycaps Groupbuy

Made from perfect details

Firstly, we carefully chose and put many different tiny mental details together to build the futuristic towers/ buildings up. It then took us a lot of time to keep them in the sky in order to create floating effect: we patiently waited for each resin layer to be dry enough to perfectly load the next layer.

As usual, we also use multiple wooden layers to produce the keycaps but always keep in mind that each layer’s surface has to be perfect. By perfect we mean clean and clear, accurate color displayed and controlled work of adhesion among layers.
