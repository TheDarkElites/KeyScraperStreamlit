[GB] 60% Z60-Evolve, Gasket, split-top enclosure with 60%, WKL or HHKB layouts
« on: Sat, 20 November 2021, 04:59:32 »
Hi, Geekhack! We are introducing the market's first split-top enclosure concept, this enabling detachable blockers design to be implemented, means the options of default 60%, WKL or HHKB layouts is now available all in one keyboard. This split-top mounting mechanism not only cuts down manufacturing costs making the price for Z60 stays in a budget build region, also enabling it to have a contrast of different material textures aesthetically.

Dimension:
  • Length: 298.7mm, width: 109mm, front-height: 17.02mm, rear-height: 29.02mm
  • Typing angle: 6.5 deg
  • Wall thickness: 6mm
  • Brass pressbar thickness: 6.5mm
  • Weight: 1kg (Case+PCB+FR4Plate+Press-bars)



Features:
  • Split top enclosure
    • Two brass press-bar is used instead of a entire top enclosure
      • This allows detachable blocker design become practical and simple
      • Minimize the waste of material, hence retain the cost in budget build range
      • Allows color and texture contrast of overall look (with Brass PressBars)
           
  • Normal, WKL and HHKB Press-Bars (7U bottom row)Updated 18 Nov
    • Normal Press Bar: will be clean of side-screw holes
    • WKL Press Bar: will have WKL blockers integrated into the bottom Press Bar
    • HHKB Press Bar: will have HHKB blockers integrated into the bottom Press Bar


Specs:
  • Case: Gasket mount
    • Centered USB-C socket
    • 17.02mm Front-height
    • Bottom: CNC machined out of whole piece of aluminum
    • Aluminum PressBars machined out of 6061, Same anodized color as case color
    • Brass PressBars(optional) CNC machined out of H62 Brass, PVD Titanium coating, Brushed finish, Nano coating(Oxidization prove)
    • Brass press-bars and brass blocker version press-bar works as weight

           
  • Plate:
    • FR4 and Carbon Fiber plates are half-plate transformable
    • Long-stripe leaves with flex cut
    • FR4 as default, Carbon Fiber and Aluminum optional

           
  • PCB:
    • Centered Daughter board with ESD protection
    • Solder version with all layouts compatible, including 'split-backspace', 'ISO enter', 'Stepped capslock', 'split-left shift' and 'split-right shift'
    • Hotswap(optional) version1 with ANSI 60% with 7U bottom row
    • Per-Key RGB light

           
  • Firmware:
    • QMK driven and VIA enabled
    • Right-hand 'shift', 'fn', 'win' and 'ctrl' keys are arrow keys when tapped
    • Layer lights placed in middle of each row, avoiding blocking, and indecating each layers

           
  • Color options:
    • E-White
    • Anodized Black
    • Anodized Ruby
    • Anodized Navy

Base Kit Include:
  • Bottom x1
  • Aluminum PressBars x2
  • FR4 Plate x1
  • Solder PCB x1
  • Accessories:
    • Case Foam x1
    • PCB Foam x1
    • Poron Gasket stipes(3.8x114mm) x12 4 extra
    • USB-C Cable x1
    • Cap/switch puller x1
    • Screws x1 set
    • Silicone foot pads x6 2 extra


Optional Parts:
  • Brass PressBars Normal layout
  • Brass PressBars WKL layout
  • Brass PressBars HHKB layout
  • Aluminum PressBars WKL layout
  • Aluminum PressBars HHKB layout
  • Carbon Fiber plate: 1.5mm thickness, matte surface
  • Aluminum plate: 1.5mm thickness, sand blasted, Nano coating


Pricing:

UPDATES - 16th Oct
Gaskets have their own walls, leaving minimum profile for eyes of the gasket chamber, keeps the integrity

Details of the joint of press-bar and case-wall, the Un-flushed joint design is to has a better transfer of different material textures
