Supported Layouts


With great pleasure, I am here to announce that the Group Buy for the _33 keyboard will be launching tomorrow at 11AM US Central Time!


BUY HERE https://www.p3dstore.com/shop/33-group-buy
(page will go live at 11AM US Central Time)

The price will be $50 for PCB/Plate combination with amazingly beautiful 3D printed cases available for an additional cost (See options below). The PCB features white soldermask with gold pads for maximum bling! The board features an ATMEGA32U2 microcontroller, USB-C, RGB underglow (via header) and VIA support (in process). The boards come fully assembled, you just bring switches and stabs (33 switches and 1 6.25u stab for the big spacebar or 36 switches and 2 2u stabs for the split spacebar layout).

The group buy will run for 1 month (September 17th) and fulfillment is expected in 6-8 weeks after that, depending on PCB manufacturing lead times. The buy is currently capped at 100 units.

If you have any questions please do not hesitate to ask!
