a few things that have gone a bit wrong and wanna make sure is communicated:

- the in stock kitting with the fix kit is as follows below:


- the 8 key in the fix kit is missing a braille empty circle in the top right. This isn't something that we can't fix sadly as there isn't a plan to do a 2nd fix kit. Hopefully this isn't a big deal as you have two 8 keys to use on your numpad, either one that has similar braille to the 6 key or one without the circle. your choice!

- there was a miscommunication about the amount of 1.75u and 2u bars that were included. if you were a participant of the GB and would like to receive another 1.75u and 2u bar, please send in a ticket to paramountkeeb and they'll get those shipped over to you. I feel it wasn't great to advertise those bars to GB participants only to not get them the keys, so we are opening sets for you to ensure you get your keys. otherwise the in stock kit is sold as is!

a few things that have gone right that i want to communicate:

this set got MADE and i am so grateful for all the help along the way. a project like this, using a new manufacturer is HARD WORK. having to create the svgs, communicate, plan, advertise, all of it is such a crazy ordeal that i applaud anyone who goes through it and see it to the end. there's lots of projects that just get left in the dust, scamming people, that i hope a few mistakes in this one is permitted and keeps you all interested in our future projects. we wanna make sure to go at a pace that is comfortable for us to ensure things get done RIGHT.

RGBEnglish started out as a XMI one off for a few buddies in a public discord, then it almost ran with aeternus (you know... the scammers!) thankfully, the aeternus staff at the time didn't want to include my name on the project even through all the hard work i already put in, and i found that UNACCEPTABLE and ended up not wanting to allow them to use this projects. believe in yourself, stick to your guns, and surround yourself with genuine people and you can achieve anything. Thank you all for the support on seeing the project through and i'll see you all again soon!
