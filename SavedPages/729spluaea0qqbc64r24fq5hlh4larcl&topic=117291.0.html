Inspiration
Inspired by the Monoliths and large stone formations of Prehistoric Europe, Menhir is designed to pay homage to their unwavering persistence. Menhir is a Premium 40% Keyboard sporting a modified HHKB layout and a flexible, leafspring top-mounted plate.

The angled exterior provides seamless integration with your desk while the curved bezels are pleasing to see and feel. The exterior design and weight are intentional in an effort to make Menhir a pain to move and pick up. Inspired by the aesthetics of the KBD661 and Titan65, and by the unexpected heft of the Neuron, Menhir weighs in at just over 2.1kg (4.6 lbs)

Quality Expectations and Closing Notes
As much as I would love for every board to be perfect, no kit can be 100% flawless. There can be small grains under the surface of the cerakote and the internal surfaces may be more coarse than external surfaces.

Premium color limited quantity and price difference is due to the high B-Stock rates and increased cerakote costs.
