FAQ
Will be expanded if necessary

What does WS stands for?

It stands for Wuque Studios. It will act as a manufacturer for this set.

No international/ortho kits?

Since this will be a doubleshot keyset, new molds get quite expensive. As of now, Wuque will not be able to cover all of the legends for NorDeUK kits for the first few sets. However, I am quite positive it will be possible in the future.

What's with the numpad kit's colors?

Design choice and simplification for MOQ.
