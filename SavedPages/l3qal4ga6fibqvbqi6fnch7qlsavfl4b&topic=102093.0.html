The Perry60v2

Isolation Mount, Acrylic, 100$.


This is the production run of the Perry60v2. After receiving lots of feedback from both the people who joined the prototype GB and the form, I have revised the design. There will also be a PCB offered with this GB. Upas from Cannon Keys generously offered to allow me to supply AN-C60 PCB as an optional add-on to the kit.



GB Form: https://forms.gle/CMrLL4Zj7HUwxejq5



Changes:

- Thinner bezels

- Uses standoffs so no protruding screws

- Stepped design

- Support for m3 feet

- HHKB & WKL versions



GB Details

- GB is limited to North America

- 100$ + 15$ shipping

- 50 units available

- Group Buy will remain open for 1 week, or until the 50-unit cap is hit.

- Invoices will be sent upon the close of GB and 48 hours will be given to pay the invoice.

- 6-8 weeks after close of GB to ship date

- 45$ optional AN-C60 PCB from Cannon Keys



Board Details

- Image Gallery: https://imgur.com/a/16fnui4

- Layout Support: https://imgur.com/a/ZO4lJKK

- Either HHKB or WKL

- Either frosted clear acrylic or glossy black: https://imgur.com/a/8PaW83Q

- Supports standard 60% pcbs

- 21.5mm front height

- Rubber strips isolating the acrylic plate from the rest of the case

- Sound test with Ergo Clears: https://soundcloud.com/slipperypeteed/perry60v2-soundtest
