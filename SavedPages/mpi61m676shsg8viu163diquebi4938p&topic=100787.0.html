Hello Everyone,
We are proud to announce that Nutcracker V2 here, A elegant Switch Opener made by TianYue Lab.
V2 has been designed after getting user feedbacks and has been given careful consideration to it.
Every aspect including ergonomics of the tool, not just the cosmetic or looks since it is a tool that has to be held in the user's hand for long periods of time during the usage.

Specs:
Cylinder shape designed.
Size in 32 x32 x 50 mm.
weight 80 grams.



Improvement works based on V1:
1)Switch bottom housing will be stuck on the nutcracker v1 cherry version.
V2: We removed the notch.
2)Gsus, HP, Yok, Sky Outemu switches bottom housing a bit bigger than Gateron/Cherry style switch.
V2: We did small tunings to make it compatible with majority popular switches.
3) V1 has Single header designed.
V2: 2 in 1 for V2.

Compatible Switches:
Cherry
Gateron
Zeal
Gsus
Holy Panda
Sky Outemu
Kalih Box
Hako Switch
Yok Switch
etc
