Synth Labs 060

Open February 24th - March 24th
at keebwerk.com


The Synth Labs 060 is a seamless gasket-mounted 60% keyboard, inspired by rack-mount electronics and sports cars.

Available in Standard 60%, HHKB, or WKL configurations.

GB Details

€429 incl. VAT
€399 outside of EU

Vended and fulfilled by keebwerk.
Estimated Fulfillment: Q1 2024



Specifications
Typing Angle: 7°
Mounting Style: Full Isolation Gasket Mount
Front Height: 19mm incl. feet
Firmware: QMK with VIA support
Compatible Switches: MX-style switches
Materials: 6063 Aluminum top and bottom chassis. PVD brass rail, and weight.
Weight: ~1.9kg unbuilt, 2.4kg fully-built
Connectivity: USB-C with custom daughterboard

Supported Layouts



Fixed-Layout Hotswap PCB w/ per-key RGB





Universal-Layout Soldered PCB


Options
Fully customizable per-part using the online configurator

3 Layout Options:
    • Standard 60%
    • HHKB
    • WKL

3 Case Colors:
    • E-White
    • Anodized Silver
    • Anodized Black

4 Accent Colors:
    • PVD Gold
    • PVD Silver
    • PVD Rose
    • PVD Black

5 Plate Materials:
    • Aluminum
    • Brass (PVD Gold)
    • FR4 with Flex Cuts
    • Polycarbonate
    • POM

2 Gasket Densities
    • Soft PORON
    • Hard PORON

2 PCB Options:
    • Fixed-Layout hotswap with per-key RGB
    • Universal-Layout soldered

Hey! Sorry for the delayed response, I saw this post but forgot to reply earlier.

CNC production is still in-progress - not much to say here, but I have some cool videos in this gallery.

We also decided for the packaging to switch to zipper carry cases! The unique dimensions/shape of this keyboard requires fairly custom packaging which are tricky to get at a low MOQ, so we figured we might as well upgrade to something nice and reusable while we're at it!

Keebwerk has recently gotten some samples of these, check these out:


I believe we will also be starting the PCB production soon - I've just sent final production files to Keebwerk for the PCBs, which are mostly the same as what we used on the prototype PCBs with some minor tweaks. Keebwerk is currently reviewing them and should be submitting these for manufacturing shortly if they're all good.
