SINGA X TGR

Quote from: HappyCatFish on Thu, 13 December 2018, 15:02:26
Hm, this is a new gasket system which honestly has me a bit skeptical. I'm curious to know what the purpose of it is in between the plate and pcb if the case is trey mount and whether or not it makes a real difference. Between the three or four notable gasket mount boards that have been produced each has used a different design, so I am excited to see more people trying their hand at it.

The screws (the tray bit) holds the PCB down, compressing the o-ring. So when you type, the downward force goes through the o-ring. When typing stops, the compressed o-ring pushes things back up, and the screws stop it from popping up. It's more gasket than tray.
