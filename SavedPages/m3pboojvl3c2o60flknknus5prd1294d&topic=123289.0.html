Cycle8
by TKD
Post edited by Click Clack

Cycle8 is the second kit following Cycle7, featuring an 80% keyboard layout that continues Cycle's design language and philosophy. It remains a high quality product aimed at budget-level pricing, with more sophisticated design, higher standard and improved craftsmanship.



As a break through introduced in Cycle7, EZ-assemble System, a ball-catch quick-assemble structure, will continue to be an integrated part of Cycle8 but with improved custom CNC components better suited for TKL frames.



This release enriches design details compared to its predecessor. As a TKL-sized keyboard, Cycle8 introduces more features:


Backlit Strip


Splitted Bottom Weight


A new PC plate material


And, most importantly, a new HE version PCB, powered by Rakka

Lastly, Cycle8 marks the conclusion of TKD's Cycle series for TKL keyboards, paving the way for future projects under TKD with entirely new design concepts.

Colorways

Totaling 14 colorways, comprising 7 anodized finishes and 7 TKDcoating coatings.

Plate

•   5052 Aluminum plate


•   PC plate (Unlike previous uses of PC material, this release features a completely new material and process for the PC plate.)


•   FR4 plate


PCB

•   Wired

Wired PCB offers the option of Soldered PCB and Hotswap PCB. The wired PCB is developed using the official QMK library, supporting VIA/VIAL.



•   Tri-mode Wireless

Cycle8's tri-mode will support open source VIA/VIAL, no longer require special software. PCB firmware is a more stable. It is equipped with two 2200mAh batteries and has implemented the functions of switching modes with hotkey. Enhanced device compatibility in Bluetooth mode.



•   Rakka HE Switch Internal

Cycle8's HE solution is provided by Rakka, featuring hardware and software developed in-house by Rakka, specifically tailored for the Cycle8 kit. It boasts strong performance, ensuring low latency while maintaining consistency between theoretical and physical performance metrics.



Key features include:
•   8000Hz polling rate
•   Adjustable actuation distance from 0.1 to 4.00 (true physical travel with no dead zones)
•   High resolution up to 0.05mm (adjustable in 0.05mm increments)

To ensure stability of the HE solution, it includes:
•   FR4 plate
•   HE PCB
•   Plate-mounted stabilizers
•   Gateron Jade HE switch



To better ensure quality of Rakka HE internal, entire internal will be fully-assembled before shipping.

Structural Options

Dual Mounting Modes

The Cycle8 features two modes of Gasket mount, allowing for structural changes to be swiftly accomplished with the assistance of the ball-catch EZ-assemble mechanism.
