GMK ELECTRIC

VISIT THE PRODUCT PAGE HERE →

GROUP BUY DATE : SEPTEMBER 6TH TO OCTOBER 6TH, 2021

ESTIMATED DELIVERY : Early Q4 2022


I'm extremely happy to say that GMK Electric is finally in group buy! The IC for this set was posted in July of 2017, meaning there were years where I thought this set would never see the light of day. Thank you to everybody in the community who encouraged me to finish what I started. Since I founded my own vendor, Vala Supply, I did more than I ever thought I could for Electric! A direct fulfillled resin artisan, matching coiled cable, and the first ever custom Vala switch. Can't wait to see Electric on everybody's boards

    A whole bunch of comprehensive updates were just pushed to our updates page (https://vala.supply/pages/updates) and to many of the project Discord channels.

    We greatly apologize for the lack of recent updates and the general obscurity of operations here at Vala Supply. We are deep into the fulfillment for ePBT Dreamscape currently and are trying to stay on top of that plus all other daily operations such as in stock fulfillment, customer service, project management, and marketing.

    Please understand that through coincidence, many sets that were ran as group buys at different times lined up and shipped to us at the exact same time, causing intense fulfillment strain. For example, GMK sped up production so fast this year that we received a shipment of 4 GMK sets at once on one occasion, and 3 sets at once on another, regardless of the fact that their group buys ran months apart.

    As much as I would love to hire 10 people to bang out everything within a few weeks, that is simply untenable for us at the moment. We are bringing in as much part-time help as we can already to help us get through this period as quickly as possible, but please keep in mind we only have 2 full time fulfillment staff who are also responsible for other logistical duties such as receiving, setting up, and moving shipments, etc.

    Altogether, we have received and will receive around 9,000 orders worth of goods within only the last few months of this year, across many different projects. Because of this we needed to stagger the planned fulfillment schedule quite considerably, and as such have products that will take a good amount of time to start fulfillment even after we receive them. We tried to balance the original GB running dates against the number of orders in a favorable way to come up with a rough schedule for all of the sets we have both on hand and in transit. This schedule is reflected in the updates for each project and once we approach those dates, fulfillment emails will be sent as usual with more concrete windows to expect your order.

    Please know that you will receive your order as soon as we can get it to you, and no orders will be left unfulfilled.
    There are no cataclysmic issues at Vala Supply, we just can't ship out this much stuff as fast as we wish we could. We acknowledge that we haven't been nearly as transparent about that as we should have been.
    The culmination of COVID delays has led to this regrettable situation where many projects ended up finishing around the same time.

    Everybody at Vala Supply greatly appreciates your patience and understanding here. We are just as excited as everyone else to move past the days of too many group buys, and hate having to push things even further back due to do these unforeseen circumstances. Finally, I apologize for not keeping everyone up to speed with all of this until now; I know radio silence can be scary, especially with what the hobby has been facing recently. I hope that these updates can help alleviate that stress and give a more clear path forward.

    Thank you,
    Vala
