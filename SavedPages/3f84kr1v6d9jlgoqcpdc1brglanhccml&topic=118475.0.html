Series 6.xx is here!


GB Confirmed to start on October 2nd, available at SandKeys.ME

This is something I am very proud of. I hope you like it as much as my cat wants to steal it!



6xx GB will go live on Sunday October 2nd, 6pm UAE Time for 1 week (last day October 9th)
FCFS. MOQ & capped @ 150 units. ETA Q1-2 2023

Price: $420
Shipping: DHL Express with a fixed cost of $35 [read after this] for international sales. Fuel surcharges are currently higher and DHL have told us next month will be closer to 40. We will fix the price based on Septembers fuel surcharges..
Vendors: Sandkeys only. So the process can be sped up 🙂
Timeline: 4 months production timeline, lets say 6ish months including QC

----Base Kit includes----
Top WK, WKL - A6063
Bottom  - A6063
Internal Brass Sheet
External brass weight
Alu plate - A5052. Colormatch with case unless stated otherwise
1.2mm PCB  + JST + AI03 C3 DB
Orings & mounting hardware

Each unit ships with hard cases, and a plain cardboard box around it. Recycle that box, I'm not wasting your GB money on fancy packaging.

----Optional Extras----
1.6mm pcbs, 1.2mm PCB
Plates - A5052, black ABS MX, Alps Alu plates
Internal sheet addons - i will give you access to the file instead to cut / 3d print your own.

Specs
  • 6 degree, 60% with xt column
  • Adjusted front height of 19.1mm (see below)
  • 7.8mm plate to top case clearance
  • WK and WKL options available
  • MX + Alps compat (with mx stabs)
  • Stock Alu 5052 plate with 1.2mm pcbs
  • 1.6mm pcbs with ABS plates available, as well as addon ALPS Alu plates
  • Internal brass weight/sheet. This is interchangeable with PC bottoms - reasoning below
  • GB edition will have clear coated brass external weight
  • Top mount standard, with optional gummy mount included. Uses 190x4mm orings
  • 1433g with current build (photo below)

No foam. With this much surface area covered in brass, its not needed.

Anodization options
  • Red (current proto)
  • Navy
  • Green
  • Silver
  • Purple
  • Pink

Adjusted front height
Measured without feet as I am changing the feet being used. Measured from the inside edge of top case to external case floor.



PCB Support
Each unit comes with the necessary C3 Unified daughterboard + 150mm medusa cable.
There are SMD LEDs on caps lock, and the 2 top left keys (acting as layer indicators)



Prototype images
This unit came with some particularly poor anodization - something I am working on to improve. Note - I take horrible photos 

GMK Red Devils


Sides - i love corsa sides, but wanted something with a much "softer" aesthetic.


Back (yikes it was a sunny day)


Internal brass weight - can't take a photo right now, heres a screenshot from a previous build stream. Yeah, that's covering a large surface area.
The gap is present on the divider between the XT columns and main alpha/mod cluster, to affect sound as little as possible.
Note the USB port is also aligned along this divider.
