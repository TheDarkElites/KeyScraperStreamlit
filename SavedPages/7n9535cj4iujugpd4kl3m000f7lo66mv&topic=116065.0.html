GB Key Information
Vendor: https://lanewareperipherals.com/
Quantity: 500 Orders
Timing: 26th February 2022 at 20:00 PST to 31st March 2022 or Until Sold Out
Projected Shipping Date: Early Q4 2022

Details
Form Factor: 75%
Mounting Method: Gasket
Layouts: Per Below
Angle: 7 Degrees
PCB: USB Type C Daughter Board, QMK/VIA, Rotary Encoder, Hotswap Option
Encoder: With Press Function
Plate: Aluminium 1.5mm with Brass Upgrade Option and FR4 Option
Built Weight: ~2.2kg / 4.85lb

LW-75 Base Kit Includes
Case: Annodized/Powder Coated Aluminium Top and Bottom
Switch Plate: Annodizes Aluminium, 1.5mm
PCB: Supports Layouts Shown Below
Knobs: 2 x Standard Knobs (Silver, one with line marking, one without)
Hardware: Screws, Bumpons, Gaskets, Practice PCB, Laneware Sticker
Coaster: Day 1 Orders Include Coaster Gift Color Matched to the Base Kit

Color Options

Annodized Green
Annodized Pink
Powder Coated White
Plate:
Matches Base Kit

Update Fequency, Terms & Conditions
We will update you on GB progress, manufacturing and shipping details at minium once every 2 weeks, or when important information becomes available.
You can expect to recieve your items before the advertised time, however uncontrollable circumstances may cause this time frame to be extended.
We aim to provide the highest quality Keyboards to our users. Our QA process should pick up any imperfections, however if a sub-par keyboard does manage to reach you, imperfections will be evaluated based on their location and size.
The outcome of this evaluation may result in a full/partial replacement, full/partial refund or return depending on the severity of the imperfection.
Specific Pantone Colors and Switch Plate .stp files will be released after the GB.
