Welcome to the GB page for GMK Tenshi.

GB is set for 10th Oct 2022
Runs till 9th Nov 2022
Thanks for your interest
Consolidating numbers now
Base and Novelty kits will be made!
As for spacebars, latin only and the metal artisan, they will be cancelled
Vendors have already been notified

Estimated delivery Q4 2023 - Q1 2024


Vendors

EU: mykeyboard.eu
SEA : HexKeyboards
AU: DailyClack Kits, Deskmats
US: Kono
CA: DeskHero
UK: proto[Typist]
CN and Worldwide: zfrontier Worldwide, Cn


Pricing

Base : $124.99
Latin Only : $59.99
Novelties : $49.99
Spacebars : $24.99

Note : these prices are from the US vendor. Please check with your local vendor for their prices.
