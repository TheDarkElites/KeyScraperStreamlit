Replacement translucent cases for those who ordered glow in the dark finish printing today. Plates to arrive at my place on Nov. 5, so I plan to have everything ready to box up and ship to Nachie for this week.

Just a little housekeeping, the tabs on the bottom housing (see attached image) are a little fragile, and are possible to snap off. If this happens, do not fret, as they have no structural significance to the assembled case, they just help with alignment. I snapped all 8 of them off on a test case for sheets and googles and it assembles and aligns perfectly, as the actual positioning is done by the grooves in the top case.

Tl;dr, don't worry if those little cylinders come off.
