[GB] GMK Nuclear Data
« on: Thu, 02 July 2020, 11:42:55 »
It's about time.

This keyset is based on the original SA Nuclear Data group buy, ran in R3 SA way back in the day. I have attained permission from the original SA GB organizer, samwisekoi to organize this. The original inspiration of the SA set comes from the ND6600 computer.

The green and orange accents are matched to the SP SA colors via RAL, the legends are GMK CP. We brought some of the original ND6600 legend symbols to GMK style, as well as some new original ideas. Biip did a killer job with these.

Interest check link: https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=106801









TYVM to Oblotzky for renders, and Biip again for the wonderful novelties and icons.

Group Buy Dates: July 4, 2020 - August 1, 2020

Vendors

US: Dixie Mech - gmknukedata.com
EU: Oblotzky - oblotzky.industries
Aus: Daily clack - dailyclack.com
SEA: ILumKB - ilumkb.com
China/international: Zfrontier - zfrontier.com (CN) | en.zfrontier.com (EN)

links will be added when they are available to me

Pricing

Base Kit: $124.99/€125.00 @ 500MOQ

Novelties: $44.99/€45.00 @ 150MOQ

Spacebar kit: $26.99/€25.00 @150MOQ

Cadet Alphas: $34.99/€35.00 @ 150MOQ

Rama Cap: $38

Deskmat: $17

More vendor pricing will be available as it is made known to me.

Expected delivery is Q2 2021 - roughly 32 weeks from order placement to shipping.

Renders on boards

tkl one by me


hbcp by me


Bauer by Dixie Mech


Alice by TGR


Kei by Beaming + Biso


Collabs!

Deskmat design, by Biip


Rama Cap - CNC Machined, Anodized and Enamel Infilled

We will do our due diligence in ensuring that it will be as good as it can be.
