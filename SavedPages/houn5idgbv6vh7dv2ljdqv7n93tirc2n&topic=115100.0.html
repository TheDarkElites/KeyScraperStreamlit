Received my board a few days ago, it is in pristine condition. Not sure if it was mentioned here but there were a few outstanding things:

1. The JST connector on the PCB is very fragile, folks have had issues with it snapping off when trying to connect the cable. Superglue and a re-solder seems to help.
2. The order form for the board mentioned that an extra daughterboard was included with the additional PCB if you ordered one. This was apparently not true and was a typo according to LZ, no extra DB included.
3. The "navy" color does not appear to be navy or even blue for that matter. The sample from the first post is distinctly blue however the color shipped is a greyish purple. Myself and several others have mentioned this but have not heard any sort of acknowledgement from LZ on this. While I get the color will vary slightly and that the nature of GBs means that things can change pretty quick, I think it is important that this is at least addressed. The REs that was just in GB for close to $1k also has this same color, at the very least the color name should change to reflect the actual color.
