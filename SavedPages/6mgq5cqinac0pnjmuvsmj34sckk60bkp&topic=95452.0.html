ALF X3 Renova

GB starts begins on May 4th 9 pm PDT

https://en.zfrontier.com/products/alf-x3-renova

40 units ONLY, FCFS

Pricing:
478USD shipping included, DHL
+48USD if you want to upgrade your bottom alu insert to brass.

Timeframe:
Ends when all units were sold or on May 14
Estimated shipping: Sep or Oct 2018

Retro + Innovation

Features:
Classic hefty design
Mid-piece design
Side accent brass pieces
Front RGB strip
Bluetooth powered by MDBT40 (*lithium battery not included due to shipping reasons)
Mini USB
PCB powered by ydkb.io
Kmini layout

Top Housing:
3 RGB Lamps or Renova Logo engrave


Colors:
Housing and plate will have the same color: White, Silver, Gray, or Black
Aluminum Insert and mid-piece will have the same color: Purple, Green, Red, or Orange
