HOLO Custom TKL Keyboard GB

Introducing HOLO- a new winkeyless TKL custom keyboard.
Designed at preserving a sleek and minimalist look, we did away with the bottom piece thus the name.
The whole keyboard will be milled from a single block of aluminium to avoid any gaps or color differences in anodizing.


Order form: https://goo.gl/forms/oeEhZwNit4ZaNJE72


Prototype (Dark Grey):














Prototype (Light Grey):














Custom Carrying Case:







Specifications:
  • Winkeyless 80% keyboard
  • 8-degree angle
  • Unibody & hollow case design
  • CNC milled brass plate (sandblasted and coated)
  • Black 80% PCB
  • Custom carrying case

Color Options:
  • Light grey
  • Dark grey
  • Blue grey

Price:
  • Keyboard, brass plate, PCB, case - $360
  • Extra PCB - $40
  • Extra brass plate - $55
  • Shipping & PayPal fees are additional
  • FCFS sale (66 units)

Shipping:
  • Shipping will be from Taiwan
  • US, Canada, Europe - $55
  • Asia - $40
  • China - SF Express

Deadline for order:
  • Nov 26. 2018

Lead time:
  • 5 - 7 months
