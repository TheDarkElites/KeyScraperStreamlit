CYBERBOARD R2/R3 Typing Experience Upgrade Kit


Making Stuff Together! | Discord


Group buy info

December 15, 2022 at 20:00 PST
Location and details: Angry Miao official site
End date: limited amount, until all units sold
Estimated fulfilment: before mid January
Pricing: 105 USD


Review



PCB

CYBERBOARD R2/R3 compatible 1.2mm multi-layer PCB with cutouts in the alpha area and RGB support. Matching transparent silicone pads are included to fit the original 1.6mm PCB stabilizers.


Plate

POM plate with individual cutouts. Silicone stands replace the copper ones between the plate and the PCB.


Foam

Supersoft slow rebound PORON gaskets, PORON foam and PORON switch pads.


Included in this flex kit

1. POM Plate - matte POM material
2. Gaskets - supersoft slow rebound material
3. All-New PCB - 1.2mm cutout PCB with lighting in semi-matte black
4. Silicone Stands - white silicone material
5. PORON Foam - supersoft slow rebound material
6. Switch Pads - 0.3mm PORON material
7. Transparent Stabilizer Pads - translucent silicone material with adhesive
8. Bottom Foam (Soft/Supersoft) - PORON slow rebound material
9. Case Foam - 0.5mm PORON material
