Group buy details:
  • GB window opens on July 17th 11 AM Pacific

Kitting Information:

Compatible Configurations:
More



*The Electrocapacitive PCB supports split backspace, split right shift, and stepped caps-lock. However, the plate offered in the group-buy only supports the depicted configuration.

  • Layout: F13 WKL TKL
  • Switch Spacing: 18.9 mm
  • MX Mounting: Top mount or gummy O-ring gasket mount
  • EC Mounting: Top mount only
  • Typing Angle: 5.5 degrees
  • Height: 21.9 mm effective keyboard height / 17.9 mm front face height (w/o bumpers)
  • Weight: ~4.0 lbs/1810 grams built
  • Top engraving: Calla lily

  • PCB Firmware: QMK w/ VIAL enabled
  • PCB Thickness: 1.6 mm
  • PCB Material: FR4
  • Connector: USB Type-C
  • Daughterboard: Universal Daughterboard

  • Case Material: 6063 Aluminum
  • Internal Weight Material: Brass
  • External Weight Material: Stainless Steel
  • MX Kit Plate Material: 5052 Aluminum or Polypropylene
  • EC Kit Plate Material: Stainless Steel

Pre-GB Interest Check Form:

Due to the length of time between original interest check and now, I wanted to collect some last minute feedback on the planned group-buy format and structure as well as guage the current interest in the project. Furthermore, with recent events in group-buy fulfillment, I do not want to start a group-buy without confidence that MOQ can be met without vendor help. The form also has the option to "reserve" a group-buy kit, which will send you an invoice that will be active through the entirety of the first day of the group-buy. The form will be open until July 14th, 2 PM Pacific.


Estimated Fulfillment Timeline:
  • Group buy (ongoing): here!
  • Processing orders (1 week):
  • Ordering from manufacturers (1 week):
  • Waiting for production (6 months):
  • Receiving inventory (1 month):
  • Packing and quality control (2 weeks):
  • Delivery (2 weeks):

All in all, we're aiming for a turnaround time of around 6-8 months (mid-late Q1 2023). Of course, delays are inevitable but I will do my best to maintain a clear line of communication throughout the fulfillment process.


Update Process:

After the group buy window ends, there will be bi-weekly updates on both discord and this Geekhack thread outlining the fulfillment progress. I plan on having separate timelines for each part, so that it will be easier to visualize the fulfillment process at each stage. Even if there was no change since the last update, I will still relay that information in a new update post.





Open Source Parts:

Everything except for the case files will be open-sourced following Group Buy completion. This includes plate files, firmware, PCB files, weight files, and anything else that could be helpful. If there are any modifications you would like made to an existing file, (such as making a split backspace and split right shift Topre plate) feel free to reach out to me and I would love to help.


Compiled Q&A Section:
More
Question: What stabilizers are compatible with the StS Topre plate?
Answer: Compatible with both Costar and Cherry plate mount. Still attempting to source compatible plate mount wires from deskeys but have not heard much from them.

Question: Has there been any interference issues with any keycaps/profiles?
Answer: So far, I have found no interferences with any keycaps I have tested. Some profiles like MT3 are harder to remove with a keycap puller but was still manageable by removing the assembly from the case before attempting to remove keycaps. I have a more extensive writeup on the second message of the IC thread.



Afterword:

Despite the challenges in finding a suitable manufacturer, I was able to use the additional time to further improve Calla. Most notably were the development of a new electrocapacitive PCB for Calla (made possible with the open-source projects of Cipulot and the sanity checking by Langer), a lower $450 base price, and collaborating with the exceptional crew at Divinikey, who will serve as the vendor for the group buy. It was an absolute journey to get here and I just want to say: thank you for your support, take care, and have a wonderful day.
