« Reply #39 on: Fri, 17 February 2023, 08:44:19 »
I'm happy to announce that we have approved the color samples from the following rounds:
  • R1 - Darker Pink for Latin Alphas in Base and Child Kit - 701C
  • R2 - Purple Accents - 7678C
  • R3 - Modifier and Sublegend Pink - 700C

Photos of each are in this thread. The purple accent turned out to be the most difficult color to match, and ePBT did not want to proceed with further rounds based on their colorimeter readings on R3 (their pics below).



That being said, R3 purple ended up being darker than R2 purple. I sent all samples to Mekibo for additional review. We agreed that R2 purple fits the renders best, and we did not want to risk a further increased timeline by asking to match to a new color code.

With that, ePBT Superstar officially enters the production queue! KBDfans was not able to provide me with an updated timeline when I asked - it won't be Q1 2023. There are about 10 sets "ahead" of us in ePBT's queue from KBDFans alone with 7 of them in a seemingly active manufacturing status.

Another full sample set will be produced before Superstar enters production, and I likely won't have any updates until then.
