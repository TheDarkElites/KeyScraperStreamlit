Novelties Explained

More
Periodic Symbol : This is the symbol that can be found on the periodic table that represents the element Mercury, its molar mass, and atomic mass.
Conical / Erlenmeyer  Flask : A common flask used in chemistry for experiments that can be used to heat or boil liquids, it is also used in titration.
Rhombohedron : This is the symbol used to represent the crystal structure of the element Mercury.
Altimeter : Although modern altimeters use what is known as an aneroid, Mercury was used in the basis and invention of barometers to sense pressure changes in the environment.
At higher altitude pressure decreases, and thus a change in the environment causes the aneroid to sense the altitude.
Alchemical Symbol : This was the symbol used to represent Hydrargyrum (Mercury) in alchemy. It is also the astrological symbol for the planet Mercury.
Thermometer Scale : Mercury is used in thermometers, however thermometers that contain a red liquid is alcohol to which red coloring has been added.

Hey everyone sorry for the super late and much needed update for GMK Mercury.

In the beginning it was a set I was and still am very passionate about. After getting the group buy date and waiting till then I wanted to have a lot of collaborators and people working on this. Many people did approach me and wanted to work with me and collaborate on this awesome set. Later during the year due to miscommunication and lack of updates I think due to the long wait time until the group buy I and other collaborators had issues and some forgot on both ends. Due to a lot of issues like this kind of fell on the back burner and I felt discouraged about the set. I am not trying to make an excuse for why I wasnt consistent or made updates, but after the year things changed and many things in real life changed living situation and all those kinds of things as well.

Currently the set is doing well and I can post a graph later today or tomorrow.

Also I will be trying to be giving more consistent updates and post on Instagram and reddit as well.

I totally understand those who currently bo longer want support this set due to communication issues and I apologize for not being more transparent and communicative about the project.

Again thank you to those who continue to support this set and I will try to be more active and transparent, as always thank you for your patience and support with this project.
I think that another reason why things ended up the way they are is because I felt a little let down by those I was working with. There were a lot of things I wanted to deliver to the community that I couldnt and therefore was more self critical on myself and ended up not really putting much effort into the project afterwards because I didnt think there was much I could provide.

For the remainder of the group buy and for future projects I will again try to be more consistent and communicative about the process the good the bad and the short comings that cant be helped or things that should be fixed or adjusted.
