SIX5 Designed by RiftKeys


Hello,

Firstly let me introduce myself, my name is Dworf and I am the owner of RiftKeys, an Australian based Keyboard Company.

We have designed our first of many keyboards by creating a unique mix of Acrylic and Aluminum.

So let me start off by introducing to you all the SIX5.


Sound Test




Details

- 65% Fixed Layout
- 5° Typing Angle
- Gasket Mount
- Stacked Acrylic Top
- Aluminum Bottom
- Plate - FR4 | Aluminum | Carbon
- Solder/ Kailh Hotswap PCB Options
- Detachable USB Type-C

PCB is compatible with QMK/VIA

Modular Acrylic - More than 25+ Color options will be available as Acrylic Kits in stock on our website in the future, to accommodate any keycap set or aesthetic you wish to achieve.



Price

The RRP of the SIX5 will be $250USD + Shipping

Estimated shipping for the GB will be June 2021

The Kit includes:

Keyboard
Keyboard Plate - FR4 | Aluminum | Carbon
Custom PCB - Solder or Kailh Hotswap
1.5mm Poron Gaskets
Rubber Strip Feet
Anodized Hardware
EVA Carry case
RiftKeys Microfibre cloth


Group Buy

There will be 2 Group Buy's

The first GB will be for the "Limited Edition" Gold Mirror + Black Aluminum Model of only 50pcs. SOLD OUT

Following this we will be the second GB of 500pcs Globally in March 2021.

This will have multiple Aluminum Base and Acrylic Top options including Various plate materials.
