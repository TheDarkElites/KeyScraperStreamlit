M A T E R I A L S  A N D  O P T I O N S
We made a GMK Aegyptus themed version of our Deluxe Keyboard Case made out of nappa pleather.
The case comes with a fully padded design. The outer square stitched pocket gives it a nice premium touch while providing space for cables, tools, etc.
Your keyboard is guarded from the YKK zipper by a safetly lip.

You have the option for a custom label (+$12) with up to 3 letters to make your bag more unique (e.g. GCW or G.C.W.).

We can make the bag as small as for a mouse as well as for a full-size keyboard, just tell us your keyboard model and dimensions in the order form.
