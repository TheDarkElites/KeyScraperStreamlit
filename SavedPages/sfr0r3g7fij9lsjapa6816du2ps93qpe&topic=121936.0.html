*keycap set rendered here is ePBT Dreamscape by tsoiab10

Product Page: Sneakbox.com
Group buy is live from 11/19 - 12/17 12/20

Updates on the project can also be found in these Discord channels:
Sneakbox
patty.cannot.design

Technical Details
Board front height: 18.5mm (measured at chin)
EKH: 22.64mm/23.64mm with feet (measured at right alt)
Typing angle: 4 degrees
Materials: aluminum 6063 (top, bottom), copper (inner weight, coated), polycarbonate (light diffuser, DB wire cover)
The external accent will be available in alu 6063, coated copper and raw SS304
Weight: ~3.1lbs built (1406.14g), 2.45lbs unbuilt (1111.301g) with the alu external accent
Mounting Style: O-Ring tab gasket mount, top mount
Standard Alice layout compatible with top-mounted USB-C PCBs, some USB-C daughterboard Alice PCBs
Manufacturer: DDS



Color Options, Plates and PCBs
Body: Black, Silver, Light Purple, Dark Red, Pink
External accent: Anodized Alu (matched to the body), Clear-Coated Copper, Raw SS304
Included plate options will be PC and Aluminum, Alu Half plates can be purchased as an add-on
You can choose between no PCB, the Sneakbox Alice Clone PCB, or the Sneakbox M5 PCBs (either hotswap or solder)





Group Buy Information
Units: 100
Pricing (@50 MOQ):
With PCB
Alyssia Standard: $430USD + shipping/tax
Alyssia Copper Upgrade: $480USD + shipping/tax
Alyssia Raw SS Upgrade: $520USD + shipping/tax

NO PCB
Alyssia Standard: $390USD + shipping/tax
Alyssia Copper Upgrade: $440USD + shipping/tax
Alyssia Raw SS Upgrade: $480USD + shipping/tax

Extras/Add-Ons:
External accents (anodized alu, copper and raw SS)
PCBs offered by Sneakbox
Alu plate, pc plate, alu half plate

Estimated fulfillment: Q2 2024

Included in the base kit
Alyssia Body, External Accent, DB Wire Cover, Light Blocker, Inner Weight
PC or Aluminum Plate
PCB or no PCB
Case screws, top mount screws, o-rings, feet
Carrying case, microfiber cloth
DB M3 standoffs (for the M5 PCBs)
Torx screwdrivers (T6, T10), Hex Nut Driver (to help screw/unscrew standoffs)

Worldwide: Sneakbox
