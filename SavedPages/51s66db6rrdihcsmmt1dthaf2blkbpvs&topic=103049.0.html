- PCB : QMK, USB Type-C (Thanks to Hineybush)
- TX Bag (TBD : Inner foam not confirmed)
- Switch plate mount : Rubber mount Technology.

[Color option]

- Switch plate (MX Aluminum) : Balck, Silver, Red and Gray
