GB Link

Typewriter 68 is a retro looking keyboard designed by scarecrow.

His account is too new to post, so I help him to post here 

You can see his IC post here: https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=98084.0

Typewriter 68 is a keyboard with:
solid and retro design
unique layout
typewriter looking case
super heavy weight
many color options including white by ALF and coffee
RGB light bar
custom engravinng!

design:


options:
mid-piece( with integral plate)
HHKB layout:


Full bottom row layout:


You can choose to have engraving, and customize!

colors:
Silver, Gray, Black, Coffee, Blue, Light Blue, Purple, Red, Orange, Pink, Gold, Green, White(upgrade)


prototype:




You can ask me questions and I will answer with my best         

Some FAQ:
Can I have no engrave sentence on the front?
YES

I dont see this in option, can I have this?
Tell me what you want and we will add!

How thick is the plate?
1.6mm. Some other keyboard design has 4mm+ plate but I think that is too many. Typing on 4mm+ plate will be too solid and a thinner plate will have more flex and let you feel the switch better! it will be made by high quality aluminum, I believe it will be as good as thicker plates!

Normal font for logo?
YES, will have picture of new design!
