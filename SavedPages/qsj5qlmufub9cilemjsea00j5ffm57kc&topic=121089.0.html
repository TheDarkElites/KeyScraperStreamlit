Unikeys Wisteria Light/Dark Keycaps



Product Link: https://unikeyboards.com/collections/wisteria-keycap-series

Our Discord: https://discord.gg/xprJb8Ta26

Hi everyone, this is Dashan from Unikeys. I am very glad to announce the unlimited GB of the Wisteria keycap series is about to start! We initially ran a limited-in-stock sale of the Wisteria keycap series thinking the quantity we had in inventory would be enough for everybody. However, we did not expect them to be such a great hit among enthusiasts, and that is why we decided to run an unlimited group buy for these keycaps. We have been working with the designer and the factory for the past two months to settle the details, and I am very happy to say that we are all completely ready now. One thing that I need to make clear before everything else is that Huo Yan Seng Mei is a factory with a lot of resources and production capacity. If the number of GB units is under 200 sets, the production should be finished in about half a month. If the GB units reach 400 sets, the production time will be about 1 month. Huo Yan Seng Mei is able to produce a high quantity of keycaps in a very short period of time, and that is one of the main reasons we decided to run the Wisteria keycap GB as the wait time won't be super crazy for our buyers. For those of you who have not heard of the Wisteria keycaps before, please feel free to look at the details and pictures posted below. Thank you!


Price

49USD for all-in-one kit

MOQ
Unlike how we claimed in the IC post, there will be no MOQ for this GB. Even if there is only one person who ordered, we will make sure to deliver it


Keycap Specs

Kitting: There is one Light all-in-one kit and one Dark all-in-one kit. They are separated kits instead of one
Material: PBT Dye-sub
Profile: Cherry Profile
Kitting: One kit including everything (Space bar kit, novelties, numb kit)
Designer: Cereal from Cat Keys
Manufacturer: Huo Yan Seng Mei


Estimated Time of Shipping
If the MOQ is under 200 sets, the production should be finished in about 2 weeks after the GB ends. If the MOQ reaches 400 sets, the production should be finished in about 4 weeks after the GB ends.
Once the production is over and the keycap sets arrive at our warehouse in Shanghai, we will start shipping as soon as possible.

Time of Sale
GB Start Date: August 18th at 5:00 PM ET
GB End Date: September 1st at 5:00 PM ET
