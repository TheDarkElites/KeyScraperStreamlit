PARTS OFFERED
Universal60 plates - DESIGNED BY JDCARPE - 25/30$ - original plates used in my two GBs before. Reliable and a favourite design by JDcarpe. Supports almost any layout imaginable. If you want winkeyless or HHKB layouts, without the winkeys i suggest going for the proprietary HHKB/winkeyless plates for better looks.



GON Nerd60 MEKANISK edition - 60$ - available in the exclusive colours mint and white. Read about the PCB here.



and will of course be available in mintblue colour!


Stabilisers - 8$ - available in both 7u and 6.25u from GON

HHKB and winkeyless plates -  UNIVERSAL  - DESIGNED BY JDCARPE - 25/30$ - these plates doesn NOT have a cutout where there are no keys used in the specific layout. This looks much better than looking through the plate and onto the PCB when there is no switch there.

Winkeyless(these are merged to a universal design in the final revision)


HHKB(these are merged to a universal design in the final revision)


These are MX compatible plates for custom 60% builds. They are not made for the original HHKB.

The winkeyless plates are 1.5u - covered 1u - 1.5u - 7u- 1.5u - covered 1u - 1.5u
1.75u + 1u or 2.75u shift on the right side.
UNIVERSAL (ISO/ANSI)
Stepped caps lock.
This is for your OG fans-
Covered means that you can't put a switch there.

The HHKB plates are : covered covered 1.5u- 1u - 1.5u - 7u - 1u - 1.5u - covered 1.5u
1.75u + 1u or 2.75u shift on the right side.
UNIVERSAL (ISO/ANSI
Covered means you can't put a switch there.
