The Peach











Introduction:
So for those of you who are not follow the IC thread, this's an aluminium housing for full size keyboard that take 10.5* slope in shapes.
Order options:
  • Standard kit: Keyboard housing and plate only.
  • Full kit: Keyboard housing + plate + parts
  • Fully Assembled: Keyboard housing + plate  + parts with free custom spings, LEDs and lube.
Color options:
There're 4 color options for this keyboard:
-Red
-Black
-Silver
-Golden
LED colors:
-White
-Blue
-Green
-Red
-Orange

PCB and parts options:
-PCB: In the last minute, we decided to go with winkeyless.kr B.87 PCB so it's the only option.
-Switches:
       + Cherry MX: Blue/Black/Brown/Red/Clear/Green
       + Gateron: Blue/Black/Brown/Red/Green/Yellow/Clear
-Custom Springs:
       + 55/59/62/65/69g
-Stabilizer;
       + 1 set of stab included 8 stabs for a full size keyboard.

Prices:
+Standard kit = 320$
+Full kit = 320$ + selected parts
+Fully assembled: 370$ + selected parts

Parts Price:
-PCB: 60$/pcs
-Switches:
      + Cherry MX: 66$/set (1 set = 110 switches)
      + Gateron: 33$/set (1set = 110 switches)
-Stabilizer: 12$/set
-Custom Springs: Free of charge
-LEDs: Free of charge
 
Group Buy Order Deadline is Saturday, 20/5/2017 and Deadline for payment will be Thursday, 25/5/2017

All keyboard will be shipped from Vietnam, ETA 3 days to USA
The ideal timeline for this GB is 1 month after all payment collected. We're trying to make it as fast as possible.
