[GB] Bio Mecha, an Evangelion inspired stone 60% keyboard case
« on: Fri, 24 March 2023, 05:27:14 »


Hey guys, Ian from Brazen Studio here.

Like many in the keyboard community we are huge fans of the iconic anime series Neon Genesis Evangelion.  Inspired by the titular giant bio-machines, we've designed special editions of our Mason60 engineered stone keyboard cases.  Available in three vibrant colorways, the keyboard cases are adorned with wild streaks evocative of the sentient savagery inside each mecha.

The cases are cast in a mold from a gypsum resin composite, then polished so that it feels smooth and heavy like marble.  This product is a case only, you will need to supply your own GH60 compatible 60% PCB, switches and keycaps!  Matching wrist rests are also available.


Discord, socials- https://linktr.ee/brazenstudio
 
Group Buy Page- https://www.brazenstudio.co/pages/biomechakeyboard

Note- These photos are of real prototypes and not renders.  The keycaps are clones (I know, I know...) of the purple and green that were recolored digitally to match the red and blue color ways.

FEATURES:
- Individually hand-crafted keyboard case
- 60% style case, should fit most GH60 style PCBs (e.g. DZ60)  (NOTE- Freebird60 PCB seems to cause issues)
- Tray mount with removable screw posts and stand offs (up to 6) and M2 screws
Silicone O-ring dampeners
- Cork liner on the bottom of the case for grip and additional dampening
- Optional EVA case foam and/or cork case dampener
- Optional matching wrist rest


Comes in 3 iconic colorways- 

Unit 01- Purple and Green
Unit 02- Red and Gold
Unit 00- Blue and White

Quality Guide

Each unit is hand made and the splatter patterns will have some degree of randomness to them.  The photos above provide some idea of how they will look but every piece is unique.  Due to our casting process, there will be some small air bubbles or blemishes, but they will not be very obvious and add to the stone texture and feel. 

The material is sealed with wax and is water resistant, but any liquids should be wiped off immediately to avoid staining.  Oils should be avoided as they will result in stains.
