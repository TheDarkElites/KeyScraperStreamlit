[GB] HolyOOPS Aluminum RGB Keyset (invoicing)
« on: Thu, 15 October 2015, 14:37:12 »
I got the idea of aluminum RGB keyset about 2 years ago but things didn't work out back then due to high costs on production and the lack of confidence on selling such high priced keycaps.

I've been working with HolyOOPS for about over a year now and the idea of making aluminum RGB keyset started coming back to me when I knew they can manage to maintain the costs on producing such keycaps at a relatively low level. Not to mention the reputation of high quality products they're known for. So I talked to them about three months ago and asked they to make some samples for me which turned out pretty satisfying. After several modifications on the original samples during the past months, now we're finally ready to push it to the market.

Specs:
Cherry profile
1.5mm thickness
Top printed and blank versions will be available.
1.25 set includes: 2x 1.25 Control, 2x 1.25 Alt, 1x 2.25 Shift, 1x 2.75 Shift, 2x 1.25 Windows, 1x 1.25 Menu.
1.5 set includes: 2x 1.5 Control, 2x 1.5 Alt, 1x 2.25 Shift, 1x 2.75 Shift.





Deadline: November 30th. I'll stop taking orders on November 30th and invoices will be sent out right after. The production lead time is about 20-30 days. Hopefully I can start shipping before Christmas.

Pricing:

GB Prices will be even lowered when we have certain amount of orders. e.g. 200 pcs.

1.25 keyset (9 keys included, retail price $89.50)
GB price $80
$75 @200pcs
$70 @300pcs.

1.5 keyset  (6 keys included, retail price $64.50)
GB price $55
$50 @200pcs


Shipping:

US – USPS First Class ($3)
US – USPS Priority ($6+)
International – USPS First Class ($12+) (shipping to Canada costs about $3 less)
International – USPS Priority ($35+)

There might be additional charges on shipping if your order contains more than 1 set.


How to order:

Paypal only. I'll be sending out invoices after the group buy ends. The invoices will include 4.4%+$0.30 Paypal fees.


ORDER HERE

1.5 Keyset - 21
