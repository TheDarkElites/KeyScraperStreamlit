Chrome & Black LEMO® Premium Handcrafted Cables

ABOUT UNICORN CABLES

Handcrafted premium artisan cables from Slovenia, Europe. We aim for perfection and excellence. All our cables are made by hand in Slovenia and not mass-produced in China. To maintain the premium look of the cables, we use the most high quality materials such as original LEMO® detachable connectors, Techflex FLEXO PET and much more. All cables are thoroughly tested and made to last the longevity of your setup.

For those of you who don't want your spouse/partner to know about the price of your hobbies, I added a feature to get a letter in your package, in the form of Congratulations on winning this item :)


Join in LEMO® GB


PHOTOS
















LEMO? WHAT'S THAT?

LEMO® is a premium quality Push-Pull detachable connector made in Switzerland.

You can choose either chrome or black LEMO® detachable connector.



COLOUR OPTIONS  - Colour Chart

We offer over 40+ Paracord colours and 10+ Techflex colours you can choose from. To view some of Paracord & Techflex combinations, visit Mockups.



USB CONNECTORS

  • USB Type-A (Nickel Plated and Gold Plated)
  • USB Type-C (Nickel Plated and Gold Plated)
  • USB Mini-B (Nickel Plated and Gold Plated)
  • USB Micro-B (Nickel Plated and Gold Plated)

We also offer USB Type-C to USB Type-C configuration.

The LEMO connectors I can see on your product page all have 4 pins, but every LEMO cable I've seen elsewhere has had 5. Can you clarify please that these are genuine?

So the LEMO® connectors we are using are Male - FGG.1B.304.CLAD62 and Female - PHG.1B.304.CLLD62. They have 4-pins because the 5th pin is unnecessary for keyboards, and we chose to go with 4-pin format. These connectors are genuine, directly from the manufacturer, and we can provide more information/evidence if you need.
