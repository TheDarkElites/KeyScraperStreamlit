ORCA by Linworks

Dolphin was a beloved keyboard known for its distinctive curved profile, first introduced in the early 2010s.
We cherished not only its appearance but also the exceptional typing experience offered by its sandwich mount.

Linworks embarked on a journey to create a unique and innovative successor to Dolphin, the Orca.
They explored various design approaches, aiming to maintain the essence of Dolphin while introducing something truly new.

After years of deliberation, we proudly present Orca, the latest addition to the Dolphin keyboard lineup.
Inspired by the legacy of Dolphin, Orca incorporates a bottom-mounting design.
This design choice delivers a lower-pitched sound with a more balanced resonance, offering a distinct typing experience.



Product Specifications:
Layout: 65%
Mounting: Bottom mount
PCB: FAve65H (hotswap, 1.6T, fixed 6.25u), FAve65S (solder, 1.6T, can use 7u)
Typing angle: 7 degrees
Base Kit: Aluminum top and bottom // PC or Aluminum plate // Brass weight (Black coating option available)
Price: USD 330 (Not including PCB)

Color Options:
Top and bottom color: Choose from silver, black, or gray (total of 9 combinations)
Plate: Aluminum (black anodizing)
Weight: Brass coating / black coating (+10 USD)
