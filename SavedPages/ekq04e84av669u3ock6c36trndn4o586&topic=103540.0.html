Based from GMK Red Samurai's Colorway
Disclaimer: This IC/GB is not affiliated to RedSuns, the designer of GMK Red Samurai. But I have his permission to use the colors for these custom switches

Join here -> GB Form: https://forms.gle/DtP4ic1yJBNaGeAVA




Red Stem, Yellow Housing
Tactile, 67g bottom out
Nylon PA Bottom, Polycarbonate Top, POM Stem
These are recolored T1s. Tactility is comparable with Holy Pandas
Force Curve and Technical Specs: https://i.imgur.com/NPVlrtk.jpg


Some Info:
  • Manufactured by Durock
  • Tactile and Linear will be available
  • Housing Material: Nylon PA Bottom, Polycarbonate Top, POM Stem (No factory lube)
  • Both are 67g bottom-out
  • MoQ: 10,000
  • Pricing: Tactile: $0.5/pc (includes paypal fees)
  • Shipping Cost: Please refer to the table below
  • MoQ per person: 10pcs (additional in multiples of 10)
  • GB ends December 23 31, 2019
  • Estimated fulfillment date: January February or March 2020
  • Shipping transit time: 12-17 days for most countries (I've only experience delays when shipping to Czech Republic)


# Join here -> GB Form (this is a new GB form that doesn't have the linear switch as an option. If you wish to join the GB even without the linear switch, please respond here): https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdhuMoYaBYg4iWU2RdAfU5jrvsAgj3qSKp234RaIO3QT7JrDg/viewform?usp=sf_link

# Invoices will be sent via paypal once there is enough responses on the GB form

# Discord Server: https://discord.gg/cgt2H6C

Thank you in advance for joining this GB! Let's make it happen
