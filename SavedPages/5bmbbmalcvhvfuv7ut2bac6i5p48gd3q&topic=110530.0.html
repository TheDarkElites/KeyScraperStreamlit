Hey Everyone long time no updates...



I dont really have a good enough excuse other than I forgot about this page... so lets just get to the news!

So firstly a lot of things have happened regarding the set


- Keyreative shifted from all reverse dyesub to injection base and dyesub/reverse dyesub

- due to ink/pigment mixing from the injection base, the mods and purple keys will be reverse dyesub on white keys

- Keyreative has improved their quality by quite a bit and you will be able to see that in the comparison of samples. They also improved their color intensity


Thats a bit of a summary of everything

Now for photos:

(Preface that images are much like renders they dont always capture everything correctly and are impacted by the screen you look at it on so take everything you see with a grain of salt. I am also not that great at photography, so I do apologize for that)


 
So at a first glance this may just seem like a bunch of keys and well its is but this also marks the development and changes of this set


- Red: These are the earliest samples of the set and these were made prior to Keyreative improvements

- Blue: These are the second round of samples I have ever received they are post the improvements from Keyreative and use Injection base colors although they improved the light pink and blue colors, they had a major issue with ink mixing with the injected pigments

- Green: these samples are the solution we came up with for the mixing of the inks by just using white base caps for all the mods

Now although the outcome for the green samples was really good I will admit the pink is a slightly darker, its not easily noticeable in most light or at a distance but the closer you put the caps together the more noticeable it is. I have asked them to try and get as close as possible to the previous pink. Also for some reason the purple came out very dark (see below), but Keyreative has already made another sample to match the purple of the Blue sample and should be shipped to me very soon.

For those who dont get why I rather use reverse dyesub instead of having the injected keys with a bit of mixing. The result were very inconsistent and because of that more likely chance of batch variance could occur. With the way people are looking at sets under a fine tooth comb its much better to do it this way. This is another example of how Keycap designing isnt just color picking either route I go isnt perfect but I have to chose the route I feel is best for you the customer.

As mentioned before photos can be very deceiving to better show the difference here are some close ups


As you can clearly see the colors are pretty similar but they are also different with the injected pink being slightly lighter


Hopefully this can show you just how bad photos can be clearly the first round light blue is quite different and very far off from the injected light blue and the Pantone card.
(Note: when I was initially designing this set because the injected base colors wasnt a thing, so colors were best chosen from the Pantone book rather than using a plastic chip)


Here is a close up lastest purple and the previous injected base purple

Now as I only recently got key for mods, so I dont have the best photos in the world also i didnt get enough keys to cover a board.



Saved the best for last the Novelty bar was done again although this time they didnt edit the file like they did before to thicken the lines
For the final version the line have been thickened from .3mm to .4mm which will make it look a like better and help counter some shrinkage.

OH nuts I almost forgot



The nutcracker have been finished for quite some time now I have been trying to get in touch with Louis to see when they will be shipped to vendors but they are done. For those wondering how come they have nutcrackers for other sets and mine was the first. Well first when Louis ran it he was using a different anodized and when he shift they took longer, and then they lost their on hand sample of my nutcracker which i had to send back in. The time really adds up, but I am really happy with how it turned out and compares to the render.



With that this is the end of my largest update, but if you would like much quicker updates I highly recommend you join my discord (Ik everyone has a discord these days too many infact) but it really is the best place for getting updates. Discord

Final notes
I would again like to apologize for my lack of updates on here I have actually been posting them on discord, but I did forget to post here. I dont want you to take my goofy writing as me not caring about being late I understand that you all paid into having my idea come to life and that I owe you constant updates. For the future I will be posting monthly/or whenever I get something which ever comes first. Also with this update I do have another set that is going into GB very soon and this time the manu has a proven consistent 3-4 month lead time (other than DMK ghost which is because the designer took a long time to approve the transparent material) DMK Minty Fresh GB starting August 15th.

Thank you very much for your time!!!
