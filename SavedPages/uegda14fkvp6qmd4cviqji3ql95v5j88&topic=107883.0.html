[GB] GMK OG Spacekeys R2
« on: Mon, 03 August 2020, 09:40:31 »
SUP YALL

OG Spacekeys R2 is here. More colors, all convex caps, and some fresh new 3U molds.

These will be manufactured by GMK.

There is a PLETHORA of keysets that these will match - it's honestly a super long list, but any/most OG set will benefit from these if you need 'em.

Currently running or soon-to-run sets that will benefit from these are GMK Dolch R5 and GMK βeta.

Kits

This time around we are doing three separate kits, each with a theme/color scheme in mind.

CC/N9

Designed to be used for any Dolch variant (which there are at least 10 around), these will bring a nice little OG kick to your Alice-layout-based board.



CP/CR

White and black. The classic colors used by a LOT of OG and re-run and new sets. The CR ones will go with literally any set with black mods/alphas.



CP/CR

L9 and U9 will complement any OG beige set, namely the 9009 runs, Classic Retro, even PBT based sets like OG dyesubs, CRP and the like.



Vendors and Pricing

hiney - $25/kit, $69 if you buy all three (nice)

Oblotzky Industries - €25/kit

Monokei - $35 SGD/kit

Daily Clack - $39 AUD/kit

Deskhero.ca - $35 CAD/kit, $97 CAD if you buy all three

zFrontier (CN) (EN) - ¥185 CNY, $27 USD/kit

This GB will run August 4-September 4, 2020.

Lead time is 20-28 weeks after order submission. I experienced a much faster-than-expected shipping with Spacekeys R1, since it was a small simple kit.

Thanks yall, will update when I can.
