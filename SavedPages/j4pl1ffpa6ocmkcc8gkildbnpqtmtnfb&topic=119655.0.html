Theres no denying that these kits are on the pricier side and very large but we worked as hard as possible so that you can stretch the most value out of each accent kit.
- Each accent kit has enough support to be thrown onto 2 builds such as a 6.25u bottom row keyboard with full right shift and a 7u keyboard with split right shift or vise versa.
- By adding an icon mod kit you can also kit out two complete boards of the same layout. (ie: R4 with icon mods and R5 with text + icon mods or vice versa).
As mentioned earlier, R5 was a non-negotiable mainstay in this set for us. We designed this set primarily to serve a gap in the existing mod kits as well as to meet our needs; not copy previous kits with extremely limited kitting.
