Quick clarification (because I see this all the time): Satan (maker of the Taobao Satan GH60) obtained permission to use the design files of the maker of the GH60, and improved upon it in multiple reiterations. It's actually better than the original, with the major kicker being not needing a breakout board for enabling in-switch LEDs.

I do like the lighting capabilities of the Satan (I have 3) but on one the USB connector came off and took the solder pads with it, another had some diodes come off with their pads. These weren't too hard to fix with some bodge wires and components. So I would say they have great features but the quality is lacking....my third one has no problems (yet). Also the GH60 files are open source so anyone can modify and have some printed/assembled if they want to.
