Order form: https://forms.gle/jQ8dLRHJbw1hioc98


There will be a raffle for 5 units of special Montage.
- Winners can replace the order with special montage for additional 500.
- 5 Special Montage for GH and numbered. (Total 15 units globally)
- Special gift from Lin will be included in the special Montage package.

More information about Montage
Montage IC https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=103013.0


Support Layouts
- WKL / WK / ISO / ANSI


Please check below detail information for your consideration.

1.     Name : Project Montage
2.     Angle :6.5 degree
3.     Material : Aluminum 6061 T6
4.     Weight : 2,650g (Not including pcb, switches and keycaps)
5.     The Basic package : Cases, pcb, 1.5t (1.5mm) alu plate, Brass Weight, Staneless Steel Block, bumpon and screws, Lin Box.
6.     Estimated Shipping : Mid of Feb 2020
7.     GB open date : 10.26 (KST)23:00 ~ 10.31 23:59
8.     QTY : 50 (5)
9.     Colors : Black Silver and Lin Choice
       - Lin Choice color is not fixed for now. I will test various colors and pick a best color. Lin Choice color will be the same for all version (KR, GH, CN)
10.   Mounting Type : Hybrid (Sandwich and bottom,top fix)
11.   Price : 350 + paypal fee + Shipping
(Estimated shipping to US will be 65, Asia 40 via Korean EMS)
* Special Montage (Full Brass) = +500 (Total 850)

12.   Options : PCB USD 40’, Brass Plate USD 50, 2T (2mm) Alu plate USD 60
13..   PCB Information
 Name : Fave87
 Material : FR-4
 Thickness : 1.6mm
 Surface Finish : ENIG- RoHS
 Copper Weight : 1oz
 * It will be SMT soldered except the RGB chips.
14.    Compatible PCB = b87 size pcb A87B is correct
