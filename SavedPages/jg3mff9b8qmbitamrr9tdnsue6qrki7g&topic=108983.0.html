tl;dr - $15 plus shipping for a deskpad; $3 each plus $4 flat USA shipping for wire keys (or free shipping with a deskpad)

Form link - this is also at the bottom

&nbsp;

**Deskpad**

These will be ready to ship in 2 months or so, depending on shipping time from Asia.  I have received a sample piece already to review, and I’m quite happy with the result.  Below are some pictures of that one.  The beige and yellow look excellent.  The gray isn’t quite as close as those, but dark colors are tougher to photograph in strong light due to the underlying material showing through more.  Graphic design by Nephlock, and he also took the studio shots.

Studio shot, full
 

Studio shot, yellow and beige


Studio shot, gray


Sun shot, full


Sun shot, full with keys


Indoor shot, full


These are very similar to the ones sold by NovelKeys.  They’re 900x400mm, 4mm thick, with a smooth texture on top.  They come with an unbranded white drawstring bag for storage.  I’m pricing these lower than most other deskpads, since I mostly made them because I wanted it for myself so I’m not trying to maximize profit.  The price is $15 per deskpad now ($18 once they’re in hand), plus shipping from 90028.  Sales tax will be collected for shipments to California addresses (basically $1 extra).  A few examples of shipping costs for a single deskpad: 

California - $9 
Denver/Seattle - $10 
Chicago/Houston - $12 
Miami/New York - $13 

International shipping would be too expensive for these, but I can check on much it would cost to send some to the GMK Mudbeam vendors (Desk Hero, mykeyboard.eu, Daily Clack, zFrontier) for cheaper distribution so please fill out the form if you’d be interested.

&nbsp;

**Wire Novelty Keys**

These are the same keys from the Novelty kit that was sold during the Mudbeam keyset GB.  The novelty kit came with 2 wire keys of each color, and I had extras made so that you can do something like this if you want:

Lots of wire!


I have both the yellow with gray wire and gray with yellow wire available for $3 each.  Shipping is a flat $4 in the USA for any number of them.  International shipping is available, and the cost will depend on your country.  If you’re also getting a deskpad then there’s no additional shipping for these, but they won’t ship until the deskpad does.  Otherwise they’ll ship within a few days of payment.

Here’s the form for deskpads and wire keys.

I’ll leave the form open for a couple weeks, and will send out invoices every few days.  Feel free to post or message if you have any questions!

&nbsp;

**Artisan Keycaps**

I’m considering doing some more commissions for matching artisan keycaps from Artkey.  The sculpts available are Bull v2 ($270), Voidwalker ($300), Exmor ($400), Medieval ($800 for 6 sculpts).  I’m not putting these on the Google form, but send me a pm here or on Discord (scud80#9392) if you might be interested in joining one of those, or if you’re not interested in those but might be interested in sculpts from other makers later on.

This is a keyset inspired by the Mudbeam colorway from Hunger Work Studio.  The colors for this kit will be custom, matched to resin samples provided by HWS.  The GB will run until October 30, and the current estimate for shipping is May 2021.

Click here to check out the colors in the set compared with some GMK standard colors.  The quarter circles were sanded and the eighth circles were not.  GMK has a semi-matte finish so the ultimate result should be somewhere between those two.

Since this is, to my knowledge, the first keyset inspired by artisan keycaps there will be quite a bit of artisan support for the set.  The first artisan raffle will happen about 2 weeks after the GB is complete, and is currently slated to have caps from Artkey, Ritual Master, LazyCaps, Fraktal Caps, Hunger Work Studio, and Destroyer Caps.  PrimeCaps will also be making matching artisans, though they may not be ready for the first raffle.  All of these will be exclusive to GB participants.  Alpha Keycaps will also be participating, with details to be provided later.  Other collabs are in discussion, and if you're an artisan maker and would like to participate please get in touch!

For the artisan raffle, GB participants will get 1 entry for each base kit purchased and 1 entry for every 2 other kits purchased (no maximum limit).  The raffle will be open for at least 24hrs to make sure everybody that wants to join it has a chance, and it will be announced on my Instagram account, here, and on Reddit.

Ok, here are the details for the artisan collab raffle, which will be open to those who previously purchased GMK Mudbeam kits in the GB.  There are ~130 total caps available in this raffle.

The raffle will be posted sometime tomorrow, and I'll announce here, on Reddit, and in my IG page.  I will leave the form open until Friday evening, and then invoices will go out over the weekend.  Each person will be able to win only once, unless there are more caps available than entries, in which case you might be able to win two.

Preferences:

On the form you will be able to specify if there are makers that you would not like to win caps from, and you will also be able to specify an order of preference for the different makers.  The caps from each maker will be randomly assigned though.  In some cases you’ll still know what you won in advance due to differences in pricing, even though it’s technically blind bag.

Shipping:

I am doing fulfillment for caps from Artkey, Destroyer Caps, and Fraktal Kaps, while Hunger Work Studio, LazyCaps, PrimeCaps, and Ritual Master will be doing their own fulfillment.  This does mean that if it ends up being possible to buy multiple caps, there would likely be two separate invoices with two separate shipping charges for those.

Shipping for all makers except for HWS and PrimeCaps will be $5 in the USA, $10 to Canada, or $15 internationally.  Ritual Master additionally offers Priority Mail in the USA for $8.  Shipping for PrimeCaps wins will be $7 in the USA, $9 to Canada, or $12 internationally.  Hunger Work Studio shipping costs are determined by their e-commerce platform based on location and desired shipping service.

Caps that are available, with prices:

Artkey: Amu, Goodoo, Raiju, Skudu (3 of each available, $80 each), Two different logo caps (1 of each available, $130 each)
I’ll have a better pic of the logos tomorrow.





Destroyer Caps: RAWR (24 available, $50 each)



Fraktal Kaps: Gray Banana with blank (5 pairs availalbe, $85 each), Beige Banana with blank (5 pairs available, $85 each), Beige Banana without blank (2 available, $60 each)
The blanks are all the same design.



Hunger Work Studio: Meet Popsi-Skull (20 available, $140 each)
If you win one of these you will need to have an account on https://hungerwork.studio/ in order to check out.



LazyCaps: Battle Bear (4 available, $60 each), Jikininkey (4 available, $60 each)



PrimeCaps: Al Bumen (~12 available, $90 each)
Final inspection hasn’t been done on these, so the final number available won’t be finalized for a day or two.



Ritual Master: Hive (7 available, $50 each), Speaker (5 available, $65 each), Watcher (14 available, $65 each)
