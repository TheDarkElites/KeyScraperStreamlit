This keyboard is designed by Glare
He was my first customer, he has completed 3 TKL by himself so far, and now I'm releasing is his 4th TKL

He has enjoyed keyboard hobbies for a long time, and I have met many customers, but I have never seen a customer who is so sincere about keyboards.
