Hello everyone and welcome to the Groupbuy for the Borsdorf, the first custom keyboard for Apple M3501 ISO Keycaps.




(Founder's Edition engraving )
Thanks to Egla and Kwerdenker for the photos

More





(Apple Sticker not included)

Typing Test:

Some time ago Westfoxtrot of Cablecardesigns created the Cyclops PCB to solve the unique predicament of using the ISO keycaps from Apple's M3501 keyboard, more commonly known as the AEKII, in a modern layout. From there on I designed a custom case to better accomodate the inherently curved caps. After a small initial private round I have partnered with mykeyboard.eu to deliver a full fledged GB for this board.

Specs:

  • Anodized Aluminium Case made in Belgium by Salvun
  • 8-Point topmount with plastic washers
  • PCB by Cutie Club, STM32 based, USB C, QMK compatible, Capslock LED for those with SKCLs who fancy it
  • Carbon Fiber Plate
  • 5° typing angle
  • comes with a high quality Swisstools Torx tool
  • 66% Layout
    More



Groupbuy figures:

  • unlimited quantity
  • runs until May 3rd
  • estimated delivery around October (Normally I'd go with a more agressive timeline but alas, we have a global pandemic happening right now. The boards may well arrive earlier but better safe than sorry)
  • 300€ kit cost with VAT
  • Anodizing available in Titanium Grey, Navy and Bordeaux
    More



  • Cerakoting in standard colors available per request for a 60€ premium

Borsdorf product page link
Google form - color selection

FAQ:

  • Which switches can I use with this? Any Alps Style switch will work, this means ALPS SKCM/SKCL, SKBM/SKBL and Matias switches (also Tai-Hao APCs but I cannot recommend that)
  • Where can I source caps? In my experience the M3501 is the most common Alps board on the European market by a decent margin, eBay and your local craigslist equivalents will cover you, please consider shooting for defect ones so functional boards don't need to be gutted
  • ne ANSI avail? Not for the time being, no. There may well be an ANSI round further down the line but no concrete plans as of writing
  • Will this work for [Alps Keyset that is not M3501 ISO]? No, Alps don't let you play fast and loose with layouts, this only works for M3501s
  • Will this work for MX? Hard no, the PCB will sit lower, things will NOT fit. Also it'll look goofy, trust me
  • Will the OG Cyclops PCBs with mini-USB fit this case? Unfortunately no, the second iteration is explicitly designed for USB-C
  • This is cool does anyone make AEK profile artisan caps? You're in luck, Gabbzan of Gabcaps does just that and has done so already
  • Can I buy this keyboard kit outside of Europe?  Yes just contact me, NecromanX here or through Discord to help you out.
  • I have a question that's not listed here. You can reach everyone involved with the project on mykeyboard's discord at https://discord.gg/m6nggAv, the channel is in the "Keyboard Projects" category
