GMK Orange Boi is co-designed by me and Typo.mk. This set is inspired by orange kitties everywhere (including my own orange boi Richard). One of the goals of this set is to raise money for cats in need! If GMK Orange Boi makes it through group buy successfully, a portion of designer profits will be donated to The Odd Cat Sanctuary, a feline rescue organization that was started to help kittens and cats (as well as an occasional dog) with "challenges"  or that have been neglected. Check out their website to learn more and to admire all of the adorable kitties that they’ve rescued!

To follow GMK Orange Boi on social media, follow us on Instagram (Ty, me), and feel free to join my Discord server.

Obviously GMK lead times are not ideal right now but we are sticking with GMK for this set for a few reasons:

- Crispness and clarity of doubleshot legends since the colors used in this set don't have a TON of inherent contrast

- Strong desire for cherry profile

- Since we ruled out dye sub, we'd need UV printed novelties for maximum cuteness!

The estimated ship date from GMK is Q1 2023, though it's possible that Q2/Q3 2023 is more realistic.

Ty and I received the 3 plastic color samples from GMK last week, and we've gone ahead and officially approved them. GMK used their new color-matching tech wherein the colors are measured by GMK for accuracy to the color references before being sent out. On our end, our biggest priority was ensuring that the samples look like the renders so that everyone gets the keycaps in the colors that we essentially promised y'all!  We're happy with how they turned out. These are just photos I took with my iPhone in a few different light settings. IMO, the colors are slightly more saturated in person, but I wanted to share the pics unedited.

The 4th color in the set, the pastel pink used for a few small details in the novelties, is UV print only, and therefore will only start to be sampled by GMK now that we've approved the plastic colors.

In non-keycap news, deskmat samples were approved as of a few months ago and are currently in production!

At some point I will get around to taking photos of the Orange Boi samples next to a few GMK stock colors so you can see the differences/subtleties, etc.
