FAQs

+ For the founder's edition, there are e-white and e-black options to choose from

+ For the PC version, you can also choose a PC top accent piece

+ Can I mix and match colors? For the Aluminum version, you can choose different colored top and bottom half of the case if you'd like.

+ Can I match a PC top/bottom with an Aluminum one? - Not in one order. You must buy either a full PC case or a full Aluminum case.

+ The inner middle to support gasket mount is an optional piece and is an upcharge (pricing soon). It comes in Aluminum e-white or e-black only

+ Sandblasted brass parts have a clear e-coating for protection applied to them

+ What is e-coating? https://clearclad.com/products/what-is-e-coat/

+ What does a gasket do? Provides more flex & bounce to typing experience than mounting the plate to the top or bottom of a case.

+ How much is shipping and when is it charged? Shipping is calculated (and invoiced) once the manufacturing process is completed and the end product is ready to be sent out. Price varies by country being delivered to

+ How many units will be made? Founder's Edition - 10 (raffle). Polycarbonate - 50 (FCFS). Aluminum - Unlimited
+ Can i buy two spots ? there was no limited on alu case you can buy much you want ,but for PC case one per person
