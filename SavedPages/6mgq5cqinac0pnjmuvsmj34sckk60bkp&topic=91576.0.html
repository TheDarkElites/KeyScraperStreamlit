[GB] CF-LXXX - Ultralite Carbon Fiber TKL Case [DONE]
« on: Thu, 14 September 2017, 13:48:07 »


ORDER FORM (1 nerD case still available)

This will be a quick, small (limited to 10 cases for round 1) run GB for light, skeleton-style carbon fiber TKL cases in the same style as my previous CF-LX group buy. Foggyflute is helping again and we'll be using the same manufacturer. Designs are already finalized and manufacturing will start at the latest before the end of the month, likely sooner. Unfortunately, because I'm at school presently, I won't be able to ship out the cases until Thanksgiving. This is an absolutely firm date, no sooner no later. Because of my last GB having quite a few extended problems and because I realize that people might be reluctant to hand over money again, pre-payment will be optional. Regardless of your choice, your spot will be reserved. I'll keep a wait list beyond the 10 buyers in case of any non-payers.

Anyone who chooses not to prepay will be invoiced when the cases arrive at my house. This should ensure that there is no uncertainty about ability to ship the product in the specified timeframe. Anyone who does not pay within 1 week will have their spot given to the first person on the waitlist.

Price
+ $169 plus shipping

Specs
+ 1.6cm matte carbon fiber
+ Frosted white/solid black acrylic midpiece with options for different PCB compatibility
+ Switch-mount PCB
+ 7.5 degree carbon fiber/acrylic feet
+ M3 screws/posts


*Notes about PCB compatibility
- There will be three options for the USB cutout location in the midpiece: GON nerD TKLV2, winkeyless B87, and Phantom. These simply address compatibility with the cutout location, so any PCBs that share these placements will be compatible.

Let me know if I'm missing anything or if there's any other information you'd like to see.
