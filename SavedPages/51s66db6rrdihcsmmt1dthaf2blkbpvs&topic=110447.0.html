Introducing the GetawayVan

GB Form: https://forms.gle/Un5uDhvRXgNcq5Us5

The gb will have a 75 board limit and a 25 3dp case limit

I got tired of waiting for van pcbs so may as well make it myself, therefore the GetawayVan is a drop-in minivan compatible pcb that features:
-Allpcb Manufacturing as they do my silks oh so pretty
-An ARM microcontroller
-Usb Type C
-ESD Protection
-Per key addressable rgb
-Alps support
-Solderable for those that dislike the current hotswap options
-multiple layouts for those that want split bars, big bars or a symmetrical look

Campsite FULL PC plates in the split layout will be available through this gb given there is enough interest, I'll be selling 3dp cases and I've worked with Jake from P3D to get GetawayVan specific pages up to make it easier on everyone.


The board has been my daily driver for the last few months and overall I feel confident in that if you want a van layout now this should be what you are looking at.
