Hey guys, R9 GB is now live and we're happy to introduce our new Zilent Tactile switches! The stems a blue shade and modeled after our Zealios, with added doubleshot rubber dampeners to decrease the noise from each keypress without feeling mushy/squishy. Bottom out is solid and quiet, without any noticeable reduction in travel.

GB pricing will end Jan 15, 2018 at 23:59 PST. After then, the pricing will be upped to pre-order/retail rates. Projected shipping for the buy is end of January-early February.

Do the Zilents produce the same effect as the R9 Zealencio Silencing Clips, without needing them?

For Cherry Silent, the mechanism makes the feel much better and different from a QMX/Zealencio clip (and zilents are supposed to have more or less the same mechanism of a Cherry silent).         

Clips are usually mushy and even on Cherry profile they are not really a stable thing, basically every/some row will sound different with clips.  While with the silenced switch you still feel the thock but is a silenced thock, the same on every raw/switch, that is imho good. 

In general, no they can't produce the same effect and a silenced switch is always better of a clip, without even saying that the Clips are supposed to fully work only on Cherry profile keycaps (bottomout + upstroke) while only on upstroke for other profiles.  Neither same sound/feel or whatever you mean.  A silenced switch is always better than a clip. 


Using both yeah is redundant like Puddsy said.
