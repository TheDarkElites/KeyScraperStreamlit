Kits

Base

Novelties

I received the R2 color samples last week and spent some time reviewing them over the weekend.

The bad news about this round of samples is that GMK only sent me 1 out of 2 colors that needed resampling from last round (yellow). My lead vendor and I are inquiring about the missing color now and hope to hear back soon - I hope it doesn't delay things too much

On to the good news: the yellow sample they sent is beautiful and has been approved! I've snapped a couple of photos:
Again, take these with a grain of salt since different monitors will display colors differently.

(First row) BoW, the approved purple-ish color from R1
(Second row) R1 yellow, R2 yellow

(Inside during the day, mixture of artificial light and natural light from window)


(Indirect natural light)

I requested a second round of yellow because the R1 yellow, while close, was more dull with a mustardy undertone. The R2 yellow sample improved on this by being more saturated and green. I'd describe it as a pleasant lemon-lime color halfway between pastel and neon. It provides the pop of color I wanted for an accent but is gentle enough that it doesn't hurt your eyes. I think it complements the purple-ish color well Hoping GMK will nail the remaining purple color sample, as that is the last thing standing between this set and the manufacturing queue now.

I also got word that the ETA for Purple-ish is early August 2023! I'm taking this with a few spoonfuls of salt, especially with the added delay of the remaining color sample. But... it would be insane if we actually hit our Q3 2023 target. Keeping my fingers crossed.
