GB Date: February 26
Calliope Interest Check


GB will be a google/jotforms raffle lasting for at least 24 hours after it opens. Link to form will be posted here and my discord. There will be 70 boards with a breakdown as follows:

20x Black Anodized
20x Silver Anodized
20x Pink Anodized
10x Lilac Anodized

Plate options:

Aluminum ($35 USD for extra plates)
Polycarbonate ($35 USD for extra plates)
Polypropylene ($35 USD per extra plates)

The pricing  for 1x PCB + 1x Plate + 1x Case will be $435 USD with PCB extras being $45 USD. Invoices will be sent out using shopify.




Once the raffle ends and invoice are sent out, there will be a 48 hour time period where you can modify the amount of plates and pcbs you wish to order. You must pay within this time period as well. Please do not DM me on Discord to change your order! Reply to shopify invoice instead
