Quote from: iNo on Fri, 29 November 2019, 17:54:50
Hey upas, I saw on Taeha's YT video he used the FR4 plate. That doesn't come included with the standard kit, right? If I'm reading it correctly it ships with a brass plate?

Yes - the standard plate is brass. I personally prefer FR4, but during the IC form, the majority of folks wanted a brass plate.

The thing about brass plates it they are less expensive in bulk, so by making it the default (which most people wanted), I could make the cost cheaper.

So that's why the standard is brass.
