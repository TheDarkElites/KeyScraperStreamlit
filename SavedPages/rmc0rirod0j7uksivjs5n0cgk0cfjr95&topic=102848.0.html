TheKeyCompany Presents: M0llY



We're thrilled to have you meet M0llY. It's a refined take on a timeless design and features an integrated USB-C hub. A fully Geometric replica of the Apple M0110A, this board is a shot of nostalgia turned up to eleven with a sleek modern revision.

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fj0qS_Urel0

https://thekey.company/collections/upcoming-releases/products/m0lly

What's Included



PCB



- QMK Firmware
- MX / Alps compatibility
- Based on the TKC1800 design; its bulletproof
- Matte black w gold silk screen
- You must solder switches, indicator LEDS, and in-switch LEDs (if used)
- All SMD components pre-soldered in factory
- 1 year TKC warranty
- USB-C output via hub

CASE/PLATE



- Aircraft Grade Aluminum 6061
- 7.5 lbs Unbuilt
- Top Plate Mount
- PVD Brass Plate, 1.5mm thick
- 7 degree typing angle
- PVD Brass Flower Weight
- PVD Brass Front Badge
- Gloss Black PVD Stainless Hardware
- Alps plates available as an add-on product
- Additional Plates, PCBs, and Front Badges are available

PLATES



BADGES



WEIGHTS



HUB



- Internal Hub Daughterboard
- USB-C output
- USB-A 3.1 input
- High Speed Data Transfer
- High Amp Charging

COLOR OPTIONS



Black / Silver
Black case. Bright Silver Plate, Weight, and Badge Finishes.



Black / Gold
Black case. Bright Gold (Brass) Plate, Weight, and Badge Finishes



Silver / Black
Apple Silver case. Black plate finish with a Dark Nickel badge and weight finish.



Pink / Black
Flat Out Fabulous pink case. Black plate Finish with a Dark Nickel badge and weight finish.



Blue / Silver
Aquatica Blue case. Bright Silver Plate, Weight, and Badge Finish



White / Black
E-White case. Black plate Finish, with a Dark Nickel Badge and Weight finish.



Get your fix for $449 for most colorways.
You asked, we deliver. White M0llY is available for $479. (E-White Coating)

LAYOUT CONFIGURATIONS

The M0llY features a Plethora of Layouts options as well as having a PCB that's both ALPS and MX compatible, with your choice of ALPS or MX plates at time of purchase!



Where Can I Order?!



https://thekey.company/collections/upcoming-releases/products/m0lly

M0llY orders begin Friday October 11th at 9pm EDT (GMT -4:00). Limit to 200 but this CAN increase based on demand. Expected time of delivery will be in Late March!

Thank you guys for your continued support of TheKeyCompany, and as always, we're proud to deliver a product that you guys can customize, adore, and be proud to put on your desk everyday, we strive to make it more than just an input device and we hope that's reflected in each and every one of our Products.
