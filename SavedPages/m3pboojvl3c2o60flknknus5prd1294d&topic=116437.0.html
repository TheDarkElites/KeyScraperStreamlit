Notes:

Why GMK?

I wanted a cherry profile in ABS, purely for personal preference. This left me with only JTK, DMK, and GMK (with maybe SP DCS). DMK did not exist as of IC and I wanted cherry so in the case I buy a face kit, it can be slapped onto any other GMK to make budget Cluck. Left with only JTK and GMK, I chose GMK at the time for smaller moqs and more flexible kitting.
