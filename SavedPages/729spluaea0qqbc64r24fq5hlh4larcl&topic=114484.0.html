BN009
9 KEY MACROPAD


We would like to introduce everyone to BN009
BN009 is an CNC machined macropad from a solid block of aluminium.
It is a 9 key macropad which had 9 switches. Each key can be configured via QMK to do just about anything you want it to.

GROUP BUY : 5th Sep - 26th Sep 2021


VENDORS

US- Dangkeebs - $65
CA- Keyspresso - $65
EU- CandyKeys - €70
CHINA- zFrontier - ¥463
ALL OTHER REGION: Binepad - $65

Estimated shipping DEC 2021
