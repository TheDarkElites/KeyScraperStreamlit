For updates and discussion please join my new Discord server!




This is the set for all you sysadmins, IT professionals, Linux users, power users
and the people like me who had to learn to launch their games from DOS when
they were kids and grew up around computers ever since. Three mod options
and a choice of accent or non-accent colour keys means you can really make
this set your own.

NovelKeys will be making a custom NK65 Superuser edition to perfectly pair with your set!
A translucent dark grey case with 353C green scanline stripes on the rear and bottom facets

RAMA

I've worked together with RAMA to create the very first KAM RAMA cap to be made available!
With this new CNC profile, other designers will be able to collaborate with RAMA on KAM caps
going forward. The Superuser RAMA will be matched to 7545 C (mods grey) with glow in the
dark green enamel infill!

Additional info:
  • KAM is a uniform keycap profile from Keyreative, the manufacturer of the KAT profile.
  • KAM is a symmetrical version of KAT R2 (that's R3 in other profiles).
  • Made of 1.65mm thick, all over dye-sublimated PBT.
  • kam_superuser will be matte, instead of glossy.
  • Official lead time from Keyreative: 8-10 months after group buy.
  • More realistic lead time estimate: 12 months after group buy.
