Updates

Update December
Today I received the color samples for GMK Pharaoh.
We will next be analyzing the colors and sending our reviews to GMK.

Once this analysis is done we will update here with more info.

Cheers!

Update January
Round 1 samples were rejected. They were not bright enough in different lighting conditions.
We have sent our analysis along with the changes we want to GMK. Hopefully we will get follow ups in the next months.

Update Feb
No updates

Update March
Plastic samples sent to GMK for R2 color matching. Waiting to get the GMK samples back.

Update April
None

Update May
Received R2 samples.

Update June
R2 samples were rejected. Although the samples were really good, I wanted a bit more orange in the color and hence was not fully satisfied. The next samples should be spot on (hopefully)

Update July
Waiting on R3 samples.

Update September
R3 color samples accepted. Waiting on production slot.

Update October
Production renders and banderole confirmed. Waiting on a slot from GMK.

Update November
No updates.

Update December
In queue.

Update January 23
No updates.

Update February 23
GMK ETA for production complete is May.

Update May 23
Finishing up production. Will be shipped to vendors soon.

Estimated Shipping : Q3/Q4 2022
