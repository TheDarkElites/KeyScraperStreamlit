If you fancy making your next board hotswappable, you've come to the right place

  • Mill Max socket 7305 - 0.21 GBP per socket (shorter, thinner flange, gold plated)
  • Mill Max socket 0305 - 0.10 GBP per socket (longer, thicker flange, tinned)

You will need 2 sockets per switch.

Shipping: 4 GBP to Europe, 6 GBP to rest of the world. However, if you're in the US you're really better off getting these locally.

Join the Group Buy here: https://forms.gle/FpLuUUTBZfaFYhYp9

  • No MOQ on individual orders. We just need to reach 2500 total order quantity for each socket type to get the lowest price point. If we don't reach the lowest price break the cost will be slightly higher (e.g. 0.13 GBP @ 500 for 0305s)
  • Price includes 21% VAT and 5% PayPal fees
  • Payment by PayPal in GBP (unfortunately I'm unable to withdraw Euros so I'd be screwed on currency exchange)
  • The GB will open for two weeks until Sunday 9 February 2020
  • I will send out invoices shortly after the GB closes. Unpaid invoices will be cancelled after 24 hours and order placed with Mouser
  • Shipping from Mouser to me will take ~5 working days
  • Once received I will count and pack the sockets and send them out to you within 2-3 of days

Reddit thread with some extra details (because bbcode sucks big time): https://www.reddit.com/r/mechmarket/comments/etr1iy/gb_mill_max_sockets_eu_r4/

If you have any questions please don't hesitate to ask.
