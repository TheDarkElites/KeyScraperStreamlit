Intro



We are delighted to announce ClickClackIO and Percent Studio second collaboration of the PT990 keyboard. The Platinum Series, born from the inspiration of the precious platinum metal, shines with its smooth and gentle lines that compliment its flat and sharply defined design. A singer’s best record sales are defined by the precious and rare metal, your desk deserves the same treatment. 


Unboxing, Building & Typing Experience

The PT990 continues Percent’s minimalist style, using G2 variable chamfers in almost all corners to create a warm, visible metal finish.
