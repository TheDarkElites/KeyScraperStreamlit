Hello Geekhack!

GB live February 1st to February 28th!

This keyset is heavily inspired by the IDE theme One Dark Pro.
I wanted to offer a Pbt set kind of discrete with a composition potential using the several kits available.

Quote
Why choose ePbt? Because the MOQ policy is way more flexible for child kits with less popularity like FR/BE/Norde/dvorak and so on. This set without this compatibility makes no real sense to me as i mainly made it for people not being able to get for example GMK Oblivion / Nord which are in the same tones / theme.

Delivering time will be confirmed at the end of GB by ePbt, we are targeting end of 2021.

Quote from: Adriano on Thu, 04 March 2021, 02:10:27
1) I got a KeyChron K6. Am i correct those "be the one" are fitting 100%?

You can cover the board but you won't have Fn1, Fn2 and Backlight keys in this set. You'll have to use Fn instead of Fn1, Code instead of Fn2 and Delete/Print/Scroll instead of Backlight.

Quote from: Adriano on Thu, 04 March 2021, 02:10:27
2) So i got the KeyChron i wonder if the "be the one" got some shine through at the white fonts or not. Tbh it is not necessary for me but would be good to know.

The keycap is totally opaque, you'll only see backlighting between the keys.

Quote from: Adriano on Thu, 04 March 2021, 02:10:27
4) GMK produces the keys. Does they still produce in Germany? If yes, are the CandyKeys Kits send directly to them? Meaning keys not around the world to be back in Germany again?

It isn't a GMK set, it is manufactured by EPBT in China. EPBT will ship to Candykeys and Candykeys will ship to you.
