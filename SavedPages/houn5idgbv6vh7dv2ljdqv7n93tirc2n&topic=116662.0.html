Hello everyone! I'd like to introduce a magical keyset called TRANSMUTATION.

Thanks so much for the support I've received for this keyset. And thanks to all the people who contributed their knowledge and opinions during the Interest Check! Also thanks to Kono for jumping in and offering to get this thing made even when a lot of manufacturers and vendors balked at the huge amount of custom symbols.
This is something that I felt like the keyboard community needed, something that really could transform a keyboard into something otherworldly, magical, sinister, dark and mysterious. Or at the very least make your coworker point and say "What the heck is THAT?!"


The Group buy is OVER. But the vendor Kono bought a few sets themselves and will sell them until sold out!

Kono Store (US)



When:

The Group Buy will be live from March 22 to April 22, 2022.

Estimated production completion is Q3 2022.

Details:

Manufactured by Kono as part of the Kono Designer Series (KDS)
PBT plastic – high durability and shine resistance
Cherry profile Keycaps
Compatible with Cherry MX switches
Fade resistant dye-sublimated and reverse dye-sublimated legends
Deskmats: stitched edge, 900x400 mm, 3mm thick
