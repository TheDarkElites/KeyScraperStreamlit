Design Concept

Hi guys, Glove is here. I am happy to bring you a new keycap theme.
 It is inspired by an adventure that a warrior saves the princess.
The warrior wears his green battle suit, climbs over hills and mountains, pass through the forest to save his princess.

Meanwhile, it is a theme about Spring Camping.
We need to go outside and camp in Spring, to feel the nature. We can go to travel, go camping, etc.



At the beginning, I intend to create different colour scheme for ALPHA kit, one is mainly Blue, the other is mainly Green.

After collecting the feedback from the community, I finally decided to remove the blue Alpha design.
Since the Green suit is more classical colour scheme for a warrior like Peter Pan.
The green suit, yellow hair and brown feather glove. Therefore, I add brown colour in green Alpha.

Since I wanted to use tow different colour on Alpha, I had to remove blue and green from MOD kit and white novel kit and used yellow characters.
However, the character is hard to recognize. Eventually, I adjusted the colour of character to green for MOD kit and White Novel.
