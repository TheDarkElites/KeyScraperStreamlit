Supported layouts:



Specifications:
  • Full Kobelco 5052 aluminum case
  • 8 degrees slope
  • brass weight
  • Plate material: anodized aluminum (grey/silver)
  • ISO and ANSI
  • Custom ps2avrGB PCB
  • Fully Programmable
  • Full LED backlighting
  • Controller parts, diodes, resistors and RGB LEDs are pre-soldered

Color options:
Black, Silver, Grey, Pink, Violet and Sky Blue


Skyblue


Violet


Pink. photo by filphil

$63 for plate + PCB (presoldered with all the components except for switches & switch LEDs)
$8 for diffuser

Quantity: Only 23 units will be made. Besides plate and PCB, all parts will be engraved with unit no.

Deadline for order/payment: The groupbuy will end as soon as the MOQ is met, would appreciate if I am able to receive the payment asap. 

Lead time: Late January
