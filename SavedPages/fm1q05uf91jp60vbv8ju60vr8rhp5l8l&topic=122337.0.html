Inspiration
KKBMoon Rabbit is a set inspired by one of my favorite childhood candies, White Rabbit.


Kits

For all alphas, the red-colored legends will be doubleshot, and the black-colored sublegends will be UV printed.
For the blue novelty caps, the white will be doubleshot, and the red will be UV printed.
For the yellow novelty caps, the blue will be doubleshot, and the red will be UV printed.**
