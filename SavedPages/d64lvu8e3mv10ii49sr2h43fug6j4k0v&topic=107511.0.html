GMK Prepress
Group Buy - Preorder before August 28, 2020 (Group Buy Ended!)

Join my Discord for (faster) updates and GIVEAWAY!: https://discord.gg/q7fxt8f

Estimated production time: 28 weeks after GB closes.
Estimated delivery: April 2021



Idea: Process Printing, Pre-print Process, CMYK Separation

This set is based on printing process that use Cyan, Magenta, Yellow, Black color (CMYK) as a standard recipe that is utilized in many types of printing machinery to attain diverse variation of colors in print industry. The process of prepress stage involves digital artwork being transferred to negative films for each CMYK colors and undergo several procedures until they are ready to be printed to papers or other media. Many of the text modifier legends are related to prepress and print process terms that many graphic designers or those who work in press industry are familiar with, instilled into this keycap set with mixture of CMYK accents and contrasting child kits that will visually enhance the overall looks of your keyboard.

Vendors
US: Kono Store
EU: MyKeyboards.eu
Oceania: Daily Clack
Asia: zFrontier(EN)
China: zFrontier(CHN)

Kits and Prices

Base Kit
Price: $149.99

Base kit offers 147 keycaps that can cover most keyboard layouts from 60%-66% up to full-sized keyboard, as well as basic ISO support. CMY colored accents for various modifier keys are also included.



Separation
Price: $49.99

Colored modifiers as an exclamation to CMY colors as seen in the first concept of the keyset. This is the way to go if you dig the initial concepts.



Poster Alphas
Price: $69.99

Kit that consists alternate colorway for R1 to R3 numbers/symbols and alphas and two rows of numpad



Novelties
Price: 59.99

Prepress icon novelties kit.



Tradition
Price: 34.99

Bottom row modifiers' alternate color kit. This kit is suggested by Huey/Manofinterests (thanks, Huey!), and I, with many other people will surely appreciate these 'traditional' accents put on this set! Contains 1.00, 1.25 and 1.50 modifiers (Cut, Art text legend) as well as 2.75, 2.25 and 1.75 and 1.25 shift (Mount) keys, plus 1.00 Fn key.



Overruns
Price: $29.99

This spacebars kit includes some extra 6.25 spacebar colors (grey, cyan, yellow), along with 6.00 HHKB/styled layout as well as split bars.



Deskmat-Papers
Deskmat size 900x400x4mm



Deskmat-Splatter
Deskmat size 900x400x4mm



Keyboard Sleeve
Made by ycpkeyboards.id



- Drawstring
- Materials: Cloth, Plastic
- Waterproof: Yes
- Cable pocket: Inside
- Sizes: 60-65%, TKL, 96, Full-sized
