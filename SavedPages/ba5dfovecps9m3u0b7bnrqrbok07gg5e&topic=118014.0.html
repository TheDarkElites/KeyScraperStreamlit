Alish 40 Group Buy.

Start date: 8/12/22
End date: 9/5/22

Kit Cost: 45$ With Pro-Micro, 37$ Without.

Product Page: https://mechwild.com/product/alish/

Discord Channel:  https://discord.gg/bJ7gBZSyJG


The Alish 40 is, as the name implies, is a 40% board with an Alice-ish layout.
This is a DIY Through-Hole, sandwich FR4 board with a bottom plate and an optional switch plate.

It features an optional rotary encoder beneath the left palm, RGB under glow, and a Pro-Micro footprint.








Kit Cost: 45$ With Pro-Micro, 37$ Without.

Estimated Fulfillment Date: 3-4 Months after GB Conclusion

There will be a build service available (Diode and Pro-Micro Soldering) for $20. 

I have partnered with MechWild to make this possible.
They have a lot of experience in the DIY FR4 kit space and I'm extremely happy to be working with them.

I'm super excited for this board and I hope you guys are too!
