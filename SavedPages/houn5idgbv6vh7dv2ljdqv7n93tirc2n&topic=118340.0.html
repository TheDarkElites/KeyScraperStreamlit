"dn60 is a seamless o-ring gasket mount 60%. My motivation behind the project was to create a two piece o-ring gasket board with mounting similar to the otd 356 mini, as most of the available o-ring 60% boards follow the unikorn drop in mounting style." - CMG

Sound Test

WKL model available!

Top and Bottom can be different colors with no extra charge!

Quality Disclaimer: A Stock boards may have interior defects, and very very minute exterior flaws. Quality concerns upon shipment will be assessed and determined if eligible for replacement.

Expected delivery: Q1 2023

Board Specs: - Front Height: 17.3mm - Typing Angle: 6.88 deg - Unbuilt Weight: 2.45lb or 1118g

Kit Includes:
  • 1x Top Case (Alu 6063)
  • 1x Bottom Case (Alu 6063)
  • 1x Brass Weight
  • 1x Plate (PC Full or Alu Full)
  • 1x Pcb (Rex60)
  • 1x 70A O-ring
  • 1x Unified DB
  • 1x JST cable
  • 4x 3M bumpons
  • All Mounting Hardware

Plate Compatibility:
  • ANSI/Tsangan/WKL bottom row
  • Full or Split R shift
  • Full or Split BS
  • Stepped or Non-stepped caps

Optional extras:
  • PCB ($32)
  • Plate ($40)

Price: $375 + shipping
