« on: Thu, 13 May 2021, 01:26:00 »
Hello, From the first IC to the GB, NIZ DUO82 has undergone three design changes and three protype. let's see what the final DUO 82 looks like
Due to the process coating, there are various color choices for DUO82. Also we make 2 designs for this case. There are A and F styles (decided by vote).
In the following pictures, only one color can be selected for the yellow part and one color can be selected for the purple part (not supported different colors). That is to say, the part with the same color in the picture only supports the overall change, and other colors can be matched freely
I must also explain one small parts, which is actually the key parts of duo82 to achieve Bluetooth. During our assembly and testing, we realized this bezel with more choice can show us more fun.so,each duo82 Suite contains 3 such parts, respectively:Black AS, Transparent AS, Brass (same color as other Bezel）
