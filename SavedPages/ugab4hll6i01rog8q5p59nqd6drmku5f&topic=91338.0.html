[GB] DSA Run - Cancelled :(
« on: Wed, 30 August 2017, 20:40:13 »
Dear all, unfortunately we're far from MOQ, so we'll have to conclude the GB without proceeding to the buy phase. All buyers on monokei.us have been refunded. I've notified KP on Taobao and CandyKeys to proceed with refunds on their end as well.

All is not lost, we're looking to get this design through another manufacturer possibly next year. Thanks again for all the support!

More
Hi everyone,

DSA Run is here! airhorns

IC Thread: https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=85825.0



Additional renders!

Where:

Get in on the buy at https://monokei.us
China/Taobao: https://item.taobao.com/item.htm?spm=a1z10.1-c-s.w13619845-14986162203.14.2c5d7ebfHpAnj&id=557915644158
EU: CandyKeys

I've setup this site for the buy, and hopefully for a few other projects that are on the way. The site is really basic now so if you have trouble with the site please do let me know, cheers.

Price:

  • Base Kit: $144
  • Addons Kit: $75

Materials:
Base kit: ABS double shot
Addons: PBT dyesub for JP alphas, ABS doubleshot for the rest


Shipping:

Shipping options are available so do choose your preferred one.

Singaporeans, you may choose to pick up the set at a location and time to be decided for free. Look for that option in the drop down menu.

Worried about VAT? Send me an email about it, or drop a note. We can work something out.

Timeline:

GB end: 20th September 2017
Please do temper your expectations, it is a SP buy after all. Currently we're looking at receiving the set in January-February, depending on production time and having it in your hands by March to April. I'm overestimating greatly here, and would rather get it to you earlier than expected, than much later.

Raffle:

This time around KeyForge has kindly provided us with a pair of artisan keycaps for the raffle. A Shishi and an Orochi in the set's colourway will be released exclusive to buyers of the set. These keycaps will be raffled off with the help of the RNG gods, and will be allocated separately so it'll only be possible to win either one. This will be a once-off colourway.

Proxy:

As of now there are no proxies for the buy, but if you require feel free to drop a note via monokei.us@gmail.com with your order number in the title and we can work out something. I've shipped to Europe many times as many of you who've traded with me can attest to that fact.

MOQ:

As of now we have a MOQ of 150 for the base kit, and 75 for the addonds kit. Do encourage your friends and family, even your mam to get in on the buy. We won't be running this a second time

Support:

Ping me on Reddit/Slack/GH or via monokei.us@gmail.com with your question/comment clearly in the title, cheers.


Lemme know your comments as always, I've tried to include as many keys as possible without increasing the price of the set by too much. Much thanks to those who have looked through the new kits and helped out.
It's pretty much just my S/O and I working on this, so do let us know if there's anything we've missed out.

Photos in case you're lost:
Base Kit
Addon Kit

Lastly, some notes on the prices. Unfortunately SP's prices seem to have gone up and we're being charged quite a substantial amount for both kits. Do keep in mind we're looking at breaking even after calculating the cost of taxes, ordering from SP and other misc things. I do understand that quite a few people have concerns with the prices, myself included but I've tried absorbing some of the costs in order to bring it down, my sincere apologies again for those who have been supporting this set all this while.
