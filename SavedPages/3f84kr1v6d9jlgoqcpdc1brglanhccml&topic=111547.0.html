Hello and welcome to the GB for SA 8010!

If you don't already know, this set is based on the Data Decisions 8010 keypunch keyboard and was designed in collaboration with FRANCO.





GB Details

The GB will run from March 5 - April 3, with shipping expected in Summer 2022.

US: Mechs & Co
CAN: ApexKeyboards
UK: [Proto]Typist
EU: Oblotzky Industries
SEA: Monokei
OCE: SwitchKeys

Alphas



TKL & 60s



Numpad

Split Bars



PROG
