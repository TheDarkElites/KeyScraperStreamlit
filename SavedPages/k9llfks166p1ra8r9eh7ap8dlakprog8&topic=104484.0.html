[GB] Project Nebula ver. S [All delivered]
« on: Sat, 01 February 2020, 09:15:20 »
General information and price of the keyboard. Prices are excluding shipping and excluding PayPal fees:

TKL Keyboard USD 420 (DBS Bank TT Rate @1.3745 = SGD 577.30)
1. Case will be top mounted, 6063 aluminium - t5
2. Brass weight - Sandblasted
3. Typing angle at 7-8 degrees
4. Silver aluminium universal switch plate
5. QMK PCB - Mini B USB port
6. Keyboard box
7. Monotone colours - Aluminium anodizing (Lavender, Silver, Pink, Dark Purple, Black)

Weight of unbuild keyboard plus pcb = 2.3kg

Additional parts：
1. PCB USD 40 (DBS Bank TT Rate @1.3745 = SGD 55.00)
2. Brass switch plate - Sandblasted USD 35 (DBS Bank TT Rate @1.3745 = SGD 48.20)

Invoices will be billed in Singapore Dollars (SGD, based on bank rates as of 1 Feb 2020).
Do note that shipping to USA will be approximately USD 50, Canada around USD 75. Rest of Asia / World will be advised.
I will invoice the exact shipping cost when I am ready for shipment.

GB will start from 02 Feb 2020 to 28 Feb 2020 (or when 100 units are ordered). Limited to 100 orders based on FCFS.
Delivery - given the uncertainty to Wuhan virus epidemic that is circulating in China, I would set a buffer time of 5 months after the GB ended; extended if the situation remains dire.

Colour options:



Order form: https://forms.gle/A14VUCcRSc1sJARn8
Will post up the form link on 2nd Feb 2020, 11 PM, GMT +8 Singapore time.

Contact:
Feel free to contact me here or via discord at Okonomiyaki#2832

Current numbers: 61/100, got the minimum MOQ to kick off productions after day 1. Heartfelt thanks to all that supported. I am keeping the form opened until all 100 orders are reached.
