GB has ended, and all order shipped.
We got some cases left.
Please check it here : https://shopkey.doyustudio.com/001kba8fc-s

Thanks for geekhack user TheEerieCold https://geekhack.org/index.php?action=profile;u=63616.
He tested several pcbs/keyboards.
I've updated report from him!

PCBs fit for this case without any modify:
Filco TKL
CM MasterKeys (From TheEerieCold)
QFR Plate + Phantom (From TheEerieCold)
KBParadise V80 (From TheEerieCold)
MK Disco (From TheEerieCold)
PCBs fit for this case, but have to modify:
Phantom (Need to add extra support inside, ie. paper, rubber...etc)
Quickfire Rapid (Only 1 mounting hole fit, but bent plate make the other screws work great, https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=90406.msg2499317#msg2499317)
LFK87 with custom SS plate (QFR copy)(bottom screw spacers required, by TheEerieCold)
Deck Francium (need washers for top screws, by TheEerieCold)
Phantom with mechanicalkeyboards.com plate (needs bottom screw spacers, by TheEerieCold)

PCBs does not fit at all
Code, no upper screw holes (From TheEerieCold)
CoolerMaster QFRi (From TheEerieCold)
CM Nova Touch (From TheEerieCold)

These mk.com plates also support Alps so you're good to go, just need switches and keycaps and you can build a new TKL Alps board =)
