[GB] FJELL (Closed for Orders)
« on: Sun, 08 January 2017, 11:06:39 »







This is a limited group buy for a high profile, heavy, high quality mechanical keyboard case for 60% keyboards.

50 units up for grabs


Specifications:

Around 2kg, 4.4lbs

Brass or Steel weights (induvidually numbered)

Anodized Aluminium

6 different colours to choose from

Steep chamfered edges

Fits 60% tray mounted keyboard to make it highly compatible and sturdy.

6x M2 screws for mounting

2x M4 screws for weight mounting

4x antislide 3M feet

8 degree angle


Confirmed compability(5/6 or more screws fit, and is properly in place with no wiggle of any sort):

GON Nerd60

Winkeyless b.face

SPRiT faceW


Base kit - 250$:

FJELL Case and accesories

Brass or Steel weight with serial number


Extras:


Brass Universal60 plate MOQ: 30 - $38




Colors:

Blue
Orange
Grey
Silver
Black
Purple

There will be provided pictures for some of the other colour samples before the raffle close.

Shipping:

~25$ worldwide tracked and insured


Fees:
Currency conversion fees, handling 5% of the total order amount.

Aluminium plates will be available from my store, and will ship free with this when you order.
PCBs will also be available in my store and able to ship with this order.
There will be more info on this once we are closer to the shipping date.


Timeline:

Raffle opens 1/8/2017
Raffle closes 1/15/2017

Shipping: Late May - Early June, since this is the first round, this might be changed.
