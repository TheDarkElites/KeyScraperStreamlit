[GB] Rukia - polycarb version - Shipping
« on: Wed, 22 May 2019, 02:23:13 »
Hello!

This is the group buy page for the polycarbonate version of the Rukia, an Alice form factor keyboard with a plastic case.
The first iteration will be in polycarbonate with a brass bottom weight.
The case and plate are compatible with the original Alice. The USB cutout supports both USB-mini and USB-C.

The group buy is limited to 50 total boards for the purpose of a high quality and a relatively fast turnaround to the buyers (90 days projected).
Half the slots are available to FCFS.

Check the interest check thread for more details: https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=100216.0

Supported layouts:
ANSI
Split backspace
Split right shift
1u/1.25u left thumb modifier
stepped/non-stepped caps lock

Plates:
Done by OneFiveNine (159) of Projectkeyboard.com
Either carbon fiber or polycarbonate
A mini group buy will be run for brass and/or Alps-compatible plates based on GB participant preference.

PCB:
Designed by Upas of Cannonkeys.com from Yuktsi's open-source layout
Arm processor
USB-C
Can be programmed with QMK.
Full LED support.
RGB underglow is pre-soldered.

Cost:
$390 for the kit, which includes case, PCB, plate, PVD brass weight, screws, and bump-ons.
Shipping invoices will be sent out after final QC is completed and kits are ready to ship. Each buyer can choose their preferred method of shipment.

Projected timeline:
90 days Finishing shipping within next two weeks, barring disaster...

The link below will open at the following time on Thursday 5/23/2019:
9:00 pm US Pacific Daylight Savings Time

International time conversion:
Friday 12:00 am US East Coast
Friday 12:00 pm Singapore/Beijing/Hong Kong
Friday 1:00 pm Japan/Korea/Philippines
