August 25th Update:

Promise87 GB was open days ago and FCFS bespoke edition sale was over. We are excited to see the community talking about the custom options they like and the marking design they want. We also know that there are many people haven’t had enough time to decide the custom options they want or come up with their custom marking before the bespoke edition sale was over, so we decide to open a raffle for 100 units of Promise87 Bespoke Edition next week.

Bespoke Edition Raffle Details
QTY: 100 units

Raffle form Link: https://forms.gle/Saizy2gR4QQU8eKM6

Raffle Response Open Time: 8 AM, Monday, PST, August 29

Raffle Response Close Time: 8 AM, Tuesday, PST, August 30

The Raffle form will be open during the time mentioned above, you can save the link beforehand.

Winner announcement Time: 3 business days after raffle closed and winners will be able to purchase from Wuquestudio.com.

    Hi Everyone,

    We are glad to share Promise87 GB is live this week! Based on our IC feedback we’ve been able to finalize the final designs and prices.

    GB Time:August 18th, 2022 - September 6th, 2022

    Promise87 Standard Edition Estimated Delivery: Start packing and sending to Wuque Studio direct customers and vendors before the end of Q1, 2023.

    Promise87 Bespoke Edition Estimated Delivery:     Start packing and sending to Wuque Studio direct customers before January 2023. They will be sent out after each one is done, so we will keep sending them out one by one during this time.

    Promise87 Highlights
    • Standard editions with 9 case colors and Aluminum/SS external weight
    • Bespoke Editions with premium customization options
    • Default SS internal weight
    • New dampener material and mounting design for more comfortable typing feel

    Promise87 Prototypes Photos - 7 Case Colors











    Promise87 Standard Editions
    • WKL Renders, All PVD Lines with Moissanite
    • Updated WK Renders: Cluster Accent Lines Moissanite
    • TWO Promise87 External Weight Laser Marking Options-With default laser marking or No Marking
    Promise87 Prices
    Promise87 Standard Edition Variations and Prices
    Promise87 Bespoke Price List     80USD more based on different standard edition variations (SS or Alu outer weight, with or without PVD lines, Laser marking).

    Brief info
    Typing angle: 5 degrees.
    Front Height: ~18.69mm
    Plate: Flexcut polycarbonate.
    Case Material: 6063 grade Aluminum top and bottom cases, SS internal weight, aluminum external weight or SS external weight
    Mounting Method: Slip on gasket mount
    Full aluminum top case, alu external weight: 1.9 kg
    Aluminum top case, SS external weight version, unassembled: 3.2 kg

    Standard Edition Contents
    1x WKL or WK top case and bottom case (Aluminum)
    1x Mirror PVD Stainless Steel or Sandblasted Anodized Aluminum external weight
    1x P1 PCB for the WKL version or 1x P2 PCB for the WK version
    1x Ai03 unified daughter board
    1x Internal stainless steel weight
    1x PC plate
    1x Poron plate foam
    1x Poron switch foam
    1x Plate and switch integrated foam
    1x Poron PCB foam
    1x Antistatic paper
    1x WS Clear Stabilizers 5x2u+1x7u
    1x Gaskets slip pack (Including 3 colors: blue, white, red)
    Screws and rubber feet

    PCB Specifications
    P1 PCB for WKL Version (Default)
    Wired, hotswap, supports QMK/VIA, split left/right shift, split backspace, stepped caps lock,1.2mm thick, flex cuts.

    P2 PCB for WK Version (Default)
    Wired, hotswap, supports QMK/VIA, split left/right shift, split backspace, stepped caps lock,1.2mm thick, flex cuts.
     For other PCBs please check the Promise87 add-on page here.

    Poron Dampener Kit
    The kit includes 3 pieces of poron dampeners below:
    -Plate Dampener between PCB and plate
    -Switch Dampener between plate and switches
    -PCB Dampener under PCB
    We suggest using poron dampeners with PC plate (Default) for best results.

    Plate & Switch Integrated Foam
    Made of supercritical foaming material, it will make the overall dampening system more stable and helps to make typing sound better.
    We suggest using integrated foam with Aluminum plate (Add-ons) for best results

    Gasket Slips
    Gasket slip design, easier to install
    Strong stability, very durable and can keep original flex after long-time usage
    Density between Poron and silicone.
    There are three kinds of gasket slips by default, they have different stiffness and users can choose anyone they like. Wuque studio will keep stocking the gasket slips and also welcome other board designers to use them.
