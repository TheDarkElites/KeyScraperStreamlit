Chapter 1

Rooke Design







Chapter 1 was designed to honour the material used, by making these materials visible in a sophisticated and striking way.




0. Contents

1. Details
2. Status
3. Editions, Photos and Renders

1. Details



- 65%
- Isolation gasket mount with leaf spring plate
- 6.5° typing angle
- 18mm front height (excluding front chamfer)
- Footprint = 340mm wide x 136.5mm deep
- Weight is between 1.8kg and 2.5kg depending on configuration
- Arrow cluster
- EC11 Rotary encoder
- ANSI and ISO plate options (6.25u only)
- Solder and Hotswap PCBs
- USB-C daughter board
- Aluminium and Polycarbonate Case Options
- Various Weight Options
- Custom Knob
- Expected Q4 Production Completion, with Q1 2023 Delivery

What's included?

- Top, bottom and weight
- PC plate as standard (ANSI/ISO)
- Knob matching chosen edition
- Hotswap/solder PCB
- Daughterboard and encoder
- Necessary screws
- Gaskets, feet and silicon plate alignment pins
- Hard carry case
- Chapter 1 comic

7. FAQ

Q: What layouts are available?
A: Only 6.25u bottom row. Normal and Split backspace. Stepped and normal Caps. ANSI and ISO

Q: What about hotswap PCB?
A: Exactly the same! Some switches on the hotswap PCB will be north facing to allow for extra layout options.

Q: Will there be a PC bottom?
A: no.

Q: Will there be RGB?
A: No

Q: Does a Rama knob fit on Chapter 1?
A: Yes! The encoder is slightly shorter than standard EC11, but the Rama knob still works perfectly.

Q: Lead time?
A: Estimated delivery of Q1 2023

It is difficult right now because everyone is used to pre covid pricing.
There are a few reasons for the pricing:

1. Since I started working on this board and posting the first IC, the price of material has almost doubled. PCB costs have increased significantly, and so have shipping prices.
2. There are multiple parts included with this board, with multiple options for each part - all parts are custom. This also includes the comic and hard carry case.
3. Most importantly, this design utilizes thick material to get the desired aesthetic.

It may be slightly more expensive that what you are used to, but it is worth every cent.
