daji Seis Cero




Details
Kits:
$460 - Standard (Wired)
or
$485 - Bluetooth (Wireless/Wired)

Specifications:
6063 Case
WKL, WK, or HHKB Tops
Coral, Violet, Denim or Charcoal
9 degrees
17.6mm Front Height (22.9mm EKH)
Plateless w/ Geonworks Tadpole Mount (80a Duro)
Seamless Front and Sides
Internal Case Screws
Stainless Steel Weight w/ Battery Compartment
Silver aluminum badge/daughterboard housing
FR4 Plate
Otus Engineering Custom 1.6mm Motherboard (Main) PCB (Matte Black)
Standard - Otus  M.2 Processor (Controller) Unit (RP2040 w/ Vial)
Bluetooth - Otus  M.2 Processor (Controller) Unit (NRF52840 w/ ZMK)
Bluetooth Kit also includes On/Off switch (requires thruhole soldering)
Bluetooth Kit does NOT include battery (See FAQs for battery details)
Otus Daughterboard
JST Cable
Thockeys Keyboard Bag
Extra Hardware
2lbs 13 oz unbuilt (no PCB or plate)
3lbs 7-11oz built (depending on configuration)

Extras:
$45.00 - Aluminum Plate (Silver Anodized)
$45.00 - PP Plate
$25.00 - FR4 Plate
$55.00 - Motherboard (Main) PCB
$15.00 - Wired Processor (Controller)
$40.00 - Wireless Processor (Controller)
$6.00 - Daughterboard & JST








Pre-production PCB


Denim
