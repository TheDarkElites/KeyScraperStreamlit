TX CP - Compact 1800 Full Alu Board
- Full aluminum case, weighs about 3.5Kg
- Angled Case
- 3mm thick stainless steel weight at the bottom

PCB
- Designed by Leeku
- Fully programmable PCB
- Full LED backlighting
- Bottom RGB Led
- Presoldered controller parts, diodes, resistors and rgb leds 

Colors: black, grey, silver, red, blue, purple, pink, gold
More



Photo:

More






Price:
- top case $60
- bottom case $170
- PCB $60
- plate (alu or ss) $30
- plate (brass) $50 (limited to 5pcs)

Closing date: 7th August 2016

Shipping
- Shipping will be directely from South Korea with EMS. 
- Shipping cost for Case + PCB + Plate will be $75 to North America, $65 to Europe and $48 to Asia.

Delivery: Mid September 2016
