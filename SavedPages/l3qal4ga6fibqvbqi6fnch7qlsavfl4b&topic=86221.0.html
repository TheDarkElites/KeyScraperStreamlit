  • Full Kobelco 5052 aluminum case
  • 8 degrees slope

  • Plate material: anodized aluminum (grey/silver)
  • ISO and ANSI
  • Custom ps2avrGB PCB
  • Fully Programmable
  • Full LED backlighting

Black, Silver, Grey, Pink, Violet and Sky Blue
