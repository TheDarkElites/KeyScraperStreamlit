size=36pt]Check your order: https://goo.gl/HXWieT


“Roses are red, Violets are blue, Everest is white, And so are you (DC60).”
Hello friends on GeekHack,
I am super excited to present our latest product: DC60 made by ALF Studio. This is the first production run of the DC60 on GH, and we are providing a large discount by pricing the products at marginal cost! Hope you will enjoy it.
Firstly, some photos of the actual sample products:









Specifications:

1. Made by 6 series aluminum.

2. Tray mount design, compatible with other GH60 PCBs.(same screw position)

3. The slope is 7 degrees.

4. It has reserved battery slot for Bluetooth PCB.(deepness is 4mm)

5. The weight is 1.2kg each case alone.

6. Each case will come with special designed box, top-quality screws, and rubber feet.


Colors:

We are providing 10 colors for your choices

The index number from 1 to 4: Black, Grey(greenish), Blue, Red
                                   5 to 9: Pink, Light Blue, Purple, Orange and Silver
For all the Prototye Photos: https://imgur.com/a/YN1AVp7

Additional options:
ALF DC60 PCB: it will be matte black color with gold logo and mini USB connector. QMK Firmware(programmable).


Plate: CNC Aluminium (9 colors from index 1 to index 9)
This PCB can support most of layouts, for example, the following three popular layouts are supported:
1. Lshift is 2u size and Rshift is 1u size
2. Lshift is 2.25u size and Rshift is 1.75u size
3. Standard ANSI 60%
More






Group Buy Time Window：
14th/July/2018 to 28th/July/2018

Prices：
Case: 80USD for regular colors, 120USD for the Everest White
Plate 18USD
PCB  35USD

Shipping
After Group Buy ends, it needs 4-5 weeks to finish all the production.(around end of Aug can ship out)
For the shipping courier, it will be SF express.(worldwide 3-6 days), roughly, the cost estimates for each DC60 set are below.
To USA, $30
To Canada, $55
To UK, $38
To AU, $47
To South Korea, $17
For more countries, please check: http://www.sf-express.com/sg/en/dynamic_function/price/

Set original place as : China-Anhui-Xiuzhou-DangShan
Set destination is your country name and key in weight as 2kg. Check the parcel price (not document).
Also, please note that all the prices are not including a 5% fee, including PayPal, currency exchange fees, etc.
