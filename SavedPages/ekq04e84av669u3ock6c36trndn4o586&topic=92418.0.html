[GB] Canoe by Percent Studios - R1 + R2 Fin.
« on: Sat, 04 November 2017, 10:41:11 »
New PCBs!

https://www.reddit.com/r/mechmarket/comments/gxtgio/gb_mechlovin_kanu_canoe_v1_compatible_pcb_plates/

For future reference:

R1: $385 / kit + fees + shipping, pcb+plate: $90 + fees + shipping.
R2: $404 / kit + shipping , pcb: $41 / pcs + shipping.

More
Hi all,

For people who already have a Canoe and want extra PCB + Plate combo (Price is in SGD):
https://monokei.us/products/plate-pcb-set-for-canoe-65-keyboard

This is the intl GB thread for the Canoe 65% keyboard designed by Percent Studios, makers of the Skog TKL as well.
This GB will be done via a raffle system, monokei (which is me i guess) will be handling the buy together with kingnestea, courtesy of Percent   





Included in the set:

Case (Top, bottom, plate, weight)
PCB

Info:

GB limited to 14 units of Ceramic White, 45 units of a mix of silver/black for a total of 59 units
Plate supports ISO
Additional PCB + plate combo available (cyan plate)

Colours:
White: Ceramic white top and bottom, red plate, red weight (done via MAO similar to Skog)
Pic of Skog below for colour reference:

Silver: Silver top, gray bottom, cyan plate, cyan weight

Black: Black top, black bottom, gray plate, gray weight


Time:
GB starts 12pm on 05 November 2017 (SGT, GMT+8) for 24 hours.
Countdown timer: https://www.timeanddate.com/countdown/generic?iso=20171105T12&p0=236&msg=Canoe+GB&font=sanserif&csz=1
Deadline for raffle entry is 12pm on 06 November 2017 (SGT, GMT+8).
Raffle winners will be announced within 24 hours in this thread, via email and of course via the appearance of the PP invoice.
Deadline for payment is 48 hours after invoices have been sent. Please request for extension if needed.

Price:
$385 for one set (case, weight, plate, PCB)
$90 for extra PCB + Plate combo (plate will be cyan in colour)

Prices exclusive of PP fees.

Shipping:

Shipping will be from Singapore via Singpost or UPS depending on locale.
Shipping invoice will be sent separately once we receive the sets.

PCB + plate for existing Canoe users:

Combo will be $93 inclusive of fees.
Shipping will be invoiced later on separately once we receive the sets.
If you're just getting the PCB + plate combo, you can purchase it via: https://monokei.us/products/plate-pcb-set-for-canoe-65-keyboard


Production:
Lead time is estimated to be 3 months, updates will be done via this thread and the email list.

Misc:

No extras for sale after the GB has ended.
Invoices will be sent via PayPal, PayPal fees are separate.
For any changes to your order, please send an email when necessary.
Please include your order number when sending an email for changes.
Support email: monokei.us at gmail dot com

Order check (To be updated): https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/17k0Jr8-Ulof6gfSsinOUegQC28D4ON9ZxYCG-n7ANHM/edit#gid=0
