« on: Tue, 26 January 2021, 00:35:36 »
Thank you for waiting patiently during our Interest Check. We appreciate the valuable feedback and advice provided by the community to help us improve our product. The physical proofing is complete and we are now ready to launch the Group Buy for the Atom 68 II.
The Atom68 II is a custom case for the NiZ Atom68 electrostatic capacitive keyboard. It is the second iteration of the Atom68 Alu (GB launched this time last year), with an improved design as well as additional case materials, colors, dome, weights,battery, and stabilizer options
Group Buy Information
1 Launch Date：2021.01.29(Beijing time 8:00)
2 Group Buy duration：the group buy will run for 10 days or until orders are placed for 200 units
3 Shipping estimate：within 3 months after the close of the GB
4 Group Buy link:https://www.nizkeyboard.com/collections/atom68-v2/products/ic-niz-atom68-alu-v2?variant=37998184038574

  7 Plate mounting system: gasket sandwich

*Basic kit includes: rubber feet, type-C daughterboard, cooper bases, rubber gasket, and screws

(1) If you already have a NiZ Atom68 keyboard and want to upgrade to the aluminum version, you can buy the basic kit and replace the case yourself.
(2) If you don't have a Niz Atom68 keyboard, you need to buy the inner parts separately (choice of: wired, Bluetooth, or Bluetooth & RGB version) in addition to the basic kit.

O Wired version includes: electrostatic capacitive PCB, dome sheet (35g/45g/60g/65g options), electrostatic capacitive switches, o-ring,spring,and stabilizers (choice of Costar or Cherry-style plate-mount stablizers)
O Bluetooth version includes: electrostatic capacitive PCB, dome sheet (35g/45g/60g/65g options), electrostatic capacitive switches,o-ring, spring,and stabilizers (choice of Costar or Cherry-style plate-mount stablizers), 5000 mA battery
O Bluetooth & RGB version includes: electrostatic capacitive PCB, dome sheet (35g/45g/60g/65g options), electrostatic capacitive switches, o-ring,spring,and stabilizers (choice of Costar or Cherry-style plate-mount stablizers), 5000 mA battery

Note: If Bluetooth or Bluetooth & RGB version is chosen, you must also select either a polycarbonate bottom case or FR4 plate, otherwise Bluetooth will not function properly

I received it (5/3/2021) in Tokyo, Japan.
I bought a PC case, FR4 plate, 35g dome, RGB Bluetooth PCB.

Here, I wrote down my thoughts on the typing feel or design and some cautions on build

Thoughts:
Exterior had almost no blemishes, little blemishes that can be seen on very closer observation. Overall, strictly controlled quality, Nice! The typing feeling is very NIZ-way. The comfort feeling of Niz is focused on my build. Comfortably warm, I would say.

More
Caution 1: The latch on the switch prevents the slider from rising.
you have to check each switch before you close your interior.
https://i.imgur.com/5K8Lv2a.jpg
https://i.imgur.com/Qv3hiOg.jpg

Caution 2: The PC button must be far from the actual button when you assemble the keyboard, or the PC button will press the button endlessly
I didn't have a pic of this. But this caution was the same as the previous Atom V1 GB. So I will link the previous pic
https://i.imgur.com/2F6JKfl.jpg
https://i.imgur.com/outzcqY.jpg
When the button is pressed, one of the buttons will solely become bright.
I lubricated this.
When I try to move, the friction is too big, so this keeps pressing the button without coming back when I press it once.

Caution 3: The battery cable is hard to be pulled out.
It does not cause any problem, but you have to have caution. But it is TIGHT!
https://imgur.com/XVrEARl

Tip 1: The gasket rubber can be seen on the top PC case, so if you have a white sticker or so, you can change its looks.
https://imgur.com/1HSEMuy

One possible flaw is the red PCB can be seen on the bottom.
