Quick summary about SpaccBoard
  • Compatibility. Universal 60% PCB. Fits in most 60% cases (TOFU, Klippe T, Fjell, Bakeneko, Salvation...) and in some 60% keyboards (HEX.3C, Prophet, Thermal, S58m, etc.) Supports ai03's Unified Daughterboard.
  • Programmability. QMK & VIA supported. Already merged into QMK and VIA masters. Preflashed with VIA firmware.
  • Appearance. SpaccBoard has nice silkscreen and awesome routing (with filleted traces!). Check photos.
  • Layouts. Wide layouts support. Fits BAE, ANSI and ISO, as well as stepped mods from NMB RT101+. Check this:
More
  • Assembly. All SpaccBoards come pre-assembled. All you need to solder is switches. Moreover, each PCB is flashed with VIA and manually tested by me.
  • Price. 40 USD.

Below is December 2021 GB info. Kept only for archieve reasons. Plz don't read.

SpaccBoard plates
Space Invaders require a plate with special, unique cutouts. We offer 2 plate types on this GB:

1. FR4, 1.2 mm, for standard traymount or Unikorn-style mount - 15 USD
These are available in 2 layouts:
  • BAE. Big Ass Enter, split backspace, full left shift
  • ISO. ISO enter, split or full backspace, split or full left shift
And in 2 colors:
  • Black
  • Red

2. Carbon fiber (CF), 1.2 mm, any layout and mounting style you want - 40 USD
To have your CF plate made, please PM me with details about your preferred layout and mount. If the mount is not standard traymount, you'll be asked to provide the original plate drawing.

If you don't want to order the plates on this GB, you can make them yourself. All source files are published on my GitHub.

GB details
  • GB dates: December 1 - December 15, 2021.
  • GB lead time: 2-3 months.
  • Price: 40 USD per PCB, 15 USD per FR4 plate, 40 USD per CF plate.
  • Registration method: Google form.
  • Payment method: PayPal invoice.
  • Shipping payment: charged separately, right before the shipping. According on the recent GA15.0 GB (was shipped 3 weeks ago), the shipping costs will be 7 - 18 USD depending on your order weight and on your location.

If you are interested, please sign up using this GB form.
