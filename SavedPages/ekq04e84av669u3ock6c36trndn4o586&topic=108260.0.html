The long awaited ePBT Blank Pink group buy is now live! The set will be available for 2 weeks and can be purchased through 4 regional vendors. There will be a limited 30 blank pink cable FCFS sale live now!

Buy it now from your regional vendor!
  • CAN/US ApexKeyboards
  • EU Mykeyboard
  • AU/OCE DailyClack
  • ASIA/CHINA KBDFans

Full Album







Timeline
  • Group Buy: Aug 21st to September 3rd

I'd also like to update the thread that all sets from Mykeyboard, Dailyclack and KBDFans should be shipping out or already shipped. All NA Sets (From ApexKeyboards) will be going through a round of QC where I will be repositioning the spacebars so the tab on the ePBT box will not be near the stem to avoid any impact during delivery. This will take a couple of days to complete as I might be rearranging each set by hand (330 sets).

Please contact the vendor where you purchased the set from to see what they can do for you. If we receive enough responses about this issue I might be able to ask ePBT to send us replacements on a case by case basis.
