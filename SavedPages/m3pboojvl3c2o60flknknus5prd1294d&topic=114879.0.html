Carbon Blue - Handmade Premium Keyboard Bags
and more...

Open for orders until August 24th 2024



Welcome! We proudly present to you our Carbon Blue Keyboard Bag.
The Carbon Blue is crafted from a durable and vibrant blue synthetic leather in carbon look. This high-quality material combines the sleek, stylish appearance of real leather with enhanced resilience and sustainability.
Inside, the bag is lined with a sturdy cotton canvas, adding a vibrant contrast and extra durability. Our bags are fully padded for superior protection and comfort, ensuring your precious peripherals stay safe and secure. Additionally, they feature a convenient outer pocket (Zipper bag)/second inner compartment (Flap bag), perfect for easy access to your essentials like cables, tools, etc.

Tailored like a suit - for storage and transport
Our bags are available in custom sizes to accommodate different keyboard dimensions (or other devices), ensuring a perfect fit for your specific needs.





More pictures: https://imgur.com/a/classic-carbon-blue-3laRAnO

Choose between two styles
Zipper or flap design? You decide.



Every bag is unique - just like you
Make your bag even more unique with an optional custom label.
