- Weight: ~3300g (assembled)
- Medium TX style bag (Merisi.Works branded zipper pullers and microfiber cloth)

Sorry for responding to all of this in one post.

I agree that a daughterboard system allows for a more "clean" look for the rear of the board. It does, however, add more layers of complexity to the entire GB and also adds more restrictions to people who would like to tinker with different PCBs and builds like Ensaum said. The Hiney h87c PCB is a very common PCB and a lot of people already have these laying around.

With regards to the non ISO comment from Handke, I am very sorry for not offering that layout option and I completely understand why one would ask for it in this specific Group Buy.
I don't want to type up an essay with reasons and excuses for not including ISO cutouts in my plate. Just know that the h87c PCB is ISO compatible and the plate mounting style is the same as the Jane V2, Whale, Koala, etc, so it will be fairly easy to source ISO plates if need be.
It is very unfortunate that ISO users are oftentimes not taken into consideration for keysets and plate layouts but I don't wish to make an inferior product for everyone when only a small fraction of potential buyers would make use of ISO.
