Join65% - Easy to build, affordable 65% GB live until 27th May

Thocc Supply is delighted to bring Join65 by KnifeLab to the GB phase! Join65 is a budget-friendly 65% featuring a fully CNCd case with a plate-gasket-mount to make building this a breeze. Also, KinfeLab has implemented interchangeable side pieces to allow further colour customizations


Changes since IC based on community feedback
Removing PCB flex cuts on the PCB for the production run
Update back weight engraving style
Remove Purple & Black, Teal Blue & E-White colour
Added Panda, Frost

Spec TLDR:
  • CNC’d aluminium case
  • PVD stainless steel weights and badges
  • Plate foam gasket mount
  • 7-degree typing angle
  • front height ~20mm
  • Hot-swap 1.2mm PCB (VIA ready, no flex cuts)
  • Size 324mm x 135.5mm x 33.4 (thickest) mm
  • 1.15~1.2kg net or ~1.38kg built
  • Custom moulded carry case

Price ~ $155 to $165 USD (your local vendor pricing may vary slightly)
GB Date: 6th May ~ 27th May (local timezone)
Fulfilment ~ End of Q3 2023
