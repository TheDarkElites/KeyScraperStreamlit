ORDER HERE  <---- Click here to order


Included in this set:
  • Aluminium top housing (Aluminum 6061) in black or silver
  • Bottom housing (11 degree angle)
  • MX Reinforcement Plate (stainless steel, aluminum or plastic)
  • PCB presoldered (resistors, diodes, smd leds, etc, but not switches)
  • Fixing bolts and rubber pads

Info:
  • Limit of 100 sets
  • Limit of 1 set per person
  • Case has an angle of 11 degrees for the aluminum bottom
  • Full keymapping
  • Macro settings
  • Supports MX switches
  • Full LED and RGB SMD LED compatible
  • Switches and stabilizers are not included (Requires Cherry PCB mounted stabilizers)
  • Supports centred caps lock or stepped caps lock

  • I will send invoices through Paypal to everyone, so you need a verified PayPal account to receive and pay. Also please check the information carefully before submitting your order. There will be Paypal fees added to the price. See below for PayPal gift payments.
  • I can only guarantee damages for 7 days from the day you receive the items.

FAQ:

Q: I'm not on the list, did I make it?
A: If you don't see yourself on the list, unfortunately you did not make it.

Q: Will you be running a R4 for this GB?
A: Most likely not and we will be limited to 100 sets.

Q: Why was my position changed?
A: I had to shift orders around to group the shipping costs.

Q: What happens if I'm 101, 102...etc.
A: If someone cancels their order, you will be bumped up.

Q: Can I change my order once the invoice has been paid?
A: Nope, no more changes.

Q: Can I gift you the payment via PayPal to save money?
A: Please send me an email with "PayPal gift" in the subject line and we can arrange something. Please include all required information as noted above to verify your identity.

Q: Elton, please let me in!

Q: Can I just buy a plate or top separately?
A: Nope, you'll either need to buy a kit or set. Pieces cannot be purchased individually.

Q: Can I just buy the plate/PCB separately?
A: Nope, you'll need to be a part of the GB.
