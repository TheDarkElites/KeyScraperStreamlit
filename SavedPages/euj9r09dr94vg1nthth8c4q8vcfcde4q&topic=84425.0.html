[GB] RAMA M4-A [SHIPPED]
« on: Wed, 31 August 2016, 00:44:52 »
Hi all, back again with my latest (and smallest!) keyboard! The M4-A!

Comprised of all through-hole components, the M4-A is designed to be a simple and easy to assemble DIY project. It features an aluminium main unibody (in clear or Space Grey anodized) and a yellow Nylon base!

Priced at $59 USD + shipping.

Minimum order quantity: 100

More info: http://rama.works/#/m4a/

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------


Product Name:


RAMA M4-A


Part Number:


RAMA-M4-A-01


-------------------------------------------------------------------------------


Product Description:


An aluminium CNC machined mini 4-key keyboard with a milled Nylon base. Completely programmable through the ATTINY85.


Sold as a DIY project which you must assemble and solder yourself.


-------------------------------------------------------------------------------


Product Color:
Space Grey Anodized Aluminium
OR
Clear Anodized


Finish:
Lightly Bead Blasted + Anodized


-------------------------------------------------------------------------------


Contents:


1 x Top Enclosure (Aluminium)


1 x Base Enclosure (Nylon)


4 x SS M3 Fixtures


1 x Through Hole Mini USB Connector


1 x ATTINY85 Socket


1 x ATTINY85


2 x Zener Diodes


2 x 68 Ohm resistors


1 x 1k5 Ohm resistor


4 x 10k Ohm resistors


NOTE: You will have to supply your own Switches

UPDATE: PCB is being updated by Machine Industries to include a beefier microcontroller which will make it much easier to program, it MAY also come completely presoldered (resistors and all) and have LED support (TBC)
