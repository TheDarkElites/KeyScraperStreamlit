The Ripple TKL

Click this title to see our coming soon page




I know many people have been waiting for details on the Ripple group buy announcement, and we're finally ready to share!
Thank you to everyone who shared their thoughts on our IC - we've listened to your feedback and now every Ripple will have a 3D weight.

For those unfamiliar with the design, you can check out our Coming Soon Page as well as the previously posted Interest Check

Inspiration:

I found the concept of "Ripple" for this keyboard while in the design process. For some boards, I start with a concept and design from there. For others - like this one - I played around a bit with drawings and design elements I could use before settling on the concept of a Ripple - the movement water makes when something is dropped in it. From there, designing the rest of the keyboard fell into place.

The contours of the top case and weight are meant to mimic water flowing, a very wavy look. The stepped corner cuts are meant to resemble expanding concentric circles like those found in a ripple, and the side cuts match the wavy-ness of the rest of the top case.
And finally - the weight (which is super cool) shows a ripple effect.

Mounting:

The plates have tabs to either stick poron to, or put o-rings around, and the top case has pockets where you can add Silicone, PE foam, EVA Foam, or Poron.
So there are 8 different combinations you can make - not to mention countless other "hybrid" combinations. This leads to a highly customizable sound for the keyboard.
You can check out some audio-only sound tests I made here on YouTube

Editions:

We'll be offering 3 different editions of the Ripple TKL at 3 different price points. Every single version has a 3D ripple weight!

Standard Edition:
Sandblasted Brass 3D Ripple Weight (Gold Color)
Sandblasted Brass Badge - 3D ripple badge
Your choice of WK or WKL top
Your choice of Solderable or Hotswap PCB ($10 upcharge)
Your choice of FR4 or Aluminum Universal Plate ($20 upcharge)
Case colors: Silver, Navy, Blue-Grey, E-White, Black

This version will cost $430


Special Edition:
Glossy PVD Brass 3D Ripple Weight
Glossy PVD Brass Badges - both 3D ripple badge and logo badge
Your choice of WK or WKL top
Your choice of Solderable or Hotswap PCB ($10 upcharge)
Aluminum Universal Plate (no upcharge)
Case colors are the same as the standard edition
PVD weights will be available in Gold or Silver
 
This version will cost $550 and will be limited to 40 units.


Founders Edition:
Black Glossy PVD Brass 3D Ripple Weight
Black Glossy PVD Brass Badges - both 3D ripple badge and logo badge
Your choice of WK or WKL top
Your choice of Solderable or Hotswap PCB ($10 upcharge)
Aluminum Universal Plate (no upcharge)
Blackberry Case Color

More

This version will cost $650 and be highly limited - only 5 units will be made available for public purchase.

PE, EVA, Poron, and Silicone top modifiers
Poron and O-ring plate tab modifiers
Daughterboard + JST Cable
4"x6" Authenticity Card
Custom Silicone Feet (bumpon compatible)
Screw Hardware and Hex Keys to use on the Screw Hardware

Renders of all available Ripple editions are located in this album
