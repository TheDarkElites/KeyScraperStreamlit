SONIC170


Hi y’all, it’s me.

Here I bring you the SONIC170, inspired by the TE OP-1 Field synthesizer.

It’s a 65% with a knob and a customizable LED macro module.

Following the feedbacks received in IC, we've removed the crucifix-looking design due to religious concerns.


INFO

Layout: WK & WKL
Mounting: Silicone "Cover" Gasket with leaf spring
Typing Angle: 7 degrees
Front Height: 18.7mm
PCB: Hotswap or Solder / QMK, Via, and Vial support
Colors: 10 Colors
Weight: PVD Black SS or PVD Silver SS
Price: $389

What's included:


Kit content:
Aluminum Top Case
Aluminum Middle Case
Aluminum Bottom Case
Stainless Steel Weight
Plate (Total of 3 plates - 1 main and 2 macro plates)
PCB (Total of 3 PCBs - 1 main and 2 macro PCBs)
Silicone Gasket Strips
Daughterboard and JST Cable
Foam Pack
Screws
10x 4u Light Module Cover
Rotary Module and Knob

Materials:
Top, Mid, Bottom Case: 6063 Aluminum

2) We made the gasket covers using silicone. Compared to poron gaskets, these are definitely less cushiony. The silicone gaskets provide isolation from the case and a soft typing experience while preventing the board from having the typical "poron gasket sound".
