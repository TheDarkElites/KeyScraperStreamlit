[GB] MARS 8.0 (sorting and shipping)
« on: Thu, 25 January 2018, 21:51:19 »
















IC: https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=93256.0  There were ups and downs but we are finally here.

The prototype looks even better than I expected.

Some details:

  • 8 colors are available.
  • Top case and bottom case can be in different colors
  • Around 8 degrees slope
  • Supported layouts on PCB: split BS, split right shift, split left shift, ISO and ANSI enter
  • Supported layouts on top case: 87 keys TKL, WKL with regular blockers (normally 84 keys) , small blockers (normally 86 keys)

Price:

  • All aluminum kit: $280
  • Upgrade to brass front accessory: +$15
  • Upgrade to brass plate: +$25
  • Upgrade to brass weight: +40


Other costs:

  • Shipping to asian countries $40, otherwise $50
  • 4.4% PayPal fee

Timeline:

  • GB ends on Feb. 10th (No MOQ, No limits)
  • Expected shipping date: before mid April

Place your order HERE

Important update:

Just got a quote for the diffuser which turns out to be very cheap.

So the decision is diffusers will be included for everyone who joins for free.

Check the render with diffuser: https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=93723.msg2554933#msg2554933


Feb 10: Order form has been closed. Manufacturing will be started after CNY. I'll also update you some photos for diffusers and keyboard with keycaps later.
