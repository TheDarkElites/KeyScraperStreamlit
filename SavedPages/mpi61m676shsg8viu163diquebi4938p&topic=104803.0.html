"Cosmos Black" Marble Wrist Rest for GMK Apollo

After a first collaboration with GMK Coral, it is now with GMK Apollo  !



Current Status (06/07) :
I received the pieces from the manufacturer and I am satisfied with the quality.
Some pieces were QCed out, but no impact on the ordered pieces.
Packaging is on-going and shipping date is mid-June

Now, place to the questions :


Why this collaboration ?

First time I saw the renders of GMK Apollo, I realized it was fitting perfectly with a photo I took long ago :



Those are a Lunar artisan made by Dwarf Factory and one of my black marble wrist rest.
Some of my black marbles really look like a window on the infinite void of space.
That is why we decided to collaborate with GMK Apollo to bring a great match of wrist rest.



What is the color this time ?

"Cosmos Black" !
I filtered all the black marbles to keep only the darkest black with only a few white inclusions.
This is to have marble wrist rests which look like "void" or dark with white stars.

You can judge directly with the photos at the bottom



How many pieces and sizes will be available ?

  • 60%: 40 pieces are already in my inventory and will be available on 25th Feb. They can be shipped within 1 to 2 weeks.
  • 65/75%: GB starting on 25th Feb, limit is 30 pieces max. Product will be shipped 4 months after the GB end.
  • TKL: GB starting on 25th Feb, limit is 30 pieces max. Same as for 65% above
  • Split,40%,..: if there are several demands, that can be a possibility 

Note: Design will be a bit different between the 60% and others.
The 60% has a flat design with 2 large rounded corners, while other sizes will have an angled design with smaller rounded corners.

Quick Renders for the 65% and TKL sizes wrist rests :


And for the dimensions of the slope:


Size :

  • 60%: 30cm long x 8cm large x 1.6cm thick, (11.8inch x 3.15 inch x 0.63 inch.)
  • 65%: 31.5cm long x 8cm large x 1.6cm thick, (12.4inch x 3.15 inch x 0.63 inch.), also fit 75% keyboards.
  • TKL: 36cm long x 8cm large x 1.6cm thick, (14.2inch x 3.15 inch x 0.63 inch.),

Price ?
  • 60%: 60USD + shipping from TW (22 USD to USA)
  • 65/75%: 65USD + shipping from TW (22 USD to USA)
  • TKL: 68USD + shipping from TW (22 USD to USA)
  • Split,40%,..: TBD, if it happens

Where to pass orders ?

It is open now, purchase and pre-order can be done on my website : https://marblek.com/product/cosmos-black/
