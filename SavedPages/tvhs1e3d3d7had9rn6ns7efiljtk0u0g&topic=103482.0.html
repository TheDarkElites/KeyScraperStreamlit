Hello guys, I am Xikii and sorry to be late for so long to start 1800MINI GB. I have introduced this keyboard several months ago on reddit and geekhack. 1800MINI GB have been run twice before in China, so maybe some people have heard of this keyboard and this is the first round GB outside China.

IC Post:https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=100827.50
GB Link:https://xikii.com/
















About Layout:
The layout is streamlined from the classical CHERRY G80-1800, as an extension of 40% keyboard. It is similar to 60% keyboard in size but has a  numpad.

The sunken design makes the hot-swap switches more stable, and increaces the hot-swap sockets' lifespan.The redesigned integrated plate covers the four corners of switches including the below both bevel sides. Such a masterly design costs a lot of processing time.


Functional Feature:
  • Arbitrary programming by TMK, up to 8 layers, no matter wired or wireless.
  • USB full-key rollover. Full RGB, more than 20 kinds of light effects.
  • Dual connectivity: USB type-c wired, or bluetooth 4.0 for wireless.
  • Rechargeable Battery: 3800 mAh Lithium battery.

Specs:
  • Make with 6063 aluminum alloys & acrylic.
  • Fully RGB, USB Type-C & Bluetooth 4.0.
  • Fully programmable with ydkb.io(TMK).
  • Pre-assembled(exclude switch and keycaps).

Colors Option:
  • E-white
  • E-yellow
  • Silver
  • Gray
  • Black
  • Red
  • Dark blue

GB Info:
  • Timeline: Nov 14th to Dec 15th.
  • Price: $380 + shipping.
  • Estimated delivery time: March 15, 2020

Packing List:
  • Switch Puller *1
  • Pre-assembled 1800mini *1（exclude switch and keycaps, include battery and pre-installed plate stabs.）
  • USB Type-C data cable*1

Optional(soon):
  • We will offer arc xda profile pbt key sets as shown in the picture.
  • Resin convex SA 2.75u spacebars.
  • Resin SA 1.75u enter.

For more updates:
Discord:https://discord.gg/x3GTCeE
Instagram:https://www.instagram.com/xikiimaker/
