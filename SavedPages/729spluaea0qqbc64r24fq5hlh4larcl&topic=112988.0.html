Duo-S by Duo Foundry




Hello mechanical keyboard enthusiasts! 1 IC and 2 prototype streams later, the GB is finally in sight! The GB is by no means the end but the start of a journey with the community and the most exciting part will be to finally have the Duo-S in the hands of all GB participants

IC thread: HERE

Duo-S
Please refer to the IC thread for more details

Case Angle: 7.0°
Materials: Aluminium top, brass acoustic frame, PC bottom
Colours: Anodised black, navy & e-white
Mounting method: Duo isolation mount
Plate: 1.5mm FR4 / sandblasted brass (only available as an extra)
PCB: Hotswap 65% by DZ
Weight: ~1kg (FR4 plate) / ~1.2kg (brass plate) fully built

GB Details

Platform: www.duofoundry.com
GB start date: 30th May, Sunday, 11pm SGT (GMT+8) / 11am EST (GMT-4)
GB end date: When slots are sold out or 5th Jun, Saturday, 11pm SGT (GMT+8) / 11am EST (GMT-4)
Price: SGD$499 / ~USD$385
Availability: 300 units FCFS
Estimated shipping date: Q1 2022
Extras: PCB, sandblasted brass plate

Duo Peg
Please refer to the IC thread for more details



A redesigned switch puller made of 3D printed carbon fiber PETG.

Platform: www.duofoundry.com
In-stock sales: 29th May, Saturday, 11pm SGT (GMT+8) / 11am EST (GMT-4)
Price: SGD$25 / ~USD$20
Availability: 60 units (more to be produced in the future)
Estimated shipping date: Within 2 weeks of order

Build Streams / Sound Tests

Minterly: https://www.twitch.tv/videos/1029799193
Fatboychard: https://www.twitch.tv/videos/1031334637

FR4 Plate vs Brass Plate Comparison (lubed and filmed Lilacs, EPBT keycaps)
