Naptime - Tuck in your artisans
Round 2. Same case, more options, lower price.

17 Jan // $255 AUD // Daily Clack
https://dailyclack.com/products/naptime-r2

Current status: Shipping



Hello again, after way more demand than anticipated for R1, I bring you Naptime R2.

There are no changes to the design, the only thing that will differ from R1 is the availability of an additional colour, a polycarbonate option and a lower price point.

Details

layout: 8x5 slots (40 total)
display angle: 10°
material: aluminium or polycarbonate body, acrylic lid
colours: black, dark grey, frosted polycarbonate
weight (alu): 1600g (~3.3lb)
weight (pc): 660g (~1.45lb)

price: 255 AUD for all options, extra lids for 20 AUD
sale type: unlimited group buy facilitated by Daily Clack (might cap at 100 units but I doubt we'll hit that)
date/duration: 17 Jan - 1 Feb
number of units/spots: unlimited
estimated shipping: Q2 2021

Given the success of round 1 and having a vendor handling the fulfilment, I feel confident in running this round as an unlimited buy.


Badchad Artisan

I'm glad to announce that Badchad has agreed to make some special Naptime x Timtam themed caps for this R2 release.

They'll be raffled to R2 buyers shortly after the GB period and I can't wait to see how they turn out.
