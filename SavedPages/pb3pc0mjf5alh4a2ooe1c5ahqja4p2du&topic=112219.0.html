FAQs (updating)

- where are the rest streams?
Minterly's Sage unit is scheduled at 16th
Apiary's Ruby unit is scheduled at 14th
MechMerlin's DewDrop unit is scheduled at 15th

- why only 900 units?
It's in-stock and we have invested at max budget months ahead
The hype is totally unexpected
We have other projects incoming this year so we can't add more units without causing a delay

- what is electrophoresis?
it's a coloring technique and it's not as rigid as anodization

- how to purchase extra pcb and plate?
you will be able to add them into cart 1 day before the sale from other product pages
if they are sold-out, extras will be removed from the cart
if Jelly is sold out earlier, your extras-only order will be cancelled
we don't provide order alteration after the order is in
i.e. no change, no cancellation, no addition or refund

- difference between hotswap plate and solder plate?
if you have hotswap plate, you are locked into ANSI layout
so even if you purchase a solder PCB, you won't be able to use ISO and tsangan or split b/s, only stepped capslock and ANSI
also, we will only provide standoffs to hotswap keyboards but not extra plates and pcbs
there won't be holes on the solder plate so you won't be able to use those standoffs
hence, don't get hotswap version and solder extra plate if you don't understand the limits here

- switch count?
81 switches are needed for the hotswap version. For solder, up to 85.

- I don't see the page? where to purchase?
the product page goes live at the time of the sale

- estimation of hype? how long will it take to sell out?
we don't know any other than that most people won't be able to get a unit
just be there a few minutes when the sale happens

- what else do I need to do?
check our Acceptable Quality Standards on our website
no returns, if you have faulty parts, we'll send you the replacements of them
