GMK Astral Light




"Astral Light is inspired by the heroes' adventures of fantasy RPG/TTRPG worlds."


For anyone who missed out, I believe that some of the vendors will be picking up extra kits.

Join their newsletter and/or follow them on socials to keep tabs.


All kits will be produced. Numbers thus far (final numbers TBA):

Base: 1052
Extension: 188
Novelties: 421
Spacebars: 339
Deskmat (Light): 175
Deskmat (Dark): 152
 

GB Dates: March 5, 2021 - April 5, 2021
Est. shipping time: Q1 2022 (production lead time ~42 weeks + sample verification)

Vendors
Project Keyboard (NA) https://store.projectkeyboard.com/collections/gmk-astral-light
DailyClack (AUS) https://dailyclack.com/collections/group-buys/products/gmk-astral-light
MyKeyboard (EU) https://mykeyboard.eu/catalogue/category/group-buys/gmk-astral_428/
Deskhero (CA) https://www.deskhero.ca/products/gmk-astral
iLumKB (ASIA) https://ilumkb.com/products/gb-gmk-astral-light

Manufacturer: GMK
Profile: Cherry
Sculpt: 1/1/2/3/4/4
Plastic: ABS
Colors:
Alphas: Custom Pantone C
Modifiers: Custom Pantone C
Accents: Custom Pantone C

Pricing
Base ($129 USD) - Covers most layouts (60%, 65%, 75%, TKL, 96-key, 1800/CP and fullsize)
Extension ($65 USD) - Extended support for UK ISO, 40s, Alice, and Split layouts. F13 and Windowed caps accommodate for aesthetics. Legends on windowed keys are pad printed.
Novelties ($38 USD) - RPG/TTRPG dice and icons.
Spacebars ($35 USD) - Extended spacebar support and accents.

Collaborations

Casper Keys North Star Artisan Cap ($38 USD)
R1 Cherry with an MX stem. Aluminum 6061-T6 bead blasted and anodized.

* North Star artisan group buy is run separately from Astral set vendors.

Available at Casper Keys: https://casperkeys.com/products/gmk-astral-north-star


Miroticaps Andromeda x Astral Light ($65 USD)
R1 Cherry with an MX Stem. Resin Casted.

* Miros Andromeda artisan is available separately from Astral set vendors and will be available on March 26th, 2021.

Available at Miroticaps Discord Server: https://discord.gg/jCJ9RbwqaF
