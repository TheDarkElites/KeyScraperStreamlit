ROUND 1 INFO

More
Hello Geekhackers and Mech friends,

It’s Team Mechlovin’ again, back with another GB. For those who don’t know, we have previously run 3 GBs for TGR compatible PCBs and Plates, and the last one (Hannah 910 Rev.2 and Infinity CE) is going to be shipped within the next few weeks.

It’s a mad world, innit? Given the current situation, it probably isn’t of optimal timing to run a GB now. However, we’ve been sitting on this project for a few months now, and since we’ve had working prototypes in our hands for more than half of that time, we’ve decided to put things in motion, rather than just sit around and wait.

To be completely transparent, there are a few things to consider regarding the impact of COVID-19:

- Lead time is around 6 weeks, instead of our standard 4 weeks. Things still work, albeit at a slower pace.

- We have been working with our trusted proxy to ship to the US, Canada and some European countries, and the rest has been handled by National post. We haven’t encountered a single issue with previous orders. However, at this moment, our proxy only ships to the US. Things may change when the orders are ready to ship in the near future, but if not, shipping to countries other than the US will be handled by National post.

WHAT’S AVAILABLE FOR THIS BUY?

- Adelais Standard PCB - compatible with TGR Alice and all clone variants. Comes with RGB underglow pre-soldered.

- Adelais RGB PCB (or Adelais en Ciel) - compatible with TGR Alice and all clone variants. Comes with RGB underglow AND RGB switch blacklight LEDs pre-soldered.

- Adelais (TGR Alice & Clones compatible) FR4 plate



Adelais Prototype assembled and fit









GENERAL GB INFORMATION

Order form: https://forms.gle/UfVPxa3pTMSNHTSCA
Album of prototype, mock-ups and finished products from previous GBs: https://imgur.com/a/T67cgJR

• Price: $48 (USD) per standard PCB; $78 (USD) per RGB PCB (Adelais en Ciel) and $30 (USD) per FR4 plate, plus international shipping from Vietnam (calculated based on buyer’s location)

• MOQ: None. Maximum order quantity: 50 Standard PCBs & 30 RGB PCBs

• Group buy form opens on Sunday, March 29th at 23:00 (GMT+7) for TEN DAYS.

• Invoices will be sent out ASAP after form submission. Payment deadline is 48 hours after the invoice is issued. Estimated production lead time: 45-50 days, so shipping is expected to begin at the end of May 2020.

You can also choose EMS express service, and while it's quite expensive, it is great if you live in Asia or Europe and want quicker delivery (normally within 10 days).

PCB & FR4 PLATE SPECS

• PCB is programmable via QMK and VIA

• PCB thickness: 1.6mm; Mini-USB port

• Powered by STM32F303 MCU. Supported layout: http://www.keyboard-layout-editor.com/?fbclid=IwAR0XQinjkr1l2LA85tTU4mpr8S8B5jxSKgPyUlH2ZP3rrKEot-AesBNrK7Q#/gists/f11c903151531006c35f368bb851a5d2

• Underglow RGBs pre-soldered for both variants. RGB backlight LEDs pre-soldered for Adelais RGB variant (Adelais en Ciel) only.

• ESD protection

• BootMagic Reset by holding ESC key and connecting USB cable

• PCB color: Matte Black solder mask with gold immersion. Sample from Hannah 910 v1 GB: https://i.imgur.com/Wi6lomQ.jpg

• FR4 Plate specs: Matte black, 1.6mm with gold bezel coating. Sample from Hannah 910 v1 Gb: https://i.imgur.com/Wi6lomQ.jpg

UPDATE 1 4/1: GB concluded. Sorting through orders. First email update sent.


ROUND 2: 09.20.2020

WHAT’S NEW (AND IMPROVED)

- We’re offering TYPE-C support for both versions (Standard with underglow and full per-key RGB) of the PCBs this time. Mini USB option is still available for the folks owning the original Alice or cases that only support it.

- Fix the tolerance issue with Durock (and probably C3) screw-in stabs at one particular position on the last round’s PCBs (please see album). The short story is we tightened the hole to provide support for clip-in stabs, but it ended up a bit too small for Durock ones. The fix does make that particular stab position incompatible with clip-ins, but we don’t think it’s much of an issue given how popular screw-in stabs these days compared to clip-ins.

- Added support for rotary encoders. The Standard version has rotary encoder support for all 3 keys on the far left cluster; while the En Ciel (full RGB) version only does so for the top left key due to the fact that its schematic is more complicated with all the pre-soldered per-key RGB LEDs.

- Added the anodised aluminum option for plates. We first offered the aluminum plate option for our previous Kanu Group buy, and since things turned out nicely, we’ve decided to bring the option to this one.

LEAD TIME AND SHIPPING

We have a limited number of the Standard USB-C PCBs and FR4 plates already in stock, so some early orders with only those items are ready to ship. In general though, the expected production time is 6-8 weeks, so shipping’s expected to start at the end of November/ early December.

Shipping during the pandemic is difficult. We have a reliable US proxy (12-14 day delivery) and a contract with DHL(3-5 day delivery) for the US and rest of the world with pretty competitive pricing. Though we’re still offering standard shipping (using national post) for non-US destinations, we highly recommend choosing the DHL option for much faster and safer shipping.
