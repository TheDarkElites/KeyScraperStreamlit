Geekark Slash

Geekark Slash is a bold gradient set by Fishykeebs. It is inspired by a blood moon sunset. The mixture of cool and warm colors brings a dynamic colorway to your desk.


Date
April 12th to May 31

Vendors
Worldwide: Keebsforall.com

Pricing and Kitting

We chose to go with a single expansive base kit. It will be priced at $80 and made of dye-subbed PBT plastic.

The base kit covers 60 to full size layouts as well as ISO, alice, split spacebar, 1800, and F13.

Why does this set feel so incredibly rushed? The single kit has numerous problems.

- Terminal ISO >< key doesn't fit with the pattern
- The two B keys are different colours and the Maja render shows that it doesn't work
- Has two 1u Alt keys but doesn't work on HHKB because of the lack of 6u and 2x1.5u Super keys
- Does NOT support 1u Fn
- Does NOT support split Plus
- Does NOT support scooped 5
- Does NOT support split Backspace or white tilde
Furthermore, the Maja render shown at the bottom isn't even a possible configuration. The left Spacebar is 2u, which isn't supported in the set.

Honestly, I think the group buy should be cancelled until some of these basic changes are worked out.
