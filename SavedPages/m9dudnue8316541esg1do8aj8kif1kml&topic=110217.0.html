The base kit price was set to $120 to help lower the price of the white alphas. They are priced barely above cost so that people who are only interested in the set with white alphas can get in without having to spend as much.
CandyKeys did not take this approach (their white alphas kit is priced normally, ie higher) which is why it seems like the US base kit price is high in comparison.

We also went with a conservative MoQ goal due to the timing of the GB (right during/after Christmas). If we were confident about hitting 1000+ kits of course price would be $100.
