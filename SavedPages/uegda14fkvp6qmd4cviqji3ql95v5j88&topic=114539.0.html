« on: Thu, 09 September 2021, 23:12:22 »




Designed by Adi

GB is OVER, all kits will be made, will be providing design files to KBDfans to initiate the sample process ASAP.

Last Updated Nov 30 2021




Vendors:
US: Vala Supply
EU: MyKeyboard.eu
UK: Proto[typist]
Latin America: Fancy Customs
AU: Switchkeys
CN/Worldwide: KBDFans
PH/SEA: Zion Studios
JP: Basekeys
CA: Ashkeebs
IN: Rectangles Store


Details:
Profile: Cherry (1-1-2-3-4-4)
Manufacturer: EnjoyPBT
Material/Printing Technique: PBT Plastic/Dye Sublimation (No reverse dye sub!)
Shipping: 5-6 month production time, estimated to ship early Q2 2022



Kitting

Base Kit - $89 - Contains support for Alice, F13 TKL, Tsangan, Basic ISO, etc.



Hiragana Alphas Kit - $26 -  Allows you to use sublegends on your keycaps, has extra tab to kit two boards when paired with base, and can be paired with 40s kit to kit a 40s board!



Numpad Kit - $18 - Allows you to kit a board with a numpad when paired with base, or just a numpad!



Spacebar Kit - $16 - Allows you to use accented 6.25/7U bars, 6U centered, off centered, and dual 3U centered spacebars in white, and purple Alice bars! Note that the accented alice bars are slightly mislabeled, the rightmost should be 2.75



Novelty Kit - $29 - A novelty kit that has multiple R4 1.5/1.25u, XT Column support, and ISO and ANSI enter novelties!



40s Kit - $29 - An innovative 40s kit that was designed to be paired with the Alternate Alphas kit as opposed to the Base Kit~ these two kits combined can kit boards like Planck, Preonic, Whimsy, Corne, etc!



Accent Kit - $29 - An expansive accent kit that allows you to pick your favorite flavor of keycaps, whether that be green apple, grape, and strawberry!



International Kit - $18 - Allows you to use most international layouts!

Collabs:
Cable: Voxelmods


Voxelmods will be offering a set of colorful cables with white push pull connectors! These cables have straight leads, and can come in either grape, green apple, strawberry, or whiteout for your minimalists out there. Please note that the push pulls in the images used are not representative of the final product: these are the connectors prior to being cerakoted, the end product will have a super sexy and durable matte white finish! Images of what the connectors will look like can be seen on the product page on Voxel's site.

Asero Foundry Metal Artisan: R4/ESC 1U ASERO CAP IS CANCELLED



Bag: Mech Fashion:
Mechfashion will be offering a cute bag to hold your keyboard adorned with the same pattern found on the desk mat, complete with a plush apple-flavored green interior and a grape-flavored purple drawstring.

Resin Artisans: I will be working with the following resin makers on collabs for this set! More details, prototypes, etc TBA.

Note that all renders are simply used in order to visualize the set. These colors represent the actual colors that the set will use, and may be changed slightly as development goes on in order to better capture the theme. Things like monitor calibration, compression, and room lighting can all affect the perception of the set colors.
