## JADE SWITCH
[0.5USD (for reference only, actual cost fluctuations by agents)]

- Switch type: Linear
- Foundry: keygeek
- Stem Material:  Modified POM (By mixing lubricant modification on the basis of POM, a higher smoothness than the original POM can be obtained)
- Top housing Material: Modified UPE (The smoothness is targeted improved on the basis of retaining the wear-resistant properties of the UPE material)
- Botton housing Material: Hardened PA66 (Hardened for greater durability and better sound performance)
- Spring material: 21mm piano steel gold-plated spring custom-made in Japan Seisen
- Pretravel: 2.0±0.3mm
- Total travel: 3.7±0.3mm
- Initial Pressure:  45±2gf
- Bottoming Pressure: 55±3gf
- Lube: Factory lubrication Krytox 205&Krytox 105
- 5-pin PCB mount
