Hello Geekhackers and Mech friends,

It’s Team Mechlovin’, back with another GB. For those who haven’t heard of us, we have previously run many successful group buys for PCBs and Plates in the past and received very positive feedback from our end users.

In this GB, we’d like to offer the KANU, a PCB & Plate solution for the Percent Canoe V1, which is one of the most popular 65% boards on the market. Similar to our previous products, the Kanu PCB is programmable via QMK and VIA, while adding split space layout support. The added layouts are also reflected in the design of the plates, which come available in FR4 and anodised aluminium.


WHAT’S AVAILABLE FOR THIS BUY?

Kanu PCB - compatible with the Percent Canoe V1 keyboard.

Kanu plates - available in FR4 and anodised aluminium (Silver, Grey, Black)









GENERAL GB INFORMATION

GB FORM: https://forms.gle/TVyZk38UGZRpD3SC6
PROTOTYPE ALBUM: https://imgur.com/a/LjlCxCR
JOIN OUR DISCORD SERVER FOR LATEST UPDATES: https://discord.gg/jqVbMpM

• Price: $45 (USD) per Kanu PCB; $28 (USD) per FR4 Plate and $38 (USD) per Aluminium Plate, plus international shipping from Vietnam (calculated based on buyer’s location)

• MOQ: None.

• Group buy form opens on Saturday, June 6th at 23:00 (GMT+7) for TEN DAYS.

• Invoices will be sent out ASAP after form submission. Payment deadline is 48 hours after the invoice is issued. Estimated production lead time: 45-60 days, so shipping is expected to begin within the first half of August.

PCB SPECS

• PCB is programmable via QMK and VIA

• PCB thickness: 1.6mm; Mini-USB port

• Powered by Atmega32u4. Supported layout: http://www.keyboard-layout-editor.com/#/gists/c373d9bbff776c659f9cc0eb9b5d45a6

• 6 RGB LEDs on the top right position (compared to 2 on the original PCB) to ensure high brightness and smooth transition effects

• No underglow LEDs

• ESD protection

• BootMagic Reset by holding ESC key and connecting USB cable. Hardware reset available.

• PCB color: Black solder mask with immersion gold

PLATE SPECS

• FR4 Plate specs: 1.6mm with gold bezel coating. (See sample in the photo album)

• Aluminium Plate specs: CNC’ed and anodised in Silver, Gray or Black.

• Fit BOTH the aluminum and PC version

SHIPPING

Shipping will be handled by my trusted proxy with USPS tracking for US-based buyers; and Standard Post (trackable) for ROW. You can also choose EMS express service, but it has been pretty unreliable as of late (things may improve when these are ready to ship). Shipping costs are quoted in the GB form.

We’d like to advance our biggest thanks to people who have been giving us their trust and support of various forms in the last 8 months or so. We feel extremely privileged to be able to provide solutions while maintaining our modest operation, especially during these difficult times. It’s been one hell of a year with non-stop craziness on all fronts, and we’re just grateful to be here and play our small part in this wonderful community.

THANK YOU HAVE A GOOD ONE!
