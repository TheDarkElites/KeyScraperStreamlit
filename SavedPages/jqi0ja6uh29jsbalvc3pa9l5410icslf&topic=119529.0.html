ALL WILL BE ONE
An Official Magic: The Gathering™ Keycap Set


The official Phyrexian keycap set compleating a W1-AT.
All use of Phryexian glyphs have been vetted by the Mother of Languages
and found to be without imperfection.

Inspiration
Many have longed for the glory of compleation to grace their keyboard. This keycap set
revels in the compleated glory of Elish Norn and her acolytes.
The keyaps are a subtle grey gradient bedighted with the glorious phyrexian script in red
contrasted contrasted against the inferior latin legends in dark grey.
Join us and bask in the glory of the ONE

Vendors
USA/Mexico: Clackeys.com
Canada: Ashkeebs.com

Set Info
Profile: Cherry
Group buy: February 13,  2023 through March 18, 2022
Estimated delivery: 6 months after GB end
Manufacturer: Infinikey
IP: Officially Licensed with Wizards of the Coast.
