Important notes to all joiners (https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=100769.msg2765022#msg2765022) and confirmation email has sent to all joiners.

Oct, 12th, 2019 Updates: all is done, preparing for shipment. https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=100769.msg2820673#msg2820673

Shipping info is available here : https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=100769.msg2828212#msg2828212








IC: https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=100540.0
GB Form: https://forms.gle/Bs5yVpaxQfbkxh9k7

Specifications:
  • Made from ALU 6063 series
  • 4mm ALU Plate
  • Sandwich Mount
  • WK or WKL Layout , NO ISO Layout supported
  • 7 degree angle
  • Type-c Hot Swappable PCB

Layout Supported
RGB Hot Swappable PCB WK Layout

RGB Hot Swappable PCB WKL Layout

QMK  Hot Swappable PCB WK Layout I

QMK  Hot Swappable PCB WK Layout II

QMK Hot Swappable PCB WKL Layout

Something need to be Noted Due to Fixed layout with Hot Swappable slots:
  • stepped Capslock is NOT supported
  • QMK Hot Swappable PCB WK Layout I and II are different PCB

Default kit (420 USD, PayPal included, Shipping excluded):
  • ALU Case * 1 (E - White / E - Yellow / Ano Black / Ano Red)
  • Plate * 1 (E - White / E - Yellow / Ano Black / Ano Red)
  • Weight (E - White / E - Yellow / Ano Black / Ano Red)
  • PCB ( RGB Hot Swappable / QMK Hot Swappable -10 USD)
  • Screws

Upgrades / Additions:
  • Upgrading to / additional Sandblast Brass Weight +30/+60 USD
  • Upgrading to / additional PVD Brass Weight +45/+80 USD (Silver/Black)
  • Additional QMK / RGB Hot Swappable PCB +45/+55 USD
  • Additional ALU Plate +50 USD
  • Additional ALU Weight +40 USD

GB Info:
  • GB spots: 50 (FCFS)
  • GB will be started at May 18th 19:00 (GMK -07:00)
  • GB will be lasted for 1 week or all 50 spots were allocated
  • Invoice will be sent after your form was submitted.
  • Estimated Shipping time will be in October.
   
Shipping Info:
Shipping will be from ShenZheng, China, via SF-Express, trackable. Each set weighs about 4.5 kg with packaging. The shipping fee will only be invoiced when keyboards are ready. Estimated shipping cost (US 50$, Korean 30$, Europe $65)
More detail shipping fee and shipping time info is available at http://www.sf-express.com/us/en/dynamic_function/price/

Terms

We'll do our best to do QC but we cannot guarantee every board is 100% perfect.
If there are blemishes, depending on the condition of the boards, possible solutions include replacement, return item chargeback, and partial refunds.

Important Notes for all joiners

Because GB form design was not designed in a perfect state, we missed some choice in it. Something more need to be check with joiners:

  • For who chose both 'upgrading sandblast weight' and 'upgrading  brass PVD weight': since that 2 options are conflict. The main difference for them is that it will be shinning with PVD. Please let us know your prefer. If you want have PVD, you can chose 'Black' or 'Silver' color for it.
  • For who chose QMK pcb combine with WK layout in base kit, there are 2 layout provided for it: WK Layout I (6.25u Spacebar) and WK Layout II (7u spacebar). We will provide WK layout I as default option. If you prefer Layout II, please let us known
  • For who chose additional QMK pcb, please let us know preferred layout. ( WKL / WK Layout I / WK Layout II )
  • For who chose additional Alu plate, please let us know preferred color (Anodizing Black / Anodizing Red / E-White / E-Yellow) and layout (WK / WKL)
  • For who chose additional Alu weight, please let us know preferred color (Anodizing Black / Anodizing Red / E-White / E-Yellow)

Any method to inform us is appreciated, reply email directly, PM in forum, etc.
