saevus: Latin adjective meaning fierce, ferocious.

Cor: Latin noun meaning heart



Update: GB Info and Vendors!
The time has finally come! We are ready to announce the GB! There will be an official Group Buy thread tomorrow.
Start Date: October 7th
End Date: October 30th
Limit: Uncapped
Delivery: 4-6 months depending on number of units sold. Will update here at least monthly.
Colors: Light Blue, Deep Navy, Lilac, Vibrant Red, Rose Gold, Silver, E-white, E-black
Tops: WK and WKL
PCB: Solder (allows for plateless), Hotswap (no plateless)

Base Price and Kit
  • $500 USD
  • Top and Bottom case (Anodized Aluminum, WK or WKL)
  • Stainless Steel Weight (sandblasted)
  • Copper Badge
  • Brass Internal Weight
  • PCB (solder or hotswap)
  • Daughterboard
  • JST cable
  • Gaskets
  • Bumpon feet
  • PC Plate
  • Extra hardware
  • Modular Carrying Case
  • Microfiber Cloth
  • Poron Case Foam

Extras
  • Extra Badge (3 PVD Options, all polished SS - Black, Rainbow, Silver)
  • PCB (Hotswap or Solder, will include gaskets)
  • Daughterboard and JST
  • Alu Plate
  • PC Plate
  • POM Plate

Regional Vendors
NA: https://mechsandco.com/
CA: https://www.ashkeebs.com/
EU: https://mykeyboard.eu/
UK: https://prototypist.net/
AU: https://prestigekeys.com.au/
Asia: https://swagkeys.com/

Supported Layouts





Specs
  • WK and WKL (prototypes in WKL because... it's superior duh )
  • 6063 Aluminum
  • Internal Weight - Brass
  • External Weight - SS
  • External Badge - Copper, PVD Polished SS (Rainbow, Black, Silver)
  • Blasting Grit 150
  • 6.5° typing angle
  • 20.6 mm Front Height
  • Cherry lip
  • PCB Gasket Mount (Plateless build option on solder PCB)
  • PCB Image
  • QMK and VIA compatible PCB
  • Centered USB-C via Unified Daughterboard
  • Internal Engravings
  • saevus logo on left
  • Cor aut Mors - Heart or Death - on right
  • Unassembled Weight - 2016g (4 lbs 7 oz)

**Update**
Changelog from prototype to final unit.
  • Removed mounting point directly under PCB. I am doing final testing with 4 mounting points on top and bottom instead of 3.
  • Adding 1 piece of poron case foam to the kit. I think the board sounds really nice without it, but it does improve the board sound slightly.
  • Working on securing horizontal alignment. Right now there is a small bit of space from left to right, so will build in tabs to secure this spacing.
