Colour inspiration
More

This glass is coloured using Cobalt and that pop that you get in the glass is what I wanted to capture in the blue of the set

https://www.pantone.com/color-finder/2738-C


A perfect match for the blue another intense colour, that is also the colour of a Cobalt based compound

https://www.pantone.com/color-finder/226-C



Cobalt in its pure form tends to be a silvery colour but not to light, and in this case RAL 7001 Silver grey worked perfectly

Scholars

Scholars Defined


#1 - Cobalt element from periodic table with Atomic number and atomic weight
#2 - Corrin Ring the core of vitamin B12 (however there is a mistake in one of the rings that will be fixed)
#3 - microorganism- Cobalt 60 is widely used for sterilization of medical  equipment using gamma irradiation
#4 - Alchemy symbol for Cobalt
#5 - Cobalt is used in Lithium Ion batteries
#6 - Vitamin B12 - Cobalt found at the center of the vitamin B12
#7 - Kobold - the mythical goblin that gave Cobalt was named after as when smelted it gave off poisonous fumes
#8 - Vase - Glass or Pottery was coloured using Cobalt
#9 - Cow - Ruminants are the only animal capable of synthesizing vitamin B12
#10 - Bohr diagram of Cobalt showing electrons orbiting the nucleus
