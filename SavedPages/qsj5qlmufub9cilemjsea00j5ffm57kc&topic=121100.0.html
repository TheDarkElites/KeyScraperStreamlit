[GB] LM70 M - FRL with macro column, rubber balls/tadpole mount

A FRL 70% with macro column of LM series

Highlight points

- Clean and beautiful design approved on the LM60 V2 model
- Silicone Rubber Balls/Geon's tadpole mount
- RGB light pass-through hole (optional)
- Many color options
- WK and WKL top available
- PCB with ISO and split space bar support
- Extra plates and top available
- Different colors for top and bottom (optional)










Please check others colors on Instagram or order link bellow.
https://www.instagram.com/p/Cvzr4QmMn2f

Our last keyboard project LM60 V2, that influenced and is design base of LM70 M



Mount type


Tadpole from Geonworks (will be included)


Mount Shock Absorption Balls - used in drones (will be included)

Alternatively It can be top mounted using 8x M3 screws.

Layout options



Details

Top Aluminum
Bottom Aluminum
Side RGB led light through Polycarbonate (no RGB available)
Case Angle: 6.5 degrees
Mounting: Rubber Shock Absorption Balls or Silicone Tadpole from Geonworks
Case Material: 6063 aluminum
Making process: CNC - sandblast - anodizing
Front Height: 19 mm
Back Height: 21 mm (with chamfer) and 32 mm (before chamfer)
Weight: ~1400g and ~1800g (mounted with brass plate)
Plate:  Aluminum, Brass, FR4 (POM on demand)
PCB Compability: Customized PCB from Y & R Studio


What will be included

- LM70 M Top
- LM70 M Bottom
- Plate
- Stainless screws
- Silicone Rubber Mounts (Mount Shock Absorption Balls)
- Tadpole from geonworks (if MOQ 40 sets sold)
- Silicone Rubber feets
- Polycarbonate inserts for the RGB holes

Timeline and fulfillment date

August: 12-30 Group Buy
September-October (first half): Production
October-November: Shipment (Will be available: Aliexpress forwarding, Fedex/DHL and Germany forwarding to EU).

Price

One set USD 164.00
Two sets USD 290.00
Thee sets USD 420.00

Prices including hotswap PCB (for more than 40 sets sold, we can offer extra solder PCB by USD 30)

Extra plate from USD 25.00
