Quote from: MeloDet on Wed, 06 December 2017, 08:33:21
Entered! BTW, if these aren't really necessary with gateron switches is there a specific switch type they are particularly useful for? I bought em just to have some on hand, but I would've assumed everyone was buying em for wobbly gats.

I believe they're generally used for Cherry switch tops. When you open the switches to modify them, the tops get loose and these are meant to take up the space so they don't rattle or wobble. Gateron and clear tops are more brittle, so they don't bend and become loose. They only fix wobble in stopping the switch top from wobbling.
