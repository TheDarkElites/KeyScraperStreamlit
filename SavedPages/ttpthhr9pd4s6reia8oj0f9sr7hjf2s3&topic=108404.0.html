GROUPBUY CLOSED
Order here.
Product information.

TYPEAU.40 Planck Edition is a high quality, thick, high profile and angled case for the OLKB/Planck PCB Rev 6+.
The case comes in 5 different colors listed below. The TYPEAU.40 was born from a desire to have a hefty,
angled case like other form factors do.

Optionally, you can include a sandblasted Brass or Stainless Steel plate with your order, manufactured by LaserBoost.
I say optionally, as your current mid/hi pro plates are fully compatible.
Renders below.

Features
- 6-degree typing angle
- High Profile
- Aluminum 6060
- 5mm thick walls
- Around 540 grams
- Tray mounted
- Precise USB Cutout

Includes
- Case (compatible with OLKB and Drop Planck PCB Rev 6+)
- Hex screws + Allen key
- Rubber feet
- a famous Dutch snack (not weed)

Optional
- Sandblasted, Brass or Stainless Steel plate. (1x2u, 2x2u and Grid support)

Pricing
Case: €120,-
Plate: €25,-
Shipping: depending on location and order size.

MOQ: 25 - HIT!

Shipping estimation: Late November

Week 1 Update

As mentioned before, we hit MOQ (25 cases) on day 1. However, that spree didn’t end as we surpassed 50 orders on Saturday :smile: . I’m so excited by the amount of people who support my project. I honestly didn’t think we would hit these numbers.

69 Cases
61 Plates

Insane!

PCB Availability
I’ve had a lot of questions regarding the PCB availability. Currently, DROP is the only place that sells Planck PCBs. Unfortunately, they removed the option to buy the standalone PCB for $55 and instead, sell a kit. The kit includes a PCB, Case and Plate for $100. If you buy that kit, you can leave out the €25 Plate to save cost as the TYPEAU.40 case is compatible with the default OLKB/DROP plate.

Accelerated Checkout issues
The past week, I’ve had some reports about Apple, Google and Shop Pay not working during the checkout. After some long support sessions with Shopify, the issue is finally resolved and those payment methods are available again. Please make sure that if you run into any issues during the checkout, to contact me on Discord or by email: hey@typeau.com

Thanks again to all you who support this project and who helped me to get to this point.

Production Update - 18 Nov 2020

Hey everyone, it’s been a little while since I've last posted an update. Simply because there wasn’t much to write about. Until now!

Last time I wrote about the first batch of the plates from LaserBoost arriving. 2 weeks after, the second and final shipment arrived. Again, they look stunning

The machining of the cases is finalized and yesterday I received the first 4 cases to inspect the dimensions and finish for each color (black, gray, red and blue). E-White is coming in a bit later as it’s currently being coated.

However, there’s a catch:
The finish is different across the board compared to the earlier sample I received. Simply, It’s less refined and not up to par to the prototype. Also the red anodization is a different color than I requested.

No need to worry as this is being reviewed by the manufacturer and I have full confidence that this will be resolved. This is the reason why I requested a sample case of each color for inspection, to catch problems like these before every single case is anodized/coated.
Unfortunately, this results in a delay. If adjustments can be made quickly, I expect to start shipping halfway trough December.

Dimensions etc. are all on point. Plates are packed, screws, tools and rubber feet are packed and all set. Just waiting for the cases with a proper finish to arrive and we’re off to shipping!

To summarize:
- Received all plates from LaserBoost.
- Received red, blue, black and gray case for inspection. E-White coming next week.
- Issue with case finish. Anodization is not as high quality like earlier sample. Being reviewed by manufacturer as we speak.
- Red case delivered by manufacturer differs too much from spec. Same as above.
- Shipping is pushed back to approximately halfway December instead of late November. This is assuming the finish issue can be resolved quickly.
- Case dimensions and tolerances are spot on.

Production Update - 23 Januari 2021

Time for another Typeau news flash.

In the last update, beginning of December, I wrote about the issues with anodization and e-white coating. Which resulted in pushing the delivery date back to late January.

Halfway through December I received the new samples which were, to my disappointment, still not up to par with the expectations they set during the prototype phase.

The issues couldn’t be resolved by redoing the anodization and therefore the majority of the cases are getting reproduced.

Unfortunately, this calls for another delay. Instead of setting false expectations by providing a new timeframe that I might not make. I decided to change to bi-weekly updates with the latest information and rough timeframes.



GOOD NEWS E-WHITE:

Production Update - 13 March 2021
Hey people, it's time for a status update-
Let's start off with the bad news, unfortunately, the original manufacturer was unable to meet the
high expectations they set and have backed out. Here's the good news, I have two alternate manufacturers
 lined up to bring us to the finish line. Continue reading for more details.

Let's start off with why this update was pushed, I received all of this today at the eleventh hour. I work with
an intermediate company (project manager) who, based on my specifications, takes care of getting a
manufacturer, finishing/coating, quality control and manages the project from the manufacturing perspective.

If you've been keeping up with the Discord updates, you may be aware that we've had some issues with
tolerances not being met and the finish/coating. Keep in mind that the first prototype that I received from them
was perfect. However, the sample production units that I've received since then have been anything but. The
manufacturer has managed to keep missing the bar that they set at the beginning.

Just when I thought we were at the finish line, the cutout for the USB-C port required a bit of rework for the
case to pass QC before it went off for finishing/coating, the manufacturer backed out.

Fortunately, the project manager has lined up two alternate manufacturers who will bring this project to life
and put the TYPEAU.40 in your hands as soon as possible.

In retrospect, this happened due to my lack of experience in the field and the manufacturer settings expectations
that they could not meet. I could have overlooked/approved some of the lesser issues with the cases just to finish
the project. However, I would have felt horrible to allow everyone who have been incredibly patient to receive
an inferior product.

I understand that you may feel frustrated and may have feelings of uncertainty, but, please bear with me as we
are still optimistic that we can deliver you a wonderful TYPEAU.40. Furthermore, I'll continue to post bi-weekly updates
to keep everyone up to date when possible, however, they may be delayed as some things are out of my control as is
today's update.

Please don't hesitate to contact me via DM, or in my Discord if you have any questions.

Lastly, I want to say thank you to everyone for your patience and your continued you support with this project and have
a great rest of your weekend!
