Hello everyone, This is Deadline. It is honorable to collaborate with DAGE (Hammer) on this keycap project.

HMC keycap after IC and several adjustments, now officially start GB

IC adjustment main content:

40s increase key Adjusted to not rely on mod kit

mod reduce the number of keys to meet the above needs, and add 1.5u esc [r2]

international add in uk iso required keys 


At the beginning, we aimed for the excellent tactile performance of CRP.

After confirming the collaboration, we also aimed for Hammer's material and combined it with the color scheme of this keycap to create a few personalized designs.

Design concept:

With the color scheme confirmed, imagine the usage scenario.

Neatness, industry, and order are the keywords that come to mind, which led to the word 'company.

'In this clean and tidy office space, HAMMER is the key to this project and also the core.

Adding the underline of 'D' symbolizes a list or department nameplate that can be seen everywhere in the company.

'H+M 1/C' is the project/employee number, and 'core' is also the first collaboration in the Crossover series.

 The 'tab' marking is meant to express that now or in the future, we will always be doing many things simultaneously, gradually becoming more and more important."

We have designed two types of metal keycaps.

One is decorated with bi-dioxide logo and color scheme.

The other is designed with a triangle and fins from the Retro66, adding a sense of depth and industry.
