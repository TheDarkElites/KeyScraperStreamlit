Master the Elements with the Elemental Deskmat Collection

Vendors: US: Mechs and Co. - Canada: DeskHero - Australia: Daily Clack - Europe: MyKeyboard.eu - China: KBDFans - SEA: ILUMKB


Thank you everyone for the support! The order has been placed with the manufacturer!

Marie of Binirias and the Mume sculpt is also doing an artisan collaboration raffle for anyone who has purchased a mat which can be seen here: Binirias x Mechs & Co.










Specifications: 900 X 400 X 4 mm with stitched edges.

Pricing: Elemental Deskmat: $22 each or $80 for the entire collection + shipping.

Shipping: Shipping through USPS Priority for CONUS orders and DHL for International orders.

Lead time: Approximately 45 days for the deskmats to be delivered to us, and then 2 weeks for order fulfillment.

Group Buy is set to run from July 16th until August 16th



Designs by: https://www.behance.net/helenpan for Mechs & Co.



Discord: https://discord.gg/a8XpG9R
