After a long wait GMK Sparta is finally LIVE on Drop.com


If you created a list of history’s most prolific warriors, the Spartans would be right at the top. Fierce and highly skilled, they were known not only for their prowess on the battlefield, but for their regimented way of life. GMK Sparta captures the spirit of Sparta with a handsome (and historically accurate) colorway representative of the Greek warriors’ red tunics and bronze lambda shields. Soft white alphas and victory red modifiers are underscored by gold legends and accents. Crafted in Germany in famous Cherry profile, GMK Sparta features a doubleshot ABS construction tough enough for any typist.
