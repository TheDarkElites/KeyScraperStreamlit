GMK TeraDrive
a joint project with yuzu#0108


A keycap set based off the Sega HTR-2106.

Group Buy: 21/01/22 - 22/02/22
Estimated Shipping: Q2 2023





Inspiration

Yuzu saw the Sega HTR-2106.
Yuzu liked it.
We wanted the keycaps.
We made this IC.








Kits

The current colour codes are as follows

Alphas RAL 000 15 00
Mods RAL 7010
Legends GMK CP

Due to the rarity of the board we can't exactly get one in front of us as of yet, we're still going through as many photos as we can in various lighting conditions to match as closely as possible.

We are actively looking to acquire one.


Base Kit

US$135




Extension Kit

US$35





JIS Mod Kit

US$52



Thanks to 2Moons and Konstantin for all their help on this kit, go check out GMK JIS WoB.


Deskmats

Based on the nostalgic box art of the TeraDrive console. Courtesy of would from Finder Works
