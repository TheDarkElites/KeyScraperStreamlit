Features:

1.New gasket mounting system ---3 installation ways for different hardness needs bring you three different typing experiences.
2.A more convenient quick disassembly design and adjustable quick disassembly force make your DIY process more convenient and fun.
3.Budget-friendly price, high performance, better-customized experience, and lower threshold allow everyone to experience higher-end customization.
4.Full CNC aluminum case, internal brass weight, enhancing the overall stability and sound performance.
5.Simple and clean design---continuing the dual-weight design of JRIS65 but Blank Back Weight.
6.Swap switch, rotary knob, Badge, and LED light sign assembly.
Give you define more possibilities and more individual and diverse appearance options to meet the cool trend needs.

                                                                                                                                                                                                                     

The GB units will make some improvements compared to the prototype:

1.Add the alignment pins where the gasket silicone column is installed, so the internal will not move up or down after assembly.
2.Changed the per-key flex cut plate to a regular flex cut to avoid the PCB may hits the bottom case and improving the typing sound.
3.Include a Customized Masking Tape to mod the flex-cut PCB for a better typing sound.
4.Adjusting the hotswap PCB socket direction of the multi-layout Hotswap PCB to be compatible with the 6.25U and 7u
5.The two bottom-case foams will change one of them as no cut foam.
6.The GB unit of the Per key flex cut PCB will adopt the red line instead of the gold line.

Flex Cut PCBs:

Multi layout Black core Hotswap&Solder PCB supports QMK/VIA


ANSI layout 5.2 Bluetooth Hotswap PCB supports LDN Software


BT PCB is cooperating with Li Dana. LDN’s PCB is famous in China because his PCBs are stable and qualified and use friendly.
It has its drive and software, which can edit the key mapping and layers. Please check the software driver interface here.
1. 8 devices can pair with at the same time, and it is easy to switch devices you want.
2. The power switch is on the PCB, easy to turn BT function on/ off.
3. The fastest response time is only 1.25ms
4. The polling rate for the BT PCB is 1ms/1000hz
5. The battery is 4400mA,  bigger battery compartment, more robust wireless mode range.

Note: We highly recommend using a PC/ POM/FR4 plate for BT PCB
because the Brass and ALU plate will have a little affection for connectivity.

Non-flex Cut PCB:

ANSI layout Black core Hotswap PCB with RGB Per Key, supports QMK/VIA


Per Key Flex Cut PCB( Add-ons only)

ANSI layout Black core Hotswap PCB with Per Key Flex Cut, supports QMK/VIA

The GB unit of the Per key flex cut PCB will adopt the red line instead of the gold line.


5 Plate Options:

Flex Cut Plates: FR4/ALU/Brass(Brass Plate Add-ons Only)

The regular FR4 plate is upgrading to the black core martial at the same price.

Non-flex Cut Plates: PC/POM

Plateless Build Options:
We highly suggest building plateless with solder PCB, 5mm extra plate foam
and bottom case foam.
The 5mm extra plate foam will bring a more soft typing feel, and the typing sound will be cleaner.
But without the 5mm plate foam, it will also work fine, and the typing feel and sound are very different.
