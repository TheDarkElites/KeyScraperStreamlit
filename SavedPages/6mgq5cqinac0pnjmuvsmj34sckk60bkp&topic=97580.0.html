Alphas: 491C
Mods: 504 C
Accents: 7413 C
Legends: 7499 C

The sample for these has been made and shipped out yesterday. Here's pretty much what happened:



I requested a revised sample for the alpha and accent colors. The other accent color was a very "burnt" orange, and this wouldn't have fit the theme of the set so I changed it from 7413 C to 1375 C.

I've also requested a sample of 492 C for the alpha color, because I'm not sure if 492 C or 491 C will look more like what I envision for the set.

I have pictures of the swatches, however, they're not 100% accurate in terms of color. They were taken under bright warm white light on a pixel 2XL.

Here are the two colors I'm deciding between for alpha color (Rows 5 and 6 from top)



Here's the legend color (Row 1). I feel like this color (and accent + mods) is perfect.
