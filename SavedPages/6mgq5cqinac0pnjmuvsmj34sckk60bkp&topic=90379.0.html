We are back!

I'm just glad that we made this happen after all these. Most people should know EVE and his capability by now, but for those who's new here, he was in charge of production of numerous custom keyboards, one of which is Mira SE run by NinjaDoc, you can find production pics in the most recent update. 

Background aside, today's group buy is for Moon custom keyboard, if you followed our IC thread, you'd probably stunned by its sexy curves, but here we go again:




Prototype album: http://imgur.com/a/2yFWZ

Price:
$435+pp fee, shipping to be billed separately.

Basic kit includes:
Alu top and bottom cases;
Custom Ordered PCB;
Alu plate, stripe insert, weight;
Stainless steel or brass Logo insert, pre-installed, not removable;
Custom Ordered Box;

Upgrade and Extra
Plate:
Upgrade to brass plate (+$20)
Order extra alu plate (+$20)
Order extra brass plate (+$35)

Weight:
Upgrade to brass weight (+$30)
Order extra brass weight (+$45)

Stripes:
Upgrade to brass stripes: (+$10)
Order extra brass Stripes:(+$20)

Specifications:
Both WK and WKL layout available;
Supports ANSI and ISO enter;
Regular and stepped caps-lock supported;
TMK firmware, fully programmable PCB;
8 degree typing surface;

Timeline:

GB opens till Jul 16, 23:59 central
Payment ends on July 31, 23:59 central
Production estimated to be 8 weeks

Prototype to help with decision-making: http://imgur.com/a/2yFWZ

Order form: https://goo.gl/UnMpVu
You'll need a second google account if you are ordering two, or send me a pm I can take care of that.

Find order status here: https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1drU_63J-pareTCKEeq2Ydo95LmoTud6rCTdXpYOYxQI/edit#gid=1582473866


Special thanks to stand.geek and gaiden for logo and weight design, they will either receive $200 cash reward, or 50% off on their first moon board order.
Also thank you to all who participated, each of you guys will receive a surprise cap made by EVE. More details to be released regarding the cap.
Last but not least thank all who supported Moon during its hardest time regardless entering the GB or not.
Together we make Democratic Republic of Moon GREAT
