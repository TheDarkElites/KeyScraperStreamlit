Hey all, I'm please to announce that Journey Deskmat GB is LIVE!


Schedule
GROUP BUY: 12 November - 12 December
Est Delivery: Q1 2022
Design by Switchlab
Manufactured by Kulermats



Inspiration
Inspired by your favorite video games, these deskmats will take you back on a journey of yesteryear. Fly back to the 8bit era of the day where pixels reigned supreme.
The design features 3 mechanical switches that are a nod to those times without overdoing it. Enjoy these beautiful deskmats at home while you grab your controller head into battle.


Renders/Design
Design comes in 2 themes, Light and Dark with the option of having the quote or quote-less.

Dimensions
900mm x 400mm x 4mm
Stitched edges
