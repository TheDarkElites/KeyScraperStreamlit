I have some PCBs and case layers left (clear plate, frosted bottom) out of the hardware and pro micros.

The PCB (this group buy will be for PCBs with gold plating on the solder pads!)

Case color options

Timeline

GB closes 1/28/19

Invoices will go out upon end of GB period, payment due by Feb 2nd

kits would ship approximately 3 weeks after GB ends (we hit Chinese new year, PCB factory is closed till Feb 11th) I'll post updates here as it progresses

The Alpha 28 is QMK compatible! (And for those ordering a kit, the Pro Micro will be pre-flashed with this layout: https://github.com/qmk/qmk_firmware/tree/master/keyboards/alpha/keymaps/vderm

PCB and case are MX(plate or PCB mount) and alps compatible.

Buy is for: PCB, case, or kit for Alpha 28 keyboard with LED underglow (kit does not include switches or keycaps)

Kit ($30) includes:
PCB
pro micro (ready to use, no programming necessary)
2 piece case (plate and bottom)
screws and standoffs
diodes
1x 2u stabilizer
RGB LED strip for underglow

Other options:

Just a Case ($10): 2 pieces of acrylic (plate and case bottom)

Just a PCB ($10): single bare board, black soldermask with white silk screen and gold plated pads

shipping for up to 3 kits or 8 PCBs (if you're getting more than that, I will provide a separate estimate)

Priority within the US $8

First class to Canada $10, rest of the world $15

Priority to Canada $25, rest of the world $35

details and assembly instructions here: https://github.com/PyrooL/Alpha
