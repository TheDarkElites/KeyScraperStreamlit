Duo-M by Duo Foundry



Hello mechanical keyboard enthusiasts! After months of designing, testing and 3 prototypes later, the GB is finally here! For this GB, we are targeting to manufacture and start delivery of the boards within 3 months. We think this is possible as we have switched to a more reliable and higher quality manufacturer (albeit more expensive but has manufactured many fine boards) and have experienced great efficiency and professionalism when working with them for our 3rd prototype.

IC thread: https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=119192.0

Duo-M
Refer to the IC thread for more details

Layout: 75% fixed hotswap + backlit
Mounting: Springs + Gasket
Case Material: Aluminium 6063
Colours: Black, Grey, Navy, Silver, Copper, Maroon (any combination)
Typing Angle: 7 degrees
Front Height: 18.5mm
Carrying Case: Duo Foundry Case
Weight: ~1.7kg

There are 720 possible colour combinations! Configure your Duo-M here: https://duofoundry.com/products/duo-m

GB Details

Platform: www.duofoundry.com
GB period: 1 Mar – 31 Mar 2023
Price: SGD$449 / ~USD$333
Estimated shipping date: Q3 2023
