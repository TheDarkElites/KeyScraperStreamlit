[GB] GMK Alchemy - now cancelled
« on: Fri, 14 May 2021, 15:10:41 »
Unfortunately I have to announce that due to low sales, the GMK Alchemy Group Buy will be cancelled shortly by all vendors.

Vendors will remove all kits and accessories from sale today and if you have already purchased anything you should receive an email shortly regarding refunds. Please contact your vendors directly if you haven't heard anything in a few days.

This news is given with a heavy heart as it's AKB's first foray into the keycap world, but I can assure you it won't be the last. For the time being I'll put all my focus into the boards we have on the horizon, with an announcement for the Aella 75% coming shortly!





Welcome to the group buy for GMK Alchemy, going live Saturday May 15th and running until Tuesday June 15th!

ETA on shipping: Q2 2022
