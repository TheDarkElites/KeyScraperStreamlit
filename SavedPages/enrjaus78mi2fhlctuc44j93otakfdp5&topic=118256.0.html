KEYKOBO BEIGE & DOLCH

Hello everyone!
I'm very happy to present to you something that I have been working on for a little while . I recently found out about a new keycap manufacturer called Keykobo and I became really interested in running some classic colorways with them following their WoB and BoW releases .
 
Group Buy Dates:
September 2nd 2022 to October 3rd 2022
Estimated lead time ~3 Months after Group Buy end from Keykobo to Vendors
 
Vendors: (Links will be added as each of the vendors group buys go live)
USA: Mechs & Co
Canada : Ashkeebs
EU: Keygem
Oceania: DailyClack
SEA: ZionStudios
UK : Prototypist
China / International : zFrontier
KR : Geon.works (Naver and Global Store links are below)
Naver store base kits
Naver store add-on kits
Geonworks Global Store
Pricing: (Pricing will be updated as each of the vendors group buys go live)


Information
Lead Time : ~3 Months after Group Buy end from Keykobo to Vendors .
Profile: Cherry
Material: ABS Doubleshot (Only exception are pad printed windowed keys in the extension kit)
Colors: Beige (U9 / L9 / CR) , Dolch (N9 / CC / CP) , CMYK (MG1 / TU2 / CV / DY / V2 / CR) , CMYW (MG1 / TU2 / CV / DY / V2 / CP)
Manufacturer: Keykobo
 
Kitting:

Beige & Dolch Base Kits
 


Beige & Dolch Extension Kits
 


CMYK & CMYW Add-on Kits
