Why we do the newest version

When we consider the upgrading for our newest 96 kit,the weight is a speical reason.So we add the brass weight for our old kit 1(Melody 96)

And because the reason,we consider more factors.

Compatibility,new function,new feeling etc.

After confirm last with our boss

We choose the same size,but use the speical acrylic bottom with brass weight.

For the situation,we upgrade our hotswap 96 V2 pcb to V3 RGB south facing hotswap pcb.

But sorry for that we still have not prepared ISO layout for this pcb now,will consider it in future.

And consider the better handle feeling in this kit,we upgrade the foam be the new two sets(one for the middle of pcb and plate,another one for pcb below).Still is EVA material.

After you add foam for this kit,we will upgrade the foam to the two sets freely.
