The Fallacy





Group Buy Date: January 25th, 2020. 5PM PST.
Countdown timer: https://www.timeanddate.com/countdown/generic?iso=20200125T17&p0=137&msg=Fallacy+Group+Buy&font=serif

Price (not inclusive PayPal fees) and Shipping:
$420 for top + bottom + weight + PCB + POM/Alu plate (+$15 for brass) + screws
$50 for additional PCB
$30 for additional POM or Aluminum plate
$45 for additional Brass plate

Shipping fees will be invoiced separately after I get the boards in hand and have QC'd them.
This should be ~10 weeks after my submission of the manufacturing order. This culminates to what I estimate to be an April ship date.
I will contact you at that time to confirm your shipping details.
Estimated to be:
~$30 CONUS
~$60 International

Order Form:
https://forms.gle/QcFFr137FTTZnPYw9
Form will start accepting submissions January 25th, 5PM PST
No longer accepting submissions!

Buy Details
To make the buy more manageable for me, this run will be limited to 50 units. One per person. FCFS.
You will not be able to edit the form after you have submitted it. Please make sure that your response is accurate.
If absolutely necessary, PM me about order changes on GeekHack or Discord (much more likely to respond).

For those that get a spot, the payment is due 24 hours after receipt of the invoice. The invoice amount will include kit price and whatever extras you chose as well as PayPal fees. This invoice will NOT include shipping fees. As stated before, that will be invoiced at a later date.

Features
  • Top Mount
  • Anodized 5052 Aluminum for the Top and Bottom Case
  • Sandblasted Brass Weight
  • 6.5 Degree Typing Angle
  • 16.5mm Front Typing Height
  • USB Mini port
  • PCB compatible With QMK
  • PCB has flex cuts
  • Brass weight spans the entirety of the alpha cluster
  • Side cutouts for grip

Color Choices
  • Black
  • Silver
  • Navy
  • Pink
Color samples: https://imgur.com/a/VqWr3JC

Supported Layouts

Will have standard Alice supported layouts
  • Split/normal backspace
  • Split/normal right shift
  • Stepped/normal capslock

Plate

The plate will be the standard 1.5mm thickness. It will be compatible with the standard Alice plate.
There will be a few choices for plate material:
  • Aluminum
  • Brass
  • POM Plastic

PCB
  • QMK Compatible
  • USB Mini port
  • Flex Cuts

Designed by Trifelife

Weight
  • "Fallacy" engraving
  • Sandblasted raw brass
  • Internally secured by screws
  • Alpha cluster has brass under it for acoustics

More Info and Updates:
Discord Server Invite: https://discord.gg/pYVsseU
