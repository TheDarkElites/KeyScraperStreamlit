Dear buyers in Time TKL group buy,

Some one have already received messages from DHL and should will receive the keyboard in this week.

At this moment I'd like to say thank you all for your support in this group buy.

Time TKL is the second keyboard we developed. It's a little crazy, yet special.
It's so special that not everyone like, but I'm glad I made it happen because you like it and joined the group buy.

Many buyers in Orange TKL group buy also joined this one. I'm so glad that I gain your trust.

Special thanks to zFrontier. They were very professional and patient through all the phases in this group buy and make sure everything goes well.

There are also three unexpected issues in the gb/keyboard.

  • The secret gift for founder editions we intended to give were custom-made desk mats. We changed our mind at last because of lack of time. The final secret gifts are not so special but are pricier than the original one.
  • The brass weights were planned to be electroplated. However, the factory found that the small brass parts will be burnt to black with normal electroplating. We decided to only polish the brass surface lightly and keep the raw brass parts as is. You can compare the electroplated brass and raw brass small parts. If your brass parts are oxidized in the future, the recommended treatment for them are applying a mixture of lemon juice + baking soda. You can also contact me if you’re not satisfied with the brass parts.
  • The design of top case is a little lack of consideration that the assembled plate is pretty difficult to be screwed in the top case. You'll need to find the correct angle to put together the plate and top case, which is tricky. A easy way to solve this problem is to solder switches while the plate is attached to the top case.

At last, we'll be responsible for all possible problems. Feel free to ask me if you get any problems. My email address is runninghack[at]gmail[dot]com. Joining our discord channel is also an easy way to find me.

January 24, 2018
Senter


Introduction






IC post

Please find the detailed information and put your order on zFrontier

They will start accepting orders on November 2nd (eastern daylight time), 8 PM, right before TopClack starts.

Price

The price will be a fixed $458 with free shipping.

The pricing is like $390 for the board + $20 for paypal transaction fee + $50 for shipping (if it was a self-organized GB)

What you get

  • Aluminum top and bottom case
  • Aluminum switch plate
  • Aluminum weights (11 parts)
  • Aluminum logo plate (The 'TIME' logo above the arrow keys)
  • PCB: compatible with both MX switches and ALPS switches
  • Other accessories: screws, neoprene damping pad, self-adhesive rubber feet, screwdriver, and so on

All the aluminum parts are either CNC machined or wire EDMed.

Fox Lab && zFrontier

Reasons why FoxLab is collaborating with zFrontier to organize and fulfill the GB for Time TKL:

  • Guarantee a successful GB. There're always risks joining GBs in the community. Working with a reputable vender is the best way we can think of to allay your concerns.
  • Avoid mistakes. zFrontier is professional on organizing the order information and sorting the boards.
  • Fast and reliable logistic solution. zFrontier has facilities in Shanghai and Hong Kong and full time employees to provide better logistic solutions
  • Customer service. With the help of zFrontier, we will provide professional after sales service for this GB.

Founder Edition

  • Founder edition has 'founder edition' engraved on the bottom weights, instead of the time of GB on normal editions.
  • Founder edition comes with an additional secrete gift for free.
  • The first 38 orders will get founder editions.
  • Old customers of Orange TKL can get founder editions as well. Please PM me you order number or send me an email.

Contact

  • Comment here or PM me
  • Click 'support' on the right corner of zFrontier's webpage
