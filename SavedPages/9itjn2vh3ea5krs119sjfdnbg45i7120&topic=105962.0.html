[GB] Hyper 7 R3 (173%)
« on: Sat, 25 April 2020, 07:40:20 »

Welcome to the Group Buy of the Hyper 7 – R3.

The Hyper 7 is a 173% keyboard started by Jae, Yiancar, and a couple others. R3 is now live which includes the main PCB with daughterboard, and options for a bent plate case (stainless steel) and option stabiliser set.

Changes from R1/R2:
– Moved to three rows on the top daughter-board to closer match the original vision. – KLE – and allows for normal 2u keycaps with stabilisers.
– New plate files. Will now be a sandwich style build with metal feet.
– PCBs will be Blue in colour

NOTE: Renders are for display purposes only and do have slight changes to the final items.

1 x PCB with daughter-board – Blue (daughter-board has options for 2u as in images, or 1u keycaps) - £125

Bent Plate Case – Top and Bottom pieces with angled supports. - £125

30 x Clip in/Screw in GMK Stabilisers - £50

SHIPPING WILL BE CALCULATED SEPARATELY. You will need to pay up to £30 for shipping once we have calculated shipping costs based on weight.

GB SCHEDULE: GB OPEN 25/04/2020 to 09/05/2020.
Around 2 months for production. Shipping should start around early-mid July.

ORDER NOW
