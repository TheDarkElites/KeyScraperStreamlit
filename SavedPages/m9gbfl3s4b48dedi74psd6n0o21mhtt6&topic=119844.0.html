CreateKeebs Net Runner Keycaps are Coming


Group Buy Live: March 20, 2023 - April 20, 2023
Estimated fulfillment date: Q2 2023

The keycap with Lucy as the protagonist - Net runner Designed a very eye-catching keycap in a cyberpunk style.
The biggest headache is when starting to design the color scheme of the keycaps. The dark blue, purple and white color matching of Lucy's clothes and the red embellishment are very memorable,
but at the same time her colorful hair is the most iconic part of the character. No matter which part is cut off, I feel that it is not perfect, so after many attempts,
I finally found a satisfactory "All in one" color scheme.
