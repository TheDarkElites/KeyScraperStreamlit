Introducing GMK Honor, a calmer and more subtle version of GMK Bushidō.
The set is featuring WS1 white and the same grey as GMK Bushidō (a darker version of  RAL 280 40 10).
IC thread

Group Buy will run from November 2nd to 30th 2020.
The estimated shipping date is September 30th 2021.
