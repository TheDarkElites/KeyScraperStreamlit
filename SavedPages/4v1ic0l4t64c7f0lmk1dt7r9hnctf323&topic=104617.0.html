FLX – Virgo: group Buy.

Link to the Google form https://bit.ly/3bicnl4

This form will become live on February 15th, 2020:
        15:30 GMT
        16:30 CET
        10:30 EST
        09:30 CT
        07:30 PST
        23:30 HKT/SPT

There are 50 public boards available, the first 10 will be allocated via FCFS to the first 10 people to fill out the google form. The remaining 40 boards will be raffled and drawn randomly from the remaining entrants. One entry per person please, duplicates will be deleted.

The form will be open for a minimum of 24 hours, more if required to make MOQ. Winners will be notified by email within 12 hours of the form closing.



Details:
12 Degree Split TKL (ala. Microsoft Natural Ergonomic 4000)
7-degree typing angle
Custom bespoke PCB with full ISO and ANSI Support
More
QMK and VIA compatibility.
USB-C via a JST daughter-board
Aluminium plate (optional Carbon Fibre, Brass, FR4, and Aluminium extras)
Grommet mounting isolates plate from chassis.
T6 CNC aluminium Top and Base pick from 5 colours for each.
Brass weight system (circa 3.5kg built).


Order options:
When ordering you can select from the following options:
Case Colours:
   
More
Aubergine Purple
   
More
Hibiscus Red
   
More
Onyx Black 
   
More
Space Silver   
   
More
Teal Green
       
You can choose any color combination you like for the Top and Base mix or match.


Extra Plates available in:
   Aluminium
   Carbon Fibre
   Brass
   FR4


Extra PCB’s will come with a Daughter-board and JST cable.

Price:
$560 excl. Shipping * +2% transaction fee
You will be billed for shipping once the boards are ready for dispatch, shipping will be from the UK and is estimated at circa $60 to North America, I will get this as low as possible when the time comes.

Expected completion date:
August 2020
I would expect to offer delivery a maximum of 6 months after orders are placed, the standard practice of overestimating is in full effect here. I will keep everyone up to date with regular updates until the boards are delivered.

If you are successful in the FCFS or Raffle, you will receive payment information to the email address provided.

*
More
Shipping rules:
   Upon successful entry into this group buy, you will be expected to pay for your keyboard @ $560 excluding shipping, once your keyboard has passed QC and is ready to be shipped, you will receive a shipping invoice, you will have a chance to change your shipping address at this time should you need to.
   I will allow ample time to pay this shipping invoice, however, Failure to pay the invoice will result in your keyboard order being cancelled and your original payment being refunded minus any incurred costs.

Brass Base: Special Edition mini raffle details.

There will be a total of 5 public special editions available, these special edition cases will have a solid brass base with polished Stainless steel weight.
These will be allocated via a mini raffle. This is how that will work...

1) Enter GB and spec a board how you want it, assume you are not getting a Brass base.
2) Make Sure to check the box in the form that will enter you into the mini raffle (final question).
3) I will draw the mini raffle at some point, before placing the order with the manufacturer (date unconfirmed at this point)
4) If you are drawn in the raffle, I will contact you to A: make sure you still want it - no penalty for opting out at this point and B: To give you the option to change the top colour if you wish.
5) You will receive a separate invoice for the base $130 in addition to the standard GB cost.

Please DO NOT enter this GB if you plan on cancelling your order if you do not get the Brass Special Edtion. Only 5 people will get one, chances are you will not be one of the 5...

Please feel free to contact me for anything, any time.

Thank you for your interest.
