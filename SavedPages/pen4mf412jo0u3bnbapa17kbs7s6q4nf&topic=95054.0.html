« on: Fri, 06 April 2018, 11:20:27 »

- Top Koala, bottom Alice

The slots for the GB will be allocated by FCFS. 40 units are available. I will enable the form at 11PM Eastern Time, 6th April.

Order form: https://goo.gl/forms/tgAwooNW6265WfMC2

More information about Alice Project 
TGR-Alice IC https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=95009.0
TGR-Alice test kit https://imgur.com/r/mechanicalkeyboards/TsK2G
TGR-Alice first teaser https://www.reddit.com/r/MechanicalKeyboards/comments/76azrc/tgr_alice/

Final design/photos:



- Engraving on the brass weight will be changed

More

- Grey top with pink bottom










Supported layouts:

- As per image https://i.imgur.com/HJEWqRq.jpg
- Split backspace
- 2.75u right shift
- ISO left shift

Specifications:
  • Full Kobelco 5052 aluminum case
  • 8 degrees slope
  • Brass weight
  • Plate material: sand-blasted brass
  • Custom ps2avrGB PCB
  • Fully Programmable
  • Full LED backlighting
  • Controller parts, diodes and resistors are presoldered

Color options:
Black, Silver, Grey, Pink and Red


- Black


- Grey/Silver


- Pink


- Red

Price: (excluding paypal fees and shipping)
$463 for case + plate + weight + PCB (Pre-soldered with controller parts, LED's resistors, and diodess)
$90 for brass plate + PCB (Pre-soldered with controller parts, LED's resistors, and diodes)
$15 for RGB Kit (Acrylic diffuser for case, RGB LEDs and Attiny85 controller)

Shipping: Shipping will be from Malaysia. Shipping cost will depend on your location. Each set weighs about 4.0 kg with packaging. Shipping fee will be invoiced only after the keyboards are ready. Estimated shipping cost (US 60$, Asia 45$, Europe $55)

Deadline for order/payment: The group buy will end as soon as the QTY is met, would appreciate if I am able to receive the payment asap. 

Lead time: 60-120 days

Thank you!
