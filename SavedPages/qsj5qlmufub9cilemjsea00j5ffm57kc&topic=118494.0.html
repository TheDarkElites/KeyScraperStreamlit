About:
More
Gridiron was developed when dededecline realized that there was not a premium, high profile case option for Planck PCBs that felt relevant in 2022. Gridiron works to give both a modern, premium case and also be incredibly affordable. The case is light but satisfying in your hand, and easy to travel with. The top mount gives a much nicer typing experience than the old school tray mounts, and was developed to give a consistent sound and feel.

Group buy details:
  • Price: $140 without PCB, $170 with PCB, with plenty of upgrades to choose from
  • Format: FCFS (unlimited)
  • Date: Tuesday October 4th - Sunday November 6th
  • Colourway options: black anodization, and a ton of cerakote upgrades available
  • Layout options: Anything a USB-C Planck PCB supports, all Gridiron PCB layouts, selected Plop PCB layouts
  • Estimated shipping timeline: March - April 2023

Board info:
  • 3° typing angle
  • 18.5mm front height without feet, 19.5mm front height with feet
  • Top mount
  • QMK and VIAL-compatible USB-C PCBs
  • Seamless side design
  • Weighs roughly 1 lbs 3 oz (540g) built

Kit Includes:
  • Case top and bottom
  • Plate of your choice
  • (Optional) Gridiron (ortho) or Plop (staggered) PCB
  • Feet
  • Screws

Extras:
  • EVA case foam: $10
  • EVA plate foam: $10
