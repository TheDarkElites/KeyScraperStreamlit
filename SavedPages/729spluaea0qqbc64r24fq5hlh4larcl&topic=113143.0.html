Group Buy is closed!!! Thank you to everyone who supported this set, I can't wait to get it to you very soon!


Signature Plastics has a lead time for DSA sets at under four months!
Expect this to get into your hands shortly after the GB closes!




DSA Historic is a 40s first doubleshot DSA (uniform) ABS set from SP inspired by the Vegas Golden Knights primary jersey, using text mods and solo numerical/mod legends as seen on DSA Quartz.

This set will be using stock Signature Plastics colors:
Slate Grey-Blue (GSM) alphas
Black (NN) modifiers
Golden (YCB) legends
Red (RA) accents


More


Allow me to elaborate on the inspiration to this set. Being born and raised in Las Vegas, there was very little to identify ourselves with when I was a kid. We were constantly poked at for not having culture, only as a city that was meant to entertain everybody else. This silly little misfit of a team brought so much to a city full of misfit locals and was able to finally give us all in this town something of OUR OWN to cheer for and identify ourselves with. Nobody thought hockey would work in the desert, everybody doubted it just as they had doubted Las Vegas locals throughout my whole life. Boy did we show them. They have been nothing but wonderful regarding community events, and were a gigantic factor during the healing process for this city after the shooting in 2017. This team made the biggest impact I have ever seen in my local community, and I would love nothing more than to simply share my love for these colors with you all.






This set was kitted for cost effectiveness for small board users, prioritizing physical compatibility for many popular small boards from Preonic to Minivan to Prime e. There will also be a child kit that includes keys that will help support larger modded smaller boards, as well as physical compatibility for 60-65% keyboards. The goal is to support the keyboards I found myself using daily made by the wonderful people of the 40% community.



$75 40's Base kit
Physically covers most small keyboards from Minivan, to Preonic, to Corne, whatever!
Worked really hard with the 40's community to make sure everybody was covered.



$45 60-65% Kit.
Wanted to make a kit that was small enough to attach to the base for larger mod 40's, while also giving compatibility to 60-65%s.

Alpha legends may not be completely accurate in these kitting pictures or renders, refer to this image to get a better idea of what the set will be using.

Hey everybody, the sets have arrived at Aeternus!



I'm generally really happy with the colors, and I'll be sure to get some more pictures in different lighting for ya'll once I have a set in my hands. Unfortunately, there's a few slight issues that we're going to attempt to resolve. In the two images below are a layout of the base kit, and the 60s kit. The only issue we've found is the alighnment of the backtick and quote key. We've e-mailed SP about this, and they will be replacing them, so expect shipping to begin right as Aeternus gets those within a few weeks.

A note unfortunately about the 6u bar, we didn't know before but SP DSA 6u bars aren't actually the standard 6u bars you would normally use. They have a different mounting point for stabs that, from what I understand, isn't a widely used footprint. tl;dr, your 6u compatible board won't be compatible with the 6u bar you'll be receiving from Historic if you bought the 60s kit. SP also unfortunately does not seem to want to provide a fix for this at the moment, as that would require a new mold. I'm really, terribly sorry about this.





Thanks again to everyone who supported this!! If you missed out, there will be a handful of extras available once they all ship out. If you have any questions or issues, don't hesitate to contact me or Aeternus.
