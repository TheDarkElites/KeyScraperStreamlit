[GB] Jūbi | 60%+ Custom Keyboard | (GB Complete)
« on: Thu, 27 February 2020, 11:28:46 »


Launch Date: February 28th
Pricing & Availability:
-50 units
-Time: 12:00PM PST
-Price: $450
-Vendor: Switchplate Peripherals
-ETA: 4-6 months after GB close

Specs & Features:
  • 6° typing angle
  • 6063 aluminium
  • 17mm front height
  • Seamless
  • Top mounted Red aluminum plate or pcb mounted (modified version of what was used on the Equinox)
  • Semi-fixed aluminum plate (plate file will be made available)
  • WT60-XT USB-C PCB by wilba.tech (QMK/VIA Compatible)
  • Case colors: Black, Silver, Dark Gray
  • Case options: Standard or WKL top
  • Polished stainless weight


Layout support:



More images:




























*Please note: The prototype was intentionally left raw, as such all machining marks are visible.*


Proto --> Production changelog:
  • Front edge of the top even with the base
  • Case corners increased fro 3mm to 5mm
  • Base corner fillets changed to match the top corner fillets
  • Top mount positions overhauled
  • PCB mount style overhauled
  • PCB mount positions overhauled
  • Weight position and size overhauled


FAQ/Other notes:
  • This board will NOT be made at the same factory as the Southpaw boards.  This will be made at a factory that I have personally used and I will have full communication with the rep that will be handling this project.  I will be providing all updates.
  • The dual mounting options will not interfere with each other no matter which option you choose to use.
  • Ion Keyboards Discord Server
