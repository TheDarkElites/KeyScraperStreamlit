ePBT Acadia

GB January 11 - February 11, 2022
Acadia is one of the most unique and beautiful National Parks in the United States. It is a connection between natures hidden treasures and mankind's achievements. When I began this project last year I had just returned from this incredible island off the coast of Maine, USA. The trip was one of the most inspirational experiences I have experienced in my, so far, short life. I wanted to take my love of the outdoors and natural beauty and share it with all the people in this community. Here is my first keyset, ePBT Acadia!

Looks like this has several ways to go sadly. Judging by the KBDfans update and several other ePBT GBs, it looks like this still has to go through sample creation, send samples to designer and if not approved, then another round of sampling until approved and once approved, only then can it be queued for production and no way do we know how long the queue list for production is and production may take anywhere from 1 month or more, packaging, shipping arrangements to vendors, transit times, vendor preparation to ship to end consumers, another transit times, and finally, delivery to end consumers. so, may be roughly another 5-8 months give or take?

That sounds right. A lot of it depends on how quickly the designer approves the samples. I think it's something like this...

  • Samples are approved (1-2 months, more depending on if further rounds are needed)
  • Into production queue (2-3 months, but it looks like this might actually be more like 6 months depending on the queue)
  • Shipped to vendors (1-2 months, depending on vendor)
  • Shipped to you (1-3 months, depending on vendor.... Kono in the US, which doesn't have a stellar reputation  )

So yeah, probably 5-10 months best case scenario. A few more months for extra samples, and a few more months depending on the production queue.

I wouldn't be surprised if this set doesn't ship until Q4 2023 or later, since it seems like there have been quite a few ePBT group buys that have dragged on for a long time lately. ePBT Solaria ran in June 2021 and still hasn't shipped, it's been "queued for production" since June (over six months). It does seem like China is easing up a lot of pandemic-related lockdowns, so maybe production times will get better soon; I know lockdowns have had a huge impact on keycap timelines.

Kono still says "Estimated production completion Q4 2022/ Q1 2023" though, so who knows
