  • 5 Colors
    • Pink
    • Silver
    • Gray
    • Red
    • Black
    • New Color for 25 MOQ: Navy
    • New Color for 50 MOQ: Blue Gray
  • Limited to 75 units FCFS
  • Standard, HHKB, WKL Top
  • Brass Through Weight, Brass and Aluminum Plates
  • 5 Degree Angle
  • 17.5mm front
  • 1.6kg | 3lbs 10oz when built
  • Weights will be numbered in corner opposite of `QXP` text
  • Plates are Milled
  • Works with standard 60% PCB. I'm not designing PCBs but a lot of people wanted one. If you order a PCB I'm going to buy a DZ60 or DO60 and upcharge and ship it to you.
  • Pricing
    • Kit Includes: Top, Bottom, Brass Weight, Choice of Plate
    • MOQ 25: $285
    • MOQ 50: $265
    • MOQ 75: $250 <--- We right here.
    • PCB: $40
    • Extra Plate: $40
    • PayPal fee of 4.4% + 0.3 will be applied

Shipping:
The boards will be bulked shipped from China to me in the US. I will then ship them out to customers from the US. I'm sorry if you live in Asia I don't have a proxy . I will charge for shipping once I receive the boards.

Order Changes:
I will not change anything once the invoice has been paid in PayPal, so please choose carefully.

Extras:
There will be some undecided amount of extras ordered (Depends on GB numbers). These will also be FCFS after all GB orders have been fulfilled and will be more expensive.

Swapping Spots:
If you want to swap your spot that's fine, but I'm not going to issue you a refund via PayPal and invoice the other person. I will ship the board to you, and you can ship it on to the next person after you receive it.

Contacting Me:
The best place to find me is on the #kb-kustoms channel on the r/mk discord.  I'll try to respond to messages on Geekhack but it will be much quicker if you use discord.  You can also message me directly at Quantrik#1337.

Invoice Timing:
Invoices will come when we hit 75 orders or when May 1st comes around.  You will have 48 hours to pay your invoice, after that the order will be submitted to the factory.

Group Buy Timeline:
Production: ~60-90 days
Shipping to me: ~14 days
QC + Shipping to you: ~14 days

Good Luck purchasing a board and thank you for supporting me if you join .
