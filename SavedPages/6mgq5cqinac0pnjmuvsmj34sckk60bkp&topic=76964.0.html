Long story short, for more information, please refer to the interest check thread here

Engineering sample:



More
















Final design:



More








Specifications:
  • Full aluminum 7075 case
  • 8 degrees slope
  • Electro nickel coated brass weight
  • Plate material: aluminum
  • Support 84 and 86/87 layout
  • ISO and ANSI
  • A87U PCB
  • Fully Programmable
  • Full LED backlighting
  • Controller parts are pre-soldered
  • Only 25 sets
Color options:
Blue grey, pink, black(hard anodize) and olive green(hard anodize) 


blue grey


pink. photo by filphil


olive green

Price: (excluding paypal fees and shipping)
$420 for case + plate + pcb
$4 for diode and resistor 
$24 for diode and resistor + soldering

Shipping:
Shipping will be from Malaysia. Shipping cost will depend on your location. Each set weighs about 3.5 kg with packaging. Shipping fee will be invoiced only after the keyboards are ready.

Deadline for order/payment: I have risked to start the production in order to have these boards ready before Christmas. So the groupbuy will end as soon as the MOQ is met, would appreciate if I am able to receive the payment asap. 

Lead time: 1 week before Christmas 

Notes:
  • I am still experimenting with the diffuser, ordered the transparent and semi-transparent acrylic from Plexiglass to test which is better. If nothing goes wrong, I will include the option to purchase diffuser when I invoice for the shipping. It should cost not more than $15 for the diffuser.
  • The plate and pcb are compatible with the R1 case.
  • If you want to make changes to the order, please PM me
  • If you are keen but can't pay soon, please PM me
  • I will visit geekhack everyday, so if you have any questions, please feel free to PM or reply to the thread
  • Invoices will be sent through Paypal, there will be Paypal fees added to the price. Unless you want to send the money through gift 

CLICK TO ORDER

Thank you!
