ePBT DuO

When?

August 23 2021 - Sept 27 2021

Vendors

US - ThocKeys
UK - Prototypist
EU - MKeu
CA - DeskHero
OCE - DailyClack
CN + Intl - kbdFans
SA - FancyCustoms

Delivery around Q2 2022.

DuO Kits

Base ($89) & Numpad ($16)


Extension ($32) & International ($18)


Macro + Novelty ($29)


DuO Photos
*Note I am not a professional, or a good photographer, some colors or contrasts will vary from screen to screen. Allow for some differences. Thought it could be fun to use photos instead of renders.
There are some slight differences between photos and kits. There were some updates after getting the samples.

Matrix 1.2 & kbdPad


Beige Kits

Modifiers ($45) & Macro + Novelty ($29)


Beige Photos

daji seis cinco by juaninamilli

This is a little passion project that's been in the slow cooker, for about the past two to three years, in one form or another. Hope you might like it.

While the set cannot be tailored to everyone's individual needs and opinions, I hope that this will both look good to those that might enjoy the idea or colors, and also still potentially be functional to those that might use Arabic in their day to day.

ePBT DuO Prototype Album Here

DuO was the winner of simon's first Battle Royale, Keycap Edition! What a fun stream that was, thank you all so much!
