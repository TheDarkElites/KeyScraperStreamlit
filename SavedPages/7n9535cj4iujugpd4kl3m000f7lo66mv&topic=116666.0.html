Never Heard of us?

As a newcomer to the community, Aiglatson Studio focuses on materializing retro-futuristic electronic designs. The studio is based in Thailand and was originated in early 2021 with three founding members that shared the same passion and desire. Ultimately, we aim to deliver uniquely designed products from the combination of art and technology that reflect the retro perception of the modern future, providing our customers with an astonishing physical and emotional experience.

Concept

The Synchronize deskmat is inspired by the concept of inner perfection. It symbolizes the balance between and the synchronization of perfection and imperfection within oneself’s mind. Although human minds are full of flaws and distorted thoughts, we believe that perfection in feeling is possible, and inner perfection only comes when we leave room for imperfection. The figure is generated using binary numbers, zeros and ones, symbolizing the potential and the creative spirit of Aiglatson Studio.
