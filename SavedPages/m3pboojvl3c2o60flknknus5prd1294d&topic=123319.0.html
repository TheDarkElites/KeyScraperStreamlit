Inspiration & Design
The design of the keyboard is inspired by the world-view setting of the game “Cyberpunk 2077”. To fit the worldview setting in 2077, the designer initially divided the keyboard into three versions. These three versions are also the three states of people:
    The first version: Natural people, without mechanical transformation, people use their flesh and blood to survive in the city. Just like most customized keyboards use metal as the main material.
    Goodra (Silver Alu Case + Lemon Yellow Alu Weight)


    The second version: People with mechanical prosthetics. To pursue stronger strength and wisdom, people change parts of their bodies into machines through mechanical transformation. Here, just like the combination of metal and organic materials on the keyboard, you can get a richer material contrast. At the same time, just like everyone adds more functions to the keyboard, the keyboard can have more possibilities.
    Special Deadpool 911 (Green Grey Alu Case + Red Acrylic Frosted Weight)


    The third version: People who digitize their consciousness. People no longer limit themselves to matter but upload their consciousness to the virtual world. Reincarnation is also the next life. The embodiment of the keyboard is to replace metal with acrylic in large quantities, just like abandoning the body.
    Special Edition - Afterlife (Green Acrylic Case + Yellow Acrylic Frosted Weight)



    Feature

    Cyber + industrial style
    The combination of different materials makes the keyboard look non-monotonous
    Exquisite and compact, can be carried anywhere and does not take up space
    Support split space that makes the layout more symmetrical
    Light strips on the front and back (color changeable)
    Front signal light: displays connection information and battery status


    Backlight: the light will shine directly on the table just like a car’s fog light




    Specifications
    Mount:Gasket Mount
    Support Windows & Mac
    Support VIA (Do not support QMK)
    Driver: ZOROKB driver
    Support ANSI layout (Do not support ISO layout)
    Support 3U, 6U space (Do not support 6.25U, 7U space)
    1.2mm Hotswap No flex-cuts PCB
    Plate material: PC
    Keyboard Weight: About 500g
    Dual mode: Wired, Bluetooth
    Rolling rate: 1000Hz (wired), 500Hz (Bluetooth)
    Battery: 2400mAh(1200mAh*2)
    Typing angle: 6.5 °
    Front height: 16.5mm
    Size: 263 x 93 x 26mm
    No full keys RGB
