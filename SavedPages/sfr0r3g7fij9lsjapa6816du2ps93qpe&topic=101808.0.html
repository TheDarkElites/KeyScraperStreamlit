Hey, thanks for your questions and support of the set!

The Mission Map deskmat is based off of this mission map:

Which is why the planets are Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune. Yes, Jupiter has very feint rings but we decided to omit them and focus on it's lines and swirls inside the planet itself. Uranus and Neptune have swirls because we based them off the novelties of the set. I understand that Uranus is mainly one solid color with no swirls, but when creating the novelty it was quite boring with either a completely hollowed out middle or the opposite - completely infilled. The swirls were our way to make them more interesting and aesthetically pleasing.
