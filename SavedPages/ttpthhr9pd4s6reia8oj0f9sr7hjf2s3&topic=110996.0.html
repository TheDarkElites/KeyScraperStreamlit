Place your orders here

NEO Palette G67 Carbon groupbuy starting on Feb 5th, at 11:00am of Hongkong Time (UTC+8) (https://www.timeanddate.com/worldclock/converter.html), ending on Feb 21st.
GB has ended.
This will be an unlimited gropubuy, there will be no MinOQ or MaxOQ.
Lead time will be 60-90 days, and will not be affected by Chinese New Year holidays.
Join NEO Keys Discord for more information and progress updates












Palette Carbon G67 kit will include:
1.   Full Torayca 3k twill weave carbon fiber case (4 layers of case body, 2 layers of feet), extra acrylic bottom layer(optional if you prefer RGB lighting)
2.   1.5mm carbon fiber plate
3.   Hotswap or soldered PCB
4.   Poron Gasket
5.   Plate/PCB foam and PCB/case foam
6.   Silicone Feet
7.   M2x8 flat head screws *32 (4 extra)
8.   M2x10 brass posts *16 (4 extra)
9.   M2x4 round head screws *6 (2 extra)
10.   M2x5 brass posts *6 (2 extra)
11.   M2x3 low profile flat head screws *16 (4 extra)
12.   M2x3.5 custom aluminum posts *8 (2 extra)
13.   USB-C Cable
14.   Microfabric cloth

Extra PCB/Plate kit available for sale as well
The kit includes:
1.   PCB
2.   Plate
3.   PCB/Plate foam
4.   Gaskets
5.   M2x3 low profile flat head screws *16 (4 extra)
6.   M2x3.5 custom aluminum posts *8 (2 extra)

PCB Features:
NEO67 Hotswap PCB
-QMK programmable
-North facing switch mount
-Per-switch SMD RGB light
-RGB blacklight
-Center mounted USB-C port


NEO67 Soldered PCB
-QMK programmable
-South facing switch mount
-ISO, Split Backspace, 7U spacebar compatible
-RGB blacklight LED x18! Ultra bright!
-Center mounted USB-C port
