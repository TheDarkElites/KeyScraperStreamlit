PLX
Gasket Mounted 60%





TECHNICAL SPECS

Typing Angle: 7o
Front Height: 21mm
Materials: aluminium top & middle case, brass bottom weight
Weight: 2.7kg built
Colours: anodised black | e-white | anodised red
Mounting Method: gasket (poron)
Plates: 1.5mm aluminium | 1.5mm polycarbonate | 1.5mm FR4 | 1.5mm POM
Top Case Options: WK | WKL | HHKB
PCB: variable soldered layout | designed by 2Moons (Basekeys) | USB-C daughterboard | configurable with QMK/VIA | No LED support | Includes ESD Protection or fixed hotswap layout
Solderable PCB Layout Options: locked tsangan bottom row | split BS | split RS | ISO | stepped caps
Hotswap PCB Layout: locked tsangan bottom row | splits BS | split RS



Soldered PCB Layout                                                                                                                      Hotswap PCB Layout


RELEASE INFORMATION

Vendor = Peac Design
Soft cap= 300 units
Dates = 1 February - 15 March
Expected Delivery = Q4 2022
Colour Options = Rosso (red) | Phantom (black) | Bliss (white)

Phantom

What’s in PLX keyboard kit?
1 x custom PLX carry case & cleaning cloth
1 x top case | anodised or e-coated aluminium
1 x mid case | anodised or e-coated aluminium
1 x weight | pvd brass
1 x plate | anodised aluminium
1 x plate foam | poron
1 x PCB (solderable), JST cable, usb-c daughterboard
21 x poron gasket strips
4 x bumpons | silicone
1 x screw and accessory bag

Note: extra plates, PCBs (solderable and hotswap), top cases, weights and accessories will also be available for purchase.
