Hey everyone,

I got a few requests to run an additional round of these boards, so I'm posting here to see if there's enough interest to fund a batch or two. Rev1.1 fixes two issues reported by Kei users (namely, JST header placement and the bottom-right indentations), expands the profile cutouts to support more boards, includes two daughterboards in the SKU, and comes in either two- or four-channel configurations.



Available now for preorder
https://senselessclay.com/

Description
A custom, universal-ish 60% PCB initially designed for the Unikorn and Kei keyboards from TGR x Singa and Monokei. Customers have mentioned compatibility with the Bakeneko from Cannon Keys as well, and this should work with the upcoming Frog Mini from Geonworks.

Comes pre-flashed with a standard ANSI layout but is intended to be customized further with QMK and VIA. Support for split Backspace, stepped Caps Lock, ANSI/ISO Enter, split Left Shift, and split Right Shift as well as ANSI (6.25U Space), Tsangan, HHKB, and Winkeyless (7U Space) bottom rows.

Features
- Pre-soldered JST header for use with breakout connections conforming to the Unified Daughterboard Standard
- Two (2) daughterboards, one UDB and the other with a 1mm USB extension (for out-of-spec implementations)
- Two (2) JST cables, 150mm and 50mm
- One (1) bookmark/ruler/straightedge/thing
- Caps Lock LED support
- ESD protection along data and power lines
- Overcurrent protection on power line
- Noise filtering on power and shield connections
- Alternative O-Ring mounting positions for case designers
- "SIXTY" ambigram by the designer on front
- Accented silkscreen on bottom highlighting traces
- Custom packaging

Price
$45 (+$2 if removing USB Connector)

If you currently own a board from R1 and were disappointed with the initial fit in the Kei, please reach out to me on Discord for a discount, hadi#8307

Timeline
Group Buy : October 16 - November 1 (may be extended if MOQ is not hit in time)
Order/Production : November 2021 - December 2021
Shipping/Fulfillment : December 2021 - January 2022
Updates will be posted on the site's 'updates' page, in the Discord server, and in this thread.

Logistics
Fulfillment currently only planned in North America, but adding a new country is easy enough if you're unable to find a proxy service and are willing to pay international shipping. Let me know if you're interested in having a board shipped to you and I'll open up the option in the store.
