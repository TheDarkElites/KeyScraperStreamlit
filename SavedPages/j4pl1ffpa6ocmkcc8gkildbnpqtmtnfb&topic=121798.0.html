Design inspiration
The inspiration for this design comes from my English shorthair cat at home. He eats and drinks my food every day! You look down on me like I'm arrogant. So I wanted to make a theme about cats under the name "Arrogant Cat".

I see many friends who have one or more cats at home, or both. I don't know if you feel the same way. Sometimes you want to strangle it, and sometimes it has to melt you with a small move.

In short, it brings us company and happiness in an era when it is less and less difficult to socialize in real life.

So I took my impression of mischief and expressed it in icon form. Use a soft lilac color scheme to match the "arrogant" theme. Not really arrogant, but a kind of company.

I hope you enjoy it, too.

This rendering is also in accordance with my cat's daily state to restore. A table full of hair is inevitable!

It is worth mentioning that in order to protect rare animals, I also designed an ICON that prohibits dragon slaying, I hope you like it.
