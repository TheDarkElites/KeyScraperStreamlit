[GB]Class 65 v2-Coming
« on: Wed, 23 February 2022, 03:55:49 »
Hi，we are MM Studio.

It's been a long while since the in-stock FCFS, and as the request of many guys, our decided to relase the Class65 v2 GB. Class65 v2 carries our understanding of the classics and our insistence on practicality.

The updates for Class65 v2 as follows:
1. Color we retain the retro white, ano-black and ano-silver, Space Gray instead of E-pink.


2. We have added Ble version ,and the wired version will be equipped with foam in the location of the battery, you can also order extras filling weight.

3. Ble version solution is provided by Dana, the battery is 3000mAh, and support custom key change, Ble version without Capslock Light. The wired version will retain the Capslock Light.


4. the PCB space bar is optimized, and it should be noted that the Ble version only supports hot-swap layout.

5.We have also make some layout changes on the Plate - to go to the long slot, which is make the typing more consistent and more liner .

For more details on Class65 v2, pls refer our Class65 IC:
https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=115182.0

More
Class65



 The Class65 designed by us, MMStudio, was inspired by
 
by the artist Florian Renner his take on the modern Gameboy gave us the idea to try to incorporate parts of its design into the board.

Please make sure to give feedback on the board on the IC FORM

Design Elements


The Class65 features a Macro column and an exploded 65 column, with a plateless design. It uses leaf springs gaskets in order to have a both soft and bouncy typing experience. There is an optional POM plate. To help give it a strong sound we implemented a hidden brass weight. We will offer two types of plates PCB mount and Plate mount. This option was done because we wanted to allow for others to chose what they wanted. The ease of access to stabs with plate mount, or the stronger connection with PCB mount. Final design decision was center USB-c Port because Centered life is best life.

With an expected price of 270 for the whole kit

The GB will be held on our site MMStudio
