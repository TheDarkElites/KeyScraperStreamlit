[GB] Wind x65 - a beginner friendly 65% with all new IXPE switch foam
« on: Thu, 30 December 2021, 12:10:20 »

Hello everyone, this is Wind studio. We are here to present you our second project: the Wind x65


First of all, we want to thank everyone who participated in the first in-stock sale and the GB of the Wind x, our first board. If you want to check it out, visit the GH IC Post

We sold over 600 pcs of Wind x, which is insane for a totally new studio, thanks everyone for your warm support!
Now that all the in-stocks of Wind x are shipped (GB units are also in production), we want to start our next project.
There were a lot of learnings for a new team like us, here we want to again thank everyone on our Discord server, who gave us so many valuable feedbacks and helped us maintain our small community, special thanks to Carb0n12!

1. Updates
December 30th, 2021: first Version online

2. General
Please join our Discord server to get any update in real time, make sure you don't miss out! We will hold a give-away of the Wind x65 shortly before the sales there!

Inspiration
Basically, we carried on with the design of the Wind x, which is a F-rowless 1800 board, where you actually have a full size numpad with 2U “0” key!, everyone who use their keyboard at work, who has the even remotely worked with Excel knows how important a 2U “0” key is, right?!



Our first project: The Wind x

Now, since the Wind x also got overwhelmingly positive feedback on it’s sound and typing feel, we kept the design language and now we can present to you: the Wind x65


the new Wind x65 in orange
What's new
Maybe existing Wind x owners already noticed, WHERE IS THE NUMPAD?!
It’s a 65%, silly. Also, we changed the bronze badge in the front to PC to let the LED below shine through, this was highly requested by our community.
Colors are the mostly the same as the Wind x, orange and yellow were received very well by be community so we wanted to keep them.
Also RIP $50USD of discord fam @KandysDaddy, who ano'd his Wind x to green in a local store... we now also offer a moss green color

Ano Black + Green

3. Product Specs
Special Feature: a plate which is easy to disassemble and with access to stabs without desoldering. Also we used special switch foams (IXPE) to further enhance the sound(optional).
Dimension: 332.51 mm x 116.20mm x 19.29mm (Length x Depth x Height)

measurements

Materials:
  • Case: 6063 Aluminum
  • Weight: Brass
  • Front accent piece: PC shine through
Mount: gasket mount
Typing angle: 7°
Weight: TBC
Others:
  • USB Type-C connection is centered and connected to separate daughter-board
  • POM or Carbon plates
  • Software: QMK, also support .json import VIA

Layout
Soldered
Soldered version support both ISO and ANSI layout, stepped caps, 1.25U left shift and Tsangan.

Hot-swap
The Hot-swap version is pretty much a regular 65%, but only in ANSI layout.


POM and Carbon Plates(both pictures: top only support hotswap PCB, bottom support both solder and hot-swap)


Colors
E-white + Yellow



Ano Black + Grey



Ano Black + Orange



Ano Black + Green



4. Prices & Sales Info
About the sales
The Wind x65 will be a instock sale.
The sales will only be held on our own webstore: Wind Studio

Date
carefully set to January 15th, 2022, could be 1 week later; will last 4 weeks or until sold out.

Shipment
In-stock items will ship about 2-4 weeks after the sale ends, the time frame coincidences with the Chinese new year this time around hence a longer packing time.

Quantity
in-stock: FCFS 500pcs (China 100pcs, overseas 400pcs)
We will specify the color split before sales start and announce it on our Discord server, come join us!

Prices
Keyboard kit: $288USD
Extras:
  • Hot-swap or Soldered PCB: $45USD
  • Foam Kit: $20USD
  • POM/Carbon (support either soldered or Hot-swap version): $40USD

Inbox Content
  • The Wind x65 keyboard case (PC accent piece and weight ensembled)
  • 0.5mm silicon sheet
  • POM or Carbon Plate
  • 3.5mm Poron PCB foam (between PCB and plate)
  • 0.5mm IXPE switch foam
  • Soldered or Hot-Swap PCB
  • 2mm Poron PCB foam (between PCB and case foam)
  • 1mm Poron Case foam (PCB Foam and bottom case)
  • Enough gasket foam (2mm)
  • 4x Bumpon
  • AIO3 daughter-board
