yeah, it's actually happening
live on Sunday, the 11th of July at 8PM BST/12PM PST

US ONLY GB Form (50 units)

UK + Intl GB Form (50 units)

Original IC











Thanks to Yoot and Wilson for pictures!

Feet-ures
Feet silkscreen art (Zero Two's, specifically)
Hotswap
RGB underglow
ISO enter
OLED w/ custom walking animation tied to WPM (art by @savscarlett00)
QMK and VIA support (unofficial, instructions in the Discord but entirely functional)
Cool curved traces

The Kit
PCB
Standoffs (black nylon)+screws
0.91in OLED
WS2812B underglow LEDs
Hotswap sockets
Reset button
THIS KIT DOES NOT INCLUDE A BACKPLATE OR SWITCH PLATE, IT IS SCUFFED.
THE PCB JUST RESTS ON STANDOFFS.

Please note that this is a DIY kit that requires soldering (both SMD and through hole) and requires switches, keycaps, 1 2u stab, and a Pro Micro or other compatible controller to complete. Flush cutters and tweezers are also highly recommended for easy placement and making things neat.

Pricing and fulfillment
Kit will be shipped by my friends Atre (in the UK) for UK and intl buyers and Yoot (in the US only). Both will be charged £11 + shipping through a Shopify invoice. US shipping will be approx $8.00, UK approx £2.50. Intl will be based on country. 50 units per proxy, FCFS. If unit cap is not hit, expect the GB to end within 7 days. Invoices will be sent a few days after GB. Expect delivery late Q3 2021 subject to delays.
