[GB] RE65 ALUMINUM (FOAMLESS STEAL!!) // Open for orders
« on: Wed, 23 November 2022, 04:13:14 »
A stellar kit for the ultimate sound from Nuxroskb!
For those who get tired of foam sound
After various prototypes, this is now official!
Inspired by the iconic design from Star Wars: The Force Awakens
RE65 Aluminum is a dazzling spaceship, running illuminating LED "fuel" and powered by the RGB front bar. And sound godly.



We, Iconlabs, are honored to be the official vendor approved by Nuxroskb to bring this stellar kit to the mass.
The stunning design of RE65 Aluminum makes it perfect for anyone who seeks out the lifestyle of the "ultimate" sound and minimalistic elegance.


GB INFO
- GB Link: https://iconlabsofficial.com/group-buy-re65-aluminum/
- Start date: Nov 24th, 2022
- End date Dec 24th, 2022
- Est fulfillment date: Late January, 2023
- Price: RE65 Aluminum Kit is available in 3 versions, starting from just $240
- Featuring our cutting-edge Configurator (on our website) for your best visualization of choices

Its side arc features a geometric design to mimic not only the look but the feel on spacecraft when combined with the deep cutout for enhanced grip and ergonomics when used.


The bottom of this stellar piece holds a brass counterweight together with 4 parallel engraved lines ensuring its heftiness while maintaining the portability at the same time.



Nuxroskb the maker has carefully calibrated the measurements of the internal chamber to create an immersive listening experience, as free as floating in space. This kit is engineered to sound best without any foam and should sound even better with the added plate foam.

The RE65 Aluminum is not a real “real entry” 65% keyboard but a fully mechanical kit that features refined detailing and premium durability with the all CNC 6061 Aluminum build. The aesthetic and acoustic focus on the RE65 Aluminum redefine what it means to be the best entry level foamless keyboard today.
This could possibly be the best foamless sounding keyboard under $250 on the market.
