gaff. 60 - HHKB
- Specifications -
  • Fixed HHKB layout.
  • Seamless design.
  • 6° Typing angle.
  • Top mount.
  • 1.5mm aluminium sprung OR solid plate.
  • Internal brass weight.
  • Pre-Assembled PCB, designed by MaartenWut.
  • QMK/Via compatible.
  • USB Type-C.
  • USB-C -> USB-C Support.
  • ESD protection.

- Layout -



Original IC thread.


- Raffle -

The raffle with open July 10th at 3pm GMT. (2pm BST, 9am EST, 3pm CET)
There will be 30 slots available. The raffle is not FCFS, and will be open for 24 hours.

Base Kit      - $400, £322, €358
Extra Plate  - $45, £36, €40
Extra PCB   - $40 £32, €36

Shipping will be calculated at checkout.

Base kit includes the case, plate, PCB, custom moulded feet, and all necessary hardware.

https://gaff.works/raffle
