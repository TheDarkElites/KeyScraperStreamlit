Spec Summary
  • 8-degree typing angle with 2 internal brass weights (E-Clear coat treated)
  • Screwless exterior design (adjusted for switch  & plate clearance and ease of assembly)
  • Gasket Leaf Spring (full plate  & half-plate, multiple materials)[/*]
  • Solder and Hot-Swap PCB with flex cut (VIA ready) 1.6mm thick
  • Carry case included





﻿Standard Kit Contents
  • Top & Bottom case with Badge, LED diffuser & weights pre-installed
  • PCB KIT (Solder / HS)
  • Full Aluminium Plate
  • JST cable cover
  • Gasket poron foams (with extra)
  • Rubber Feet
  • Assembly Screws + Torx driver
  • Badge light-blocking foam
  • 3u stabilizer wire x 2
  • Anti Static sheet, Poron case & plate foam
  • Carry Case
﻿Extras
  • Full & Half plates in FR4, Aluminium, Full Plates in PC, POM
  • Solder & Hot-swap PCB with Daughterboard and cable
  • 3u stabilizer wire pack (2 wires per pack)
  • Gaskets
  • Foam Pack
  • Additional Screws
  • Feets
