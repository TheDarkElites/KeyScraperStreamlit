HI

I'd like to introduce my custom KBD called as "BOK.CP"
I have been preparing this KBD and soon we will be able to meet this KBD.





[Specifications]
•Limit of 50 sets
•Aluminum case 6061
•8 degrees slope
•Brass weight
•Support material: Brass
•ISO and ANSI
•Custom PCB by Leeku
•Fully Programmable
•Full LED backlighting
•Controller parts, diodes and resistors are presoldered
•Layout
  - Capslock : Cherry / OEM
  - Bottom
    1.5 1.5 7.0 1.5 1.5
    1.25 1.25 1.25 6.25 1.5 1.5
  - Numpad + : x1 / x2

[Color options]
•Anodizing option : Black, Grey and Silver
•Antique option : Gold-Gray, Red-Gray (This is not anodizing. New surface treatment.)
※.About antique surface treatment.
1. plating with chrome.
2. plating with copper.
3. strip copper away by hair line.
4. preventing fingerprint coating.



[Price]
price is not including Paypal fees and Shipping cost.
I will send invoices through Paypal to everyone, so you need a verified PayPal account to receive and pay. Also please check the information carefully before submitting your order.
(1) Base Kit (Top + Bottom + brass plate + PCB + brass weigh bar)

     - In case of Antique option, +20USD
(2) Optional
     - Additional Brass plate support : 55USD
