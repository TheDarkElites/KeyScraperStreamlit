DCS Honeywell
Hello Geekhack. I have been disgruntled with the lack of options when it comes to Signature Plastics DCS. Barring a few projects that have been going on over the last few months, there have been very little in ways of offerings for DCS. This, and an admiration for the timeless color-way that is Honeywell, together has prompted the idea of trying to organize a run of DCS Honeywell - credit to Miruim and 7bit for bringing this classic colourway to us in the first place via Deskthority.

Timeline
Group Buy: April 05 - May 05
Delivery: 4 - 5 months

Vendors
US: Divinikey
EU: Keygem
Asia: Geonworks
ME: Sandkeys
UK: Prototypist
AUS: Daily Clack

Price
Base: $125

Details
Material - ABS
Legends - Doubleshot
Colors - WFK, GPA, RAS

Kit


Renders


Inspired Honeywell Hall Effect Keyboard by Honeywell



900cl by Pontus



600cl by Pontus

Discord
Follow more of my projects here.

livingspeedbump/Andy for his blessing.

Seoulcialite for the valuable information he has given to me.
NathanAlphaMan for doing fantastic renders. It's great to see DCS models getting better and better.
Pontus for cool renders.
Geon and every vendor for making this possible.
huey for giving me the opportunity to help revive this project.
You for reading.
