The Liminal

A 50% Focused around affordability and superb "base-kit" compatibility, making it simple to outfit with normal full-size keysets.

Group Buy To Run
March 28th - April 1st (10:00AM CST)
Here on P3D's Store Front

- 50 KITS MAXIMUM WILL BE UP FOR PRE-ORDER -

LINK TO INTEREST CHECK THREAD





The Liminal Keyboard came about when I saw the new style of a few sub-60's that focused there boards on being easier to outfit with the ABUNDANCE of gmk sets we have on the market today.  A lot of big sets run with 40's specific kits but that is almost always an additional cost.  Us 40's folk are die-hard makers and enjoy the hobby not just for its $8000 Keycult TKL's or $400 Avenues... we like to BUILD!.  Thats half the fun of the hobby!

The Equinox, TMOv2, and others were amazing boards that sold well and really opened people up to the ideas of sub-60s without the "need" for specialized 40's kits. 

BUT SATISFIED I WAS NOT I SAY!

I wanted MORE.

After speaking with Perrine of P3D, he expressed interest in designing and running a case of his design and running GB for the Liminal. I am very excited to be able to work with such a fantastic maker and well established vendor within the community.

The Liminal is a fun, new, 50% keyboard kit that's a great way for newcomers to dip your toes in the small form factor, or for seasoned 40% veterans looking for a fresh layout.


Liminal PCB
Main Features:

- Concept and PCB Design by Me The_Royal
 
 - Spectacular Keycap Compatibility (can be outfitted with most GMK and ePBT base kits)

 - Full ISO and ANSI Configurations (Split Left-Shift, ISO Enter, as well as 1.25u "Enter")

 - Split and Full-Size Bottom Row Options

 - USB Type-C Connection

 - RGB LED Indicator below "CAPSLOCK" Key

 - Breakout Connection Pins for RGB Underglow Strips

 - Aesthetically pleasing PCB (for the classy folk who care).

 - Sub 11mm Height (Basic FR4 Kit) Height

 - High Quality 3D Printed PLA Case (optional upgrade) for a discounted price only for this GB

 - Fully Supported and Powered by QMK (I will be trying to Get VIA setup to be an option as well

GB Opens for Orders Tomorrow (3/28/20) 10:00AM CST* on The P3D Store Front

Pricing:

- $55 Basic PCB and FR4 Plate Kit

 - $35 3D Printed Case (Optional Add-On).

*There will be a few extras, but pricing will be $75 per kit, and $55 per printed case post Group Buy.

Basic Kit Includes:

 - x1 Assembled Liminal PCB (Not Including Diodes or Switches) [Black/Gold]
 - x1 ANSI FR4 Plate [Black/Gold]
 - x1 ISO FR4 Plate [Black/Gold]
 - x1 FR4 Base Plate [Black/Gold]
 - x60 SMD and Through Hole Diodes (Your choice of which to use, to be soldered on by you)
 - x9 Brass Standoffs
 - x20 SS Torx Screws (With Allen Key Included)

*Required but NOT Included

    - USB Type-C cable
    - MX style switches and keycaps
    - Screw in or snap in PCB Mount Stabilizers (plate mount not supported)
    - Patience
    - Soldering Iron for assembly of switches and diodes


3D Printed Case Add-On Includes:
- Color Options

 - x1 P3D Designed Liminal Top Case
 - x1 P3D Designed Bottom Panel
 - All Necessary Hardware

3D Printed Case is completely optional in this Group Buy. It will be printed in house, and will be held to the same rigorous quality control standards of my other available cases. The cases will be available after the Group Buy ends, but pricing will increase $20 (see note *).

Shipping Time and Group Buy Agreements:

 - The Buy will last until April 1st, 2020 unless all 50 Units are sold.

 - Shipping time is estimated to be 6-8 weeks post GB end date. (This estimate does try to take into account current known delays due to the virus outbreak.)
