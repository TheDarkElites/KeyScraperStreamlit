Key Details

DSS 99¢ is a 40s-oriented keyset, with one robust kit to support a huge variety of 40s and 30s layouts. To accompany the base kit, we've partnered with Pseudoku/Asymplex to offer convex DXX minibars (designed to emulate a convex version of DSS R3) in sizes from 1u to 3u, as well as DSS ISO Enter. The Asymplex keys are available in two styles: opaque and artisan.
  • GB from August 24 - September 23
  • Cost: 115 USD (or local equivalent)
  • Vendors: Sneakbox (U.S.), KeebSupply (E.U.), and ergokeyboard.sg (Asia)
  • Manufactured by Signature Plastics
  • All SP stock colors (colormatching complete):
    • Alphas: VDR on VBV
    • Mods: RFJ on VCO
    • Accents: WA on RFJ
  • Doubleshot ABS in DSS profile (1-2-3-4-3 sculpt)
  • Trashcon Icons by TrashMan
  • Matching convex minibars and ISO Enter by Asymplex
  • Renders by Liv (OJ)
  • Original IC thread
