[GB] Custom printed sleeves - Mechfashion - September/October GB
« on: Sat, 01 December 2018, 17:19:33 »
Hi, geekhack!

Gallery of finished sleeves:
Check here!




Custom printed sleeves consist of spill-proof polyester outside, fleece inside, and foamy fabric between the exterior and interior to make the keyboard safer. On top of the sleeve there is either cordlock or magnetic flap closing. A pocket is available inside or outside of the sleeve. Every sleeve is made to order.

What you can customize?
  • side A & B of the exterior (just send me a link to pictures/files or RGB code)
  • color of interior (https://imgur.com/a/AlBUjLH)
  • size of the sleeve (it can be either tailored to specifc keyboard model or be a little bit oversized to fit every keyboard from specific size category))
  • for cordlock closing: color of the cord (https://imgur.com/a/xyd9d0o) and colors of tunnel closing on side A and side B (just tell me what colors you want to match or send RGB codes)
  • for magnetic flap closing: color of exterior and interior part of flap (just tell me what colors you want to match or send RGB codes)
  • let me know if you want a pocket

Example of the clear specification:
https://pastebin.com/5sb6wBds

I’ll be happy to help you with creating a design.
I can recommend colors for various parts of the sleeve, help you with designing pictures for the exterior or even make/edit them for you according to your description.
Just contact me!

Price: 38-41$ (depending on size)
Shipping price is 8$ worldwide
Payment via PayPal & credit/debit cards.

To place an order or just to contact me please use one of the following options:
- PM me here,
- email peter@mechfashion.com
- live chat on my website https://www.mechfashion.com/

Thank you,
Peter
