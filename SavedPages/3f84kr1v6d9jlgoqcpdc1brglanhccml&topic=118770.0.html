V65 R2 features:
  • 65% Standard Layout with a knob
  • VIA supported PCB with encoder
  • Aluminum alloy case with 3 colors. All CNC and powder coated.
  • Gasket mount (poron)
  • Included plate foam and case foam (all poron)
  • Included IXPE PCB foam
  • 2 modes: Wired + Bluetooth
  • South-facing per-key LEDs
  • Hot-swappable
  • Included keycaps.
  • Included screw-in stabilizers
  • USB-C daughterboard for maximum flex
  • PC Plate & FR4 Plate
  • ABS Doubleshot Translucent Keycaps included (comes with default color depending on your case choice)
Yes, you don't read it wrong, a whole set of keycap is included when you purchase this V65 kit. And it's translucent ABS doubleshot!
