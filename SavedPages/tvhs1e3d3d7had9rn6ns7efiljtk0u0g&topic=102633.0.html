The time has come. Gaskety goodness in PLA with FR4 plates - some would call this the ultimate symphony of keyboard acoustics.

iT SoUndS gOoD

50 kits to be made.
$185 per kit, which includes:

1x Top Case
1x Bottom case
1x KBD75v2 PCB (QMK, RGB, USB-C)
1x Universal FR4 plate
1x kit of the gaskets and hardware required to fasten everything

Extra tops are $15/ea. The seamless design enables the whole color of the keyboard to be changed simply by swapping the top.

Timeline:

Sept 27 - GB start
Oct 10 - GB ends (or sooner if all spots fill up)
All invoices are to be paid 3 days after the GB ends.
Production of cases - 1 to 1.5 months.
Mid November - Shipping from keyboardbelle to you.

Shipping will be invoiced at the time of sending.
