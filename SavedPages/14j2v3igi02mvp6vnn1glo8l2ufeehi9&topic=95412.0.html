Yes, I tested them. I have mine on my AEK64 right now with no issues.
If I hit the far edges of the spacebar really hard, the stabilizers hit the plate, but it hasn't hampered usage at all.

The Signature Plastics alps stems don't have that little "shelf" like the AEK caps do, so it is possible to insert the stem REALLY far into the switch. I found that backing the stem out a bit helps with preventing plate strikes.
