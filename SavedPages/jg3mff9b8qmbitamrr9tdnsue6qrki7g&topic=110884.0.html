We interrupt your program for an



Unfortunately GMK now decided, after approving the Novelties for GroupBuy (and making me adjust 3 minor details / lines prior to GB start), to not manufacture them due to them being to close to copyright material.
Directly after that, we decided to not only pull the Novelty Kit from vendor homepages but to completely re-design them. And from my / our point of view, after stressing really hard about getting this done ASAP, they are even better now.

They don't anymore show any too obvious game-referencing graphics, but are bound way more into the actual lore of the games and are a tribute to many insider jokes and audio queues of the game.

We have:

1. The aperture. Nothing has changed here.
2. IT IS A LIE. Nothing has changed here.
3. The cake. The cake is not only a lie, but is has been redesigned to match the new novelties' style.
4. A Science graphic, reffering to the many science-related audio queues. Mainly: "I think we can put our differences behind us. For science. You monster."
5. A lemon, referring to the many hilarious lemon-audio queues, like:  "When life gives you lemons, don't make lemonade. Make life take the lemons back!"
6. A burning house, referring to the audio queue: "I'm the man who's going to burn your house down! With the lemons! I'm going to get my engineers to invent a combustible lemon that burns your house down!"
7. WATCH OUT! LASERS! DON'T GET BURNED!
8. A simple heart. Solely a hommage to a certain cube shaped object.

Here they are:



In most cases vendors already sent out an email to customers who already purchased the Novelty Kit about the change and giving you the option to either get a refund or keep the new Kit.
If not, this email will arrive with you shortly.

I deeply apologize that this happened, but after getting the Novelties approved for GroupBuy by GMK, nobody saw this coming.
I did my best to resolve this issue to the best of my abilities, and to be quite honest, I am very happy with the change to now see the Set not being as 'obvious' as before but really paying a tribute for lovers of the game.
