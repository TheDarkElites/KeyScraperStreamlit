Thanks for your interest! Yes, they are pretty different than Matias. They have a much sharper tactility but don't have the second bump caused by the contact leaf. The click is a bit higher pitched and louder. They seem to fit the description of Amber Alps more than Whites (although I haven't tried Ambers personally)


I can't say if you would like them or not, since keyfeel is pretty subjective, but personally I would take these over Matias and definitely over Cherry blues. In fact, I'm going to build a 60% with these as my portable keyboard.
