Hi all we start !



IC - https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=110301.0



GB start: 20.01.2021 18.00 UTC
GB end:  20.02.2021 18.00 UTC
GB will end in a month from the start or when we sell all 150 kits



Group buy - GB will be FCFS, kits are limited to 150 + 150 Extra tops.
!!!IMPORTANT!!!! Only 1 kit and 1 extra top allowed per one person. Selling spots will lead to permanent ban for this and all our future group buys.
Plate CAD files will be publicly available after GB.





All the information are on the website - https://santigo.com.ua/product/gb-monoflex-keyboard-60/ and Discord - https://discord.gg/HQyEWAMpMF
Thank you all for your support


DESCRIPTION
•Manufactured by SantiGo Customs in Europa
•Design by  bizunow
•6.5 Degree Typing Angle
•Plate mounting style – something similar to sandwich mount (because plate has special cutout where it will be put and pressed between top and bottom, top and bottom between themselves)
•Material: Aluminum case 60xx series
•Carbon fiber leaf-spring plate (regular / half)
•POM plate (regular / half )
•PCB: type  C with daughterboard   and compatible with QMK, and hopefully VIA support
•Layout: 60% with  ANSI/ISO . As an extra option we have 3 different available HHKB/WKL/Regular
•Colors - In base kit Anodizing and Extra options Cerakote +35 eur all colors you can chek in web site





Price kit 225 eur Each KIT includes:
• 1x – Case (Top and Bottom)
• 1x – PCB Type C with daughterboard by keyten and ILWrites
• 2x Plate – One plate Carbon fiber Leaf spring or regular or Half depending on your choice and one POM plate regular Half or Regular.
• 2x – Two foams (from felt): one for bottom, second between PCB and plate
• 80x – Switch films from my friend the_mihalich
• 10x – Screws м2
• 8x – Board legs (4 black 4 with skull image (huge thank to T0mb3ry for boneyard picture)






Shipment and delivery time: - 4-5 month after GB Worldwide ship 32.5eur (Italia 15 eur) 

How much does the keyboard weigh?
The weight of the keyboard built is around 1.3kg +-
