Important note!  If you have a high profile case, you will need to attach the pro micro flat on the board, do not put the plastic standoff on the header pins under the micro or the bezel parts won't fit over it! For the other case options this is not necessary.

tape the pro micro in place on top of the board, but don't cover all the pins


drop the pins in


tape over them


solder a couple of the pins that are exposed to hold the header in place


remove the tape and solder the rest of the top of the pins


clip the pins off on the back side


solder the back side

Re: [GB] Houndstooth Split Plaid/RoMac style keyboard kits and PCBs (Shipping)
« Reply #23 on: Mon, 23 September 2019, 19:27:38 »
General assembly order:

Solder Pro Micro, reset switch, and Diodes

add stabilizers at this point as well if you are using them

put the spacer layer on, and drop in the hex standoffs, screw the round standoffs onto the threads under the PCB


place the plate on top and install the switches and 2x5 pin header, make sure the left header gap faces down, and the right half faces up



Solder in the switches, then install the bottom spacer over the standoffs, and then screw on the case bottom


last, install the case top screws and bezel layers for the high profile case.

The Pro micro is preprogrammed with this layout (it's missing some of the punctuation, sorry about that, so it'll need to be reflashed)

The QMK folder is avaialble here https://github.com/worldspawn00/Firmwares
