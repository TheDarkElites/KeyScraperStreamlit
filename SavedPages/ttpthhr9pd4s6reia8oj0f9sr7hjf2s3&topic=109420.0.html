Hi everyone, been a long time keyboard enthusiast but a first time IC/GB runner so bear with me.

Introducing...

Rukia Wrist Rest

Rukia has been one of my favorite board for awhile and I decided to make my own wrist rest and share it with the community. I receive permission and the wrist rest file directly from Jaxx already so I'll let pictures do the talking!



Enter GB Here

GB Timeline: 11/3/2020 - 11/17/2020
MOQ: 20 units in each material
Location: limited to US and Canada. However, will order limited extras that will be open to internationals
Shipping: Roughly $10 via USPS priority mail in US, $15-20 for Canada.
Price: PC $80 / Alu $72 + shipping. This is my estimate for 20 units. Apologies for higher prices than most but I do use a slightly higher price manufacture just because ease of communication/previous transactions. If total units exceed estimate and price is cheaper, will apply differences toward shipping cost.
Time: 2-5 weeks
Color: Grayish Light Turquoise (More gray hue than Pantone below)


Details on quality/fitment:
The fit will be perfectly aligned. However, in order for aluminum to anodize, there will need to be holes for it hook and hang. Therefore, there will be 2 drill holes on the back of the wrist rest where it oxidize and liquid will drip down; resulting in tiny bleed spot as pictured below. My solution to this will be putting bump-on that will be included in the purchase.



For alice-style (non Rukia) owners, this will also kind of work for your board but with a caveat.
Rukia is an alice-style board but with slightly different bottom contour. This wristrest will still fit your alice-style board but most likely will have a slight gap. Please refer to picture below for a rukia wrist rest on a Lubrigante
