Good, classic set back with KKB this time!
My orange and grey caps bring all the boys to the yard.

GB: Apr 30th to Jun 2nd
Estimated delivery - Q4 2024


DISCORD



---
K I T T I N G


BASE kit. Includes support for alice, limited 40s, HHKB, etc




NORDE kit. Get this for that authentic skidata look.




EXTENSION kit. Windowed keys, orange accents, terminal accents.




ALERT kit, with cooooolllll translucent keycaps




R5 kit, for da retro bois. NEW ALTGR R5 mold made for this.




Relegendables kit. Includes front print from ESC to F12 and 3 blanks.




UK kit. Will be included for free with every base kit purchase from Prototypist.




HIBI ARTISAN. Very excited about this, please make this happen. This is a homage to the original skidata keyboard, and its ball thingy.



-----
C O L O R S  &  S A M P L E S

Colors are be matched to GMK N9/V2/AE
