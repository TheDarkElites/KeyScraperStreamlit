Just wondering, is there a reason all PCBs out there are either hotswappable or Bluetooth, but never both? I can’t figure out why...

Good question. Hotswappable means single layout. Bluetooth, especially long battery life Bluetooth, is a relatively new feature to keyboard PCBs. We added tons of layout options on the Bluetooth PCB to meet everyone's needs. As for the wired version, I decided to use the WKL layout becuase I like this layout a lot and there's no hot-swappable 60% WKL PCBs available on the market afaik.
