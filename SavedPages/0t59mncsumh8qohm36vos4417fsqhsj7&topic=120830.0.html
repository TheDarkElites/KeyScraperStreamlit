Arc

Ergo 65XT, Available for pre-order now on shostudios.co




Arc is an ergonomic 65% keyboard with a 2-row macro column, split at a 12-degree angle. It features the familiar gummy O-ring mount and a front-facing walnut/maple accent.


Contents
  • Specifications

1. Specifications

Case Angle: 7.25°

Front Height (min-max): 19mm-22.3mm

Net Weight: 2kg (built with alu plate, Gateron X & GMKs)

Case Materials: 6063 Aluminium, Brass (internal-weight), Walnut/Maple (front accents)

Hardware Materials: SUS304 Stainless Steel (case screws & daughterboard screws)

Plate Materials: 6061 Aluminium & Carbon Fibre (1.5mm thickness)

PCB:
  • 1.6mm FR4 (no flex cuts) designed by Mechlovin’
  • QMK & VIAL Configurable
  • Daughterboard with ESD protection

Layout Options:


Keyboard mount:

Gummy O-ring with mounting posts


2. Sales & Pricing

Base kit price: SGD 630 (USD 468)

Kit includes:
  • 1x Keyboard Case (hardware pre-assembled)
  • 1x Solder-only PCB, daughterboard & JST cable
  • 6x Custom Silicone Feet
  • 1x Carrying Case

50 units min., 100 units max.
Targeted Completion: Early-Late Q4 2023

4. FAQ

  • Who are you and what is shostudios?
    I am the former designer and co-founder of Hex Keyboards. Having left the brand early this year, I have set up shostudios as a personal outlet to work on bringing my passion projects to life.
  • Will _____ color/wood/plate be available?
    Unfortunately not. I will be working on fulfilment alone for these projects so I’d like to keep things simple by reducing the number of SKUs I'm offering. I will be releasing the plate and wood accent files towards the end of fulfilment. I may also add a raw case option if there is enough demand for it. Let me know in the form linked below.
  • Why the 100 units cap?
    I will be working with limited space and manpower (my pair of hands) when it comes time for fulfilment. So even though I have 3 years of experience in keyboard fulfilment with my previous venture, I’d rather not stretch myself too thin.
  • Will you be engaging with any international vendors?
    Not for this project.
