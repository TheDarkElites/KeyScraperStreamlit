[GB] HSA White on Black - Beige on Brown
« on: Mon, 04 November 2019, 08:01:53 »
HSA or HALF SA is a double shot ABS keycap profile with a glossy finish.  As the name implies, it’s a shorter variation on the SA profile while still being spherical and sculpted.
It is a brand new profile created by JTK.
The keycaps come packaged in a JTK keycap tray

Available sets

HSA White on Black


HSA Beige on Brown
