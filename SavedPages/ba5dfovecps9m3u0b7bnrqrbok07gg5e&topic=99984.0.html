    Our shipping prices are a fixed prices per country.  We make no profit on shipping.  Even more we actually don't put the full cost from our partners on our customers when ordering regular products like keyboards. With groupbuys like this we have an absolute minimal profit. The profit on this set is 0,61 EUR vat exclusive. In this case we cant take a part of the shipping cost on us or we simply are losing money on the project.   So we either have to make the kit more expensive or be clear about just setting the shipping price at the actual cost.

    For products we stock under a certain value we can ship untracked at a better deal but we cant risk losing a single package with groupbuys with minimal profit.
