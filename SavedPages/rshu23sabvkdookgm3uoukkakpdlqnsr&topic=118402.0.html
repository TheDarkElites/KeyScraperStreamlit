« on: Wed, 21 September 2022, 04:15:46 »



 
The FLX Virgo is back for Round 2!

GB Details:
- There will be 200 available units for the GB, spots will be raffled.
- The raffle will be open for 24 hours from noon 12:00 CEST, the 23rd of September
- Raffle will be held here: https://raffle.antipode.no/ , you have to be logged into discord to be able to enter the raffle.
- We will be available on discord for any support/questions http://discord.antipode.no and here on GH.
- When entering the raffle, you will be asked which color Virgo you want, and if you want extras like the VirgoPad and/or wristrest.
- Winners will be drawn on Sunday/Monday. Winners will receive an e-mail from us, where you will be directed to our website to register an account, once logged into your account (with the e-mail you entered the raffle with), you can add 1 Virgo of your choice and as many extras as you want. You will have 24 hours to check out and pay your invoice.
- Anyone will be able to buy any extras without entering the raffle. This includes the VirgoPad, PCBs, plates, wrist rests and carrying bags.
- We expect to ship the boards in May 2023.

Details:
- 12-degree Split TKL
- 7 degree typing angle
- Custom PCB, VIA, Vial and QMK compatible with USB-C daughterboard, layout here:
More


- Top, and bottom made from 6063 aluminium
- Comes with a 5052 aluminium plate as standard for Alu. board, and PC plate for PC board, with additional plate options in Brass, Pc, POM, FR4 and CF.
- Grommet mounting isolates plate from chassis
- Brass weight with PVD finish
- Options to add a matching VirgoPad
- Options to add a matching (or non-matching) wrist rest
- Comes with a custom carrying case that fits the board, extra plates, PCBs, and the optional wrist rest.
- Board uses AKB-Feet
- Fully built Alu. Virgo weighs 3540 grams, wrist rest in Alu. weighs 1500 grams, VirgoPad weighs 850 grams.

Regarding the remaining products that still haven't been shipped:

We have shipped all the orders that we’ve been able to ship with everything that arrived with the first batch from the manufacturer.

According to the original timeline, the remaining cases was supposed to be shipped this week. Sadly, that plan has been delayed.

Due to some QC issues found by the manufacturer on the PC units, the timeline has slipped, and it appears that some cases will have to be remade.

It is very regretful that we are only now learning about this, but there is not much we can do. The manufacturer has indicated that they will need another 3 weeks to re-make and QC before they can be packaged and shipped to us.

Once we receive the remaining keyboards, we’ll complete our QC inspection and start shipping them out as soon as we can.

Originally, we created all the shipping labels for all orders. Since this is now dragging out longer than anticipated, we’re going to start deleting these labels and changing the status of your order from fulfilled to unfulfilled. The next time you receive a shipping notification from us, you can expect to see your package start moving within a day or two.

We thank you very much for your patience and we sincerely apologize for the delay. If you have any questions, leave a comment here or contact us through contact@antipode. no
