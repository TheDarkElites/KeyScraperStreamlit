[GB] Prime_E - COMPLETE
« on: Sun, 28 April 2019, 22:55:00 »


Prime_E. A 45% keyboard. This GB will run for 2 weeks. Choose between four case colors and 2 indicator LED options: all white or RGB mix. Indicator LEDs will NOT come pre-soldered.

IC Thread and some development history

Full image gallery

Discord discussion

Features:
  • 4.75mm integrated switch plate
  • Inner alphas rotated inward at 8deg
  • 6deg incline
  • 3 indicator LEDs (choose all white or RGB mix)
  • All aluminum 2-part case
  • 4 anodized color choices: Black, Silver, Gray and Red
  • Hotswap via Kailh sockets
  • USB-C
  • QMK
  • Via

Kit includes:
  • Top and bottom case parts
  • PCB, assembled (except for indicator LEDs)
  • Screws
  • Bumpon pads

Price: $320 for a full kit. Extra and standalone PCBs are $40. Shipping is not included.

GB Period: 2 weeks (May 3 - May 17)
ETA: Estimated to ship 4 months after GB period ends

Received notice that all PCBs are in route to me.

After programming and QAQC, i will be shipping out orders that contained only a PCB(s). If you ordered an extra/spare PCB, it will ship along with your case.

Also, for those that wanted just the PCB for a DIY project, basic 2D plate files can be found HERE . And by basic, I mean basic. There are no holes for standoffs or screws. I did this simply because there are too many different types on construction and hardware to consider to make any sort of universal design. If you do not have the means to modify the drawings yourself, get with me and I can add some holes where you want them. Also, the files are provided in DXF format. If you want some other format let me know. I should be able to produce most file types.
