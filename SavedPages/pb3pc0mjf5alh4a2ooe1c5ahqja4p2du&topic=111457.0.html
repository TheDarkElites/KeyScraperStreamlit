"i made this in my garage" Deskmat Groupbuy Info

Hello everyone!!

After a successful interest check, we're very excited and proud to announce that the "i made this in my garage" Deskmat group buy is a go and now LIVE! After having taken in the feedback from you guys and making some changes, we've officially settled on 3 colorways: Classic Beige with Rainbow, Charcoal Grey with Rainbow and Charcoal Grey with Color Strip! We unfortunately had to cut out the Classic Beige with Color strip as it was not as popular as the other colorways and would have had issues hitting MOQ for it. Nonetheless, we'd like to say a huge thank you for everyone for the support and helping bring this deskmat to life!

Inspired by the Apple Macintosh, we officially present the group buy for the "I made this in my garage" Deskmat!

Classic Beige with Rainbow



Charcoal Grey with Rainbow



Charcoal Grey with Colour Strip



Specifications:
  • 900 x 400 x 4 mm
  • Rubber bottom
  • Stitched edges
  • Cloth top
  • vintage-y pastel-y goodness
