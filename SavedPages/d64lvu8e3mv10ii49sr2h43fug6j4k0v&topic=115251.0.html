[GB] Mechlovin' Infinity87.5 - F12 & F13 TKL Compatible PCB
« on: Mon, 08 November 2021, 21:07:26 »
The Infinity87.5 is the latest iteration in our Infinity PCB lineup, designed to work with a variety of F12 and F13 TKL boards. We had a final prototype ready along with a plan for mass production back in July, but that unfortunately coincided with a massive COVID wave which kept us in lockdown for more than 2 months. We’ve been trying to get back on our feet (fulfilling group buys, catching up with commission projects…), and are now finally in a good position to put this in motion.

We have also teamed up with our good friend at cbbplanet, so that we can provide Infinity87.5, as well as other Mechlovin’ products to our North American customers with faster and cheaper shipping.


[PLEASE READ CAREFULLY] COMPATIBILITY, STAB ORIENTATION, PCB THICKNESS AND RGB
- The Infinity 87.5 is in the popular A87 form factor with Top-sided USB Type-C and supports BOTH the F12 and F13 footprints, so it’s compatible with a number of TKL boards like Geonworks Frog/F1-8x; KFE-CE; Singa Jaguar, among others.

- There are TWO different variants available: 1.2mm thick with South-facing spacebar and 1.6mm with North-facing spacebar. Please check your board and switchplate and consider your preference for thickness to make sure you go for the right option. If you have any questions regarding compatibility, get in touch by joining our Discord Server.

- Mini-USB PCBs are not available in this pre-order. We will likely make a small number of Mini-USB Infinity87.5 for in-stock sale after fulfilling this pre-order.

- The PCB supports RGB underglow and per-key RGB for Row 1 (for the artisan keycaps enthusiasts out there)


PCB SPECS
• PCB is programmable with QMK and VIA

• PCB thickness: 1.6mm and 1.2mm (with stabiliser shims/spacers included).

• Powered by Atmega32u4 MCU

• Supported layouts

• RGB underglow pre-soldered

• Row 1 RGB LEDs pre-soldered

• USB TYPE-C

• Cherry MX only

• ESD protection

• PCB color: Matte Black (1.2mm version) and Cold White (1.6mm version). Immersion gold finish.




South-facing Spacebar


North-facing Spaceber


GENERAL GB INFORMATION

[NORTH AMERICA] PRE-ORDER FROM OUR NA PROXY: CBBPLANET

[OTHER REGIONS] FILL OUT OUR PRE-ORDER FORM (though it takes more time and effort, we prefer to do the manual Google form & Paypal invoice method so we can better manage individual orders and not have those mixed up with other orders from our site).

• Pre-order Price (shipping excluded): $55

• MOQ: None.

• Pre-order opens from 8 November for 15 days.


LEAD TIME AND SHIPPING

The expected production and QC time is 6 weeks, so we’re looking to start shipping around Late December 2021. We're likely to initiate a head start on production based on early numbers.

Read more about our shipping arrangements HERE. Thank you!
