'Barebones Stormtrooper'
MaNiFeX's Group Buy #01

Goals:
 - Quality
 - Reasonable
 - Transparent process

Features:
 - GMK Keyset with Black on White color way (CR on CP)
 - ANSI, ISO, TKL, 60%, %40, HHKB, and Winkeyless layouts
 - Traditional legends

Information:
Barebones Stormtrooper (BBST) is a low-cost, full featured keyset using the popular black on white color way.  Like a stormtrooper, it's mostly white with black accents, simple, easy to read, and won't be very distinguishable.  It will get the job done, though!

Pricing:
NOT ACCEPTING ORDERS

- Join and pay before payment is due to GMK: GB price ($100USD)
- After payment is due to GMK, but before caps are delivered to you: GB price +10% ($110)
- After you've received the caps: GB price +20%  (11/1ish)

Shipping:
All shipping done through USPS using PayPal address.  Shipping prices will scale depending on quantity ordered.  To be reflected on invoice.

- Domestic: $5 USD
- Domestic with Tracking/Insurance: $7.50 USD
- International: $15 USD

3rd Party Adjudication:
fknraiden has graciously volunteered to be my adjudicator in the case I become unresponsive.  We have known each other, personally, for over a year and run a club that hosts workshops regularly.  He also knows where I live, so...  you'll get your set!
