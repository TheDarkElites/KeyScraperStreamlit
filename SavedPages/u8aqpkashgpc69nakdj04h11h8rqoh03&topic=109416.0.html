What are Fast Springs?
They're progressive springs thatg act the opposite of "slow progressive" springs, so it's light on the downstroke but takes a little more to bottom out, it should make typing even nicer for people like me that happen to have gotten used to browns over time and can't type faster on anything else but ew browns
