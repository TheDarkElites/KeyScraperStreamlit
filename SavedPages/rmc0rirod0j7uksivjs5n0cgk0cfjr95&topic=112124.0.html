Hi everyone, the PLA60 is live and ready to roll. Some models ship sooner than others - all that info can be found on the website below:

https://rndkbd.com/products/pla60-bottom-half



A quick list of features:
  • 12-point Poron gasket isolation mount
  • Clear Bottoms & 12+ color choices for the top piece
  • Seamless shroud design. Change the whole color of the keyboard with 6 screws.
  • QMK+VIA PCB with USB-C. Solder or Hotswap
  • FR4 and POM plate options
  • Tons of layout options, ANSI, ISO, WKL, HHKB (true and tsangan), split space, split backspace, split right shift
  • Excellent RGB diffusion in the translucent colors.
  • 3D printed right here in Calgary, Alberta, Canada

Prices (In CAD):
  • $139.99 FR4 + Solder kit
  • $149.99 POM + Solder kit
  • $164.99 FR4 + Hotswap kit
  • $174.99 POM + Hotswap kit

Timeline:
Solder PCB units ship sooner, as those are in stock. Hotswap units are a short GB that I plan to fulfill in 4-8 weeks.
