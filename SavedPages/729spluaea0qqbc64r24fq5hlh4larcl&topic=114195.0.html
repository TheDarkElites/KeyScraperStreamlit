    The base kit is made to support as many common layout as possible.
    Unfortunately in order to support the full JIS layout, we have 172 keys in just the base kit, including 5 spacebars. We will also need to fund an entire new mold to create the 4.5u spacebar. This pricing is made with minimum margin. The new mold will also be the part which will take up the most manufacturing time, which will of course, still be shorter than other manufacturers. This is also done with stricter QC than most other new PBT manufacturer too, all of these contribute to needing slightly more lead time and pricing.
