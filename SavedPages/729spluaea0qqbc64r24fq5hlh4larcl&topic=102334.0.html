Thank you for coming in.

BOCC is an embodiment of utility and aesthetics. This unique layout not only serves properly on a daily use basis, but also is a great layout to display the beauty of our beloved keysets in a unique form.

The design philosophy of this board is inspired by the first carrier of this unique layout - Kmac Mini. The robust utility it offers and the aesthetic of the whole board is pure joy to look at. We hope not only to bring back this layout to our beloved community, but also we would like to try something new this time - a non-traditional mounting system.

Traditional top mounting system requires the screws to lock in the plate from the bottom case to the top case, while having the PCB suspended in between when it is fully assembled. We hope to alleviate, if not eradicate the reverbs that the suspension of the PCB would cause, thus, the mounting system we proposed will be having the PCB screwed and locked in to the bottom case, while the plate will be screwed in and locked in place on the top case.Another thought we put into this design is that we hope that whoever appreciate hot-swappable PCB could easily change the plate by unscrewing it off the top case and swap it to another material to experience different typing feel. Last but not the least, user can enjoy the plateless set up on this mounting system as well.

Quote from: liquidvel on Mon, 23 September 2019, 15:09:28
I don't understand why the polycarb version is as expensive as the aluminium one?

A block of polycarb costs more than a block of aluminum. So in terms of raw material it already costs more.

It must be machined slower to avoid warping and discoloration and the yield is lower.

The idea that plastic is a cheap or cheaper material is a fallacy. Yes, in HUGE scale production, injection molded plastics can be cheaper than metal parts HOWEVER this is only after massive upfront costs to prepare a manufacturing line to produce them and is only relevant for mass production. Even the biggest KB group buys dont even come close to this sort of scale.

If you like poly, get poly, if you like alu, get alu. Both have their advantages and disadvantages, but neither is a cheaper option.

Reasons as follow:
We have decided to extend the GB to 3rd Oct, 2019 GMT+8 00:00. The reason for the extension is because we have not released the typing demo and the tear down video yet and we believe it would leave bad taste in our beloved community's mouth if we proceed to conclude the GB without fulfilling one of the most anticipated requests.
I dearly hope that I will be receiving the early birds by this weekend, so I could have time to assemble it, videotape it and upload to you guys. This is the best we can do for the moment. We cannot postpone any longer due to the fact that in the first place, we have already planned to conclude this GB before the Chinese national day - 1st Oct - which will be a 10 days holiday in China, resulting in paralyzing the production of BOCC inevitably. Regardless if I'm receiving the early birds on time, this will be the set date for the end of BOCC GB.

Any updates on this? Thread has been a bit quiet for awhile.

Hi there, sorry for the lack of update on the thread, I've been putting all the updates solely on Discord, and that is our fault.
Let me update you a bit.

Firstly, the packaging has been finalized and made


Secondly, due to the delay caused by the inspections conducted on factories from environmental regulators, imposed by the Chinese government law that was enacted recently(which is the main reason the production was prolonged for a month or so), we have upgraded the the side-weight and weight from sand blast to PVD clear; although this means that it will diminish our profit margin by a lot, we think it is a good gesture to show our dedication on delivery a good and promising item to our customers.


During the time, we have detected that the PCBs were defective, and that we had already remade orders and hopefully it will catch up the speed. In before we had already announced that the color will be red, but to differentiate the faulty from the corrected one, we have decided to make the latest PCB to classic green.

There will be no BT compatible HS PCB due to technical issues, we had tested multiple times but the stability of the BT didn't pass through and we figured that rather than offering a half-baked PCB, we would rather take it out from the menu. In light of that, we had made extra PCBs so that people who were waiting for HSBT PCB could at least get other kind of PCB.

At this stage, all BOCC has already been made, in terms of coloring(anodized and ecoated), what's left is surface processes and PVD coating; which will be taken place after CNY. If there is no hiccups, QC will take another 2 weeks upon receiving BOCC on our side. The current plan is that we will send out batch by batch so to ease the long wait.

If you would like to see more elaborated updates, please do not hesitate to join our Discord - https://discord.gg/5kCVk9q for more news! Thanks!
