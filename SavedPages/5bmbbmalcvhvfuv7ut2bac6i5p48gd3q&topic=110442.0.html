It's been awhile since I've updated this so I thought I'd relay the update/information.

Still working on getting more of the R1 samples, I've sent multiple messages and have yet to receive a reply, my best guess on when they will get back to me is when Crusader is coming up in the queue for production. I don't work for keyreative so everything I am saying here is speculative, I have nothing to confirm what I am saying is correct, just a theory on when Crusader might ship.
