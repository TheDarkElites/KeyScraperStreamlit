W1-AT
Inspired by IBM Model F AT Layout

GB Date , 28th October - 10th November

GEON DISCORD

IC Thread





Specifications

3D Viewer
- Mount : PCB Top mount (Plateless) with Silicone pins (a.k.a Tadpole) and Screw Top Mount
- Typing Angle : 8˚
- Front Height : 17.1mm 2D Drawing
- Materials
: Top and Bottom Cases - A6063-T6
: Weight - Brass with Silver Ceramic Coat
: PCB - FR4 1.6mm (Designed by Yiancar)
- Weight(Built) - 2625g


Included

- Cases (Top and Bottom)
- Weight
- QMK/VIA compatible PCB
- Unified C3 USB-C daughterboard
- Tadpoles (80a)
- Feet (Black)
- ESD Protection Film
- Carrying Case (1680D Fabric)
- 10u wires (x2) and 10u Spacebars (x4, DCS profile by Signature Plastic)



COLOR

Top case - Black / Navy / Burgundy / Pink / Harbor Gray / Raw
Bottom case - Silver / Raw
Weight - Silver Ceramic Coated


Mount



Tadpole Mount
- The Tadpole is silicon material and comes in two different hardnesses, 80a is a standard and 50a available as an option.




Top Mount with screws



Supported Layout



- Caps Lock / Number Lock / Scroll Lock / Kana (Language Toggle) / Layer are Indicated by SMD LEDs
- 7u and 10u Spacebars


Vendors & Price

Swagkeys : From 370USD - Worldwide and Exclusive South Korea & China(Swagkeys QQ)

Hexkeyboards : From 510SGD - Worldwide

- Swagkeys : Based in South Korea
- HexKeyboards - Based in Singapore



Estimated Shipping date

July , 2022
