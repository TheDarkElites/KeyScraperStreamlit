[GB] GMK Lux - Shipping
« on: Tue, 12 May 2020, 12:45:51 »


Inspired by luxury items, GMK Lux is a refined black and gold set that was originally pitched by BeamingRobot and picked up by Zambumon in 2018.


Where To Buy

🇦🇺 Australia -  Daily Clack
🇨🇦 Canada - Desk Hero
🇸🇬 Asia - ILUMKB
🇪🇺 Europe - Oblotzky Industries
🇺🇸 USA - TX Keyboards


Timeline

- Orders will open on May 12th, 2020, and will close on June 7th, 2020.
- Estimated shipping date: Q1 2021 (on or before March 28th, 2021)





Proposed Kits



Core | MOQ: 250

Lux features a large Core kit, which provides layout coverage for standard and winkeyless full-size, 1800, TKL, 75%, 65%, FC660-like layouts, or 60% keyboards. Some notable inclusions are 1.00u, 1.25u, and 1.50u Lux keys; accent Esc, Enter, and Arrows; convex 2.00u, 2.25u, and 2.75u keys and second B for ergonomic keyboards such as the TGR Alice, or AI03 Meridian. The kit also features UK-ISO layout support as well as a second Tab key so you can reuse some of the modifiers if needed.




Space Bars| MOQ: 100

Additionally, we are also offering a space bar kit that extends Lux’s layout support by including two 6.00u spacebars, allowing us to cover HHKBs, Gok’s 7V, and Cherry G80-1800 keyboards. The kit also includes accent space bars for standard and split keyboards.



















What People Say About This Set

It's like Skiidata but in yellow. - Hugo Keys

GMK Sandstorm was better. - BlackSimon

A long awaited, GMK set that isn’t Blue; GMK Lux. - Daily Clack
Bruh. - Krelbit



Specs

- Made in Germany by GMK electronic design GmbH
- Cherry profile (1-1-2-3-4-4)
- Compatible with MX style switches
- 1.5mm thick double-shot ABS plastic

Disclaimers

Kindly note that the images are of 3D renders and are for illustration purposes only. The final colors may differ slightly.

Keyboards are shown for demonstrative purposes only and are not included with the purchase of these keycaps.
