❖About❖
Hi! I'm 12keyboards, the other co-designer of GMK Posh.

The term “posh” — which describes stylish luxury — stormed into British slang around the late 18th century. Its grasp on pop culture has only expanded since. Designed by annlovesbears and 12keyboards, GMK Posh radiates desktop chic; its gentle his-and-hers palette should satiate your strongest fashionable impulses. Lock in your first-class reservation before it’s too late!




❖Colours❖
Pink: RAL 3015
White: GMK CP
Navy: RAL 5022


❖Kits❖

❖Base Kit❖
Base Kit offers full size compatibility including 60% / 65% / 75% / TKL layouts, as well as numpad and basic ISO support.


❖Alt Alphas❖
Alternate Alphas Kit offers pink on white alphas, if navy on white isn't your cup of tea.


❖Alt Mods❖
Alternate Mods Kit offers blue on pink mods. It almost has the same compatibility as the base kit, as well as Alt Gr and Menu mods.


❖Forties❖
40's Kit includes split spacebars, as well as staggered and ortholinear layouts.


❖Spacebars❖
Spacebars kit is offered in additional colours and sizes.


❖Novelties❖


❖Keylabs Keycaps❖
