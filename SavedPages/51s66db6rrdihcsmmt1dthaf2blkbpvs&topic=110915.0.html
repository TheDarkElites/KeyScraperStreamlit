Normally I would have walked away when I saw ePBT, but it so green and tempting I just wanted to ask and see if anyone know if these problems have been resolved yet?

- This will be reverse dye-sub, so it will not be ABS like GMK. However, in the past, ePBT has done relatively well color matching, but I will not say that it will be "actual plastic like GMK/SA (ABS)"

- As for spacebars, I BELIEVE they have improved their spacebar molds drastically and at least the two sets that I've received in hand from their recent drops, there is a noticeable improvement.

- As for how low the keycap sits, that is likely due to warping. I believe a majority of dye-subs (including CRP) sits relatively higher than GMK due to this, but I do think it is not noticeable as prior. The more recent ones (Ivory/Cool Kids) have shown signs of improvements compared to even my ePBT GOK BoW set.
