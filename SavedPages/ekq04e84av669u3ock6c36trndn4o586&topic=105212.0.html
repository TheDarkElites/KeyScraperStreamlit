MAK WongBox

The WongBox is a budget friendly 7x7 case with an acrylic lid that can be had in a plethora of colours. When we say budget, we mean it; this one won’t hurt your wallet. It starts at just $35! This sale will run from March 20th, 9:00AM PST to April 3rd, 9:00PM PST. We will be invoicing as orders come in and production will begin immediately following the close of this group buy (if not sooner).




PLACE YOUR ORDER WITH THE ORDER FORM
Each WongBox comes with:
  • One lid of your choice, clear or smoke
  • A rubber band to hold the lid secure during travel

DESK DISPLAY STAND
Desk display stands can be ordered in your colour of choice. Same price for standard or premium colours.



STANDARD COLOURS*
  • White
  • Black
  • Grey
  • Purple
  • Blue
  • Pink
  • Red
*THESE ARE NOT FINAL, this is to give you an idea of what we are aiming for.

PREMIUM COLOURS
  • Blazing Copper
  • Carbon Dust
  • Stone White
  • Zeal™ Blurple™

PRICING
$35: Standard colour + your choice of lid
$50: Premium colour + your choice of lid
$10: Extra lid
$13: Desk display stand
$15: Shipping to Canada and USA (other destinations will need to be calculated)

TIMELINES
We are aiming to have this group buy finished in two-three months. With COVID-19 things are changing daily; we will do our best to keep you up to date if any events affect our timelines.

If you have any questions, feedback or suggestions, please PM us here or on discord, Wongstongs#8281.

WongBox Production Progress Tracker:
- Cases: underway
- Lids: done
- Stands: underway
- Rubber bands: purchased and being shipped
- Mailing boxes: purchased and being shipped
- Al Bumens: underway
