[GB] UM-70 | 3D Printed 65% Split Keyboard (Ended, All Orders Shipped)
« on: Tue, 06 April 2021, 20:05:25 »
UM-70 is a 70 keys split keyboard in 65% layout with RGB, macro keys, OLED screen and encoder housed in PLA 3D printed cases. It also supports audio for the beeps and the bops. Perfect for those who want to try out various functionalities QMK offers with a single board.



FEATURES

IN-SWITCH RGB WITH TRANSLUCENT PANELS


OLED SCREEN


ENCODER


AUDIO (BUZZER)


COLOURS (See photos in gb page)
  • Galaxy Purple
  • Galaxy Black
  • Vertigo Grey
  • Vertigo Jade
  • Orange
  • Baby Blue
  • Mint
  • White
  • Marble Effect

SPECS
  • 70 Keys QWERTY split in 65% layout
  • OLED Screen
  • Encoder (15mm rotary knob)
  • In switch and side penal RGB
  • Top Mounted
  • RF4 Plates
  • 3 Degree typing angle
  • USB-C
  • Hot-swap
  • QMK, VIA Compatible
  • PLA FDM 3D Printed Housing
  • Left 170 x 120 x 25mm
  • Right 205 x 120 x 25mm

PCB Layout


SOLDERING IS REQUIRED
PCB is partly assembled only. Hot-swap sockets, RGB LEDs, TRRS jack and OLED screen require soldering. Assembly guide will be provided.

£135 per kit plus shipping
