Quote from: tragedyfoundus on Mon, 08 January 2018, 08:37:39
I have to ask, what's the advantage of a brass plate? I would be using tactile switches, most likely Zealios or something. If that matters at all.

Brass is more dense than aluminum, and gives a more rigid base for switches. It won't flex as much on key presses so presses and bottoming out feels more firm. Also the density gives a slightly deeper sound on bottoming out.

Many people prefer more dense plates for linear switches, and more flexible plates for tactile switches but that's personal preference
