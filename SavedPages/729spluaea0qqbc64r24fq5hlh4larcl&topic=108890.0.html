Welcome to the GMK Noire GB Thread





This set will be Live: October 4, 2020 - November 4, 2020
Est. shipping time: Q2 2021 (production lead time ~34 weeks + sample verification)

Inspired by a need for a set that would work well on as many boards as possible, regardless of design, layout, budget or color; and inspired by midcentury modern and minimalist design, GMK Noire is currently live on the following vendor sites:

USA: Project Keyboard
Canada: Deskhero
EU: Oblotzky Industries
Asia: iLumkb
Australia: Daily Clack

Manufacturer: GMK
Profile: Cherry
Sculpt: 1/1/2/3/4/4
Plastic: ABS

Kitting and Pricing


Base Kit


 $140 USD / €129,00 EUR / $190.00 CAD

Featuring a large Core kit heavily inspired by Zambumon's GMK Lux kitting, which provides layout coverage for standard and winkeyless full-size, 1800, TKL, 75%, 65%, FC660-like layouts, or 60% keyboards. Other notable inclusions are accent Esc, Enter, and Arrows; convex 2.00u, 2.25u, and 2.75u keys and second B for ergonomic keyboards such as the TGR Alice, or AI03 Meridian. The kit also features UK-ISO layout support.



Novelties


$45 USD / €39,00 EUR / $62.00 CAD

Created in collaboration with Nephlock of GMK Metropolis fame, and made to compliment a majority of layouts and boards.


Deskmats:

Deskmats will be standard 900x400mm size, 4mm thick, stitched edge.
Mockups (refer to palette for color samples)

$17 USD / €22,00 /$24.00 CAD Each

Division


Diffraction


Disintegration



Salvun Metal Caps:

aluminum, brass, and copper respectively

All caps are coated with black cerakote with a laser etched design.



On an Exent with GMK WOB


Aluminum Pricing:
$45 USD/ €45,00 EUR / $62.00 CAD

Copper and Brass Pricing:
$50 USD/€49,00 EUR / $69.00 CAD

All caps have been clear coated to prevent patina.
