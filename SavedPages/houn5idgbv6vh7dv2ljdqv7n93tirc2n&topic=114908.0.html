[GB] Bubble75 Gasket Mount Keyboard Kit GB is live
« on: Sat, 09 October 2021, 11:35:37 »
Hi everyone,

GREAT NEWS! We've launched a very special Bubble75 keyboard today!
Please check the Prototype album: https://imgur.com/a/Dyoqnc6

The Bubble 75 is a premium, compact, gasket mount keyboard kit designed to be screwless. We have also modified the internal structure details to improve the typing feel and sound. There have been flex cuts placed throughout the plate to ensure a soft typing feel. We hope it can stand out among the current lineup of affordable, yet premium 75% keyboards.

Default Specifications:
6063 Alu case
Gasket Mount
PCB with USB-C daughterboard
QMK/VIA Supported Hotswap PCB
PC plate（flex cut design)
6° Typing Angle
RGB
Poron Dampening pads
EVA and PE Foam Pad Included

Colors:White, Blue, Black, Green, Sky blue, White top case and Black bottom case, Yellow top case and Black bottom case, Red top case and Black bottom case, Gray top case and Black bottom case
Weight:Silver, Black, Gold Alu weight, and Gold Brass weight
Note:Alu weight for the standard edition, Brass weight for the premium edition
Add-ons:Hotswap PCB, Soldered PCB, Brass plate, FR4 plate, POM plate, Alu plate, and Poron gaskets pack

Streamers/YouTubers:
Keybored: Blue color（Sound test with PE foam and EVA foam）
Bored bear: Green color (Sound test with EVA foam)
Alexotos: White color ( Installed and Sounds test )
Andy Teaches Tech: White top case and Black bottom case（Installed and Sounds test)

Bubble 75 Group buy info#
Variations
Pricing: Pricing may change with exchange rates, but only slightly if at all.
Standard Edition Price: $292 USD
Premium Edition Price: $315 USD
GB Time: Oct. 8th - Oct. 25th, 2021(We will close the group buy earlier than the end date if we reach the limit.)
Estimated Delivery: April 2022 (The delivery time of vendors is estimated to be a little later, due to most vendors have opted for shipment via the sea, so it will take a bit of time to reach them.)

Vendors list:

Rest of the world: Velocifire- Premium and Velocifire- Standard

Please join our discord channel at https://discord.com/invite/cqSbaTtKcv
And also feel free to comment here.
Thank you!
