GMK Cocogoat



A keyset inspired by the Plenuline Gaze, Ganyu. Designed by MSA.

GB over - total time: 29 minutes

Vendor: Switchmod Keyboards
A single vendor will be used due to extremely limited nature of the project.
While most of the details are finalised, any feedback and suggestions are most welcome.

Project page





Base Kit - $159.99 USD




250 kits will be made, and only 200 will be sold online during the GB period, to comply with HoYoverse's merchandising authorization guidelines.
The group buy will be FCFS.
There will be a 2 kit/address restriction for 24 hours, in which after this restriction will be lifted.
Any remaining sets after the group buy and support windows have closed can/will be sold in-person.

The estimated shipping timeline of GMK Cocogoat is 38 weeks from GB completion.

The alphas use cangjie sublegends. (Many thanks to Grundlemere for providing them)
2 colors out of the 4 used are custom.

Note: The R1 bow novelty will be partially UV printed due to having more than 2 colors.


Deskmat - $24.99 USD





Dimensions: 900x400x4mm. The deskmat will use rough surface. Illustration by Clairemxj

Pictures courtesy of LightningXI from LightningKeyboards
