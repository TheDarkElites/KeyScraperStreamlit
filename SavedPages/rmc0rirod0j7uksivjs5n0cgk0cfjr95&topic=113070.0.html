Live your sci-fi cyberpunk fluorescent dream with GLITCH.

An all CNC stacked plastic case with CNC milled polycarbonate bottom. Silicone sandwich mount, custom silicone pad foot.

All built to house a beautiful silkscreened PROTOZOA PCB, or a CFTKB.com Mysterium PCB of your choice.

Each GLITCH kit contains:

GLITCH Top Layers - CNC acrylic (3)
Polycarbonate Plate - Frosted - ANSI + ISO (universal)
Silicone PCB Mount Sheet (Clear)
Polycarbonate Base Unit - Black Smokey
Stainless Steel Badge
Custom Hardware - Black
Silicone Foot - Black
Custom Carrying Bag
Protozoa Microfiber Cloth

Each FROST kit contains:

FROST Top Layers - CNC Polycarbonate (3)
Polycarbonate Plate - Frosted - ANSI + ISO (universal)
Silicone PCB Mount Sheet (Clear)
Polycarbonate Base Unit (Frosted Clear)
Stainless Steel Badge
Custom Hardware - Gray
Silicone Foot - White
Custom Carrying Bag
Protozoa Microfiber Cloth
