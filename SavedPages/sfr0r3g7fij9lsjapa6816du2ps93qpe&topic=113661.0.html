Yup, here's a general update and an update on the Iron165 Collab:
In general, everything is running just fine.
In regards to the Iron165 Collab (previously announced on reddit):
The collaboration will be sold as in stock with the units being ordered soon. There will be 15 units available.Each Iron165 kit will come with a top piece, bottom piece, forged carbon plate, custom cerakoted weight and two custom cerakoted badges (one S+R and one Pacific). Lastly, each raffle winner for the collaboration will win an invoice for a GMK Pacific Base Kit (this will be required to be bought). If someone wins that has already purchased a base kit, they will have the chance to provide a receipt with the same email address as their raffle entry indicating such and then the base kit will be removed from their invoice.
Pricing and date are still TBA but date should be a few months out because the order is going to be put in soon.
