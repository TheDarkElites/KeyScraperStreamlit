[GB] FROG TKL - Sales start on 30st June
« on: Thu, 17 June 2021, 03:23:30 »

Hello everyone, i'm Geon from south korea
I'm going to start selling Frog TKL, It will be sold every month on In-stock

The sales will be in-stock, not group buy, and the vendor is Swagkeys in S.Korea. 


IC Thread
Geon Discord
More Detail
Frog TKL Pictures by Markerchun


Sales Start at 30th June 11:00am (GMT +9)
Count Down






Sales Information

1. Price
- 285 USD for Case (250 USD for 30 Streamers)

Included
- Top, Bottom Cases
- Inner, Outer ceramic coated Brass weights
- Aluminium Plate with 80A hardness Tadpoles (Pink Color Plate for Dark Green - ANSI only)
- Long Feet
: Housing color - Feet color // Black - Black , Silver - Cool Gray , Dark Green - Leather Brown)
- Carrying Case

NOTE
- PCB is NOT INCLUDED (Must be purchased separately)

2. Color
- Black (90units)
- Silver (90units)
- Dark Green (90units)
: additional 33 units for South Korea Exclusive (because Paypal is not supported in Korean shopify store)

3. Layouts
- F12 Winkey (50% of the total)
- F13 Winkeyless (50% of the total)

4. ADD-ONs

4-1) PCBs (NOT INCLUDED → MUST BE PURCHASED SEPARATELY)
- F12, F13 Solderable by Xelus - 45 USD
- F12 Tsangan 86key Hotswappable by Xelus - 60 USD

: Limited to 2 PCBs per single order (due to limited supply of PCBs)
: F12 87 layout and F13 Tsangan Layout will be sold October (You have to order separately)

4-2) Plate
- Aluminium (Ansi and ISO) - 40 USD
- FR4 Half-Plate by Gondolindrim - 28 USD
- FR4 by Gondolindrim - 28 USD

4-3) Feet (1 set - 2 pieces)
- Black / Cool Gray / Leather Brown - 8 USD

4-4) Tadpole (14 pieces)
- 80a Hardness Silicone - 6 USD  (Default)
- 50a Hardness Silicone - 6 USD

5. Shipping
- Processing time : It will take about 2~3 weeks (maximum 3 weeks). During the processing time, orders will be shipped out sequentially (each day, there will be shipped out orders)
- Estimated shipping time : 3~5 days (via DHL Express)

6. Sales
- FCFS (First-come-First-Served) basis
- Frog TKL (Housing) & Add-ons will be sold separately
  1. Housing will be sold first (to make a fair condition for everyone)
  2. Within 48Hrs, there will be a separate link for the Add-ons (you can purchase desired add-ons)
    - NOTE : There are limitations for purchasing Add-ons to "merge" with the housings (due to box size limitation).

  Limitations are the followings : (all the following items could be merged together)
     - Up to 100 switches
     - TWO PCBs
     - ONE additional plate
  3. Swagkeys will automatically merge the items to the origianal order (Frog TKL housing)
     - NOTE : If you purchase more than "merge capacity" →  It will be shipped out separately (merging orders to save on the shipping fee will not be possible)


7. Vendor
- Swagkeys, KR
