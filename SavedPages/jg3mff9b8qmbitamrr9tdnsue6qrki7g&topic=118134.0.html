[GB] Aurora Input Series 80 WKL - Group Buy open
« on: Fri, 19 August 2022, 02:13:30 »


As the 65% version is now wrapped up, it's finally time for 80 WKL! The price is not cheap, we ran the numbers and with material and electricity cost rising, and the fact that the total machine time per board is about 3 hours (1h45m for the weight alone!) means there's quite a lot of effort going into getting this made. Also keep in mind that it includes German VAT by default, when the shipping country is set to outside of the EU the VAT will be dropped, so you are not double charged upon import into your country.

Specifications
- Manufactured by Salvun in Belgium
- Material: Aluminium case, Stainless Steel weight
- Colors: Black, Silver, Gray, Red or Blue housing, Stainless Steel weight remains silver
- Layout: 80% Winkeyless with both ANSI and ISO variants available
- Typing angle: 8°
- Width: 357.61mm
- Depth: 134.61mm
- Height: 19.69mm (front), 38.35mm (back)
- Weight: Approximately 3.8kg, depending on configuration
- Plate material: Aluminium (anodized) or Brass (bead blasted and clearcoated)
- Plate thickness: 1.5mm
- Plate mounting style: 6-point top mount leaf spring with optional poron pads
- Interface: USB-C
- Electronics: WT80-G GRAPHITE (Solder) / WT80-H1 GRAPHITE PCB (Hotswap) designed by wilba.tech, QMK compatible and fully programmable with VIA configurator
- Designed by Oblotzky and Zambumon, in collaboration with Salvun and Wilba

Contents
- Aluminium Top Case
- Aluminium Bottom Case
- Stainless Steel Weight Insert
- PCB with USB-C daughter board, solder or hotswap specified by layout selection
- Plate, specified by layout selection
- Poron Pads
- Screws
- Bumpons
- Carrying case

Pricing: 669€ with 19% DE VAT included, may vary depending on country if inside the EU. 563€ for outside the EU.

Store page
https://oblotzky.industries/products/aurora-input-series-80
https://oblotzky.industries/products/aurora-input-series-80-accessories (for additional PCB / Plates)

Shipping estimate:
Everything wrapped up by end of August 2023, fulfillment will be in batches, based on color. Salvun is in Belgium so logistics are easier and I don't have to wait for everything to complete first.

Limits:
7 days or if 1,000 units are hit (just a safeguard).

Alexotos has also scheduled a build stream for tonight (19th August, launch day), I'll link the VOD later on when it's available.

Let me know if you have any questions!
