Hi there everyone,

We really appreciate your patience on the switches, and now we're glad to announce that the GB will officially start this coming Friday(30/10/2020)!


Specifications: -
  • Housing Color: PANTONE 2084 C
  • Stem Color: PANTONE 802 C
  • Top Housing: Transparent Polycarbonate
  • Bottom Housing: Opaque Nylon
  • Stem: POM
  • Spring: THIC THOCK SPRING V2 - DL SERIES 65G
  • Ergo clear-esque, 5pin, LED compatible

We are using the latest mold to manufacture Shogōki switch


Price: $5.50 /10
GB Date: 30th October 2020
GB Duration: 1 Month
Production: 2-3 Months


Vendors:
US/Canada: ApexKeyboards
EU: MyKeyboardEU
UK: Proto[Typist]
Asia: ThicThock
Oceania: DailyClack

Enjoy!
