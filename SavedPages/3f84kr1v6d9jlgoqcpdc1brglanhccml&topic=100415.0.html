Final design/photos:







More





m5 screws will be replaced by 4*m2.5 screws.





Supported layouts:
- WK or WKL
- ANSI
- ISO

Specifications:
- Full Kobelco 5052 aluminum case
- 6.9 degrees slope
- 1 pcs of coated stainless steel weight (triangular bar)
- 1 pcs of carbon fibre bottom plate
- 1 pcs of sandblasted/coated internal brass weight
- CNC milled carbon fibre switch plate
- ps2avrGB PCB
- Fully Programmable
- Full LED backlighting
- Controller parts, diodes, and resistors are pre-soldered

Color options:
The top and bottom aluminium case come in black colour only. The stainless steel weight comes in either black or multicolour.


- Multicolour coated stainless steel weight 


- Black color coated stainless steel weight

Price: (not inclusive of PayPal and shipping fees)
$550 for case + plate + weights + PCB (Pre-soldered with controller parts, LED's resistors, and diodes)
$45 for additional carbon fibre plate
$35 for black anodized aluminum plate
$35 for additional PCB

Shipping: Shipping will be from Malaysia. Shipping cost will depend on your location. Each set weighs about 4.5 kg with packaging. The shipping fee will only be invoiced when keyboards are ready. Estimated shipping cost (US 60$, Asia 50$, Europe $65)

Deadline for order/payment: The group buy will end as soon as the QTY is met, would appreciate if I can receive the payment asap. 

Lead time: 100-160 days

Thank you!
