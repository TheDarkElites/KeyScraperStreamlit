This update has been long overdue, keycaps, keyboards, etc have been shipped out or are shipping out from all vendors since the beginning of November. Lots of delays all around but I'm glad we made it through and thanks again for everyone's support in this project!

This is a fan made project and has been cleared to run from the IP holder.

GMK Samples have arrived and been approved on the first round!




Are you fully devoted?

In collaboration with FinalEleven, I would like to introduce everyone GMK Devoted



Ikki68 Aurora x Devoted


Samples from the factory (plate color will be different)



About

More
A question to consider...Are you willing to join us in this journey? Whisper to yourself...I am not afraid. Into the darkness...I will go ahead and find a way. Do not feel loneliness. Now Stand up! Shout! Let out your Passionate Voice! Louder! And don’t look back! Are you ready to join us in this journey? A journey for ….keycaps. Now...I ask you this question….Are you fully devoted?

This set was inspired by the band, Roselia. Based on the series BanG Dream! A media franchise that has an anime, concerts, music and a mobile rhythm game. Roselia is known for their gothic inspired designs, their colorful characters and their powerful music. The color scheme is based on the logo's gradient and the band's official color. The colors have been matched physically with a RAL book with official merch.

Who is Roselia?


Color Matched

Colors: CR Black, RAL 280 20 30, RAL 300 80 15
The main inspiration came from the logo and these colors are referenced from Bandori Fandom. These colors were used as a starting point and have been color matched with various official merch with our RAL Design book.








Kits
Kits are finalized.


The kits are designed to fit most 60, 65, 70, 75, 660, TKL, 96, 1800, Full-size keyboards in ANSI, tsangan/WKL, ISO, HHKB, Alice and more.
The novelties are based on each member of Roselia. All novelties and logos were designed by ItszBrian.

dub MUSIC EXPERIMENT - Base Kit
-Basic JIS support with the currently available molds.



Galaxy - Novelties Kit
Key profiles are:
First row: R1
Devoted key: R3 enter key
Piano: R4 shift key
The rest: R4
-All novelties meet GMK's manufacturing tolerances



SPACE - Spacebars Kit
-2u, 2.25u, 2.75u, 3u, 6u centered and 6u off centered are the space bar sizes
