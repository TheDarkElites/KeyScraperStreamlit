Welcome to the group buy for the MB-44!




Photo Album: https://imgur.com/a/hjTMvT4

Typing test and build stream: https://www.twitch.tv/videos/975409245?t=01h51m38s


Timeline
Starts at 11am CDT on Friday, April 9th.
First come first serve.
Closes on April 30th or until 200 units are sold.
Expecting 3 months turnaround from close of group buy.


Color Options and Details:
• Blue-Grey
• Purple
• Green
• Black

• 40% keyboard with 65%-esque blocker
• 7 degree typing angle
• ESD protection
• Optional rotary encoder support
• Powered by QMK

Layout



Vendor: Aeternus

The base kit will be $250 USD plus applicable taxes and shipping and will include:
• Anodized aluminum case in blue-grey, green, purple or black
• FR4 switch plate
• PCB mainboard and daughterboard
• Hardware, bumpons, JST cable.


The following add-ons will be available for purchase through Aeternus:
• Plate foam ($10 USD)
• Polycarbonate plate ($25 USD)
• Extra mainboard PCB ($35 USD)
• EC11 rotary encoder ($3 USD)


For other plate materials, P3D will be offering MB-44 plates in POM and PETG! These will be available for purchase on https://p3dstore.com/

If those materials don't quite tickle your fancy, the plate file will be made available after the close of group buy.


Thank you for looking!

Quick update:

All components for the MB-44 group buy have arrived at Aeternus and sorting is starting this week. Shipping to follow shortly after, of course. Additionally, we had a happy accident with our manu and have actual lilac cases open for preorder!



https://store.aeternus.co/collections/mb-44/products/mb-44-extras

Please read the following for info from Aeternus on how this came to be:

Due to some miscommunications with the factory the anodizing color of the purple units was different from what we had originally requested. I won't go into details as to how this happened as it's not entirely important, and will be avoidable in future GBs. We felt that delivering cases that were a drastically different color from the initial prototype photos would be dishonest. Rather than re-anodizing, which can cause problems with fitment/finish, we decided to order a new set of cases in the advertised purple color. Since we ordered new cases, we now have a surplus of a very nice (albeit wrong) lilac color! Because these cases are not part of the GB orders, we will be opening up pre-orders in order to help offset the cost of purchasing the new cases. The lilac cases will include the same hardware as the GB units, and during pre-order they will be the same price as GB.

Please note that because we ordered a specific number of cases, we cannot exchange your group buy order with the lilac cases. Your best bet is to try to find someone to trade, or wait until you receive your GB boards and selling aftermarket.

All group buy orders and lilacs will ship at the same time.

Extras of all colors will be made available after all group buy orders have shipped. If you're interested in any of the other colors but were not able to join during group buy, fear not! We will post an update to when extras will be available for purchase.
