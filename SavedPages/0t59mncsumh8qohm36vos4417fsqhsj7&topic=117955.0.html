Tetrix 60

As you can probably tell, it's inspired by Gameboy and Tetr*s ✧٩(ˊωˋ*)و✧

To keep it short, it's a screwless PCB gasket mount 60%


Renders:





Proto Pics:








See more color combinations HERE

Features:
 - 6063 Aluminum Top Case
 - 6063 Aluminum Bottom Case
 - Alu or Brass Weight (Silver, Black, Burgundy, Gray, Brass)
 - Customizable Badge (Same as Weight Options)
 - PC/Alu Plate
 - PCB Gasket Mount (Can Build Plateless with 5pin)
 - 1.6mm Hotswap PCB with Multi-layout (QMK/VIA support)
 - 6.5 Degrees Typing Angle
 - HHKB, WKL Top Available
 - Poron Foams (Use if you want)

Colors:
 - Silver, Black, Burgundy, Gray.
