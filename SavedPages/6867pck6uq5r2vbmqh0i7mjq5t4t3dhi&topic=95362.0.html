I do have to agree with Avid on this one, there is a lot of frankly unnecessary fluff to this kit that just isn't contributing in a meaningful manner: the duplicate Control/Ctrl keys, the dupe windows keys, dupe menu keys, all the JTK novelties that don't really have much of a place in this set. This is all just unnecessary and raising the cost when it could either be lower or use those spots for thematically appropriate novelties in the base kit.

The difference between Control and Ctrl is purely aesthetic, and a minimal difference at that, so I don't see the need for adding 4 more keys to the kit for ones that already exist. Anyone buying into a GMK GB has to deal with it being either Ctrl or Control and not getting much of a choice, so I don't see why this should be different especially so when JTK is meant to be a budget alternative to GMK and stuffing the base kit with functionally useless keys is contrary to that.

Same goes with the windows keys, except they shouldn't really be windows icons anyhow, they should be either "Code" or novelties that fit with the set for the sake of being OS agnostic and being able to fit in with more layouts.

Menu keys is the same dealio, don't need 2 different designs, should just be "Menu" and be done with it.

And no need for all these JTK specific novelties in the kit, the cherries I could tolerate as they're pretty neutral and relevant to the Cherry profile, but there's no reason to have the JTK logo and the star and anchors in there taking up useful space and money in the kit. Each of those keys could either be removed for lower kit cost or replaced with any of the numerous thematically appropriate novelty designs available for this set.

I'd also like to point out a few minor issues I see with the set: 1.75u shift has no text just icon wtf is this, numpad enter doesn't have an icon with the text R4 End key when there's already a R4 PgDn, 1.5u backspace is kinda uggo.

In the end of the day, I'll probably still be buying this because it's an amazing colorway finally in a profile I like better than DSA, but the kit design doesn't make me feel all that good about what I'm actually paying for here and what I could potentially be paying for instead of this.

That's their "base" kit, not even sure you can remove those caps.  For $78 *shipped* the full base kit is substantially cheaper than the equivalent GMK kit would be.
I've modded someone's keyboard that had JTK black on light cream base set, and that was an earlier set, I think.  It did not in any way disappoint me, and I have multiple GMK sets and also vintage Cherry doubleshots. 

And in terms of legend improvement discussion, let's not forget about the uneven legend width in Cherry/GMK sets for certain caps which is completely accepted and glossed over.  Also some mold issues some recent GMK sets had (on the underside of the caps) as well as some colors being pretty bad for LED bleed-through.  If you want high quality at low price and don't mind some shine, just stick with vintage Cherry doubleshots.  If you want new colorways, then both manufacturers have their issues, but one costs much less than the other.
