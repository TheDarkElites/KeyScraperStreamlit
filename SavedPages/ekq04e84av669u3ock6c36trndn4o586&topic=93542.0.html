Case:
  • Anodized aluminum
  • Default colors: Black, Silver, and Gray
  • Custom colors:  Gray blue, Dark red, Brown, Rose gold, Purple
Plate:
  • Anodized aluminum
  • Default colors: Red, Blue, Gold, Silver
  • Brass plate
Badge(for HHKB like layout):
  • Default: Brass
  • Titanium
Pics of Colors: https://imgur.com/a/F6KXj

Features of PCB:
  • Black PCB. Gold pads
  • Type C interface
  • In-switch LED Position: South (not the same as you see in photos of previous GBs)
  • RGB led animation on the right side (see the pic)
  • TMK firmware, online editor supporting: http://ydkb.io/?ca66#
  • QMK supporting: https://github.com/qmk/qmk_firmware/tree/master/keyboards/ca66 (Thanks Bobnaive and James for putting it up)
    (Switch leds are not working correctly if you use QMK Configurator to compile atm. Waiting to be fixed.)
  • Flashing guilde: https://goo.gl/LDJ5KV (for Windows)
                            https://goo.gl/Yj5kpM (for Mac)
Layout:


Price:
  • Case: 275USD/pc for default colors (including case, alu plate, PCB and brass badge if you choose HHKB like layout)
  • Custom colors: +16USD
  • Brass plate: +22USD (you will get one Aluminum plate and one brass plate)
  • Titanium Badges: +26USD (you will get one Brass badge and one Titanium badge)
  • additional PCB: +27USD
  • Shipping fee: 45USD (for all locations)
  • 4.4% Paypal fee
  • Other details will be listed in the invoice
Timeline:
  • Order deadline: Feb 15th
  • Estimated shipping date: May 15th
  • No quantity limit
  • Invoices will be sent out through GB time, and deadline of payment will be Feb 15th.
