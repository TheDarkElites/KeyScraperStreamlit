[GB] Honeyboard60 - All cases shipped out
« on: Tue, 08 October 2019, 17:30:03 »
Hello everyone! We are excited to announce this flash group buy announcement!
 
The Honeyboard60 is a 60% 6061 Aluminum sandwich mount case, that is buffed to a shiny polish before anodizing to give it that beautiful glossy look! Inside of our case you'll find a honeycomb design which gives our board a unique and deep sound profile. 




This round closes on Oct 14,2019. Invoices will be sent out on close date and you will have 48 hours to pay your invoice.


Group Buy Status: All cases have been shipped out, we have extras for any issues anybody finds with their product.


SPECIFICATIONS


Case Angle: 4 Degrees.
Material : 6061 Aluminum Base, Plate, and High profile Standard or HHKB Top.
Plate Layout: ANSI/ISO Universal
Price: $290.00 USD + Shipping for Basic colors - $310.00 USD + Shipping for premium colors.
Shipping: $25.00 US and Canada, $55.00 International.
Timeline: GB is a GO! We ship out Mid-november 2019!
Basic Base + lid colors (Anodized):
  • Black
  • Blue
  • Red
Premium Base + Lid colors (Anodized):
  • Rose Gold
  • Gold/Brass
  • Orange
  • Purple
Plate Colors (Anodized):
  • Black
  • Blue
  • Red
  • Gold/Brass
  • Rose Gold
  • Purple




RENDERS



Renders By Macsurfy!
