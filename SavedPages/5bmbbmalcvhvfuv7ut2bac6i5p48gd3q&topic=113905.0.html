"2021s must have set" - Blacksimon, probably



TRAILER



GB DATES
August 1 - September 1, 2021
Fulfilment Estimate: Q4 2022


PRICING




INSPO
Right on man! The 80s were huge for me. Firstly, I was born in the 80s, and thanks to my older siblings I grew up playing the 1986 Sega classic and love everything about the Retrowave iconography. Thus, GMK Retrowave was born.This is unashamedly out there 80s. Bright, fluorescent colours. Unmistakable retrowave novelties and ultimately a bit of fun! Excellent.

Now, to set the scene:
Its 1987. You’ve just come off Venice beach on a hot July afternoon as the sun is setting. But your day has only just begun. You run to the arcade. You grab a cola, your quarters and head to your favourite seat. You strap in, load your coins, hands on the wheel and wait. 3….2….1…. DRIVE.
You pass each car with ease, changing the station on the radio as you note the time left on your clock. You speed up level by level as day turns to night but you’re not slowing down. You look out beyond the cyan palm trees, towards the jagged hills as their glow increases and you begin to dream. It’s just you, your F40 and a never ending road, driving towards that neon sun.

KITS
I like big base kits and I cannot lie. Numpad and UK-ISO in base. This is the 80s, small kits aren't a thing yet.

Hi all,
Finally some new updates.

We've locked in all three colour samples from GMK. Unfortunately, I was not able to sample a third round of pink to my original Pantone due to regulations on GMK's side. Due to new limits with regards to some pinks and reds, GMK cannot increase the saturation I'd asked for (Getting it closer to my reference Pantone than what I had currently).

This was disappointing as unfortunately (to my knowledge anyway), GMK have never discussed such limitations previously. We've seen sets run in the past such as Future Funk (2020) that used very vibrant pinks (And the legislation quoted to me was from 2006 so it's even more confusing) but in any event, it is unfortunately what it is.

Nevertheless, the second of the pinks is actually quite beautiful and I've attached some really quick pictures under some different lighting so you can see how they all meld together. (Please note this was not the pink photographed above, but the samples were very close together in terms of hue)

Will keep everyone posted as to progress!
