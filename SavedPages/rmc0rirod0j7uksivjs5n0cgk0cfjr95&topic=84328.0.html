Hi, everyone

After the long wait, we can finally start this GB. I am so exciting. I think we have a great IC(https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=83590.0 )and we will have a great GB too.

Here are the final design:










Features:
Aluminum 6063t
CNC made cases
Anode
7 degree slope
Fully programmable
Full LED backlighting
NO ISO in the final design.

The kit will include:
Top case(wk or wkl)
Middle case
Bottom case
Plate(full or partial)
PCB
Logo D(made of alu and anode)
Cable (Can be upgraded)
Screws and rubber mat

Color option: black,silver,red,blue,gray,pink(for all the layer,plate,logo D)



NOTES:
The cable will be fixed on PCB with xh2.54(no need of soldering,just plug-in like usb).The kit will include one cable.
Anyone want to upgrade the cable with YABRO PS2 is extra $10.

Payment method:
Paypal    I will send invoice ASAP

GB will be end at 12th September.

Price:

The kit is $260
extra PC plate(moq is 10): $15
extra plate:$15
extra PCB:$25
Yarbo ps2 cable:$10
Your total price will be:kit+extra(if there any)+shipping+paypal fee

Shipping cost:
$35 each kit with tracking
the shipping cost may be higher if you order more extra stuff($5 every 500g)

lead time:
With in 90 days after GB

If you want join this GB,write the form below.Thanks
