Costs?
See vendor pages, but like 129€/135$.

When?
Group buy is open now until October 4th 2020. Ships from GMK 32 Weeks after GB closes, so around end of May. Add a few weeks for delays and inter-continental airfreight & importing for most vendors.

Collabs?
None. I opted not to create a RAMA cap, at least not for this round. Matching both ABS plastic and anodized aluminium independent from each other based on a RAL scares me too much. Instead I hope to make one in a possible future round where I have the ABS on hand which can be used by the RAMA cap manufacturer to match against, giving us a better match.

We are currently 5/7 Colors done matching. Orange and Violet are going through another round, both colors are not vibrant enough yet, Orange is a bit washed out and Violet is basically Lavender.

I was starting to get really anxious about the base color, wasn't happy with any of the samples I had received so far, they were too blue so more like deep navy rather than dark gray with a blue note. Not really GMK's fault as there wasn't a pantone/ral that matched it closely enough (that's what you get for being stupid and making a set based on an IDE theme that had its colors chosen freely from ~16.8 million available HEX values). But luckily, I found that one of the discarded Dracula modifier colors was a perfect match! So thanks pikku, one man's trash is another man's treasure.

Once the remaining two colors are approved, we would go into production pretty soon after.

About two weeks ago I gave GMK the green light to order all material as I was finally happy with the new Violet I received during my trip out to them. From that point, it takes about 6 weeks for the material to arrive, with another 6 weeks for production and sorting. As two weeks have already passed, we are now roughly 10 weeks from the set shipping out from GMK if all goes well.

Sorry for the color matching to take so long, the Violet color space is poorly covered in both RAL and Pantone so it took quite a bit of back and forth with the color supplier to get it to this point. Purples and Lavenders are plenty in both color systems, but not Violet.
