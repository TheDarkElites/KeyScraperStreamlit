Liquid wasd deskmats by ocean.setups


Discord | Instagram | Website | Interest Form



The liquid wasd deskmat connects the gaming community with the mechanical keyboard community! Since i am coming from the gaming community and discovered custom keyboards a year ago i try to convince more and more people from the gaming community to switch from a "normal" gaming keyboard to a custom keyboard. "Wasd" is used by many games and should represent the gaming part in the design while the fact that this deskmat is a custom design sold via custom keyboard shops should represent the mech community.
