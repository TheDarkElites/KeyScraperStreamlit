Update: Due to new REACH (Registration, Evaluation, Authorisation and Restrictions of Chemicals) regulations in the EU, one of the pigments that made up our alpha keycap colour is now on the new blacklist and thus the keycaps cannot be produced with that specific composition. GMK's supplier has adjusted the composition to include all REACH-approved pigments and is sending the sample to our designer for approval.

Alphas were affected.
