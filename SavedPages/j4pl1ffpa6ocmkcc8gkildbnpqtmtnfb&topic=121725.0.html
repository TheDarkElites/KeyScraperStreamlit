Intro
Hello, everyone, This is ClickClack and we would like to introduce a new partnered studio named "Mortal Studio", who dedicates to promote Chinese traditional culture through custom products. We are delighted to have the opportunity to introduce their first-ever release on ClickClack featuring four themes based on Chinese Zodiac, namely Tiger, Rabbit, Dragon, and Snake themed keycaps.

Tiger, Rabbit, Dragon and Snake



Design

What are the Twelve Chinese Zodiac Signs?

The Chinese Zodiac Signs, are one of the most representative elements of traditional Chinese folk culture. These signs are associated with people's birth years and consist of twelve animals, namely Rat, Ox, Tiger, Rabbit, Dragon, Snake, Horse, Sheep (or Goat), Monkey, Rooster, Dog, and Pig. This system reflects the ancient reverence for animals in Eastern culture, and it operates on a twelve-year cycle. For instance, this year, 2023, is the Year of the Rabbit. See more @Wikipedia - https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Chinese_zodiac.

Inspiration

The four designs we are introducing this time use various Chinese traditional elements, such as Chinese paper-cutting, traditional animation, ancient bronze patterns, and traditional window decorations to depict the characteristics of the Tiger, Rabbit, Dragon and Snake.

Yin Tiger - Chinese paper-cutting


Mao Rabbit - traditional animation


Chen Dragon - ancient bronze patterns


Si Snake - traditional window decorations


New Keycaps Mold

Mortal Studio has developed a set of new molds specifically tailored for the twelve Chinese zodiac signs, optimized for the hot-sublimation production process. In this development, they have eliminated the design of reinforcing stems in small keycaps, reinforced the thickness of the top of the keycaps to ensure they remain unchanged in high-temperature conditions. To make the installation easier, row index and key size markings are added on the back of each individual key.





This keycap set has is the heaviest PBT keycaps among regular keycap sets.

In terms of color matching accuracy, Mortal Studio has undergone up to 600 rounds of color optimization, including adjustments to the film and ingredient formulations.

Ultimately, Mortal Studio's PBT Keycap sets have achieved saturation comparable to ABS injection molding, and precision in image reproduction surpasses the competition by far. The details of all design patterns have been perfectly represented. The design drafts, prominently featuring dragons, have reached a level of fidelity in sublimation that is unparalleled across the entire internet.
