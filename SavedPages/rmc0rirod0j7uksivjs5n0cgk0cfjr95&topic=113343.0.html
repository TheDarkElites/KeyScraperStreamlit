Info

This set comes with 134 keycaps that make the set compatible with a variety of different sized boards such as 60%, 64, 68, 84, 87, and obviously full-sized boards.
Check out the all keycaps sizes in the pic above.
The keycaps are made with PBT plastic material, but are unique in that they do not have that rough, scratchy surface that is usually associated with PBT keycaps.
Legends and color are applied through a dye-sublimation process.
