R1 GB collection on Go-fit60

More detailed info at the IC page for Go-fit60:
[IC]“Go-fit60”, A 60% Bluetooth 5.0 Barebones Kit, by entergogo

Thanks to all of you who provided feedback and encouragement throughout the Interest Check. For those who have not notified the IC for Go-fit60, it is a 60% barebones kit that has the following features:

Key Features:
1. Bluetooth 5.0 (support up to 3 devices)
2. Full keys programmable
3. Hot-swap (Kailh socket)
4. Anodized Aluminum 6063 case
5. With battery indicators

Here’s the video that will give you a quick walk-through on Go-fit60's sound with two kinds of switches - TTC red switch and Gateron blue switch:
 




More pictures at https://imgur.com/a/cAWXkPi

Specs:
1. Layout: standard 61 keys, ANSI (6.25 u space bar)
2. Tray mount style, low profile case
3. Aluminum 6063 case
4. Plate thickness: 1.5mm,
5. 7-degree angle
6. Cherry stabilizers, plate clip-in (pre factory lubed)
7. Type-C port
8. Case and Plate color options: Black, E-white, Grey





More pictures at https://imgur.com/a/cAWXkPi

R1 GB collection on Go-fit60

PCB Details:
1. Hot-swap (Kailh socket)
2. Bluetooth 5.0 (support up to 3 devices), 3500mAh Lithium battery
3. Full keys (not include FN key) customizable via driver
4. Dual-mode, Wire/Wireless, charging when cable plugged
5. USB Type-C breakout
6. Black solder mask with gold surface finish
7. Low battery/charging indicators
8. Switch direction: South-facing (in case of keycaps interference issue)
9. ESD protection was added






More pictures at https://imgur.com/a/cAWXkPi

The purchase on Go-fit60 kit has four options:
Option 1: The original kit includes 1 PCB (RGB enabled), 1 plate, and 1 aluminum case
Also contains 1 set of cherry stabilizers and 1 set screws
(switches and keycaps are not included in the kit)
Option 2: The original kit includes 1 PCB (no RGB), 1 plate, and 1 aluminum case
Also contains 1 set of cherry stabilizers and 1 set screws
(switches and keycaps are not included in the kit)
Option 3: 1 PCB (RGB enabled)
Option 4: 1 PCB (no RGB)
