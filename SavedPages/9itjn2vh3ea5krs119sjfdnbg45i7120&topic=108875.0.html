Hello once again!
We are back again with another entry into our HEX series,
this time with a southpaw fullsize keyboard!
Introducing the 6C: Interest Check (Check here for proto pictures and other details)
Discord


6C with JTK Azure(GB in November!)

Details:
Aluminum 6061
WK, WKL Layouts available
Gasket Mounted
6.9 degree typing angle
PCB & Daughter-board included. USB C
Colors: Black, E-White, Silver
Aluminum (default), PVD Sandblasted Brass, FR4(available as add-on only)
Knob Material: Aluminum (default, anodized to case colour of choice), PVD Sandblasted Brass(available as add-on only)
Quantity:300++


Pricing:
Base Kit: 420 SGD includes
Aluminum Top, Bottom and Knob with color of choice( Black, Silver, E-White(+20 SGD) )
Aluminum Plate, Teal Anodized, Brass Plate(+30 SGD)
PCB + Daughter-board
Rotary Encoder
Indicator LEDs


Add-ons:
PVD Sandblasted Brass Knob: 45 SGD
Extra PCBs: 60 SGD each
Plates: PVD Sandblasted Brass(80 SGD), Aluminum(55 SGD), FR4(40 SGD)

Group Buy Format: Unlimited for 30s after we hit the 300 order mark
Group Buy Countdown: Link
Group Buy Link: Store
We estimate production to start right after the group buy.
A realistic estimated fulfillment date would be around 03/2021 - 04/2021

Cheers!
