    1. Intro
    Hello everyone!
     Have you ever been awed by the beauty of geometric aesthetics? Simple geometric shapes, when combined, can create countless patterns and designs. Today, we're excited to introduce a keyboard inspired by geometric forms. Without further ado, let's dive into the details of this unique keyboard!



    2. Design
    The designer used lines to outline geometric shapes, including single-sided (double-sided) trapezoids, parallelograms, triangles, polygons and other common shapes to fit the design theme.


    Badge
    On the right side of the kit is a badge with a height difference to highlight the sense of hierarchy, so that the front of the entire keyboard will not be too "flat". In order to interpret the minimalist theme of geometry, no fonts or content are designed.


    Interior
    The interior of the kit uses a sandblasted brass cover to cover the battery and small board, making the interior more refreshing and simple. Several geometric figures are lightly outlined on the cover to fit the theme, making it more spacious and visually impactful.

    Note: It is normal for unpolished brass to have surface oxidation after several months of use and is not covered by after-sales service.


    Side
    Because of the existence of the middle frame, after assembly, part of the middle frame will be exposed on the side, so the middle frame is designed to be the same color as the badge, so that the overall color of the kit will have a better sense of hierarchy, and the contrasting colors on the side will also enhance the appearance of the kit.


    Weight
    The bottom of the Geometry 70% uses a piece of PVD stainless steel, with a more complex geometric figure in the middle. This figure is the theme logo of the kit, indicating geometry. The protruding inverted triangle below is matched with the mirror trapezoids on both sides, and the trapezoids are sunken to increase the level.

    There is a row of barcodes on the lower right side of the weight. If you use Baidu to scan, you can find the Geometry 70% keyboard on Baidu. This is also a more interesting design point, and it also makes the back not so monotonous.




    3. Features
    2 Mounts Support
    Geometry 70% can support WK or WKL layout. You can choose the layout you like when you purchase.


    WK/WKL Layout Support
    The Geometry 70% supports dual structure switching between Top mount and Gasket mount. It is the best choice for those who want to experience multiple structures.


    Multi-layer Structure
    The multi-layer structure design greatly improves the typing experience of Geometry 70%. It meets the needs of ordinary users and avid typists.


    2 Types Of Connections
    Support Bluetooth and Type-C wired 2 connection modes. The dual-mode connection meets users’ different usage requirements, and there are 3 devices can be freely switched in Bluetooth mode.

    Long-Lasting Battery Life
    The Geometry 70% offers a 4000mAh battery, delivering up to 1-2 weeks of usage. Long battery life ensures uninterrupted work and entertainment, allowing you to stay productive and stay connected wirelessly for longer, without the need for frequent charging. What's more, it allows you to charge and use at the same time in wired mode.

    VIA Support
    VIA is a tool that enables keyboard configuration. With the VIA configurator, you can map keys to the physical layout of your keyboard.
