--- Please note that this is the Version 2 of the original Space65 named CyberVoyager, aka CV65
--- For information regarding the original Space65, please check out: https://keyboard.guide/board/space65/, Kudos to Discord member rys for making the amazingly detailed guides and info page for Space65 and Think6.5

Hey my keeb friends, after receiving great feedback and testing the prototypes via the Interest Check, it's time SPACE65:CyberVoyager is ready for the Group Buy.


SPACE65:CyberVoyager is a keyboard based on the original Space65, it is redesigned by AirPotter, Oldcat, and Demos to make it a more awesome board. And we will be working together during the GB process.
I will primarily be the ground man responsible for designing, manufacturing, fulfillment, and update the progress.
And Oldcat will be coordinating with me for designing and communication, Demos responsible for the design and production of PCB.

Livestream build recording by Alexotos: https://www.twitch.tv/videos/582635204
Typing Test: https://www.twitch.tv/alexotos_live/clip/MistyBoringEagleShadyLulu
Gray screwdriver: https://clips.twitch.tv/SpeedySavoryLapwingOpieOP
There will be another build stream by Alex this Friday (4/10) 7PM ET using Gateron Inks without the foams!

Picture of the finished build (with GMK 8008) taken by Alexotos

Review by BT (Brandon Tayler)

https://www.alexotos.com/space65-cyber-voyager/
Review by Alextos

Gray Studio also plans to hold regular giveaways starting this month hosted on OldCat's Instagram page, our first giveaway item will be a Think artisan cap which ends Friday, April 10.

[Key Features]
1. Premium-quality and affordable 65% keyboard
2. PCB: Refined based on R1 PCB. Purple color, Type-C（Support C-C and C-A), QMK, ESD protection, Added reset button
3. Mounting Method: Top mount; with two noise and vibration reduction pads
4. No switch leakage views
5. Carbon fiber plate or Aluminum plate
6. Manufacturer: gray studio and factory
7. Keyboard Design: Airpotter & oldcat
8. PCB Design: DEMO Studio

[Price (PayPal fees included)]
Base Kit  $299 (all parts included, see picture below), Shipping fee is $40 worldwide via DHL Express International (typically 3-day to US or Canada)

Extra items for purchase:
1*PCB: $42
1*LED Badge (single color)：$10
1*LED Badge Collector (With the default LED badge，you will have a total of 10 colors of LED Badge): $60
1*Aluminum Badge: $20
1*Plate (Aluminum /Carbon Fiber): $25
1*Weight: $35
1*Rubber feet (5 pieces):  $1.5
1*Vibration reduction pad (compatible with R1 Space65)：$9.5
1*Noise reduction pad (compatible with R1 Space65): $7.5

Some limited extras will be listed on Graystudio.club after GB fulfillment at $399 for a kit.


[Colors Options]

Color Options Image Library: https://imgur.com/a/XZ5sTa8


Note: A=Anodization; E=Electrophoresis

[Layout Support]



[Timeline]
GB starts: April 3, 7:30 ET (The GB will be live for 2 weeks). GB ended on April 17.
GB Fulfillment: Start fulfilling 3-5 months after GB ends. Currently projected to ship in September.

[1*SPACE65:CyberVoyager Based Kit Includes]
Each unit will include the following:
1x case, top and bottom
1x LED badge
1x weight
1x badge
1x L-shaped badge
1x PCB
1X LED diffuser
1x vibration reduction pad
1x noise reduction pad
1x screwdriver (graystudio brand)
1x tweezer (do test the PCB before soldering!)
1x instructional manual
Plus screws, rubber feet, some backup SMD LEDs, box designed by airpotter.



[Join the Group Buy]

Please visit Graystudio.club to join the Group Buy. Information and updates will be sync'd to Geekhack on this page.


Each board may not be 100% perfect but we will try our best to do it 100% perfectly. A special note regarding the PCB, every PCB we produce in this round will go through 2 rounds of QC, but there is always a slim chance the QC fails (<1%). A word of wisdom for all first time GB friends, always test the PCB before you solder the board. If there are any issues or dead keys (note the Fn key will not show up) with the PCB upon arrival of the board, please email me and request a free replacement (with proof showing dead keys with a tweezer). Do not solder before tweezer testing the PCB. Once soldered we, unfortunately, cannot provide free replacements.

Thanks all!
