Leaf 80 - 80% custom keyboard kit

Information:

    • Default kit:
      • aluminu top and bottom cases
      • aluminum plate (MX or 1.2mm ALPS)
      • ATmega32A PCB with USB Mini
      • aluminum bottom leaf shape accessory
      • rubber feet and screws
  • Price for default kit: $320 without shipping
  • 2kg with aluminum bottom accessory or 2.8 kg with brass weight
  • 19mm front
  • 5.5 degree angle
  • Case layout: standard, WKL
  • PCB layout:
    • MX switches: ANSI and ISO for both WK and WKL
    • ALPS switches: ANSI WKL, staggered capslock


Colors

Case colors:
  • E-coating black *
  • E-coating white *
  • MAO white
  • Anodizing yellow
  • Anodizing red
  • Anodizing blue
  • Anodizing green
  • Blue=>purple gradient anodizing **

0. Brass: only available for plate and weight. Brass parts will be polished or sand-blasted.

* Electrophoretic coating (e-coating) is technically similar to powder coating but way smoother, even smoother than most anodizing. The black and white e-coating are both matte colors.
** Gradient anodizing: blue on left and purple on the right side. The proportion of two colors will be 5:5 to 7:3. The separation line of top and bottom housings won't be exactly the same.












- GB link on zFrontier: kit, extra parts
- GB opens until 08/25 Eastern Time (ET)
- Expected shipping date: November 2018


Update: As several people asked, I decided to make a small batch of leaf 80 without the "leaf" engraving on the top case. Please PM me with your order # and screenshot if you want to remove the engraving. [/list]
