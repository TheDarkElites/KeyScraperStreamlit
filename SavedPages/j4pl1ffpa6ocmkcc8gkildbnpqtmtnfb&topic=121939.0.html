Long Boi Group Buy

Product Page | Discord Server

Background

Hello everyone! Jack from Pikatea here. It's a well known fact that bigger is better with most things in life. This keyboard comes in 2 variants, a 30 inch (762mm) or 7ft-2in (2184mm) size. The 7ft version can be converted to 44 inches (1117mm). It features 2 numpad positions with layout options that you'd expect from a mechanical keyboard plus a rotary encoder and a small macro cluster. Both versions are gasket mounted. The 30 inch version is made with 3mm stacked matte black acrylic. It hides fingerprints and sounds great! The 7ft versions is printed in matte black PLA. It takes a week of constant printing!

                                                                                                                                                                                                                     


Group Buy Info

* Base Price: $135 USD (30 inch version) and $335 USD (7ft version)
* GB open: November 20th 2023
* GB closed: December 20th 2023
* Estimated Fulfillment: April 15th 2024

Specs

* QMK, VIA, and VIAL compatible
* Plate mounted rotary encoder
* RP2040 Controller
* Gasket Mounted
* 30 inches (762mm) or 7ft-2in (2184mm). The 7ft version can become 44 inches (1117mm) by removing the side bezels.
* 1.7kg (30inch version) and 2.7kg (7ft version)
