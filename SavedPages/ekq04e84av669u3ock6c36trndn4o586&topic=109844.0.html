About Artisan Case v2.5
The original premium Artisan Case is back with another edition.

Changes include:
1) Refined bezel with added thickness
2) Stainless steel timestamp ring
3) Machined display stand with added thickness, allowing for portrait or landscape view
4) Improved tolerances on the lid
5) Refined radius on inner cells


Specifications
- 54 slots (6x9)
- Precision machined CNC acrylic case
- Stainless steel timestamp ring
- Acrylic display stand (machined & allowing for portrait view)
- Glass bead-blasted finish
- PlexiGlass hard-coated lid with UV protection - more details on PlexiGlass UV Hard Coat
- Charcoal travel foam
- Translucent silicone travel band
- Translucent bump-on x 4
- Cell dimensions (LxWxH): 0.83" x .0.83" x 0.81"
- Cell wall height: 0.34"
