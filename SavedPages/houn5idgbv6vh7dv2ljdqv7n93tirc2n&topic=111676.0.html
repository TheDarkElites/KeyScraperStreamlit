BNR1
1 KNOB MULTIPLE JOB



BNR1 is an CNC machined rotary encoder knob from a solid rod of aluminium.
It can be configured via QMK flashable Atmega32U4 to do just about anything you want it for. It uses EC11 encoder with no detent that means it is smooth when you rotate the knob.
It takes both rotary and click input.

GROUP BUY : 14th March - 29th March 2021

BNR1 UPDATE (16th June,2021)


We have shipped BNR1 to all of our vendors and orders received at our platform.

No need to worry, we are shipping replacements. Only some orders are facing this issue and many of them have received the perfect product.

If you found any issue in your order, just drop a mail at service@binepad.com we will serve at our priority.
