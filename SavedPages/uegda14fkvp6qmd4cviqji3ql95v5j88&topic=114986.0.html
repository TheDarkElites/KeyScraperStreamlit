Specifications

Keyboard
  • 60% keyboard
  • Top mount
  • 5° typing angle
  • 20.5mm real front height
  • 6061 aluminum
  • WK only
  • Plum, Jet Black, and Dark Grey colorways 
Plate
  • Support for split backspace, stepped caps lock, split right shift, and 7u and 6.25u spacebars
  • Available in polypropylene, aluminum, and sandblasted brass
PCB
  • Custom solderable ANSI PCB
  • Support for split backspace, stepped caps lock, split right shift, and 7u and 6.25u spacebars
  • Center-placed USB-C, no RGB lighting
  • QMK and VIA support
  • Designed by Cutie Club
