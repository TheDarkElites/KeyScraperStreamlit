Morgan 40 is now live!

GB: July 31 - Aug 5

ETA: Dec, 2019

Morgan 40 is a portable wireless ortho with kailh hotswap sockets and equipped with the Unifying wireless module which is a superior solution over Bluetooth.

With the 2.4GHz wireless, there's no need for pairing, just plug and play and instant wake up!

Acrylic cutouts are available to work as signal exist to ensure stability. An extra Acrylic piece with random color pattern will be provided for you to swap out

Brass version (Aluminum Top with Gold PVD Brass bottom housing) available, limited to 3 units. On Aug 3rd, 10:00 p.m. PDT



Theoretical battery life is 3 months per battery. Morgan 40 can be powered by up to 4 CR2032 batteries.

An USB Unifying 2.4 GHz signal receiver - U2U Pro will be provided.

  • Unifying 2.4 GHz Wireless Connection
  • Beadblasted and anodized aluminum housing
  • Top + Bottom two piece housing design
  • Aluminum nametag
  • No visible screws and screw holes on every surface
  • Acrylic + brass weight (PVD) design
  • 5° slope
  • Acrylic signal exist
  • Kailh hotswap sockets
