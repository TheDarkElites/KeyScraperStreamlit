REFUNDS ARE BEING ISSUED
PLEASE CHECK YOUR EMAIL



Mech27-TKLv2
Canadian Made

The Mech27-TKLv2 is my second design, and I've cut no corners with this production run.  Custom printed packaging, foam, local machining (I live 10 minutes from the shop!), and alignment pins built right into the case.

What's in the Mech27-TKLv2 Kit?
  • 6061 Aluminum Case available in Alloy Silver, Meteorite Black, Navy Blue, and Classic Red.
  • 210 Brushed Brass plate supporting split left and right shift, split backspace, and ansi/iso enters.
  • Genuine Cherry PCB-mount stabilizers.
  • TX87 PCB with JiGon mapper/firmware.
  • Screws and bumpons.

How much will it cost?

$399.99 USD + Shipping.  It will be insured for, and declared at full value.  Shipping will be calculated at checkout but estimates are provided below.





How many will be made, and how can I get one?

With a MOQ of 50 units and a hard limit of 50 units, they will be sold via raffle entered below.  Winners will be provided a one-time use coupon code to be used during checkout at Mech27.com via email. Colour will be chosen at checkout.
