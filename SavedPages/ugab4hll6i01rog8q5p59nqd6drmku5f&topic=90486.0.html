I'm happy to announce that I've finished tweaking all the images for this next group buy of the Cherry, Topre, and ALPS logos. These are cut by me out of vinyl as a single layer to be applied to your car window, computer case, bicycle, or anything with a smooth finish that you can think of! I've done my best to get the colors accurate for the "oem" ones as well. I hope you like them!

Group buy will run until Friday 21st of July.
Any leftovers will be sold through http://studiosofthewild.com

Price: $2 each
...if you buy 6 or more, only $1.50 each! (yay math!)

Order here:
Google form: https://goo.gl/forms/RRlW936EUJeSC3Zb2

Shipping:
I will be mailing these out as the orders come in - no long waits! Please be patient in case i need to resupply my vinyl and allow for extra delivery time for international orders. I will cover postage in the conus, international will need to toss in an extra dollar.

Warranty:
Most importantly I want everyone to love their stickers so if for any reason you're having a problem contact me and I will make it right. I highly recommend you read this brief guide before you apply your stickers!
