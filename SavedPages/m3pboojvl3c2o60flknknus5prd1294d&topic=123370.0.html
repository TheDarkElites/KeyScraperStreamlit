Why this pricing?

More
I wanted to take a moment to explain the reasoning behind the price point. The hobby has evolved significantly since 2020-2022. In order to ensure the keyboard will be made, I had to lower the minimum required units.

Additionally, the manufacturers involved in this project are among the most reputable and high-quality in the hobby. You can expect to receive a flawless, perfectly finished unit.

Moreover, the keyboard kit includes everything you'll need for a fully functional keyboard (except for switches, stabs, and keycaps), so you don't have to purchase the plate or the PCB separately. Apart from the fact that the keyboard itself is bigger than usual, meaning more material to remove resulting in a more expensive manufacturing process.

General Info


  • 7.5° typing angle
  • Tadpole aluminum plate & Polycarbonate pin mount plate options
  • Front height ≈18mm
  • Capslock and NumLock indicator LEDs
  • Centered USB-C Unified C4 Daughterboard
  • Multi-Layout options
  • Solder PCB configurable with VIA
  • Widened JST channel for easier assembly
  • The top face on the blocker on both Stainless Steel versions is polished
  • The tadpole mount plate is compatible with GeonWork's tadpoles
  • 2.5M bottom-top screws
  • 2M for daughterboard and weights screws

Lead time 3-5 months
