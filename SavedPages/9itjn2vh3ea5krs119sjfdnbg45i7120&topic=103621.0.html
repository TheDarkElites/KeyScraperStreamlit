Hey all, I have some really unfortunate news. Due to our lack of due diligence, we failed to recognize that the clover we planned on using for the RAMA caps and deskmats are intellectual property of University of Notre Dame; we thought we would be able to use them. However, we have come to the realization that we may potentially be infringing upon IP rights. After much discussion, the vendors and I have decided to officially cancel the sale of the RAMA caps and deskmats, and all who purchased them will be refunded in full. I am so sorry for allowing this to happen. We hope that you will continue to support the set moving forward.

On the bright side, I have updates on the Latrialum collaboration. There will be two variants available: one that is solely blue, and one that has more white. Please find pictures above in the GB thread.

Also, new renders have been added to the thread under "More Renders". We added a render of the Dark Horse Kit on the Keycult No1 Rev 1, and Rudy base with accents on the Keycult No1/65. We hope you enjoy those.

Again, I'm so sorry about this.
