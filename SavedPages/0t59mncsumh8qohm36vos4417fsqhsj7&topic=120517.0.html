The GB has now closed. We expect to be able to ship within 1.5-2 months. If you wish to contact us, please use our email or join our discord server. More production updates to come.

Today we announce a penultimate update for the Marboard (1). We madethis keyboard because we wanted a premium, handmade, non-mass produced product that was truly unique, and stone seemed to be a good place to start. Thus was the concept for the Marboard (1) born: A fully marble, handmade 60% case available in Carrara (White) and Nero Marquina (Black) variants. It has an EKH of 30mm, a typing angle of 5 degrees,and a weight of 2.5kg. The Marboard (1) allows for Gummy O-Ring gasket mounting and tray mounting via removable standoffs.

After posting the ICs we decided to set a MOQ of 10, which we have now hit and a soft cap of 20.
The GB started on the 1st of May, and will last until June 15th. We hope to ship within two months.
It is priced at £300 + Shipping, which will be about £30 depending on where you live.

Configuration Options: White or black marble, Tray or gummy o-ring mounting.

Shipping:

Shipping world wide, from the UK, normally around the £30 mark.
