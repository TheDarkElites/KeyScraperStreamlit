[GB] Matrix Lab Abel+ Ver.Dark (Shipped)
« on: Mon, 11 December 2017, 23:07:06 »

    Today I am very excited to announce our debut GB on western community: Abel+ Ver.Dark

    UPDATE:

    Please check your GB Info HERE
    Any issue on your order please PM me asap, thanks!

    08/05/2018 - All shipped, tracking added!
    08/02/2018 - Partly shipped, still working on some korea orders
    04/15/2018 - Delayed shipment for another month sorry
    03/22/2018 - Estimate shipping date: April 5th, 2018
    03/04/2018 - Production completed, will start shipping in two weeks.
    12/22/2017 - Payments transferred to our team. Production scheduled.
    12/16/2017 - Entry closed, waiting for production.
    12/14/2017 - Invoices sent. Collecting payments.
    12/13/2017 - 33 Orders collected, entry closed and will start invoicing. Thank you all for joining the GB!

    Features
    • Successor of the original "Abel" keyboard with new bottom and lighting design
    • RGB strip lighting for the back side
    • Winkeyless Layout
    • Switches and stabilizers are not included (Requires Cherry PCB mounted stabilizers)
    • MX Switches only and none programmable



    Included in this set
    • Black aluminium top housing in subtle matte anodized finish with highlighted edge design
    • Bottom housing in theme color*
    • Aluminium MX Plate in theme color*
    • Aluminium back nameplate in theme color*
    • Stainless steel under side nameplate
    • Strip block for RGB effect
    • Pre-soldered PCB (resistors, diodes, smd leds, etc, but not switches)
    • Pre-soldered mini PCB strip for RGB effect from the back side
    • Screws, rubber pads and all the other necessary accessories
    • A premium gift box with etched Matrix Lab logo
    *Theme color choose from red cyan blue black and gold, actual color could be slightly different from samples color




    Price: $300

    Notes
    • Limit of 1 set per person
    • Estimate lead time: 3 months after GB ends
    • Shipping cost: $35 for ASIA, $55 for NA and $75 for EU
    • 3.9%+0.3 PP fees will be added to the Paypal invoice
    • A verified PayPal account is needed to receive invoice and make payments. Please check the contact information and shipping address carefully before submitting your order.
    • Place order ONLY if you could make payments in 48 hours after invoiced, otherwise I will cancel your order.
    • Item will be shipped from Shenzhen, China by SF Express. The final carrier could be different depends on your location.

    [/list]
