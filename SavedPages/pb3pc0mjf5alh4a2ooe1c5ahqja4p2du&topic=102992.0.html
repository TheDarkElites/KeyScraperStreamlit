« on: Fri, 18 October 2019, 21:13:53 »
Replacement Spacebar form: https://forms.gle/buCRYBKDQzphhSxR6
Syruplabs has partnered up with Hammerworks to present a re-run of the sets from R2.

These keysets feature classic colourways based on vintage keyboards or manufacturers.
Hammer uses thick PBT keycaps and crisp dye-subbing to present the vibrant red APL sublegends, deep blue Arabic sublegends, colourful Tulip keys and orange DESKO keys.
 Join the GB and be entered in a buyer's giveaway for an additional base set of choice.

update: Of all the R2.2 runs that are going on in various regions we have been told by Hammer that ours is scheduled to ship before the CNY (Early-mid January).
QC should last about a month so expect shipping in February at that rate or earlier.

update 25/10/2019: CRP2.2 will be using R3 Molds which implement tighter tolerances and reduce greatly the risk of twisted stems!!!!

The major differences from the previous R2 run are:
All prices are in CAD and includes all appropriate fees.
-Arabic set on R2 Molds
-a 65/75% compatibility kit
-a 1800 compatibility kit

If you are interested please join here: https://forms.gle/vNs8H4wceVqinTQw6

We will also running a giveaway for a base set on Reddit: https://www.reddit.com/r/mechmarket/comments/djtbn2/gb_giveaway_and_crp_r22_update/


All prices are in CAD and includes all appropriate fees.

EU Novelties      $32 CAD




GB will be open until the end of the month. Invoices must be paid by November 1st. (timezone=EST)



FAQs

Q: What if I filled in the form earlier and lost my editing link or cannot edit my form?

A: Just fill out a new form I will delete your previous entries.


Q: Is there ISO support?

A: No it will not be offered this round.


Q: Where can I contact you guys?

A: hello@syruplabs.ca, reddit, or discord. You can join our discord here (https://discord.gg/rNTVtJC). We are generally most responsive on discord > reddit > e-mails.


Q: Is this group buy legit?

A: If you are skeptical about the legitimacy of the group buy you can find our authorization signed by hammer here(https://imgur.com/a/CsHVZzh) and on the front page of the form.


Q: How long is the GB open for? When are you going to ship?

A: The GB will be open until the end of the month. Estimated shipping is 3 months after we send payment (~end of January/Beginning of February).


Q: Why is the Arabic Base Set so much more expensive than the other sets?

A: After talking with Hammer the Arabic dye-subs originally including sub-legends on the numpad and various other keys as well. So the Base set is not only the TKL layout offered in the other base sets but also the numpad, wkl, and r5 kits that are dye-subbed in respect to the set.


Q: What happens if my keycaps are bad after receiving them?

A: The plan we agreed upon with Hammer is to collect all the sets that have quality issues and send them back to him for replacements. We will not cover return shipping. The goal is to catch any major quality issues during the initial QC phase and not send those out. So we hope that this will not become a prominent issue with the buy.


Q: Can I change my order?

A: If you have paid your invoice and want to add items we can send you another invoice for those items. If you have paid your invoice and are removing items or want a refund. We will charge a 5% re-stocking fee due to changes in the paypal policy. In general, please be sure of your order before you pay the invoice.


Q: Will you underdeclare for orders going to __?

A: Our official answer is that we will not underdeclare any of the products we sell. We are a registered Canadian business and we must comply to our regulations.


Q: I had a bad set from R2/R1 (some previous run), can I go through you guys to get them replaced?

A: Our stance on any previous group buys is that we will not pursue concerns or questions from previous group buys. It may seem unfair from the buyer's perspective but we do not feel particularly responsible for issues in buys that we were not apart of. If we were to pursue any concerns or questions from previous GBs it would bog down our current run. We want to give our best efforts in dealing with and running our current GB. It would be unfair to our participants and us to deal with previous issues. We are sorry if this stance is not satisfactory. We hope you and the others who have asked will resolve these issues.

Sincerely,

Syrup Labs.
