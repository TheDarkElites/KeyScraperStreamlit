DGL TKL
by Chanmin Kang

Thanks to many people who helped me to get this to GB, starting with IC.


Interest Check post


Features
 - Luxurious and original design keyboard
 - Sandwich-mount
 - Indicator LEDs
 - Centered USB Type-C Port
 - Pointed plate or Brass weight
 - RGB point strip located at the bottom of the front
 - 4T (4mm) plate for quiet and uniform typing feels
 - QMK supported PCB
 - 20mm height from the front
 - Supports both ANSI and ISO layouts

Included
Case
 - CNC machining 606X aluminum case
 - Approx. 3 kg weight
 - Sandwich-mount
 - 7-degree typing angle
 - Plates, PCBs and point plates can be purchased additionally
 - Top and bottom can be ordered in two-tone colors differently.

Plate
 - Cherry MX switch support (Alps compatible plates will be released later)
 - The 4mm thick plate provides a more stable switch holding and uniform typing experience.
 - There are three plates. Brass, FR4, aluminum
 - The FR4 plate includes a 2.4mm gasket to match the thickness.
 - The brass plate is made of a precision CO2 laser and has a deburring finish.

Weight / Point Plate
 - Add color points at the bottom of the keyboard.
 - If you want, you can choose brass weight option

PC Diffuser
 - sandblasted Polycarbonate diffuser makes the RGB strip located in front of the keyboard look more natural.
 - If you don't like RGB light, you can use brass bar instead of PC diffuser.

PCB
 - QMK firmware Support
 - Cherry MX / Alps SKCM compatible
 - All SMD components are pre-soldered in the factory.
 - Switch, indicator LED, switch LED (Optional) soldering required

Spring
 - For buyers only, we offer 90 compatible springs for Cherry MX for free.
 - You can choose between 50g, 57g, 64g, and 71g.
 - This spring, which we have produced, has passed the Vertical Profile Test, Lubricative Test, Surface Roughness Test.
 - We put a lot of effort into this spring, and it's made very precisely. The spring has an error rate of ±0.5 g. It is glossy and upright when compared to other springs.


Support layout


Options
Top Case type
 - Standard (ANSI / ISO)
 - Winkeyless (ANSI / ISO)


Top color / Bottom color / Point plate color
 - Anodizing: Black, Gray, Silver, Red, Orange gold, Metal Green, Rose gold, Champagne gold
 - Cerakote™: White, Beige, Gray, Pink, Navy, Yellow

Plate color / material
 - Aluminium: Black, Gray, Silver, Red, Orange gold, Metal Green, Rose gold, Champagne gold
 - Brass
 - FR4 (with Gasket)

Pricing

 - Base kit - $450 (Anodizing, Cerakoat™ charges additional cost depending on the option)
 - Extra PCB - $40
 - Extra Plate - $45

Includes in Basekit (Anodizing Option)
 - 1x 606X Aluminium Top Case (Standard or WKL)
 - 1x 606X Aluminium Bottom Case
 - 1x 606X Aluminium 4T Cherry MX Plate
 - 1x 606X Aluminium Back connector guide Part (black)
 - 1x Sandblasted clear acrylic diffuser
 - 1x DGL-80 PCB
 - 1x DGL Spring pack (for Cherry mx, 90pcs)
※Can be modified as desired in the above configuration (additional cost incurred)
※Shipping charges are not included and the price varies depending on the destination.
