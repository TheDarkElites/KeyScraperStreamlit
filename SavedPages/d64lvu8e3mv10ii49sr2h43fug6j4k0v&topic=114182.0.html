CAKE60_R1


A lightweight gasket-mount Plexiglas® keyboard


Design direction

Plexiglas® material
High clarity and brightness
Improved scratch resistance vs polycarbonate

Every board is hand polished, dyed and sand blasted

Unikorn style gasket mount with 70 A o-ring

Sculpted underside highlights the PCB

Lightweight, weighs the same as an HHKB

Unique sound signature

Square bumpons as an homage to OEM vintage boards

CAKE logo is inspired by the wedge shape of the 60% keyboard form factor

Fixed 7U layout

8° typing angle

I'm struggling to understand all the praise this is getting. It seems to be just a one piece 60% being sold for $400, which is more expensive than the unikorn.

Thanks for the comment. I acknowledge that this board is on the pricy end for the following reasons:

1) This isn't my first GB, but it is my first keyboard GB. I am approaching cautiously with a brand new supplier, and I am using this round to test their quality and reliability. The direct reason for the price is in relation to only running 20 boards, meaning the individual unit cost to manufacture is significantly higher.

2) In order to incentivize people to join, I am offering to choose your own color, essentially allowing every participant to have their own 1-off colorway.

3) If all goes well, I'll run a larger future buy with fixed colors at a lower price.

Thx again for the feedback

All cakes have arrived and are in good order. I have begun invoicing and shipping out to the GB participants and will continue to do so over the next few days. Below are some pics of the boards I've checked so far, they really turned out amazing and the colors are bright and vibrant

A few reminders to the group buy participants:

1) The cases are all polished, dyed, and bead blasted by hand. As such, you may notice some extremely small micro-scratches or blemishes on the surface. Nothing I have seen so far I would consider requesting a remake from the manufacture. If anyone has an issue with a particular detail or discrepancy in the surface finish, please reach out to me and we can review together

2) Please remember that this board will only work with clip-in stabs!

3) Please remember this is a fixed 7U layout and will not work with 6.25U or ISO layouts

Thanks and more updates to come!
