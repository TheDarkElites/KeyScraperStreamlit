[GB] JCS Arabic | Dyesub Beige with Arabic Sublegends!
« on: Thu, 14 December 2023, 12:58:14 »



We're making a *****in' (and affordable) Arabic keycap set.
Inspired by Cherry G81-3000SAA, it will be good dyesub from JCS(leekbros),
and it will have good layout support for a variety of keyboards.

Also - a first for leekbros - UK ISO in base!



---

D A T E S

This set will run between Jan 3rd, 2024 till Feb 14th, 2024
Estimated delivery time is March - April 2024



MY DISCORD   /  thebloodyezkb's DISCORD

K I T T I N G


Base kit

S P E C S

Dyesub PBT, produced by JCS (Leek bros)
Cherry Profile 1-1-2-3-4-4

Six sublegend colors to choose from:
Black / Blue / Red / Green / Purple / Orange
