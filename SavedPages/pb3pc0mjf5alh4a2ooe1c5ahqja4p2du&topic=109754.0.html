[GB] Aurora Input Series 65 - Completed
« on: Sun, 22 November 2020, 15:50:46 »


Full info page with fluff text: https://oblotzky.industries/pages/aurora-input-series-65

Specifications
- Manufactured by Salvun in Belgium
- Material: Aluminium case, Stainless Steel weight, Brass or Aluminium plate
- Colors: Black and Silver
- Layout: 65% with both ANSI and ISO variants available
- Typing angle: 8°
- Width: 314.75mm
- Depth: 110.80mm
- Height: 19.57mm (front), 35.00mm (back)
- Weight: Approximately 2.25kg, depending on configuration
- Plate mounting style: 6-point top mount leaf spring
- Interface: USB-C
- Electronics: WT65-G PCB designed by wilba.tech, QMK compatible and fully programmable with VIA configurator
- Designed by Oblotzky and Zambumon, in collaboration with Salvun and Wilba
- Comes with a EVA foam carrying case

Price for a full kit with Top and Bottom housing, weight, plate, pcb, bumpons, screws and a carrying bag
529€ (479€ for non-EU) with Brass plate
519€ (469€ for non-EU) with Aluminium plate
59€ extra PCB
49€ extra Brass plate
39€ extra Aluminium plate

Shipping cost, worldwide via DHL Premium (an accelerated service of standard DHL, NOT DHL EXPRESS). Tracked and insured.
Germany: 5€
Europe: 15€
North America: 45€
Asia: 45€
Africa: 55€
Oceania: 55€
Central and South America: 55€

How to enter
Prepare for the raffle:
- Step 1: Join the Koi Raffle Discord Server (https://discord.gg/yxqVMrw)
- Step 2: Verify your account on the Discord server by following the instructions in the #registration channel
- Step 3: Go to the Koi Raffle Website (https://www.koi-raffle.com/) and authorize with your Discord account

The raffle will open on November 23rd 2020 at 6pm CET (9am PST) and remain available for 12 hours. Once the raffle is closed, 200 winners will be randomly selected from the entries and receive their invoices before the end of the week. The invoice will be sent to the E-Mail of your Discord account that you can see on the koi raffle website, but you will then be able to use the link in the E-Mail to check out with your account on the store (https://oblotzky.industries/). Invoices will go out in waves. You will have 48 hours to pay your invoice, if you miss to do so, a new winner will be selected for the slot.

There are 200 units available through the raffle.
Methods of payment include Credit Cards and PayPal. Check my store at oblotzky.industries for more payment methods.
Ships from Germany on or before July 31st 2021.

Rules
In the raffle form, you will be asked if your keyboard will ship to the EU or not. Answer this correctly, if you select No and then end up placing your order for example to France, your order will be canceled and your spot is lost. This is to determine if I need to charge VAT or not. For now, UK still pays VAT! So answer yes if you are from the UK.
Only enter once, and do not enter for another person.
Slots are not transferable. You can forfeit it, and a new winner will be selected.

Update #2

significant progress has been made for this project, so I am sharing some information today. Salvun has completed the machining of the top and bottom case parts a few weeks ago and sent them to the anodizer. We expect that anodization and the following QC will be done in the second half of July. Weights have also been completed and are now being bead blasted while the aluminium parts are going through anodization. We estimate that I will be receiving the order by the end of July so that I can start fulfilling orders.


The plates have been received, QC'd and approved.


The carrying bags have also arrived! They are identical to the Dixie Keyboard bags that I offered late last year (and are currently in stock!) in terms of specifications, the branding is different as you can see


I have used the newly arrived plates to build up the two pre-production units that I showed off in the previous update. Both ANSI and ISO are working great!





That's it for today, I will update when we get close to fulfillment. In that likely final update there will be the chance given to change the address if necessary.

As usual, take care and best of health,

- Oblotzky
