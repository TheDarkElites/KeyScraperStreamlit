The GH80-3700

...is the way to make your Cherry numpad modern again. (Note: this is just a PCB, no case included)
It has pads to solder your own cable to, or you can use any USB-C cable while still keeping the vintage look,
as the cable connector is hidden inside. There is room for a rotary encoder as well.
For all your indication needs, the top row has individually controllable led locations just like the numpad toggle key does.

It also features a fuse and an ESD protection chip to protect the MCU and other parts of the PCB.

Features

  • Designed to fit the Cherry G80-3700 numpad case
  • Compatible with QMK Firmware and VIA Configurator (if you flash a VIA compatible .hex file)
  • USB type C with C-C support
  • ESD protection and fuse
  • 5 individual LED locations, can all be used in QMK
  • Rotary encoder support (top right key position)
  • ISP header
