KeyKobo Yōkai
By: Aad and Izeri

A keyset themed around supernatural Japanese beings, also known as Yōkai

GB live from July 29th-August 29th!

Ships Q4 2024-Q1 2025



Vendors
US: Mekibo
CA: Ashkeebs
OCE: SwitchKeys
CN: zFrontier
EU: Keygem
UK: ProtoTypist
KR: Geonworks

Pricing


Specs
Cherry Profile
ABS
Keykobo Double-shot + UV
Colors are both RAL and GMK Standard


Kits

Base features support for most non-numpad layouts while including alice and 3-key 40s support.


Novelties has support for Macro column as well as R1-R4 1U art and basic modifier art.


Numpad supports numpad.
