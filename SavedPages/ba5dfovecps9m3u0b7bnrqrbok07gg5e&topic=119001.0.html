The Deep Deskmat R3



Hello everyone! I am proud to announce the Group Buy for the Deep deskmat will begin around this December 1, 2022! Thank you to everyone who participated in the Interest Check and reached out regarding this deskmat.

When I just started in this hobby, I barely had enough space on my desk for more than one keyboard, more so a 900x400mm deskmat. I kept running out of space for other things, I thought wouldn't it be fun if you could make your desk expand downward making it deeper for more space? Thus I made this Deep deskmat design for fun and ran an IC which was then received well by the community.

In celebration of my 3 years in the hobby of metal rectangles, I decided to commemorate it with 3 of the colors most special to me from R1 and top voted colors from the IC! I have also redesigned the line thickness from R2, and the colors used from R1. Both Light and Hackerman are R1 originals and have updated versions for this new round.


Duration:

- End date: December 31, 2022
- ETA Manufacturing: Q1 2023-Q2 2023 (Depends on location of vendor with ideally no delays)
