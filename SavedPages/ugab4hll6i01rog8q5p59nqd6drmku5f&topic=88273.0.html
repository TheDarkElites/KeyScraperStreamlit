[GB] Sentraq S65-X Keyboard Kit
« on: Thu, 16 March 2017, 12:01:15 »
ORDER HERE: https://sentraq.com/collections/group-buys/products/gb-s65-x-rgb-diy-kit


Price: $99 for base kit







This kit comes with everything you need to build a 65% keyboard with RGB underglow. It uses QMK firmware which is completely reprogrammable and allows you to control every aspect of your keyboard. It comes in a variety of colors such as rose gold, champagne, gunmetal, etc. Upgrades are available for cherry switches, XDA keycaps, SIPs, and more.

Base Kit:
  • Assembled PCB (all diodes, resistors, RGB LEDs, and controller presoldered.)
  • Aluminum/Acrylic Sandwich case w/ screws and rubber feet
  • Authentic Cherry PCB Screw in stabilizers
  • 73 Gateron switches (upgrade to cherry available)
  • Mini USB cable
  • Basic 1.5mm thick PBT Keycaps

The group buy will stop collecting orders around 3/25. Orders should start shipping out around 2 months after 3/25. Updates will be posted here: https://sentraq.com/pages/group-buy-status
