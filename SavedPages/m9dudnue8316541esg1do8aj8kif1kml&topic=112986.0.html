Copied and pasted from an announcement made on the discord:

tl;dr: Shipping will be DELAYED without a super concrete timeline, address change form will be still opened until further notice.

Now here is why it's delayed and also what happened for the past two weeks since the shipping supposed to start:
- For the PC case, during QC (quality control), Jacky found certain cases had stripped screw holes and they are on the way back to be redone (not only for the screw hole, but also re-sanding the case after the screw hole re-worked)
- For the e-beige and e-white cases, during QC, Jacky found not small amount of internal defects, and they are back to e-coating again. They looked okay before e-coating, possibly because the surface being sanded or not easily to discover these defect spots until they are coated.
- For anodized cases, they are not fully done at beginning of December (that's why they are down in the original shipping list), some finished products did not have the perfect quality that Jacky expected and currently the factory is waiting for new material for the anodization process so it's on pause at the moment.

We will update once we have more information regarding any batch of the cases, and hopefully next update is good news and shipping starts. And I will try to nudge Jacky to provide update at least every two weeks so everyone could get the latest status since it's already delayed.

Hopefully this update gave the best transparency for this Group Buy and thanks for everyone's patience and understanding! 

(Personally I'd wish to get a great QC'ed product than a defect one, so I'm really glad Jacky took the time to make sure the final finish of Piggy passing the high standard QC, but I also understood everyone's expecting to get the Piggy before Christmas and it's not likely to happen now
