Hey guys, R8 GB is now live and it's expected to have a quick turnaround.

GB pricing will end Nov 15, 2017 at 23:59 PST. Projected shipping for the buy is end of November - early December. Zeal60 PCBs might take a bit longer to mid December.

A few notable changes & improvements have been made based on customer feedback.

- 67g spring has been updated to make it a bit more snappy than previous rounds. 67g spring acoustics have also been improved. Customers that loved our 67g weight will love the changes even more.

- 78g spring has been updated to give it a crisper/sharper tactile bump, similar to 67g. It's now very snappy and those that loved 67g will like the feedback provided by our new 78g Zealios. The bump is now very bold and distinct. Sharp. Crisp. Tactile.

- Zeal60 Rev 3 will now feature a gold plated Mini USB port! This is most likely industry first, as I have never encountered another device with a gold plated Mini USB. PCB color will be dark purple this time, with a black edges. I expect the final product to look very sharp. Will provide pictures once samples are produced!
