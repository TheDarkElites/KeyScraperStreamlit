Group Buy for E6-V2 Custom 60% Keyboard.
    E6-V2



    CHECK YOUR ORDERS HERE: https://goo.gl/ECrWvU

    Hello everyone,
    This keyboard is designed and made by E-Team Mech Key (This team is the co-operation between Vietnamese and Chinese).

    Interest Checks topic: https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=90507.0

    Specifications:
    • Anodized aluminum 6063 case
    • Classic design
    • 8 degrees slope
    • Curved top 
    • Anodized aluminum or polished brass Counterweight and Middle piece
    • Stainless Steel, Carbon Fiber, Polycarbonate and Aluminum are available for switches plate selection
    • Custom engraved text on the top badge (Name Plate)
    • Layouts supported: ISO and ANSI (6.25u, 7u spacebar and HHKB layout) | Both stepped and normal Caps Lock is supported
    • Fully Programmable using Bootmapper Client or QMK (32u4)
    • RGB bottom side LEDs
    • Available colors: Red, Black, Silver, Blue, Purple. Special color: White anodized and White micro-arc oxidation (These two colors are more expensive a little bit)

    THERE ARE TWO DIFFERENT VERSIONS OF E6-V2:
    • Custom PCB with middle USB port (The case and the PCB are smaller, they look more beautiful in my opinion. PCB will be available after the GB. You can buy it anytime on our website.)
    • Standard 60% PCB with left-sided USB port  (You can either use any standard 60% PCB or buy our PCB) | Photo render here: http://i.imgur.com/WqAvhdC.jpg
