I will keep this GB post short and sweet. Any renders and or information has most likely been already posted in the original IC. I will mainly be using this post as a source of information on the updates of the current GB running for ePBT dreamscape.


GB will run through June 15th-July 15th

Vendors:
CA: https://www.ashkeebs.com/
US: https://vala.supply/
OCE:https://dailyclack.com/
SEA:https://ilumkb.com/
EU:https://candykeys.com/
CN:https://kbdfans.com/
KR:https://swagkeys.com/
PH:https://zionstudios.ph/
UK:https://prototypist.net/

Pricing Per Region:

Please note the prices may vary slightly per corresponding vendor and whats listed above.

Lead Times:
Currently I have been given an estimate of around 6 months by ePBT, however due to the large amount of orders and the number of different colors I believe an approximate 8 month lead time would be more realistic (~March 2022).

As of creating this post MOQ was met within the first couple of hours on release, so everything will be made :]
Additionally I want to make it clear, currently I don't believe that the color samples/prototypes will arrive before the GB date as I asked for an update a couple of days ago and was given nothing. If you are really concerned about color (which I would be considering that its 7 different colors) I would heavily advise you purchase the set on extras sales. I understand this might not be the most uplifting message about the set, however I want to remain as transparent with everyone as possible, I hope you understand my situation. :]

Update 1 9/15/21:
It has been approximately 2 months since the end of the group buy for dreamscape and so I thought I would do one mass update including the collabs as well. So first off lets start off with obviously epbt dreamscape; unfortunately we are still awaiting prototypes due to the large queue for EPBT, however desk mats have already been prototyped, I have yet to receive them in the mail yet, but I will be uploading some pictures when I do. Now for the collaborations:

Luxe Cables: Possible delays concerning the ETA of the cables, mainly due to the natural disasters happening causing flooding and other issues.

Crafted by Annie: These gorgeous caps have already been finished a while ago. I want a second run :C

Wuque Studio (Ikki x Dreamscape board): Undergoing color matching

Amusing Atelier: Should be shipped out within 2 weeks for those who participated as I believe production is wrapping up

Miroticaps: on track for the estimated shipping date of end of September

Honey Bunz Creations: 5 more weeks left before these beautiful things are ready to be sent out.

Latrialum: I'm not sure when they’re going to be raffled out, but when I do. I’ll let yall know

Obviously, not everything is set in stone, things are bound to happen and ETA’s will change accordingly, however I will do my best to keep you guys updated.
