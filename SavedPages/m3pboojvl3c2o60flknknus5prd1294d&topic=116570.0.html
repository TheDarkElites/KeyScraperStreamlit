UPDATE:

So production has finished and I am disappointed to say the least.

After 2 years of a slow color matching process, it turned out to be useless. It was already not going very smoothly, but the final production color is completely different from any sample I have received before. The legends turned almost Bordeaux red, the blue is very bright.

MilkyWay states that during the color matching process, they were changing molds in order to produce better quality keycaps. Due to that process change, colors were not able to be reproduced. They are aware of the issue and are dealing with the problem. I am very sorry for the people who bought the set and received something different from expected.

For people considering buying extras, I have asked MilkyWay to update the product page with images of the actual set. The current renders are not close to the final product.
