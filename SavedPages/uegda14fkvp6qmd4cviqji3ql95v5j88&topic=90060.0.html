    UK78

    Inspired by the XT and 1800 layouts the UK78 is a 60%+Numpad fully programmable keyboard.



    UK78 features:

    • ISO AND ANSI Supported Supported Layouts
    • MX and Alps Compatible!
    • Custom PCB that can be cut to a 65% or 60%, restistors, diodes and MCU presoldered as standard with limited DIY bare PCBs available by request
    • Underglow lighting and switch LED lighting
    • A single piece aluminium case with acrylic 'vanity' base to view the pcb
    • 5 degree incline achieved by custom CNC'd aluminium feet or a full width aluminium base
    • Stainless Steel/Aluminium/Brass plate in Universal MX (_t:2 cherry pcb stabs)and Alps(_t:4 cherry pcb stabs) layouts with the option for specific layouts to be requested(increased time and cost)

    Parts needed for a full kit:
    • Top case
    • PCB
    • Plate
    • For the bottom either:
      • Separate Feet(acrylic diffuser recommended)
      • Fullsize Base(acrylic diffuser optional)
      • Acrylic Diffuser/Base (for no incline base)


    Colours Available:  RAL9005(Jet Black-CR), Pantone 533 C(Dark Blue), Pantone 446C(Dark Grey-N9), RAL2004(Orange) and Silver/Natural
    1800 Style                                                           Fjell Style

    Pair of feet(with acrylic base)                               Fullsize Base

    Pricing:(Initial price based on an MOQ 20, incl. UK customs charge)
    • Case Top
      • Fjell style top case: £125
      • 1800 style top case:£110
    • Base/Feet
      • Separate Feet: £70
      • Fullsize Base: £170
    • PCB: £45
    • Plate:
      • SS/Alu: £20
      • Brass: £30
    • Acrylic base/diffuser: £6
    • Shipping Estimate:
      • UK: £15-20
      • USA: £35-40
      • Asia: £40-45
      • Europe: £25-30
