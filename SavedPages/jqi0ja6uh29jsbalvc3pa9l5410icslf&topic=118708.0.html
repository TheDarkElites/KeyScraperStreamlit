Kitsune

Kitsune FCFS international GB is live over at Mechkey:

https://mechkey.store/blogs/store-updates/kitsune-international-gb





Discord
Instagram

Typing Tests

Proto 1 - Top Mount
Proto 2 - Top Mount 
ScuffedBoards Typing Test of Proto 2
LightningXI’s VOD of Proto 1

Group Buy Information

Group Buy Date: November 4th (7AM PDT start)
Group Buy Type: FCFS via Google Form on Mechkey
Unit cap: 60 units

What's Included

- Aluminum Top and Bottom Case
- External Brass Weight or Aluminum Weight
- Aluminum Plate
- Mechlovin’ Infinity 87.5 PCB (Soldered)
- Hard Carrying Case & Microfiber cloth
- Geon Feet
- Screws: x8 M2.5 and x12 m2

Pricing

Kit Price (USD):
- Kitsune Aluminum Kit: $400
- Kitsune Brass Kit: $430

Extra Price (USD):
- Extra External Brass Weight: $80
- Extra External Aluminum Weight: $60
- Extra Mechlovin' Infinity PCB: $50 (max 1 additional PCB per GB Order)
- Extra Aluminum Plate: $50
- Extra PC Plate: $45

Specs

Mount: Top and Gummy O-Ring
- Layout: F13 TKL | WKL
- PCB: Infinity 87.5 variant from Mechlovin'
- Materials:
    o Top and Bottom Case: Aluminum 6063
    o Weight: Clear Coated Brass or Aluminum 6063
    o Plate: Aluminum 5052 (All plates are color matched to the top and bottom case.
Excluding the Silver kit – which comes with a red plate), Polycarbonate | ANSI and ISO support
- Colors:
    o Top and Bottom Case: Anodized Silver, Burgundy & Black
    o Weight: Clear Coated Brass and Aluminum weight color options (Black, Silver, Gray, Burgundy and Red)
- Top arrow cluster engraving: Hiragana or no engraving options
- Heft: 1.6kg (Alu Weight Configuration), 2.1kg (Brass Weight Configuration), 2.48kg built
- Adjusted Front Height(s) & Typing Angle:



- Supported Layouts:


Aluminum & PC Plate renders:



Shipping

Shipping will be conducted by Mechkey and it will be dispatched from Vietnam to the US via DHL for approximately $50-60.
International shipping may differ and should be asked within Mechkey's Discord.

Lead Time

Approximately 3-4 Months (I’m considering leeway for manufacturing delays, transportation to vendor(s), QC, and packing included in this timeline)
