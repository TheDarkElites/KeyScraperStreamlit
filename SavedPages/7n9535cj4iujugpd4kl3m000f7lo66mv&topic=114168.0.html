THERA 75



Hello Geekhack,

    This is our third Groupbuy of Createkeebs, called Thera75 based on the Island of Thera. This board is different as it is Plateless and Gasket Mounted, but also has leaf spring cuts on the PCB. This board is trying to achieve amazing typing experience at great value. A current price of $260 USD for one full kit, making it a great budget high end board.
