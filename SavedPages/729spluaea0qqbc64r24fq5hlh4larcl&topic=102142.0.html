Included in this set:
  • Aluminium top housing (Aluminum 6061) in black, gray or silver
  • Bottom housing (11 degree angle)
  • MX Reinforcement Plate (stainless steel, aluminum or plastic)
  • PCB presoldered (resistors, diodes, smd leds, etc, but not switches)
  • Fixing bolts and rubber pads

Info:
  • Limit 100 sets
  • Case has an angle of 11 degrees for the aluminum bottom
  • Full keymapping
  • Macro settings
  • Supports MX switches
  • Full LED and RGB SMD LED compatible
  • Switches and stabilizers are not included (Requires Cherry PCB mounted stabilizers)
  • Supports centred caps lock or stepped caps lock

Yeah, confusing video. He runs his fingernails over it, which leaves a layer of fingernail on the surface. Effectively, the surface of the board is sanding his nail (as it is harder than a finger nail). This happens now if you do it on most boards. I accidentally do it on my black eagle all the time. It just wipes off. You're not actually scratching the anodization.

The trouble people generally have with Gray anno is streaking. This is the real 'risk' of the color, but I would trust that Duck wouldn't send out poorly anodized products.
