Trailer 2:

Hi everyone,
We are psychetype and we would like to introduce our first project, the Navi60.







The Navi60 is a 60% top mount/o-ring mount with a full CNC aluminum case and brass weight.

This project is a part of the #lainttl  (time to live) program. We aim to provide a high quality keyboard for both keyboard and Lain enthusiasts. https://www.nbcuni.co.jp/rondorobe/anime/lain/ttl/

Design:

Its design is inspired by Abe’s original design of Lain’s personal Navi computer/keyboard. The kit comes with a two-piece case with a brass weight that is engraved with the Copland OS logo.







Navi60 Specifications
(Case + PCB designed by Neil from DeskDaily)
•7° typing angle
•Effective Keyboard Height (EKH) = 22.94mm
•Case weight (including Brass weight) = 914g
•Case material: 6063 Aluminum w/ Brass Weight
•Brass weight sandblasted + anti-fingerprint/anti-tarnish coating
•Hardware Materials: 18-8 Stainless Steel (case screws/daughterboard screws)
•Mounting support: Top mount/O-ring mount
•Plate Material: FR4, Aluminum, POM

PCB Specifications:
•PCB Thickness: 1.6mm
•Unified Daughterboard includes ESD protection + fuse: https://github.com/Unified-Daughterboard/UDB-C-Legacy
•QMK/VIA supported
•PCB-mount stabilizer support




Case color options:

Red Anode


Green Anode


Silver Anode


Black Anode


E-Beige


PCB options:
•ANSI hotswap
•ISO hotswap
•Solder
Layout options:

Hotswap ANSI/ISO (not inclusive)


Solder (Supports ANSI/ISO/JIS, split backspace, split shifts, Tsangan bottom row, split 3u-1u-3u space, 7u space, stepped caps lock)


New plate design on the left, old design on the right. Both files will be available in the Discord during GB. Split-spaces and Minila space will require clip-in stabs


What's Included:
•Two-Piece CNC Aluminum case w/ Brass weight•
•PCB
•Plate
•Unified Daughterboard w/ JST Cable
•Plate foam
•USB-C cable
•Medium size O-ring from Graveshift (40A stiffness)
•Keycap/Switch puller
•3M Rubber feet + Screws
•2mm hex key + 1.5mm hex key
•Durock v2 stabilizers (smokey black kit with 2 additional 3u wires)
•psychetype pin
•psychetype sticker pack (art from Orange of OranArt Studio)




Box art by mitiminami777

Short announcement as Navis are already starting to arrive for some:

When you receive your package, you should have received the main keyboard box containing the case, PCB(s), and plate(s) as well as a separate accessory box.

Please confirm your accessory box has the following essential items:
  • Durock v2 stabilizer kit
  • Daughterboard
  • Daughterboard screws + rubber feet + JST cable
  • Plate screws
  • O-ring
  • Plate foam
If you are missing any of these items, you can email support@psychety.pe or join our Discord and open a ticket: https://discord.gg/DFGrQAtfnc. You can also message us on Geekhack. Then we can ship any missing item directly to you. If you are missing any of the extra items (stickers, pin, hex key, usb cable) you can send us a message for these as well, of course

Vendors closer to China will receive Navis soon while the rest of the vendors will probably receive them in May or June by the latest. If you need to change your address, please do so now by contacting your vendor. If you live in/near the Asia region, you can expect the boards to arrive from April to early May. For the rest of the vendors, I would expect the boards to arrive from May to early June.

And lastly, thank you all for your support and patience, we are finally at the last stages!!
