Introducing the Group Buy for:

Paw

A project by dingusxmcgee and KnoblesseOblige
Photo credit to Hoodrow Thrillson


Paw is an arrow cluster macropad, aimed at expanding any desk setup with increased functionality, sleek design and convenience.
With onboard Bluetooth, brass accents and a sandblasted polycarbonate case, Paw will be a standout piece no matter where you use it.

More pics by Hoodrow and Even more pics by Hoodrow

Short Demo Video
https://imgur.com/a/h3HnCPl


Specs
Sandblasted Polycarbonate Top and Bottom(frosted)
Sandblasted, Top mount Brass plate
Sandblasted Brass weight
Approximately 81 grams built
4 Onboard RGB LEDs
PCB includes ESD protection and optional Hotswap
Onboard Bluetooth functionality
Note: Due to licensing conflicts, Paw will not ship, nor be compatible with, main QMK. It will ship with ZMK, and Bluetooth-first firmware that has more permissive licensing and better battery life optimizations. A QMK fork will be available to self-compile if you would like to pursue that method. Please note that ZMK will not have feature parity with QMK, though it is being actively developed and maintained. You can find more information here: https://zmkfirmware.dev
150mah battery (approximate usage rates: 30 days of active use and 60 days standby)
Note: values are approximate and subject to change based on usage and firmware used

Quote from: bisoromi on Mon, 09 November 2020, 10:21:10
Can you turn it off when not in use?

We had a on/off switch on the prototypes, but had issues getting good fit with the switch so that it was switchable with a finger. Because of this and to simplify the pcb and case, we removed the switch. So far, battery life has been amazing(with qmk BLE or ZMK), lasting over a month on a charge. We dont think there will be any issue with removing the power switch(battery will be connected to PCB by a JST connector).
