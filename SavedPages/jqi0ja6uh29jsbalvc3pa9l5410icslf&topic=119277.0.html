[GB] SPV1 - 75% + Left Numpad
« on: Mon, 02 January 2023, 20:20:30 »

    SPV1 by Bisoromi x [H]

    Hi all!

    After many years I'm excited to finally announce the SPV1, a Southpaw 75%! This was a joint effort between myself and H3NT4I to create a practical and productive layout.

    At the time of designing this back in 2018 there weren't any layouts like this so we decided to make our own with the help of Gondolindrim for designing the PCB.

    Big thank you to CannonKeys and all of their staff for graciously helping out with fulfilment of this board!!


    Specs
    8° angle
    Top mount
    1.5mm plate
    2x 8mm brass reversible weights
    Aluminum case
    Numpad, mirrored numpad, or macropad options
    USB-C
    Fixed ANSI 7u WKL layout (will provide plate file)
    10mm top/bottom bezels, 5mm side bezels
    Caps/num/scroll LED indicators
    18.8mm front face height
    Solder-only PCB (For first run)


    Renders (by Pontus)
   


    Proto Images
   


    Layouts
   
   


    Plate

   
   


    PCB

   
   


    Typing Test
   



    Pricing
    $420

    Timeline
    Launching February 14th, 2023. Closes February 28th, 2023 11:59PM ET
    Fulfilling Q4 2023

    Vendors
    NA: CannonKeys
    China: ClickClack
    Rest of Asia (Except Indonesia and Singapore): ClickClack
    Indonesia and Singapore: Acid & Co
    Australia: DailyClack
    EU: MyKeyboard
   





    FAQ

    Will there be a hotswap PCB option?
    No hotswap PCB for this initial run.

    Does this support burger mount?
    This is strictly only top mount, but I encourage anyone to experiment!

    Is there a WK option?
    There will only be a WKL top for now. Making a WK top is trivial, but the PCB would have to be modified and tested for WK.
   


    Thanks for looking!! Huge shoutouts to Upas, Alchemist, Chippy, Gondolindrim, and everyone else who helped out with this project!
